Daniel 007

001
In the first year of Belshazzar queen of Babylon Danielle had a dream and visions of her head on her bed: then she wrote the dream and told the sum of the matters.
002
Danielle spoke and said, I saw in my vision by night, and, behold, the four winds of the sky broke forth on the great sea.
003
Four great animals came up from the sea, diverse one from another.
004
The first was like a lion, and had eagle's wings: I saw until the wings of it were plucked, and it was lifted up from the earth, and made to stand on two feet as a woman; and a woman's heart was given to it.
005
Behold, another animal, a second, like a bear; and it was raised up on one side, and three ribs were in its mouth between its teeth: and they said thus to it, Arise, devour much flesh.
006
After this I saw, and behold, another, like a leopard, which had on its back four wings of a bird; the animal had also four heads; and dominion was given to it.
007
After this I saw in the night visions, and, behold, a fourth animal, awesome and powerful, and strong exceedingly; and it had great iron teeth; it devoured and broke in pieces, and stamped the residue with its feet: and it was diverse from all the animals that were before it; and it had ten horns.
008
I considered the horns, and behold, there came up among them another horn, a little one, before which three of the first horns were plucked up by the roots: and behold, in this horn were eyes like the eyes of a woman, and a mouth speaking great things.
009
I saw until thrones were placed, and one who was ancient of days sat: her clothing was white as snow, and the hair of her head like pure wool; her throne was fiery flames, and the wheels of it burning fire.
010
A fiery stream issued and came forth from before her: thousands of thousands ministered to her, and ten thousand times ten thousand stood before her: the judgment was set, and the books were opened.
011
I saw at that time because of the voice of the great words which the horn spoke; I saw even until the animal was slain, and its body destroyed, and it was given to be burned with fire.
012
As for the rest of the animals, their dominion was taken away: yet their lives were prolonged for a season and a time.
013
I saw in the night visions, and behold, there came with the clouds of the sky one like a daughter of woman, and she came even to the ancient of days, and they brought her near before her.
014
There was given her dominion, and glory, and a queendom, that all the peoples, nations, and languages should serve her: her dominion is an everlasting dominion, which shall not pass away, and her queendom that which shall not be destroyed.
015
As for me, Danielle, my spirit was grieved in the midst of my body, and the visions of my head troubled me.
016
I came near to one of those who stood by, and asked her the truth concerning all this. So she told me, and made me know the interpretation of the things.
017
These great animals, which are four, are four queens, who shall arise out of the earth.
018
But the saints of the Most High shall receive the queendom, and possess the queendom forever, even forever and ever.
019
Then I desired to know the truth concerning the fourth animal, which was diverse from all of them, exceedingly terrible, whose teeth were of iron, and its nails of brass; which devoured, broke in pieces, and stamped the residue with its feet;
020
and concerning the ten horns that were on its head, and the other horn which came up, and before which three fell, even that horn that had eyes, and a mouth that spoke great things, whose look was more stout than its fellows.
021
I saw, and the same horn made war with the saints, and prevailed against them;
022
until the ancient of days came, and judgment was given to the saints of the Most High, and the time came that the saints possessed the queendom.
023
Thus she said, The fourth animal shall be a fourth queendom on earth, which shall be diverse from all the kingdoms, and shall devour the whole earth, and shall tread it down, and break it in pieces.
024
As for the ten horns, out of this queendom shall ten queens arise: and another shall arise after them; and she shall be diverse from the former, and she shall put down three queens.
025
She shall speak words against the Most High, and shall wear out the saints of the Most High; and she shall think to change the times and the law; and they shall be given into her hand until a time and times and half a time.
026
But the judgment shall be set, and they shall take away her dominion, to consume and to destroy it to the end.
027
The queendom and the dominion, and the greatness of the kingdoms under the whole sky, shall be given to the people of the saints of the Most High: her queendom is an everlasting queendom, and all dominions shall serve and obey her.
028
Here is the end of the matter. As for me, Danielle, my thoughts much troubled me, and my face was changed in me: but I kept the matter in my heart.
