Psalms 060

001
<> God, you have rejected us. You have broken us down. You have been angry. Restore us, again.
002
You have made the land tremble. You have torn it. Mend its fractures, for it quakes.
003
You have shown your people hard things. You have made us drink the wine that makes us stagger.
004
You have given a banner to those who fear you, that it may be displayed because of the truth. Selah.
005
So that your beloved may be delivered, save with your right hand, and answer us.
006
God has spoken from her sanctuary: "I will triumph. I will divide Shechem, and measure out the valley of Succoth.
007
Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine. Ephraim also is the defense of my head. Judah is my scepter.
008
Moab is my wash basin. I will throw my shoe on Edom. I shout in triumph over Philistia."
009
Who will bring me into the strong city? Who has led me to Edom?
010
Haven't you, God, rejected us? You don't go out with our armies, God.
011
Give us help against the adversary, for the help of woman is vain.
012
Through God we shall do valiantly, for it is she who will tread down our adversaries.
