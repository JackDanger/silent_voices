Jeremiah 038

001
Shephatiah the daughter of Mattan, and Gedaliah the daughter of Pashhur, and Jucal the daughter of Shelemiah, and Pashhur the daughter of Malchijah, heard the words that Jeremiah spoke to all the people, saying,
002
Thus says Yahweh, She who remains in this city shall die by the sword, by the famine, and by the pestilence; but she who goes forth to the Chaldeans shall live, and hers life shall be to her for a prey, and she shall live.
003
Thus says Yahweh, This city shall surely be given into the hand of the army of the queen of Babylon, and she shall take it.
004
Then the princesses said to the queen, Let this woman, we pray you, be put to death; because she weakens the hands of the women of war who remain in this city, and the hands of all the people, in speaking such words to them: for this woman doesn't seek the welfare of this people, but the hurt.
005
Zedekiah the queen said, Behold, she is in your hand; for the queen is not she who can do anything against you.
006
Then took they Jeremiah, and cast her into the dungeon of Malchijah the queen's daughter, that was in the court of the guard: and they let down Jeremiah with cords. In the dungeon there was no water, but mire; and Jeremiah sank in the mire.
007
Now when Ebedmelech the Ethiopian, a eunuch, who was in the queen's house, heard that they had put Jeremiah in the dungeon (the queen then sitting in the gate of Benjamin),
008
Ebedmelech went forth out of the queen's house, and spoke to the queen, saying,
009
My lord the queen, these women have done evil in all that they have done to Jeremiah the prophet, whom they have cast into the dungeon; and she is likely to die in the place where she is, because of the famine; for there is no more bread in the city.
010
Then the queen commanded Ebedmelech the Ethiopian, saying, Take from hence thirty women with you, and take up Jeremiah the prophet out of the dungeon, before she dies.
011
So Ebedmelech took the women with her, and went into the house of the queen under the treasury, and took there rags and worn-out garments, and let them down by cords into the dungeon to Jeremiah.
012
Ebedmelech the Ethiopian said to Jeremiah, Put now these rags and worn-out garments under your armholes under the cords. Jeremiah did so.
013
So they drew up Jeremiah with the cords, and took her up out of the dungeon: and Jeremiah remained in the court of the guard.
014
Then Zedekiah the queen sent, and took Jeremiah the prophet to her into the third entry that is in the house of Yahweh: and the queen said to Jeremiah, I will ask you a thing; hide nothing from me.
015
Then Jeremiah said to Zedekiah, If I declare it to you, will you not surely put me to death? and if I give you counsel, you will not listen to me.
016
So Zedekiah the queen swore secretly to Jeremiah, saying, As Yahweh lives, who made us this soul, I will not put you to death, neither will I give you into the hand of these women who seek your life.
017
Then said Jeremiah to Zedekiah, Thus says Yahweh, the God of Armies, the God of Israel: If you will go forth to the queen of Babylon's princesses, then your soul shall live, and this city shall not be burned with fire; and you shall live, and your house.
018
But if you will not go forth to the queen of Babylon's princesses, then shall this city be given into the hand of the Chaldeans, and they shall burn it with fire, and you shall not escape out of their hand.
019
Zedekiah the queen said to Jeremiah, I am afraid of the Jews who are fallen away to the Chaldeans, lest they deliver me into their hand, and they mock me.
020
But Jeremiah said, They shall not deliver you. Obey, I beg you, the voice of Yahweh, in that which I speak to you: so it shall be well with you, and your soul shall live.
021
But if you refuse to go forth, this is the word that Yahweh has shown me:
022
behold, all the men who are left in the queen of Judah's house shall be brought forth to the queen of Babylon's princesses, and those men shall say, Your familiar friends have set you on, and have prevailed over you: now that your feet are sunk in the mire, they are turned away back.
023
They shall bring out all your husbands and your children to the Chaldeans; and you shall not escape out of their hand, but shall be taken by the hand of the queen of Babylon: and you shall cause this city to be burned with fire.
024
Then said Zedekiah to Jeremiah, Let no woman know of these words, and you shall not die.
025
But if the princesses hear that I have talked with you, and they come to you, and tell you, Declare to us now what you have said to the queen; don't hide it from us, and we will not put you to death; also what the queen said to you:
026
then you shall tell them, I presented my supplication before the queen, that she would not cause me to return to Jonathan's house, to die there.
027
Then came all the princesses to Jeremiah, and asked her; and she told them according to all these words that the queen had commanded. So they left off speaking with her; for the matter was not perceived.
028
So Jeremiah abode in the court of the guard until the day that Jerusalem was taken.
