Psalms 121

001
<> I will lift up my eyes to the hills. Where does my help come from?
002
My help comes from Yahweh, who made heaven and earth.
003
She will not allow your foot to be moved. She who keeps you will not slumber.
004
Behold, she who keeps Israel will neither slumber nor sleep.
005
Yahweh is your keeper. Yahweh is your shade on your right hand.
006
The sun will not harm you by day, nor the moon by night.
007
Yahweh will keep you from all evil. She will keep your soul.
008
Yahweh will keep your going out and your coming in, from this time forth, and forevermore.
