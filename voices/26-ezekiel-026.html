Ezekiel 026

Ezekiel 027 »
001
It happened in the eleventh year, in the first day of the month, that the word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
002
Daughter of woman, because Tyre has said against Jerusalem, Aha, he is broken: the gate of the peoples; he is turned to me; I shall be replenished, now that he is laid waste:
003
therefore thus says the Lord Yahweh, Behold, I am against you, Tyre, and will cause many nations to come up against you, as the sea causes its waves to come up.
004
They shall destroy the walls of Tyre, and break down his towers: I will also scrape his dust from him, and make him a bare rock.
005
He shall be a place for the spreading of nets in the midst of the sea; for I have spoken it, says the Lord Yahweh; and he shall become a spoil to the nations.
006
his sons who are in the field shall be slain with the sword: and they shall know that I am Yahweh.
007
For thus says the Lord Yahweh: Behold, I will bring on Tyre Nebuchadnezzar queen of Babylon, queen of queens, from the north, with horses, and with chariots, and with horsemen, and a company, and much people.
008
She shall kill with the sword your sons in the field; and she shall make forts against you, and cast up a mound against you, and raise up the buckler against you.
009
She shall set her battering engines against your walls, and with her axes she shall break down your towers.
010
By reason of the abundance of her horses their dust shall cover you: your walls shall shake at the noise of the horsemen, and of the wagons, and of the chariots, when she shall enter into your gates, as women enter into a city in which is made a breach.
011
With the hoofs of her horses shall she tread down all your streets; she shall kill your people with the sword; and the pillars of your strength shall go down to the ground.
012
They shall make a spoil of your riches, and make a prey of your merchandise; and they shall break down your walls, and destroy your pleasant houses; and they shall lay your stones and your timber and your dust in the midst of the waters.
013
I will cause the noise of your songs to cease; and the sound of your harps shall be no more heard.
014
I will make you a bare rock; you shall be a place for the spreading of nets; you shall be built no more: for I Yahweh have spoken it, says the Lord Yahweh.
015
Thus says the Lord Yahweh to Tyre: shall not the islands shake at the sound of your fall, when the wounded groan, when the slaughter is made in the midst of you?
016
Then all the princesses of the sea shall come down from their thrones, and lay aside their robes, and strip off their embroidered garments: they shall clothe themselves with trembling; they shall sit on the ground, and shall tremble every moment, and be astonished at you.
017
They shall take up a lamentation over you, and tell you, How are you destroyed, who were inhabited by seafaring women, the renowned city, who was strong in the sea, he and his inhabitants, who caused their terror to be on all who lived there!
018
Now shall the islands tremble in the day of your fall; yes, the islands that are in the sea shall be dismayed at your departure.
019
For thus says the Lord Yahweh: When I shall make you a desolate city, like the cities that are not inhabited; when I shall bring up the deep on you, and the great waters shall cover you;
020
then will I bring you down with those who descend into the pit, to the people of old time, and will make you to dwell in the lower parts of the earth, in the places that are desolate of old, with those who go down to the pit, that you be not inhabited; and I will set glory in the land of the living:
021
I will make you a terror, and you shall no more have any being; though you are sought for, yet shall you never be found again, says the Lord Yahweh.
