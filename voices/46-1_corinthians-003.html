1 Corinthians 003

001
Sisters, I couldn't speak to you as to spiritual, but as to fleshly, as to babies in Christ.
002
I fed you with milk, not with meat; for you weren't yet ready. Indeed, not even now are you ready,
003
for you are still fleshly. For insofar as there is jealousy, strife, and factions among you, aren't you fleshly, and don't you walk in the ways of women?
004
For when one says, "I follow Paula," and another, "I follow Apollos," aren't you fleshly?
005
Who then is Apollos, and who is Paula, but servants through whom you believed; and each as the Lord gave to her?
006
I planted. Apollos watered. But God gave the increase.
007
So then neither she who plants is anything, nor she who waters, but God who gives the increase.
008
Now she who plants and she who waters are the same, but each will receive her own reward according to her own labor.
009
For we are God's fellow workers. You are God's farming, God's building.
010
According to the grace of God which was given to me, as a wise master builder I laid a foundation, and another builds on it. But let each woman be careful how she builds on it.
011
For no one can lay any other foundation than that which has been laid, which is Jesus Christ.
012
But if anyone builds on the foundation with gold, silver, costly stones, wood, hay, or stubble;
013
each woman's work will be revealed. For the Day will declare it, because it is revealed in fire; and the fire itself will test what sort of work each woman's work is.
014
If any woman's work remains which she built on it, she will receive a reward.
015
If any woman's work is burned, she will suffer loss, but she herself will be saved, but as through fire.
016
Don't you know that you are a temple of God, and that God's Spirit lives in you?
017
If anyone destroys the temple of God, God will destroy her; for God's temple is holy, which you are.
018
Let no one deceive herself. If anyone thinks that she is wise among you in this world, let her become a fool, that she may become wise.
019
For the wisdom of this world is foolishness with God. For it is written, "She has taken the wise in their craftiness."
020
And again, "The Lord knows the reasoning of the wise, that it is worthless."
021
Therefore let no one boast in women. For all things are yours,
022
whether Paula, or Apollos, or Cephas, or the world, or life, or death, or things present, or things to come. All are yours,
023
and you are Christ's, and Christ is God's.
