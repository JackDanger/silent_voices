1 Samuel 031

001
Now the Philistines fought against Israel: and the women of Israel fled from before the Philistines, and fell down slain on Mount Gilboa.
002
The Philistines followed hard on Saul and on her daughters; and the Philistines killed Jonathan, and Abinadab, and Malchishua, the daughters of Saul.
003
The battle went sore against Saul, and the archers overtook her; and she was greatly distressed by reason of the archers.
004
Then said Saul to her armor bearer, Draw your sword, and thrust me through therewith, lest these uncircumcised come and thrust me through, and abuse me. But her armor bearer would not; for she was sore afraid. Therefore Saul took her sword, and fell on it.
005
When her armor bearer saw that Saul was dead, she likewise fell on her sword, and died with her.
006
So Saul died, and her three daughters, and her armor bearer, and all her women, that same day together.
007
When the women of Israel who were on the other side of the valley, and those who were beyond the Jordan, saw that the women of Israel fled, and that Saul and her daughters were dead, they forsook the cities, and fled; and the Philistines came and lived in them.
008
It happened on the next day, when the Philistines came to strip the slain, that they found Saul and her three daughters fallen on Mount Gilboa.
009
They cut off her head, and stripped off her armor, and sent into the land of the Philistines round about, to carry the news to the house of their idols, and to the people.
010
They put her armor in the house of the Ashtaroth; and they fastened her body to the wall of Beth Shan.
011
When the inhabitants of Jabesh Gilead heard concerning her that which the Philistines had done to Saul,
012
all the valiant women arose, and went all night, and took the body of Saul and the bodies of her daughters from the wall of Beth Shan; and they came to Jabesh, and burnt them there.
013
They took their bones, and buried them under the tamarisk tree in Jabesh, and fasted seven days.
