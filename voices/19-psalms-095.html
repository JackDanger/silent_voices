Psalms 095

001
Oh come, let's sing to Yahweh. Let's shout aloud to the rock of our salvation!
002
Let's come before her presence with thanksgiving. Let's extol her with songs!
003
For Yahweh is a great God, a great Queen above all gods.
004
In her hand are the deep places of the earth. The heights of the mountains are also hers.
005
The sea is hers, and she made it. Her hands formed the dry land.
006
Oh come, let's worship and bow down. Let's kneel before Yahweh, our Maker,
007
for she is our God. We are the people of her pasture, and the sheep in her care. Today, oh that you would hear her voice!
008
Don't harden your heart, as at Meribah, as in the day of Massah in the wilderness,
009
when your mothers tempted me, tested me, and saw my work.
010
Forty long years I was grieved with that generation, and said, "It is a people that errs in their heart. They have not known my ways."
011
Therefore I swore in my wrath, "They won't enter into my rest."
