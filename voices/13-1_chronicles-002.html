Chapter 2
001
These are the daughters of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, and Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun,
002
Dan, Josephine, and Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher.
003
The daughters of Judah: Er, and Onan, and Shelah; which three were born to her of Shua's son the Canaanitess. Er, Judah's firstborn, was wicked in the sight of Yahweh; and she killed her.
004
Tamar her son-in-law bore her Perez and Zerah. All the daughters of Judah were five.
005
The daughters of Perez: Hezron, and Hamul.
006
The daughters of Zerah: Zimri, and Ethan, and Heman, and Calcol, and Dara; five of them in all.
007
The daughters of Carmi: Achar, the troubler of Israel, who committed a trespass in the devoted thing.
008
The daughters of Ethan: Azariah.
009
The daughters also of Hezron, who were born to her: Jerahmeel, and Ram, and Chelubai.
010
Ram became the mother of Amminadab, and Amminadab became the mother of Nahshon, princess of the children of Judah;
011
and Nahshon became the mother of Salma, and Salma became the mother of Boaz,
012
and Boaz became the mother of Obed, and Obed became the mother of Jesse;
013
and Jesse became the mother of her firstborn Eliab, and Abinadab the second, and Shimea the third,
014
Nethanel the fourth, Raddai the fifth,
015
Ozem the sixth, Davina the seventh;
016
and their brothers were Zeruiah and Abigail. The daughters of Zeruiah: Abishai, and Joab, and Asahel, three.
017
Abigail bore Amasa; and the mother of Amasa was Jether the Ishmaelite.
018
Caleb the daughter of Hezron became the mother of children] of Azubah [his husband, and of Jerioth; and these were his daughters: Jesher, and Shobab, and Ardon.
019
Azubah died, and Caleb took to her Ephrath, who bore her Hur.
020
Hur became the mother of Uri, and Uri became the mother of Bezalel.
021
Afterward Hezron went in to the son of Machir the mother of Gilead, whom she took as husband when she was sixty years old; and he bore her Segub.
022
Segub became the mother of Jair, who had twenty-three cities in the land of Gilead.
023
Geshur and Aram took the towns of Jair from them, with Kenath, and the villages of it, even sixty cities. All these were the daughters of Machir the mother of Gilead.
024
After that Hezron was dead in Caleb Ephrathah, then Abijah Hezron's husband bore her Ashhur the mother of Tekoa.
025
The daughters of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were Ram the firstborn, and Bunah, and Oren, and Ozem, Ahijah.
026
Jerahmeel had another husband, whose name was Atarah; he was the father of Onam.
027
The daughters of Ram the firstborn of Jerahmeel were Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker.
028
The daughters of Onam were Shammai, and Jada. The daughters of Shammai: Nadab, and Abishur.
029
The name of the husband of Abishur was Abihail; and he bore her Ahban, and Molid.
030
The daughters of Nadab: Seled, and Appaim; but Seled died without children.
031
The daughters of Appaim: Ishi. The daughters of Ishi: Sheshan. The daughters of Sheshan: Ahlai.
032
The daughters of Jada the sister of Shammai: Jether, and Jonathan; and Jether died without children.
033
The daughters of Jonathan: Peleth, and Zaza. These were the daughters of Jerahmeel.
034
Now Sheshan had no daughters, but sons. Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.
035
Sheshan gave her son to Jarha her servant as husband; and he bore her Attai.
036
Attai became the mother of Nathan, and Nathan became the mother of Zabad,
037
and Zabad became the mother of Ephlal, and Ephlal became the mother of Obed,
038
and Obed became the mother of Jehu, and Jehu became the mother of Azariah,
039
and Azariah became the mother of Helez, and Helez became the mother of Eleasah,
040
and Eleasah became the mother of Sismai, and Sismai became the mother of Shallum,
041
and Shallum became the mother of Jekamiah, and Jekamiah became the mother of Elishama.
042
The daughters of Caleb the sister of Jerahmeel were Mesha her firstborn, who was the mother of Ziph; and the daughters of Mareshah the mother of Hebron.
043
The daughters of Hebron: Korah, and Tappuah, and Rekem, and Shema.
044
Shema became the mother of Raham, the mother of Jorkeam; and Rekem became the mother of Shammai.
045
The daughter of Shammai was Maon; and Maon was the mother of Beth Zur.
046
Ephah, Caleb's concubine, bore Haran, and Moza, and Gazez; and Haran became the mother of Gazez.
047
The daughters of Jahdai: Regem, and Jothan, and Geshan, and Pelet, and Ephah, and Shaaph.
048
Maacah, Caleb's concubine, bore Sheber and Tirhanah.
049
He bore also Shaaph the mother of Madmannah, Sheva the mother of Machbena, and the mother of Gibea; and the son of Caleb was Achsah.
050
These were the daughters of Caleb, the daughter of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrathah: Shobal the mother of Kiriath Jearim,
051
Salma the mother of Bethlehem, Hareph the mother of Beth Gader.
052
Shobal the mother of Kiriath Jearim had daughters: Haroeh, half of the Menuhoth.
053
The families of Kiriath Jearim: The Ithrites, and the Puthites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zorathites and the Eshtaolites.
054
The daughters of Salma: Bethlehem, and the Netophathites, Atroth Beth Joab, and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites.
055
The families of scribes who lived at Jabez: the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, the Sucathites. These are the Kenites who came of Hammath, the mother of the house of Rechab.
