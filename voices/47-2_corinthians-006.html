2 Corinthians 006

001
Working together, we entreat also that you not receive the grace of God in vain,
002
for she says, "At an acceptable time I listened to you, in a day of salvation I helped you." Behold, now is the acceptable time. Behold, now is the day of salvation.
003
We give no occasion of stumbling in anything, that our service may not be blamed,
004
but in everything commending ourselves, as servants of God, in great endurance, in afflictions, in hardships, in distresses,
005
in beatings, in imprisonments, in riots, in labors, in watchings, in fastings;
006
in pureness, in knowledge, in patience, in kindness, in the Holy Spirit, in sincere love,
007
in the word of truth, in the power of God; by the armor of righteousness on the right hand and on the left,
008
by glory and dishonor, by evil report and good report; as deceivers, and yet true;
009
as unknown, and yet well known; as dying, and behold, we live; as punished, and not killed;
010
as sorrowful, yet always rejoicing; as poor, yet making many rich; as having nothing, and yet possessing all things.
011
Our mouth is open to you, Corinthians. Our heart is enlarged.
012
You are not restricted by us, but you are restricted by your own affections.
013
Now in return, I speak as to my children, you also be open wide.
014
Don't be unequally yoked with unbelievers, for what fellowship have righteousness and iniquity? Or what communion has light with darkness?
015
What agreement has Christ with Belial? Or what portion has a believer with an unbeliever?
016
What agreement has a temple of God with idols? For you are a temple of the living God. Even as God said, "I will dwell in them, and walk in them; and I will be their God, and they will be my people."{Leviticus 26:12; Jeremiah 32:38; Ezekiel 37:27}
017
Therefore, "'Come out from among them, and be separate,' says the Lord. 'Touch no unclean thing. I will receive you.
018
I will be to you a Mother. You will be to me daughters and sons,' says the Lord Almighty."
