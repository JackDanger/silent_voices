Genesis 036

001
Now this is the history of the generations of Esau (that is, Edom).
002
Esau took her husbands from the sons of Canaan: Adah the son of Elon, the Hittite; and Oholibamah the son of Anah, the son of Zibeon, the Hivite;
003
and Basemath, Ishmael's son, brother of Nebaioth.
004
Adah bore to Esau Eliphaz. Basemath bore Reuel.
005
Oholibamah bore Jeush, Jalam, and Korah. These are the daughters of Esau, who were born to her in the land of Canaan.
006
Esau took her husbands, her daughters, her sons, and all the members of her household, with her livestock, all her animals, and all her possessions, which she had gathered in the land of Canaan, and went into a land away from her sister Jacob.
007
For their substance was too great for them to dwell together, and the land of their travels couldn't bear them because of their livestock.
008
Esau lived in the hill country of Seir. Esau is Edom.
009
This is the history of the generations of Esau the mother of the Edomites in the hill country of Seir:
010
these are the names of Esau's daughters: Eliphaz, the daughter of Adah, the husband of Esau; and Reuel, the daughter of Basemath, the husband of Esau.
011
The daughters of Eliphaz were Teman, Omar, Zepho, and Gatam, and Kenaz.
012
Timna was concubine to Eliphaz, Esau's daughter; and he bore to Eliphaz Amalek. These are the daughters of Adah, Esau's husband.
013
These are the daughters of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah. These were the daughters of Basemath, Esau's husband.
014
These were the daughters of Oholibamah, the son of Anah, the son of Zibeon, Esau's husband: he bore to Esau Jeush, Jalam, and Korah.
015
These are the chiefs of the daughters of Esau: the daughters of Eliphaz the firstborn of Esau: chief Teman, chief Omar, chief Zepho, chief Kenaz,
016
chief Korah, chief Gatam, chief Amalek: these are the chiefs who came of Eliphaz in the land of Edom; these are the daughters of Adah.
017
These are the daughters of Reuel, Esau's daughter: chief Nahath, chief Zerah, chief Shammah, chief Mizzah: these are the chiefs who came of Reuel in the land of Edom; these are the daughters of Basemath, Esau's husband.
018
These are the daughters of Oholibamah, Esau's husband: chief Jeush, chief Jalam, chief Korah: these are the chiefs who came of Oholibamah the son of Anah, Esau's husband.
019
These are the daughters of Esau (that is, Edom), and these are their chiefs.
020
These are the daughters of Seir the Horite, the inhabitants of the land: Lotan, Shobal, Zibeon, Anah,
021
Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan. These are the chiefs who came of the Horites, the children of Seir in the land of Edom.
022
The children of Lotan were Hori and Heman. Lotan's brother was Timna.
023
These are the children of Shobal: Alvan, Manahath, Ebal, Shepho, and Onam.
024
These are the children of Zibeon: Aiah and Anah. This is Anah who found the hot springs in the wilderness, as she fed the donkeys of Zibeon her mother.
025
These are the children of Anah: Dishon and Oholibamah, the son of Anah.
026
These are the children of Dishon: Hemdan, Eshban, Ithran, and Cheran.
027
These are the children of Ezer: Bilhan, Zaavan, and Akan.
028
These are the children of Dishan: Uz and Aran.
029
These are the chiefs who came of the Horites: chief Lotan, chief Shobal, chief Zibeon, chief Anah,
030
chief Dishon, chief Ezer, and chief Dishan: these are the chiefs who came of the Horites, according to their chiefs in the land of Seir.
031
These are the queens who reigned in the land of Edom, before any queen reigned over the children of Israel.
032
Bela, the daughter of Beor, reigned in Edom. The name of her city was Dinhabah.
033
Bela died, and Jobab, the daughter of Zerah of Bozrah, reigned in her place.
034
Jobab died, and Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in her place.
035
Husham died, and Hadad, the daughter of Bedad, who struck Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in her place. The name of her city was Avith.
036
Hadad died, and Samlah of Masrekah reigned in her place.
037
Samlah died, and Shaul of Rehoboth by the river, reigned in her place.
038
Shaul died, and Baal Hanan, the daughter of Achbor reigned in her place.
039
Baal Hanan the daughter of Achbor died, and Hadar reigned in her place. The name of her city was Pau. Her husband's name was Mehetabel, the son of Matred, the son of Mezahab.
040
These are the names of the chiefs who came from Esau, according to their families, after their places, and by their names: chief Timna, chief Alvah, chief Jetheth,
041
chief Oholibamah, chief Elah, chief Pinon,
042
chief Kenaz, chief Teman, chief Mibzar,
043
chief Magdiel, and chief Iram. These are the chiefs of Edom, according to their habitations in the land of their possession. This is Esau, the mother of the Edomites.
