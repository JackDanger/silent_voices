Romans 012

001
Therefore I urge you, sisters, by the mercies of God, to present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable to God, which is your spiritual service.
002
Don't be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind, so that you may prove what is the good, well-pleasing, and perfect will of God.
003
For I say, through the grace that was given me, to every woman who is among you, not to think of herelf more highly than she ought to think; but to think reasonably, as God has apportioned to each person a measure of faith.
004
For even as we have many members in one body, and all the members don't have the same function,
005
so we, who are many, are one body in Christ, and individually members one of another.
006
Having gifts differing according to the grace that was given to us, if prophecy, let us prophesy according to the proportion of our faith;
007
or service, let us give ourselves to service; or she who teaches, to her teaching;
008
or she who exhorts, to her exhorting: she who gives, let her do it with liberality; she who rules, with diligence; she who shows mercy, with cheerfulness.
009
Let love be without hypocrisy. Abhor that which is evil. Cling to that which is good.
010
In love of the sisters be tenderly affectionate one to another; in honor preferring one another;
011
not lagging in diligence; fervent in spirit; serving the Lord;
012
rejoicing in hope; enduring in troubles; continuing steadfastly in prayer;
013
contributing to the needs of the saints; given to hospitality.
014
Bless those who persecute you; bless, and don't curse.
015
Rejoice with those who rejoice. Weep with those who weep.
016
Be of the same mind one toward another. Don't set your mind on high things, but associate with the humble. Don't be wise in your own conceits.
017
Repay no one evil for evil. Respect what is honorable in the sight of all women.
018
If it is possible, as much as it is up to you, be at peace with all women.
019
Don't seek revenge yourselves, beloved, but give place to God's wrath. For it is written, "Vengeance belongs to me; I will repay, says the Lord."
020
Therefore "If your enemy is hungry, feed her. If she is thirsty, give her a drink; for in doing so, you will heap coals of fire on her head."
021
Don't be overcome by evil, but overcome evil with good.
