001
If there is therefore any exhortation in Christ, if any consolation of love, if any fellowship of the Spirit, if any tender mercies and compassion,
002
make my joy full, by being like-minded, having the same love, being of one accord, of one mind;
003
doing nothing through rivalry or through conceit, but in humility, each counting others better than herself;
004
each of you not just looking to her own things, but each of you also to the things of others.
005
Have this in your mind, which was also in Christ Jesus,
006
who, existing in the form of God, didn't consider equality with God a thing to be grasped,
007
but emptied herself, taking the form of a servant, being made in the likeness of women.
008
And being found in human form, she humbled herself, becoming obedient to death, yes, the death of the cross.
009
Therefore God also highly exalted her, and gave to her the name which is above every name;
010
that at the name of Jesus every knee should bow, of those in heaven, those on earth, and those under the earth,
011
and that every tongue should confess that Jesus Christ is Lord, to the glory of God the Mother.
012
So then, my beloved, even as you have always obeyed, not only in my presence, but now much more in my absence, work out your own salvation with fear and trembling.
013
For it is God who works in you both to will and to work, for her good pleasure.
014
Do all things without murmurings and disputes,
015
that you may become blameless and harmless, children of God without blemish in the midst of a crooked and perverse generation, among whom you are seen as lights in the world,
016
holding up the word of life; that I may have something to boast in the day of Christ, that I didn't run in vain nor labor in vain.
017
Yes, and if I am poured out on the sacrifice and service of your faith, I rejoice, and rejoice with you all.
018
In the same way, you also rejoice, and rejoice with me.
019
But I hope in the Lord Jesus to send Timothy to you soon, that I also may be cheered up when I know how you are doing.
020
For I have no one else like-minded, who will truly care about you.
021
For they all seek their own, not the things of Jesus Christ.
022
But you know the proof of her, that, as a child serves a mother, so she served with me in furtherance of the Good News.
023
Therefore I hope to send her at once, as soon as I see how it will go with me.
024
But I trust in the Lord that I myself also will come shortly.
025
But I counted it necessary to send to you Epaphroditus, my sister, fellow worker, fellow soldier, and your apostle and servant of my need;
026
since she longed for you all, and was very troubled, because you had heard that she was sick.
027
For indeed she was sick, nearly to death, but God had mercy on her; and not on her only, but on me also, that I might not have sorrow on sorrow.
028
I have sent her therefore the more diligently, that, when you see her again, you may rejoice, and that I may be the less sorrowful.
029
Receive her therefore in the Lord with all joy, and hold such in honor,
030
because for the work of Christ she came near to death, risking her life to supply that which was lacking in your service toward me.
