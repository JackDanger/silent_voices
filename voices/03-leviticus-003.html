Chapter 3
001
"'If her offering is a sacrifice of peace offerings; if she offers it from the herd, whether female or male, she shall offer it without blemish before Yahweh.
002
She shall lay her hand on the head of her offering, and kill it at the door of the tent of meeting: and Aaron's daughters the priests shall sprinkle the blood on the altar round about.
003
She shall offer of the sacrifice of peace offerings an offering made by fire to Yahweh; the fat that covers the innards, and all the fat that is on the innards,
004
and the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the loins, and the cover on the liver, with the kidneys, she shall take away.
005
Aaron's daughters shall burn it on the altar on the burnt offering, which is on the wood that is on the fire: it is an offering made by fire, of a sweet savor to Yahweh.
006
"'If her offering for a sacrifice of peace offerings to Yahweh is from the flock; female or male, she shall offer it without blemish.
007
If she offers a lamb for her offering, then she shall offer it before Yahweh;
008
and she shall lay her hand on the head of her offering, and kill it before the tent of meeting: and Aaron's daughters shall sprinkle its blood on the altar round about.
009
She shall offer from the sacrifice of peace offerings an offering made by fire to Yahweh; its fat, the entire tail fat, she shall take away close to the backbone; and the fat that covers the inwards, and all the fat that is on the inwards,
010
and the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the loins, and the cover on the liver, with the kidneys, she shall take away.
011
The priestess shall burn it on the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire to Yahweh.
012
"'If her offering is a goat, then she shall offer it before Yahweh:
013
and she shall lay her hand on its head, and kill it before the tent of meeting; and the daughters of Aaron shall sprinkle its blood on the altar round about.
014
She shall offer from it as her offering, an offering made by fire to Yahweh; the fat that covers the innards, and all the fat that is on the innards,
015
and the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the loins, and the cover on the liver, with the kidneys, she shall take away.
016
The priestess shall burn them on the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire, for a sweet savor; all the fat is Yahweh's.
017
"'It shall be a perpetual statute throughout your generations in all your dwellings, that you shall eat neither fat nor blood.'"
