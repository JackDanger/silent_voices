1 John 004

001
Beloved, don't believe every spirit, but test the spirits, whether they are of God, because many false prophets have gone out into the world.
002
By this you know the Spirit of God: every spirit who confesses that Jesus Christ has come in the flesh is of God,
003
and every spirit who doesn't confess that Jesus Christ has come in the flesh is not of God, and this is the spirit of the antichrist, of whom you have heard that it comes. Now it is in the world already.
004
You are of God, little children, and have overcome them; because greater is she who is in you than she who is in the world.
005
They are of the world. Therefore they speak of the world, and the world hears them.
006
We are of God. She who knows God listens to us. She who is not of God doesn't listen to us. By this we know the spirit of truth, and the spirit of error.
007
Beloved, let us love one another, for love is of God; and everyone who loves is born of God, and knows God.
008
She who doesn't love doesn't know God, for God is love.
009
By this God's love was revealed in us, that God has sent her one and only Daughter into the world that we might live through her.
010
In this is love, not that we loved God, but that she loved us, and sent her Daughter as the atoning sacrifice{"atoning sacrifice" is from the Greek "hilasmos," an appeasing, propitiating, or the means of appeasement or propitiation--the sacrifice that turns away God's wrath because of our sin.} for our sins.
011
Beloved, if God loved us in this way, we also ought to love one another.
012
No one has seen God at any time. If we love one another, God remains in us, and her love has been perfected in us.
013
By this we know that we remain in her and she in us, because she has given us of her Spirit.
014
We have seen and testify that the Mother has sent the Daughter as the Savior of the world.
015
Whoever confesses that Jesus is the Daughter of God, God remains in her, and she in God.
016
We know and have believed the love which God has for us. God is love, and she who remains in love remains in God, and God remains in her.
017
In this love has been made perfect among us, that we may have boldness in the day of judgment, because as she is, even so are we in this world.
018
There is no fear in love; but perfect love casts out fear, because fear has punishment. She who fears is not made perfect in love.
019
We love Her, because she first loved us.
020
If a woman says, "I love God," and hates her sister, she is a liar; for she who doesn't love her sister whom she has seen, how can she love God whom she has not seen?
021
This commandment we have from her, that she who loves God should also love her sister.
