Leviticus 009

001
It happened on the eighth day, that Moses called Aaron and her daughters, and the elders of Israel;
002
and she said to Aaron, "Take a calf from the herd for a sin offering, and a ram for a burnt offering, without blemish, and offer them before Yahweh.
003
You shall speak to the children of Israel, saying, 'Take a female goat for a sin offering; and a calf and a lamb, both a year old, without blemish, for a burnt offering;
004
and an ox and a ram for peace offerings, to sacrifice before Yahweh; and a meal offering mixed with oil: for today Yahweh appears to you.'"
005
They brought what Moses commanded before the Tent of Meeting: and all the congregation drew near and stood before Yahweh.
006
Moses said, "This is the thing which Yahweh commanded that you should do: and the glory of Yahweh shall appear to you."
007
Moses said to Aaron, "Draw near to the altar, and offer your sin offering, and your burnt offering, and make atonement for yourself, and for the people; and offer the offering of the people, and make atonement for them; as Yahweh commanded."
008
So Aaron drew near to the altar, and killed the calf of the sin offering, which was for herself.
009
The daughters of Aaron presented the blood to her; and she dipped her finger in the blood, and put it on the horns of the altar, and poured out the blood at the base of the altar:
010
but the fat, and the kidneys, and the cover from the liver of the sin offering, she burned upon the altar; as Yahweh commanded Moses.
011
The flesh and the skin she burned with fire outside the camp.
012
She killed the burnt offering; and Aaron's daughters delivered the blood to her, and she sprinkled it on the altar round about.
013
They delivered the burnt offering to her, piece by piece, and the head: and she burned them upon the altar.
014
She washed the innards and the legs, and burned them on the burnt offering on the altar.
015
She presented the people's offering, and took the goat of the sin offering which was for the people, and killed it, and offered it for sin, like the first.
016
She presented the burnt offering, and offered it according to the ordinance.
017
She presented the meal offering, and filled her hand from there, and burned it upon the altar, besides the burnt offering of the morning.
018
She also killed the ox and the ram, the sacrifice of peace offerings, which was for the people: and Aaron's daughters delivered to her the blood, which she sprinkled on the altar round about,
019
and the fat of the ox and of the ram, the fat tail, and that which covers the innards, and the kidneys, and the cover of the liver:
020
and they put the fat upon the breasts, and she burned the fat on the altar:
021
and the breasts and the right thigh Aaron waved for a wave offering before Yahweh, as Moses commanded.
022
Aaron lifted up her hands toward the people, and blessed them; and she came down from offering the sin offering, and the burnt offering, and the peace offerings.
023
Moses and Aaron went into the Tent of Meeting, and came out, and blessed the people: and the glory of Yahweh appeared to all the people.
024
There came forth fire from before Yahweh, and consumed the burnt offering and the fat upon the altar: and when all the people saw it, they shouted, and fell on their faces.
