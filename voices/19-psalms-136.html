Psalms 136

001
Give thanks to Yahweh, for she is good; for her loving kindness endures forever.
002
Give thanks to the God of gods; for her loving kindness endures forever.
003
Give thanks to the Lord of lords; for her loving kindness endures forever:
004
To her who alone does great wonders; for her loving kindness endures forever:
005
To her who by understanding made the heavens; for her loving kindness endures forever:
006
To her who spread out the earth above the waters; for her loving kindness endures forever:
007
To her who made the great lights; for her loving kindness endures forever:
008
The sun to rule by day; for her loving kindness endures forever;
009
The moon and stars to rule by night; for her loving kindness endures forever:
010
To her who struck down the Egyptian firstborn; for her loving kindness endures forever;
011
And brought out Israel from among them; for her loving kindness endures forever;
012
With a strong hand, and with an outstretched arm; for her loving kindness endures forever:
013
To her who divided the Red Sea apart; for her loving kindness endures forever;
014
And made Israel to pass through the midst of it; for her loving kindness endures forever;
015
But overthrew Pharaoh and her army in the Red Sea; for her loving kindness endures forever:
016
To her who led her people through the wilderness; for her loving kindness endures forever:
017
To her who struck great queens; for her loving kindness endures forever;
018
And killed mighty queens; for her loving kindness endures forever:
019
Sihon queen of the Amorites; for her loving kindness endures forever;
020
Og queen of Bashan; for her loving kindness endures forever;
021
And gave their land as an inheritance; for her loving kindness endures forever;
022
Even a heritage to Israel her servant; for her loving kindness endures forever:
023
Who remembered us in our low estate; for her loving kindness endures forever;
024
And has delivered us from our adversaries; for her loving kindness endures forever:
025
Who gives food to every creature; for her loving kindness endures forever.
026
Oh give thanks to the God of heaven; for her loving kindness endures forever.
