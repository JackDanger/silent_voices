Psalms 081

001
<> Sing aloud to God, our strength! Make a joyful shout to the God of Jacob!
002
Raise a song, and bring here the tambourine, the pleasant lyre with the harp.
003
Blow the trumpet at the New Moon, at the full moon, on our feast day.
004
For it is a statute for Israel, an ordinance of the God of Jacob.
005
She appointed it in Joseph for a testimony, when she went out over the land of Egypt, I heard a language that I didn't know.
006
"I removed her shoulder from the burden. Her hands were freed from the basket.
007
You called in trouble, and I delivered you. I answered you in the secret place of thunder. I tested you at the waters of Meribah." Selah.
008
"Hear, my people, and I will testify to you, Israel, if you would listen to me!
009
There shall be no strange god in you, neither shall you worship any foreign god.
010
I am Yahweh, your God, who brought you up out of the land of Egypt. Open your mouth wide, and I will fill it.
011
But my people didn't listen to my voice. Israel desired none of me.
012
So I let them go after the stubbornness of their hearts, that they might walk in their own counsels.
013
Oh that my people would listen to me, that Israel would walk in my ways!
014
I would soon subdue their enemies, and turn my hand against their adversaries.
015
The haters of Yahweh would cringe before her, and their punishment would last forever.
016
But she would have also fed them with the finest of the wheat. I will satisfy you with honey out of the rock."
