Psalms 094

001
Yahweh, you God to whom vengeance belongs, you God to whom vengeance belongs, shine forth.
002
Rise up, you judge of the earth. Pay back the proud what they deserve.
003
Yahweh, how long will the wicked, how long will the wicked triumph?
004
They pour out arrogant words. All the evil-doers boast.
005
They break your people in pieces, Yahweh, and afflict your heritage.
006
They kill the widow and the alien, and murder the fatherless.
007
They say, "Yah will not see, neither will Jacob's God consider."
008
Consider, you senseless among the people; you fools, when will you be wise?
009
She who implanted the ear, won't she hear? She who formed the eye, won't she see?
010
She who disciplines the nations, won't she punish? She who teaches woman knows.
011
Yahweh knows the thoughts of woman, that they are futile.
012
Blessed is the woman whom you discipline, Yah, and teach out of your law;
013
that you may give her rest from the days of adversity, until the pit is dug for the wicked.
014
For Yahweh won't reject her people, neither will she forsake her inheritance.
015
For judgment will return to righteousness. All the upright in heart shall follow it.
016
Who will rise up for me against the wicked? Who will stand up for me against the evil-doers?
017
Unless Yahweh had been my help, my soul would have soon lived in silence.
018
When I said, "My foot is slipping!" Your loving kindness, Yahweh, held me up.
019
In the multitude of my thoughts within me, your comforts delight my soul.
020
Shall the throne of wickedness have fellowship with you, which brings about mischief by statute?
021
They gather themselves together against the soul of the righteous, and condemn the innocent blood.
022
But Yahweh has been my high tower, my God, the rock of my refuge.
023
She has brought on them their own iniquity, and will cut them off in their own wickedness. Yahweh, our God, will cut them off.
