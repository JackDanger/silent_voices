Chapter 20
001
"For the Empire of Heaven is like a woman who was the master of a household, who went out early in the morning to hire laborers for her vineyard.
002
When she had agreed with the laborers for a denarius a day, she sent them into her vineyard.
003
She went out about the third hour, and saw others standing idle in the marketplace.
004
To them she said, 'You also go into the vineyard, and whatever is right I will give you.' So they went their way.
005
Again she went out about the sixth and the ninth hour, and did likewise.
006
About the eleventh hour she went out, and found others standing idle. She said to them, 'Why do you stand here all day idle?'
007
"They said to her, 'Because no one has hired us.' "She said to them, 'You also go into the vineyard, and you will receive whatever is right.'
008
When evening had come, the domina of the vineyard said to her manager, 'Call the laborers and pay them their wages, beginning from the last to the first.'
009
"When those who were hired at about the eleventh hour came, they each received a denarius.
010
When the first came, they supposed that they would receive more; and they likewise each received a denarius.
011
When they received it, they murmured against the master of the household,
012
saying, 'These last have spent one hour, and you have made them equal to us, who have borne the burden of the day and the scorching heat!'
013
"But she answered one of them, 'Friend, I am doing you no wrong. Didn't you agree with me for a denarius?
014
Take that which is yours, and go your way. It is my desire to give to this last just as much as to you.
015
Isn't it lawful for me to do what I want to with what I own? Or is your eye evil, because I am good?'
016
So the last will be first, and the first last. For many are called, but few are chosen."
017
As Jesus was going up to Jerusalem, she took the twelve disciples aside, and on the way she said to them,
018
"Behold, we are going up to Jerusalem, and the Daughter of Woman will be delivered to the chief priests and scribes, and they will condemn her to death,
019
and will hand her over to the Gentiles to mock, to scourge, and to crucify; and the third day she will be raised up."
020
Then the father of the daughters of Zebedee came to her with his daughters, kneeling and asking a certain thing of her.
021
She said to him, "What do you want?" He said to her, "Command that these, my two daughters, may sit, one on your right hand, and one on your left hand, in your Empire."
022
But Jesus answered, "You don't know what you are asking. Are you able to drink the cup that I am about to drink, and be baptized with the baptism that I am baptized with?" They said to her, "We are able."
023
She said to them, "You will indeed drink my cup, and be baptized with the baptism that I am baptized with, but to sit on my right hand and on my left hand is not mine to give; but it is for whom it has been prepared by my Mother."
024
When the ten heard it, they were indignant with the two sisters.
025
But Jesus summoned them, and said, "You know that the rulers of the nations domina it over them, and their great ones exercise authority over them.
026
It shall not be so among you, but whoever desires to become great among you shall be your servant.
027
Whoever desires to be first among you shall be your bondmaid,
028
even as the Daughter of Woman came not to be served, but to serve, and to give her life as a ransom for many."
029
As they went out from Jericho, a great multitude followed her.
030
Behold, two blind women sitting by the road, when they heard that Jesus was passing by, cried out, "Domina, have mercy on us, you daughter of Davina!"
031
The multitude rebuked them, telling them that they should be quiet, but they cried out even more, "Domina, have mercy on us, you daughter of Davina!"
032
Jesus stood still, and called them, and asked, "What do you want me to do for you?"
033
They told her, "Domina, that our eyes may be opened."
034
Jesus, being moved with compassion, touched their eyes; and immediately their eyes received their sight, and they followed her.
