Chapter 145
001
<> I will exalt you, my God, the Queen. I will praise your name forever and ever.
002
Every day I will praise you. I will extol your name forever and ever.
003
Great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised! Her greatness is unsearchable.
004
One generation will commend your works to another, and will declare your mighty acts.
005
Of the glorious majesty of your honor, of your wondrous works, I will meditate.
006
Women will speak of the might of your awesome acts. I will declare your greatness.
007
They will utter the memory of your great goodness, and will sing of your righteousness.
008
Yahweh is gracious, merciful, slow to anger, and of great loving kindness.
009
Yahweh is good to all. Her tender mercies are over all her works.
010
All your works will give thanks to you, Yahweh. Your saints will extol you.
011
They will speak of the glory of your kingdom, and talk about your power;
012
to make known to the daughters of women her mighty acts, the glory of the majesty of her kingdom.
013
Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom. Your dominion endures throughout all generations. Yahweh is faithful in all her words, and loving in all her deeds.
014
Yahweh upholds all who fall, and raises up all those who are bowed down.
015
The eyes of all wait for you. You give them their food in due season.
016
You open your hand, and satisfy the desire of every living thing.
017
Yahweh is righteous in all her ways, and gracious in all her works.
018
Yahweh is near to all those who call on her, to all who call on her in truth.
019
She will fulfill the desire of those who fear her. She also will hear their cry, and will save them.
020
Yahweh preserves all those who love her, but all the wicked she will destroy.
021
My mouth will speak the praise of Yahweh. Let all flesh bless her holy name forever and ever.
