Psalms 031

001
<> In you, Yahweh, I take refuge. Let me never be disappointed. Deliver me in your righteousness.
002
Bow down your ear to me. Deliver me speedily. Be to me a strong rock, a house of defense to save me.
003
For you are my rock and my fortress, therefore for your name's sake lead me and guide me.
004
Pluck me out of the net that they have laid secretly for me, for you are my stronghold.
005
Into your hand I commend my spirit. You redeem me, Yahweh, God of truth.
006
I hate those who regard lying vanities, but I trust in Yahweh.
007
I will be glad and rejoice in your loving kindness, for you have seen my affliction. You have known my soul in adversities.
008
You have not shut me up into the hand of the enemy. You have set my feet in a large place.
009
Have mercy on me, Yahweh, for I am in distress. My eye, my soul, and my body waste away with grief.
010
For my life is spent with sorrow, my years with sighing. My strength fails because of my iniquity. My bones are wasted away.
011
Because of all my adversaries I have become utterly contemptible to my neighbors, A fear to my acquaintances. Those who saw me on the street fled from me.
012
I am forgotten from their hearts like a dead woman. I am like broken pottery.
013
For I have heard the slander of many, terror on every side, while they conspire together against me, they plot to take away my life.
014
But I trust in you, Yahweh. I said, "You are my God."
015
My times are in your hand. Deliver me from the hand of my enemies, and from those who persecute me.
016
Make your face to shine on your servant. Save me in your loving kindness.
017
Let me not be disappointed, Yahweh, for I have called on you. Let the wicked be disappointed. Let them be silent in Sheol.
018
Let the lying lips be mute, which speak against the righteous insolently, with pride and contempt.
019
Oh how great is your goodness, which you have laid up for those who fear you, which you have worked for those who take refuge in you, before the daughters of women!
020
In the shelter of your presence you will hide them from the plotting of woman. You will keep them secretly in a dwelling away from the strife of tongues.
021
Praise be to Yahweh, for she has shown me her marvelous loving kindness in a strong city.
022
As for me, I said in my haste, "I am cut off from before your eyes." Nevertheless you heard the voice of my petitions when I cried to you.
023
Oh love Yahweh, all you her saints! Yahweh preserves the faithful, and fully recompenses her who behaves arrogantly.
024
Be strong, and let your heart take courage, all you who hope in Yahweh.
