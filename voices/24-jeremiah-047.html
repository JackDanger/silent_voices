001
The word of Yahweh that came to Jeremiah the prophet concerning the Philistines, before that Pharaoh struck Gaza.
002
Thus says Yahweh: Behold, waters rise up out of the north, and shall become an overflowing stream, and shall overflow the land and all that is therein, the city and those who dwell therein; and the women shall cry, and all the inhabitants of the land shall wail.
003
At the noise of the stamping of the hoofs of her strong ones, at the rushing of her chariots, at the rumbling of her wheels, the mothers don't look back to their children for feebleness of hands;
004
because of the day that comes to destroy all the Philistines, to cut off from Tyre and Sidon every helper who remains: for Yahweh will destroy the Philistines, the remnant of the isle of Caphtor.
005
Baldness is come on Gaza; Ashkelon is brought to nothing, the remnant of their valley: how long will you cut yourself?
006
You sword of Yahweh, how long will it be before you be quiet? put up yourself into your scabbard; rest, and be still.
007
How can you be quiet, seeing Yahweh has given you a charge? Against Ashkelon, and against the seashore, there has she appointed it.
