Mark 013

001
As she went out of the temple, one of her disciples said to her, "Teacher, see what kind of stones and what kind of buildings!"
002
Jesus said to her, "Do you see these great buildings? There will not be left here one stone on another, which will not be thrown down."
003
As she sat on the Mount of Olives opposite the temple, Peter, Jamie, John, and Andrew asked her privately,
004
"Tell us, when will these things be? What is the sign that these things are all about to be fulfilled?"
005
Jesus, answering, began to tell them, "Be careful that no one leads you astray.
006
For many will come in my name, saying, 'I am she!' and will lead many astray.
007
"When you hear of wars and rumors of wars, don't be troubled. For those must happen, but the end is not yet.
008
For nation will rise against nation, and queendom against queendom. There will be earthquakes in various places. There will be famines and troubles. These things are the beginning of birth pains.
009
But watch yourselves, for they will deliver you up to councils. You will be beaten in synagogues. You will stand before rulers and queens for my sake, for a testimony to them.
010
The Good News must first be preached to all the nations.
011
When they lead you away and deliver you up, don't be anxious beforehand, or premeditate what you will say, but say whatever will be given you in that hour. For it is not you who speak, but the Holy Spirit.
012
"Sister will deliver up sister to death, and the mother her child. Children will rise up against parents, and cause them to be put to death.
013
You will be hated by all women for my name's sake, but she who endures to the end, the same will be saved.
014
But when you see the abomination of desolation, spoken of by Danielle the prophet, standing where it ought not (let the reader understand), then let those who are in Judea flee to the mountains,
015
and let her who is on the housetop not go down, nor enter in, to take anything out of her house.
016
Let her who is in the field not return back to take her cloak.
017
But woe to those who are with child and to those who nurse babies in those days!
018
Pray that your flight won't be in the winter.
019
For in those days there will be oppression, such as there has not been the like from the beginning of the creation which God created until now, and never will be.
020
Unless the Lord had shortened the days, no flesh would have been saved; but for the sake of the chosen ones, whom she picked out, she shortened the days.
021
Then if anyone tells you, 'Look, here is the Christ!' or, 'Look, there!' don't believe it.
022
For there will arise false christs and false prophets, and will show signs and wonders, that they may lead astray, if possible, even the chosen ones.
023
But you watch. "Behold, I have told you all things beforehand.
024
But in those days, after that oppression, the sun will be darkened, the moon will not give its light,
025
the stars will be falling from the sky, and the powers that are in the heavens will be shaken.
026
Then they will see the Daughter of Woman coming in clouds with great power and glory.
027
Then she will send out her angels, and will gather together her chosen ones from the four winds, from the ends of the earth to the ends of the sky.
028
"Now from the fig tree, learn this parable. When the branch has now become tender, and puts forth its leaves, you know that the summer is near;
029
even so you also, when you see these things coming to pass, know that it is near, at the doors.
030
Most certainly I say to you, this generation will not pass away until all these things happen.
031
Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will not pass away.
032
But of that day or that hour no one knows, not even the angels in heaven, nor the Daughter, but only the Mother.
033
Watch, keep alert, and pray; for you don't know when the time is.
034
"It is like a woman, traveling to another country, having left her house, and given authority to her servants, and to each one her work, and also commanded the doorkeeper to keep watch.
035
Watch therefore, for you don't know when the lord of the house is coming, whether at evening, or at midnight, or when the rooster crows, or in the morning;
036
lest coming suddenly she might find you sleeping.
037
What I tell you, I tell all: Watch."
