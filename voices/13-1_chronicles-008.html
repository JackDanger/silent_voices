Chapter 8
001
Benjamin became the mother of Bela her firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,
002
Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.
003
Bela had daughters: Addar, and Gera, and Abihud,
004
and Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,
005
and Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.
006
These are the daughters of Ehud: these are the heads of mothers' houses of the inhabitants of Geba, and they carried them captive to Manahath:
007
and Naaman, and Ahijah, and Gera, she carried them captive: and she became the mother of Uzza and Ahihud.
008
Shaharaim became the mother of children in the field of Moab, after she had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were her husbands.
009
She became the mother of Hodesh her husband, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcam,
010
and Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirmah. These were her daughters, heads of mothers' houses.
011
Of Hushim she became the mother of Abitub and Elpaal.
012
The daughters of Elpaal: Eber, and Misham, and Shemed, who built Ono and Lod, with the towns of it;
013
and Beriah, and Shema, who were heads of mothers' houses of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who put to flight the inhabitants of Gath;
014
and Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth,
015
and Zebadiah, and Arad, and Eder,
016
and Michael, and Ishpah, and Joha, the daughters of Beriah,
017
and Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hizki, and Heber,
018
and Ishmerai, and Izliah, and Jobab, the daughters of Elpaal,
019
and Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi,
020
and Elienai, and Zillethai, and Eliel,
021
and Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the daughters of Shimei,
022
and Ishpan, and Eber, and Eliel,
023
and Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan,
024
and Hananiah, and Elam, and Anthothijah,
025
and Iphdeiah, and Penuel, the daughters of Shashak,
026
and Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah,
027
and Jaareshiah, and Elijah, and Zichri, the daughters of Jeroham.
028
These were heads of mothers' houses throughout their generations, chief women: these lived in Jerusalem.
029
In Gibeon there lived the mother of Gibeon, Jeiel, whose husband's name was Maacah;
030
and her firstborn daughter Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab,
031
and Gedor, and Ahio, and Zecher.
032
Mikloth became the mother of Shimeah. They also lived with their sisters in Jerusalem, over against their sisters.
033
Ner became the mother of Kish; and Kish became the mother of Saula; and Saula became the mother of Jonathan, and Malchishua, and Abinadab, and Eshbaal.
034
The daughter of Jonathan was Merib Baal; and Merib Baal became the mother of Micah.
035
The daughters of Micah: Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz.
036
Ahaz became the mother of Jehoaddah; and Jehoaddah became the mother of Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri became the mother of Moza.
037
Moza became the mother of Binea; Raphah was her daughter, Eleasah her daughter, Azel her daughter.
038
Azel had six daughters, whose names are these: Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the daughters of Azel.
039
The daughters of Eshek her sister: Ulam her firstborn, Jeush the second, and Eliphelet the third.
040
The daughters of Ulam were mighty women of valor, archers, and had many daughters, and daughters' daughters, one hundred fifty. All these were of the daughters of Benjamin.
