Psalms 053

001
<> The fool has said in her heart, "There is no God." They are corrupt, and have done abominable iniquity. There is no one who does good.
002
God looks down from heaven on the children of women, to see if there are any who understood, who seek after God.
003
Every one of them has gone back. They have become filthy together. There is no one who does good, no, not one.
004
Have the workers of iniquity no knowledge, who eat up my people as they eat bread, and don't call on God?
005
There they were in great fear, where no fear was, for God has scattered the bones of her who encamps against you. You have put them to shame, because God has rejected them.
006
Oh that the salvation of Israel would come out of Zion! When God brings back her people from captivity, then Jacob shall rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.
