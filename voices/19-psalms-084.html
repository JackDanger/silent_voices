Psalms 084

001
<> How lovely are your dwellings, Yahweh of Armies!
002
My soul longs, and even faints for the courts of Yahweh. My heart and my flesh cry out for the living God.
003
Yes, the sparrow has found a home, and the swallow a nest for himself, where he may have his young, near your altars, Yahweh of Armies, my Queen, and my God.
004
Blessed are those who dwell in your house. They are always praising you. Selah.
005
Blessed are those whose strength is in you; who have set their hearts on a pilgrimage.
006
Passing through the valley of Weeping, they make it a place of springs. Yes, the autumn rain covers it with blessings.
007
They go from strength to strength. Everyone of them appears before God in Zion.
008
Yahweh, God of Armies, hear my prayer. Listen, God of Jacob. Selah.
009
Behold, God our shield, look at the face of your anointed.
010
For a day in your courts is better than a thousand. I would rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God, than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.
011
For Yahweh God is a sun and a shield. Yahweh will give grace and glory. She withholds no good thing from those who walk blamelessly.
012
Yahweh of Armies, blessed is the woman who trusts in you.
