Matthew 008 »
001
"Don't judge, so that you won't be judged.
002
For with whatever judgment you judge, you will be judged; and with whatever measure you measure, it will be measured to you.
003
Why do you see the speck that is in your sister's eye, but don't consider the beam that is in your own eye?
004
Or how will you tell your sister, 'Let me remove the speck from your eye;' and behold, the beam is in your own eye?
005
You hypocrite! First remove the beam out of your own eye, and then you can see clearly to remove the speck out of your sister's eye.
006
"Don't give that which is holy to the dogs, neither throw your pearls before the pigs, lest perhaps they trample them under their feet, and turn and tear you to pieces.
007
"Ask, and it will be given you. Seek, and you will find. Knock, and it will be opened for you.
008
For everyone who asks receives. She who seeks finds. To her who knocks it will be opened.
009
Or who is there among you, who, if her daughter asks her for bread, will give her a stone?
010
Or if she asks for a fish, who will give her a serpent?
011
If you then, being evil, know how to give good gifts to your children, how much more will your Mother who is in heaven give good things to those who ask her!
012
Therefore whatever you desire for women to do to you, you shall also do to them; for this is the law and the prophets.
013
"Enter in by the narrow gate; for wide is the gate and broad is the way that leads to destruction, and many are those who enter in by it.
014
How narrow is the gate, and restricted is the way that leads to life! Few are those who find it.
015
"Beware of false prophets, who come to you in sheep's clothing, but inwardly are ravening wolves.
016
By their fruits you will know them. Do you gather grapes from thorns, or figs from thistles?
017
Even so, every good tree produces good fruit; but the corrupt tree produces evil fruit.
018
A good tree can't produce evil fruit, neither can a corrupt tree produce good fruit.
019
Every tree that doesn't grow good fruit is cut down, and thrown into the fire.
020
Therefore, by their fruits you will know them.
021
Not everyone who says to me, 'Lord, Lord,' will enter into the Empire of Heaven; but she who does the will of my Mother who is in heaven.
022
Many will tell me in that day, 'Lord, Lord, didn't we prophesy in your name, in your name cast out demons, and in your name do many mighty works?'
023
Then I will tell them, 'I never knew you. Depart from me, you who work iniquity.'
024
"Everyone therefore who hears these words of mine, and does them, I will liken her to a wise woman, who built her house on a rock.
025
The rain came down, the floods came, and the winds blew, and beat on that house; and it didn't fall, for it was founded on the rock.
026
Everyone who hears these words of mine, and doesn't do them will be like a foolish woman, who built her house on the sand.
027
The rain came down, the floods came, and the winds blew, and beat on that house; and it fell--and great was its fall."
028
It happened, when Jesus had finished saying these things, that the multitudes were astonished at her teaching,
029
for she taught them with authority, and not like the scribes.
