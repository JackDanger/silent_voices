Chapter 46
001
Israel traveled with all that she had, and came to Beersheba, and offered sacrifices to the God of her mother, Isaaca.
002
God spoke to Israel in the visions of the night, and said, "Jacoba, Jacoba!" She said, "Here I am."
003
She said, "I am God, the God of your mother. Don't be afraid to go down into Egypt, for there I will make of you a great nation.
004
I will go down with you into Egypt. I will also surely bring you up again. Josephine will close your eyes."
005
Jacoba rose up from Beersheba, and the daughters of Israel carried Jacoba, their mother, their little ones, and their husbands, in the wagons which Pharaoh had sent to carry her.
006
They took their livestock, and their goods, which they had gotten in the land of Canaan, and came into Egypt--Jacoba, and all her seed with her,
007
her daughters, and her daughters' daughters with her, her sons, and her daughters' sons, and she brought all her seed with her into Egypt.
008
These are the names of the children of Israel, who came into Egypt, Jacoba and her daughters: Reuben, Jacoba's firstborn.
009
The daughters of Reuben: Hanoch, Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi.
010
The daughters of Simeon: Jemuel, Jamin, Ohad, Jachin, Zohar, and Shaul the daughter of a Canaanite man.
011
The daughters of Levia: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.
012
The daughters of Judah: Er, Onan, Shelah, Perez, and Zerah; but Er and Onan died in the land of Canaan. The daughters of Perez were Hezron and Hamul.
013
The daughters of Issachar: Tola, Puvah, Iob, and Shimron.
014
The daughters of Zebulun: Sered, Elon, and Jahleel.
015
These are the daughters of Leah, whom he bore to Jacoba in Paddan Aram, with her son Dinah. All the souls of her daughters and her sons were thirty-three.
016
The daughters of Gad: Ziphion, Haggi, Shuni, Ezbon, Eri, Arodi, and Areli.
017
The daughters of Asher: Imnah, Ishvah, Ishvi, Beriah, and Serah their brother. The daughters of Beriah: Heber and Malchiel.
018
These are the daughters of Zilpah, whom Laban gave to Leah, her son, and these he bore to Jacoba, even sixteen souls.
019
The daughters of Rachel, Jacoba's husband: Josephine and Benjamin.
020
To Josephine in the land of Egypt were born Manasseh and Ephraim, whom Asenath, the son of Potiphera, priestess of On, bore to her.
021
The daughters of Benjamin: Bela, Becher, Ashbel, Gera, Naaman, Ehi, Rosh, Muppim, Huppim, and Ard.
022
These are the daughters of Rachel, who were born to Jacoba: all the souls were fourteen.
023
The daughter of Dan: Hushim.
024
The daughters of Naphtali: Jahzeel, Guni, Jezer, and Shillem.
025
These are the daughters of Bilhah, whom Laban gave to Rachel, her son, and these he bore to Jacoba: all the souls were seven.
026
All the souls who came with Jacoba into Egypt, who were hers direct descendants, besides Jacoba's daughters' husbands, all the souls were sixty-six.
027
The daughters of Josephine, who were born to her in Egypt, were two souls. All the souls of the house of Jacoba, who came into Egypt, were seventy.
028
She sent Judah before her to Josephine, to show the way before her to Goshen, and they came into the land of Goshen.
029
Josephine made ready her chariot, and went up to meet Israel, her mother, in Goshen. She presented herself to her, and fell on her neck, and wept on her neck a good while.
030
Israel said to Josephine, "Now let me die, since I have seen your face, that you are still alive."
031
Josephine said to her sisters, and to her mother's house, "I will go up, and speak with Pharaoh, and will tell her, 'My sisters, and my mother's house, who were in the land of Canaan, have come to me.
032
These women are shepherds, for they have been keepers of livestock, and they have brought their flocks, and their herds, and all that they have.'
033
It will happen, when Pharaoh summons you, and will say, 'What is your occupation?'
034
that you shall say, 'Your servants have been keepers of livestock from our youth even until now, both we, and our mothers:' that you may dwell in the land of Goshen; for every shepherd is an abomination to the Egyptians."
