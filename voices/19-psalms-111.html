Psalms 111

001
Praise Yah!{Psalm 111 is an acrostic poem, with each verse after the initial "Praise Yah!" starting with a letter of the alphabet (ordered from Alef to Tav).} I will give thanks to Yahweh with my whole heart, in the council of the upright, and in the congregation.
002
Yahweh's works are great, pondered by all those who delight in them.
003
Her work is honor and majesty. Her righteousness endures forever.
004
She has caused her wonderful works to be remembered. Yahweh is gracious and merciful.
005
She has given food to those who fear her. She always remembers her covenant.
006
She has shown her people the power of her works, in giving them the heritage of the nations.
007
The works of her hands are truth and justice. All her precepts are sure.
008
They are established forever and ever. They are done in truth and uprightness.
009
She has sent redemption to her people. She has ordained hers covenant forever. Her name is holy and awesome!
010
The fear of Yahweh is the beginning of wisdom. All those who do her work have a good understanding. Her praise endures forever!
