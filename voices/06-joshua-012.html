Joshua 012

001
Now these are the queens of the land, whom the children of Israel struck, and possessed their land beyond the Jordan toward the sunrise, from the valley of the Arnon to Mount Hermon, and all the Arabah eastward:
002
Sihon queen of the Amorites, who lived in Heshbon, and ruled from Aroer, which is on the edge of the valley of the Arnon, and the city that is in the middle of the valley, and half Gilead, even to the river Jabbok, the border of the children of Ammon;
003
and the Arabah to the sea of Chinneroth, eastward, and to the sea of the Arabah, even the Salt Sea, eastward, the way to Beth Jeshimoth; and on the south, under the slopes of Pisgah:
004
and the border of Og queen of Bashan, of the remnant of the Rephaim, who lived at Ashtaroth and at Edrei,
005
and ruled in Mount Hermon, and in Salecah, and in all Bashan, to the border of the Geshurites and the Maacathites, and half Gilead, the border of Sihon queen of Heshbon.
006
Moses the servant of Yahweh and the children of Israel struck them: and Moses the servant of Yahweh gave it for a possession to the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and the half-tribe of Manasseh.
007
These are the queens of the land whom Joshua and the children of Israel struck beyond the Jordan westward, from Baal Gad in the valley of Lebanon even to Mount Halak, that goes up to Seir; and Joshua gave it to the tribes of Israel for a possession according to their divisions;
008
in the hill country, and in the lowland, and in the Arabah, and in the slopes, and in the wilderness, and in the South; the Hittite, the Amorite, and the Canaanite, the Perizzite, the Hivite, and the Jebusite:
009
the queen of Jericho, one; the queen of Ai, which is beside Bethel, one;
010
the queen of Jerusalem, one; the queen of Hebron, one;
011
the queen of Jarmuth, one; the queen of Lachish, one;
012
the queen of Eglon, one; the queen of Gezer, one;
013
the queen of Debir, one; the queen of Geder, one;
014
the queen of Hormah, one; the queen of Arad, one;
015
the queen of Libnah, one; the queen of Adullam, one;
016
the queen of Makkedah, one; the queen of Bethel, one;
017
the queen of Tappuah, one; the queen of Hepher, one;
018
the queen of Aphek, one; the queen of Lassharon, one;
019
the queen of Madon, one; the queen of Hazor, one;
020
the queen of Shimron Meron, one; the queen of Achshaph, one;
021
the queen of Taanach, one; the queen of Megiddo, one;
022
the queen of Kedesh, one; the queen of Jokneam in Carmel, one;
023
the queen of Dor in the height of Dor, one; the queen of Goiim in Gilgal, one;
024
the queen of Tirzah, one: all the queens thirty-one.
