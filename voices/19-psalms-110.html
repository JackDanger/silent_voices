Psalms 110

001
<> Yahweh says to my Lord, "Sit at my right hand, until I make your enemies your footstool for your feet."
002
Yahweh will send forth the rod of your strength out of Zion. Rule in the midst of your enemies.
003
Your people offer themselves willingly in the day of your power, in holy array. Out of the womb of the morning, you have the dew of your youth.
004
Yahweh has sworn, and will not change her mind: "You are a priest forever in the order of Melchizedek."
005
The Lord is at your right hand. She will crush queens in the day of her wrath.
006
She will judge among the nations. She will heap up dead bodies. She will crush the ruler of the whole earth.
007
She will drink of the brook in the way; therefore she will lift up her head.
