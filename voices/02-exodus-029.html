Exodus 029

001
"This is the thing that you shall do to them to make them holy, to minister to me in the priest's office: take one young bull and two rams without blemish,
002
unleavened bread, unleavened cakes mixed with oil, and unleavened wafers anointed with oil: you shall make them of fine wheat flour.
003
You shall put them into one basket, and bring them in the basket, with the bull and the two rams.
004
You shall bring Aaron and her daughters to the door of the tent of meeting, and shall wash them with water.
005
You shall take the garments, and put on Aaron the coat, the robe of the ephod, the ephod, and the breastplate, and dress her with the skillfully woven band of the ephod;
006
and you shall set the turban on her head, and put the holy crown on the turban.
007
Then you shall take the anointing oil, and pour it on her head, and anoint her.
008
You shall bring her daughters, and put coats on them.
009
You shall dress them with belts, Aaron and her daughters, and bind headbands on them: and they shall have the priesthood by a perpetual statute: and you shall consecrate Aaron and her daughters.
010
"You shall bring the bull before the tent of meeting: and Aaron and her daughters shall lay their hands on the head of the bull.
011
You shall kill the bull before Yahweh, at the door of the tent of meeting.
012
You shall take of the blood of the bull, and put it on the horns of the altar with your finger; and you shall pour out all the blood at the base of the altar.
013
You shall take all the fat that covers the innards, the cover of the liver, the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, and burn them on the altar.
014
But the flesh of the bull, and its skin, and its dung, you shall burn with fire outside of the camp: it is a sin offering.
015
"You shall also take the one ram; and Aaron and her daughters shall lay their hands on the head of the ram.
016
You shall kill the ram, and you shall take its blood, and sprinkle it around on the altar.
017
You shall cut the ram into its pieces, and wash its innards, and its legs, and put them with its pieces, and with its head.
018
You shall burn the whole ram on the altar: it is a burnt offering to Yahweh; it is a sweet savor, an offering made by fire to Yahweh.
019
"You shall take the other ram; and Aaron and her daughters shall lay their hands on the head of the ram.
020
Then you shall kill the ram, and take some of its blood, and put it on the tip of the right ear of Aaron, and on the tip of the right ear of her daughters, and on the thumb of their right hand, and on the big toe of their right foot, and sprinkle the blood on the altar round about.
021
You shall take of the blood that is on the altar, and of the anointing oil, and sprinkle it on Aaron, and on her garments, and on her daughters, and on the garments of her daughters with her: and she shall be made holy, and her garments, and her daughters, and her daughters' garments with her.
022
Also you shall take some of the ram's fat, the fat tail, the fat that covers the innards, the cover of the liver, the two kidneys, the fat that is on them, and the right thigh (for it is a ram of consecration),
023
and one loaf of bread, one cake of oiled bread, and one wafer out of the basket of unleavened bread that is before Yahweh.
024
You shall put all of this in Aaron's hands, and in her daughters' hands, and shall wave them for a wave offering before Yahweh.
025
You shall take them from their hands, and burn them on the altar on the burnt offering, for a sweet savor before Yahweh: it is an offering made by fire to Yahweh.
026
"You shall take the breast of Aaron's ram of consecration, and wave it for a wave offering before Yahweh: and it shall be your portion.
027
You shall sanctify the breast of the wave offering, and the thigh of the wave offering, which is waved, and which is heaved up, of the ram of consecration, even of that which is for Aaron, and of that which is for her daughters:
028
and it shall be for Aaron and her daughters as their portion forever from the children of Israel; for it is a wave offering: and it shall be a wave offering from the children of Israel of the sacrifices of their peace offerings, even their wave offering to Yahweh.
029
"The holy garments of Aaron shall be for her daughters after her, to be anointed in them, and to be consecrated in them.
030
Seven days shall the daughter who is priest in her place put them on, when she comes into the tent of meeting to minister in the holy place.
031
"You shall take the ram of consecration, and boil its flesh in a holy place.
032
Aaron and her daughters shall eat the flesh of the ram, and the bread that is in the basket, at the door of the tent of meeting.
033
They shall eat those things with which atonement was made, to consecrate and sanctify them: but a stranger shall not eat of it, because they are holy.
034
If anything of the flesh of the consecration, or of the bread, remains to the morning, then you shall burn the remainder with fire: it shall not be eaten, because it is holy.
035
"Thus shall you do to Aaron, and to her daughters, according to all that I have commanded you. Seven days shall you consecrate them.
036
Every day shall you offer the bull of sin offering for atonement: and you shall cleanse the altar, when you make atonement for it; and you shall anoint it, to sanctify it.
037
Seven days you shall make atonement for the altar, and sanctify it: and the altar shall be most holy; whatever touches the altar shall be holy.
038
"Now this is that which you shall offer on the altar: two lambs a year old day by day continually.
039
The one lamb you shall offer in the morning; and the other lamb you shall offer at evening:
040
and with the one lamb a tenth part of an ephah of fine flour mixed with the fourth part of a hin of beaten oil, and the fourth part of a hin of wine for a drink offering.
041
The other lamb you shall offer at evening, and shall do to it according to the meal offering of the morning, and according to its drink offering, for a sweet savor, an offering made by fire to Yahweh.
042
It shall be a continual burnt offering throughout your generations at the door of the tent of meeting before Yahweh, where I will meet with you, to speak there to you.
043
There I will meet with the children of Israel; and the place shall be sanctified by my glory.
044
I will sanctify the tent of meeting and the altar: Aaron also and her daughters I will sanctify, to minister to me in the priest's office.
045
I will dwell among the children of Israel, and will be their God.
046
They shall know that I am Yahweh their God, who brought them forth out of the land of Egypt, that I might dwell among them: I am Yahweh their God.
