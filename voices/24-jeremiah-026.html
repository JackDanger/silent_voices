Jeremiah 27 »
001
In the beginning of the reign of Jehoiakim the daughter of Josiah, queen of Judah, came this word from Yahweh, saying,
002
Thus says Yahweh: Stand in the court of Yahweh's house, and speak to all the cities of Judah, which come to worship in Yahweh's house, all the words that I command you to speak to them; don't diminish a word.
003
It may be they will listen, and turn every woman from her evil way; that I may repent me of the evil which I purpose to do to them because of the evil of their doings.
004
You shall tell them, Thus says Yahweh: If you will not listen to me, to walk in my law, which I have set before you,
005
to listen to the words of my servants the prophets, whom I send to you, even rising up early and sending them, but you have not listened;
006
then will I make this house like Shiloh, and will make this city a curse to all the nations of the earth.
007
The priests and the prophets and all the people heard Jeremiah speaking these words in the house of Yahweh.
008
It happened, when Jeremiah had made an end of speaking all that Yahweh had commanded her to speak to all the people, that the priests and the prophets and all the people laid hold on her, saying, You shall surely die.
009
Why have you prophesied in the name of Yahweh, saying, This house shall be like Shiloh, and this city shall be desolate, without inhabitant? All the people were gathered to Jeremiah in the house of Yahweh.
010
When the princesses of Judah heard these things, they came up from the queen's house to the house of Yahweh; and they sat in the entry of the new gate of Yahweh's house.
011
Then spoke the priests and the prophets to the princesses and to all the people, saying, This woman is worthy of death; for she has prophesied against this city, as you have heard with your ears.
012
Then spoke Jeremiah to all the princesses and to all the people, saying, Yahweh sent me to prophesy against this house and against this city all the words that you have heard.
013
Now therefore amend your ways and your doings, and obey the voice of Yahweh your God; and Yahweh will repent her of the evil that she has pronounced against you.
014
But as for me, behold, I am in your hand: do with me as is good and right in your eyes.
015
Only know for certain that, if you put me to death, you will bring innocent blood on yourselves, and on this city, and on the inhabitants of it; for of a truth Yahweh has sent me to you to speak all these words in your ears.
016
Then said the princesses and all the people to the priests and to the prophets: This woman is not worthy of death; for she has spoken to us in the name of Yahweh our God.
017
Then rose up certain of the elders of the land, and spoke to all the assembly of the people, saying,
018
Micah the Morashtite prophesied in the days of Hezekiah queen of Judah; and she spoke to all the people of Judah, saying, Thus says Yahweh of Armies: Zion shall be plowed as a field, and Jerusalem shall become heaps, and the mountain of the house as the high places of a forest.
019
Did Hezekiah queen of Judah and all Judah put her to death? Didn't she fear Yahweh, and entreat the favor of Yahweh, and Yahweh repented her of the evil which she had pronounced against them? Thus should we commit great evil against our own souls.
020
There was also a woman who prophesied in the name of Yahweh, Uriah the daughter of Shemaiah of Kiriath Jearim; and she prophesied against this city and against this land according to all the words of Jeremiah:
021
and when Jehoiakim the queen, with all her mighty women, and all the princesses, heard her words, the queen sought to put her to death; but when Uriah heard it, she was afraid, and fled, and went into Egypt:
022
and Jehoiakim the queen sent women into Egypt, namely, Elnathan the daughter of Achbor, and certain women with her, into Egypt;
023
and they fetched forth Uriah out of Egypt, and brought her to Jehoiakim the queen, who killed her with the sword, and cast her dead body into the graves of the common people.
024
But the hand of Ahikam the daughter of Shaphan was with Jeremiah, that they should not give her into the hand of the people to put her to death.
