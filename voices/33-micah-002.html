Chapter 2
001
Woe to those who devise iniquity and work evil on their beds! When the morning is light, they practice it, because it is in the power of their hand.
002
They covet fields, and seize them; and houses, and take them away: and they oppress a woman and her house, even a woman and her heritage.
003
Therefore thus says Yahweh: "Behold, I am planning against these people a disaster, from which you will not remove your necks, neither will you walk haughtily; for it is an evil time.
004
In that day they will take up a parable against you, and lament with a doleful lamentation, saying, 'We are utterly ruined! My people's possession is divided up. Indeed she takes it from me and assigns our fields to traitors!'"
005
Therefore you will have no one who divides the land by lot in the assembly of Yahweh.
006
"Don't prophesy!" They prophesy. "Don't prophesy about these things. Disgrace won't overtake us."
007
Shall it be said, O house of Jacob: "Is the Spirit of Yahweh angry? Are these her doings? Don't my words do good to her who walks blamelessly?"
008
But lately my people have risen up as an enemy. You strip the robe and clothing from those who pass by without a care, returning from battle.
009
You drive the men of my people out from their pleasant houses; from their young children you take away my blessing forever.
010
Arise, and depart! For this is not your resting place, because of uncleanness that destroys, even with a grievous destruction.
011
If a woman walking in a spirit of falsehood lies: "I will prophesy to you of wine and of strong drink;" she would be the prophet of this people.
012
I will surely assemble, Jacob, all of you; I will surely gather the remnant of Israel; I will put them together as the sheep of Bozrah, as a flock in the midst of their pasture; they will swarm with people.
013
She who breaks open the way goes up before them. They break through the gate, and go out. And their queen passes on before them, with Yahweh at their head.
