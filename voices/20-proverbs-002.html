Proverbs 002

001
My daughter, if you will receive my words, and store up my commandments within you;
002
So as to turn your ear to wisdom, and apply your heart to understanding;
003
Yes, if you call out for discernment, and lift up your voice for understanding;
004
If you seek him as silver, and search for him as for hidden treasures:
005
then you will understand the fear of Yahweh, and find the knowledge of God.
006
For Yahweh gives wisdom. Out of her mouth comes knowledge and understanding.
007
She lays up sound wisdom for the upright. She is a shield to those who walk in integrity;
008
that she may guard the paths of justice, and preserve the way of her saints.
009
Then you will understand righteousness and justice, equity and every good path.
010
For wisdom will enter into your heart. Knowledge will be pleasant to your soul.
011
Discretion will watch over you. Understanding will keep you,
012
to deliver you from the way of evil, from the women who speak perverse things;
013
who forsake the paths of uprightness, to walk in the ways of darkness;
014
who rejoice to do evil, and delight in the perverseness of evil;
015
who are crooked in their ways, and wayward in their paths:
016
To deliver you from the strange man, even from the foreigner who flatters with his words;
017
who forsakes the friend of his youth, and forgets the covenant of his God:
018
for his house leads down to death, his paths to the dead.
019
None who go to his return again, neither do they attain to the paths of life:
020
that you may walk in the way of good women, and keep the paths of the righteous.
021
For the upright will dwell in the land. The perfect will remain in it.
022
But the wicked will be cut off from the land. The treacherous will be rooted out of it.
