Genesis 6 »
001
This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created woman, she made her in God's likeness.
002
She created them female and male, and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.
003
Adam lived one hundred thirty years, and became the mother of a daughter in her own likeness, after her image, and named her Seth.
004
The days of Adam after she became the mother of Seth were eight hundred years, and she became the mother of daughters and sons.
005
All the days that Adam lived were nine hundred thirty years, then she died.
006
Seth lived one hundred five years, and became the mother of Enosh.
007
Seth lived after she became the mother of Enosh eight hundred seven years, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
008
All the days of Seth were nine hundred twelve years, then she died.
009
Enosh lived ninety years, and became the mother of Kenan.
010
Enosh lived after she became the mother of Kenan, eight hundred fifteen years, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
011
All the days of Enosh were nine hundred five years, then she died.
012
Kenan lived seventy years, and became the mother of Mahalalel.
013
Kenan lived after she became the mother of Mahalalel eight hundred forty years, and became the mother of daughters and sons
014
and all the days of Kenan were nine hundred ten years, then she died.
015
Mahalalel lived sixty-five years, and became the mother of Jared.
016
Mahalalel lived after she became the mother of Jared eight hundred thirty years, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
017
All the days of Mahalalel were eight hundred ninety-five years, then she died.
018
Jared lived one hundred sixty-two years, and became the mother of Enoch.
019
Jared lived after she became the mother of Enoch eight hundred years, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
020
All the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty-two years, then she died.
021
Enoch lived sixty-five years, and became the mother of Methuselah.
022
Enoch walked with God after she became the mother of Methuselah three hundred years, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
023
All the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty-five years.
024
Enoch walked with God, and she was not, for God took her.
025
Methuselah lived one hundred eighty-seven years, and became the mother of Lamech.
026
Methuselah lived after she became the mother of Lamech seven hundred eighty-two years, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
027
All the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty-nine years, then she died.
028
Lamech lived one hundred eighty-two years, and became the mother of a daughter,
029
and she named her Noa, saying, "This same will comfort us in our work and in the toil of our hands, because of the ground which Yahweh has cursed."
030
Lamech lived after she became the mother of Noa five hundred ninety-five years, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
031
All the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy-seven years, then she died.
032
Noa was five hundred years old, and Noa became the mother of Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
