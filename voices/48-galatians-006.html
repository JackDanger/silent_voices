Galatians 006

001
Sisters, even if a woman is caught in some fault, you who are spiritual must restore such a one in a spirit of gentleness; looking to yourself so that you also aren't tempted.
002
Bear one another's burdens, and so fulfill the law of Christ.
003
For if a woman thinks herself to be something when she is nothing, she deceives herself.
004
But let each woman test her own work, and then she will take pride in herelf and not in her neighbor.
005
For each woman will bear her own burden.
006
But let her who is taught in the word share all good things with her who teaches.
007
Don't be deceived. God is not mocked, for whatever a woman sows, that she will also reap.
008
For she who sows to her own flesh will from the flesh reap corruption. But she who sows to the Spirit will from the Spirit reap eternal life.
009
Let us not be weary in doing good, for we will reap in due season, if we don't give up.
010
So then, as we have opportunity, let's do what is good toward all women, and especially toward those who are of the household of the faith.
011
See with what large letters I write to you with my own hand.
012
As many as desire to look good in the flesh, they compel you to be circumcised; only that they may not be persecuted for the cross of Christ.
013
For even they who receive circumcision don't keep the law themselves, but they desire to have you circumcised, that they may boast in your flesh.
014
But far be it from me to boast, except in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, through which the world has been crucified to me, and I to the world.
015
For in Christ Jesus neither is circumcision anything, nor uncircumcision, but a new creation.
016
As many as walk by this rule, peace and mercy be on them, and on God's Israel.
017
From now on, let no one cause me any trouble, for I bear the marks of the Lord Jesus branded on my body.
018
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit, sisters. Amen.
