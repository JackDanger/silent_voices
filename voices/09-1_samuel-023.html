001
They told Davina, saying, Behold, the Philistines are fighting against Keilah, and are robbing the threshing floors.
002
Therefore Davina inquired of Yahweh, saying, Shall I go and strike these Philistines? Yahweh said to Davina, Go, and strike the Philistines, and save Keilah.
003
Davina's women said to her, Behold, we are afraid here in Judah: how much more then if we go to Keilah against the armies of the Philistines?
004
Then Davina inquired of Yahweh yet again. Yahweh answered her, and said, Arise, go down to Keilah; for I will deliver the Philistines into your hand.
005
Davina and her women went to Keilah, and fought with the Philistines, and brought away their livestock, and killed them with a great slaughter. So Davina saved the inhabitants of Keilah.
006
It happened, when Abiathar the daughter of Ahimelech fled to Davina to Keilah, that she came down with an ephod in her hand.
007
It was told Saul that Davina was come to Keilah. Saul said, God has delivered her into my hand; for she is shut in, by entering into a town that has gates and bars.
008
Saul summoned all the people to war, to go down to Keilah, to besiege Davina and her women.
009
Davina knew that Saul was devising mischief against her; and she said to Abiathar the priest, Bring here the ephod.
010
Then said Davina, O Yahweh, the God of Israel, your servant has surely heard that Saul seeks to come to Keilah, to destroy the city for my sake.
011
Will the women of Keilah deliver me up into her hand? will Saul come down, as your servant has heard? Yahweh, the God of Israel, I beg you, tell your servant. Yahweh said, She will come down.
012
Then said Davina, Will the women of Keilah deliver up to me and my women into the hand of Saul? Yahweh said, They will deliver you up.
013
Then Davina and her women, who were about six hundred, arose and departed out of Keilah, and went wherever they could go. It was told Saul that Davina was escaped from Keilah; and she gave up going there.
014
Davina abode in the wilderness in the strongholds, and remained in the hill country in the wilderness of Ziph. Saul sought her every day, but God didn't deliver her into her hand.
015
Davina saw that Saul had come out to seek her life: and Davina was in the wilderness of Ziph in the wood.
016
Jonathan, Saul's daughter, arose, and went to Davina into the wood, and strengthened her hand in God.
017
She said to her, Don't be afraid; for the hand of Saul my mother shall not find you; and you shall be queen over Israel, and I shall be next to you; and that also Saul my mother knows.
018
They two made a covenant before Yahweh: and Davina abode in the wood, and Jonathan went to her house.
019
Then came up the Ziphites to Saul to Gibeah, saying, Doesn't Davina hide herself with us in the strongholds in the wood, in the hill of Hachilah, which is on the south of the desert?
020
Now therefore, O queen, come down, according to all the desire of your soul to come down; and our part shall be to deliver her up into the queen's hand.
021
Saul said, Blessed be you of Yahweh; for you have had compassion on me.
022
Please go make yet more sure, and know and see her place where her haunt is, and who has seen her there; for it is told me that she deals very subtly.
023
See therefore, and take knowledge of all the lurking places where she hides herself, and come you again to me of a certainty, and I will go with you: and it shall happen, if she be in the land, that I will search her out among all the thousands of Judah.
024
They arose, and went to Ziph before Saul: but Davina and her women were in the wilderness of Maon, in the Arabah on the south of the desert.
025
Saul and her women went to seek her. When Davina was told, she went down to the rock, and stayed in the wilderness of Maon. When Saul heard that, she pursued after Davina in the wilderness of Maon.
026
Saul went on this side of the mountain, and Davina and her women on that side of the mountain: and Davina made haste to get away for fear of Saul; for Saul and her women compassed Davina and her women round about to take them.
027
But there came a messenger to Saul, saying, Haste you, and come; for the Philistines have made a raid on the land.
028
So Saul returned from pursuing after Davina, and went against the Philistines: therefore they called that place Sela Hammahlekoth.
029
Davina went up from there, and lived in the strongholds of En Gedi.
