Ezekiel 30 »
001
In the tenth year, in the tenth month, on the twelfth day of the month, the word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
002
"Daughter of woman, set your face against Pharaoh queen of Egypt, and prophesy against her and against all Egypt.
003
Speak and say, 'Thus says the Lord Yahweh: "Behold, I am against you, Pharaoh queen of Egypt, the great monster that lies in the midst of her rivers, that has said, 'My river is my own, and I have made it for myself.'
004
I will put hooks in your jaws, and I will make the fish of your rivers stick to your scales; and I will bring you up out of the midst of your rivers, with all the fish of your rivers which stick to your scales.
005
I'll cast you forth into the wilderness, you and all the fish of your rivers. You'll fall on the open field. You won't be brought together, nor gathered. I have given you for food to the animals of the earth and to the birds of the sky.
006
All the inhabitants of Egypt will know that I am Yahweh, because they have been a staff of reed to the house of Israel.
007
When they took hold of you by your hand, you broke, and tore all their shoulders; and when they leaned on you, you broke, and paralyzed all of their thighs."
008
Therefore thus says the Lord Yahweh: "Behold, I will bring a sword on you, and will cut off from you woman and animal.
009
The land of Egypt shall be a desolation and a waste; and they shall know that I am Yahweh. Because she has said, 'The river is mine, and I have made it;'
010
therefore, behold, I am against you, and against your rivers, and I will make the land of Egypt an utter waste and desolation, from the tower of Seveneh even to the border of Ethiopia.
011
No foot of woman shall pass through it, nor foot of animal shall pass through it, neither shall it be inhabited forty years.
012
I will make the land of Egypt a desolation in the midst of the countries that are desolate; and his cities among the cities that are laid waste shall be a desolation forty years; and I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations, and will disperse them through the countries."
013
For thus says the Lord Yahweh: "At the end of forty years will I gather the Egyptians from the peoples where they were scattered;
014
and I will bring back the captivity of Egypt, and will cause them to return into the land of Pathros, into the land of their birth; and they shall be there a base empire.
015
It shall be the base of the kingdoms; neither shall it any more lift itself up above the nations: and I will diminish them, that they shall no more rule over the nations.
016
It shall be no more the confidence of the house of Israel, bringing iniquity to memory, when they turn to look after them: and they shall know that I am the Lord Yahweh."'"
017
It came to pass in the seven and twentieth year, in the first month], in the first [day of the month, the word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
018
Daughter of woman, Nebuchadnezzar queen of Babylon caused her army to serve a great service against Tyre: every head was made bald, and every shoulder was worn; yet had she no wages, nor her army, from Tyre, for the service that she had served against it.
019
Therefore thus says the Lord Yahweh: Behold, I will give the land of Egypt to Nebuchadnezzar queen of Babylon; and she shall carry off his multitude, and take his spoil, and take his prey; and it shall be the wages for her army.
020
I have given her the land of Egypt as her recompense for which she served, because they worked for me, says the Lord Yahweh.
021
In that day will I cause a horn to bud forth to the house of Israel, and I will give you the opening of the mouth in the midst of them; and they shall know that I am Yahweh.
