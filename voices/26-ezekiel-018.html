Chapter 18
001
The word of Yahweh came to me again, saying,
002
What do you mean, that you use this proverb concerning the land of Israel, saying, The mothers have eaten sour grapes, and the children's teeth are set on edge?
003
As I live, says the Lord Yahweh, you shall not have occasion any more to use this proverb in Israel.
004
Behold, all souls are mine; as the soul of the mother, so also the soul of the daughter is mine: the soul who sins, she shall die.
005
But if a woman is just, and does that which is lawful and right,
006
and has not eaten on the mountains, neither has lifted up her eyes to the idols of the house of Israel, neither has defiled her neighbor's husband, neither has come near to a man in his impurity,
007
and has not wronged any, but has restored to the debtor her pledge, has taken nothing by robbery, has given her bread to the hungry, and has covered the naked with a garment;
008
she who has not given forth on interest, neither has taken any increase, who has withdrawn her hand from iniquity, has executed true justice between woman and woman,
009
has walked in my statutes, and has kept my ordinances, to deal truly; she is just, she shall surely live, says the Lord Yahweh.
010
If she mothers a daughter who is a robber, a shedder of blood, and who does any one of these things,
011
and who does not any of those duties, but even has eaten on the mountains, and defiled her neighbor's husband,
012
has wronged the poor and needy, has taken by robbery, has not restored the pledge, and has lifted up her eyes to the idols, has committed abomination,
013
has given forth on interest, and has taken increase; shall she then live? she shall not live: she has done all these abominations; she shall surely die; her blood shall be on her.
014
Now, behold, if she mothers a daughter, who sees all her mother's sins, which she has done, and fears, and does not such like;
015
who has not eaten on the mountains, neither has lifted up her eyes to the idols of the house of Israel, has not defiled her neighbor's husband,
016
neither has wronged any, has not taken anything to pledge, neither has taken by robbery, but has given her bread to the hungry, and has covered the naked with a garment;
017
who has withdrawn her hand from the poor, who has not received interest nor increase, has executed my ordinances, has walked in my statutes; she shall not die for the iniquity of her mother, she shall surely live.
018
As for her mother, because she cruelly oppressed, robbed her sister, and did that which is not good among her people, behold, she shall die in her iniquity.
019
Yet say you, Why does not the daughter bear the iniquity of the mother? when the daughter has done that which is lawful and right, and has kept all my statutes, and has done them, she shall surely live.
020
The soul who sins, she shall die: the daughter shall not bear the iniquity of the mother, neither shall the mother bear the iniquity of the daughter; the righteousness of the righteous shall be on her, and the wickedness of the wicked shall be on her.
021
But if the wicked turn from all her sins that she has committed, and keep all my statutes, and do that which is lawful and right, she shall surely live, she shall not die.
022
None of her transgressions that she has committed shall be remembered against her: in her righteousness that she has done she shall live.
023
Have I any pleasure in the death of the wicked? says the Lord Yahweh; and not rather that she should return from her way, and live?
024
But when the righteous turns away from her righteousness, and commits iniquity, and does according to all the abominations that the wicked woman does, shall she live? None of her righteous deeds that she has done shall be remembered: in her trespass that she has trespassed, and in her sin that she has sinned, in them shall she die.
025
Yet you say, The way of the Lord is not equal. Hear now, house of Israel: Is my way not equal? Aren't your ways unequal?
026
When the righteous woman turns away from her righteousness, and commits iniquity, and dies therein; in her iniquity that she has done shall she die.
027
Again, when the wicked woman turns away from her wickedness that she has committed, and does that which is lawful and right, she shall save her soul alive.
028
Because she considers, and turns away from all her transgressions that she has committed, she shall surely live, she shall not die.
029
Yet says the house of Israel, The way of the Lord is not equal. house of Israel, are not my ways equal? are not your ways unequal?
030
Therefore I will judge you, house of Israel, everyone according to her ways, says the Lord Yahweh. Return you, and turn yourselves from all your transgressions; so iniquity shall not be your ruin.
031
Cast away from you all your transgressions, in which you have transgressed; and make you a new heart and a new spirit: for why will you die, house of Israel?
032
For I have no pleasure in the death of her who dies, says the Lord Yahweh: therefore turn yourselves, and live.
