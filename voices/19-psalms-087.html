Psalms 087

001
<> Hers foundation is in the holy mountains.
002
Yahweh loves the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob.
003
Glorious things are spoken about you, city of God. Selah.
004
I will record Rahab and Babylon among those who acknowledge me. Behold, Philistia, Tyre, and also Ethiopia: "This one was born there."
005
Yes, of Zion it will be said, "This one and that one was born in him;" the Most High herself will establish him.
006
Yahweh will count, when she writes up the peoples, "This one was born there." Selah.
007
Those who sing as well as those who dance say, "All my springs are in you."
