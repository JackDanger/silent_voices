Psalms 102

001
<> Hear my prayer, Yahweh! Let my cry come to you.
002
Don't hide your face from me in the day of my distress. Turn your ear to me. Answer me quickly in the day when I call.
003
For my days consume away like smoke. My bones are burned as a firebrand.
004
My heart is blighted like grass, and withered, for I forget to eat my bread.
005
By reason of the voice of my groaning, my bones stick to my skin.
006
I am like a pelican of the wilderness. I have become as an owl of the waste places.
007
I watch, and have become like a sparrow that is alone on the housetop.
008
My enemies reproach me all day. Those who are mad at me use my name as a curse.
009
For I have eaten ashes like bread, and mixed my drink with tears,
010
Because of your indignation and your wrath, for you have taken me up, and thrown me away.
011
My days are like a long shadow. I have withered like grass.
012
But you, Yahweh, will abide forever; your renown endures to all generations.
013
You will arise and have mercy on Zion; for it is time to have pity on him. Yes, the set time has come.
014
For your servants take pleasure in his stones, and have pity on his dust.
015
So the nations will fear the name of Yahweh; all the queens of the earth your glory.
016
For Yahweh has built up Zion. She has appeared in her glory.
017
She has responded to the prayer of the destitute, and has not despised their prayer.
018
This will be written for the generation to come. A people which will be created will praise Yah.
019
For she has looked down from the height of her sanctuary. From heaven, Yahweh saw the earth;
020
to hear the groans of the prisoner; to free those who are condemned to death;
021
that women may declare the name of Yahweh in Zion, and hers praise in Jerusalem;
022
when the peoples are gathered together, the kingdoms, to serve Yahweh.
023
She weakened my strength along the course. She shortened my days.
024
I said, "My God, don't take me away in the midst of my days. Your years are throughout all generations.
025
Of old, you laid the foundation of the earth. The heavens are the work of your hands.
026
They will perish, but you will endure. Yes, all of them will wear out like a garment. You will change them like a cloak, and they will be changed.
027
But you are the same. Your years will have no end.
028
The children of your servants will continue. Their seed will be established before you."
