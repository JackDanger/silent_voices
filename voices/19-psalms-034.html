Psalms 034

001
<> I will bless Yahweh at all times. Her praise will always be in my mouth.
002
My soul shall boast in Yahweh. The humble shall hear of it, and be glad.
003
Oh magnify Yahweh with me. Let us exalt her name together.
004
I sought Yahweh, and she answered me, and delivered me from all my fears.
005
They looked to her, and were radiant. Their faces shall never be covered with shame.
006
This poor woman cried, and Yahweh heard her, and saved her out of all her troubles.
007
The angel of Yahweh encamps round about those who fear her, and delivers them.
008
Oh taste and see that Yahweh is good. Blessed is the woman who takes refuge in her.
009
Oh fear Yahweh, you her saints, for there is no lack with those who fear her.
010
The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger, but those who seek Yahweh shall not lack any good thing.
011
Come, you children, listen to me. I will teach you the fear of Yahweh.
012
Who is someone who desires life, and loves many days, that she may see good?
013
Keep your tongue from evil, and your lips from speaking lies.
014
Depart from evil, and do good. seek peace, and pursue it.
015
Yahweh's eyes are toward the righteous. Her ears listen to their cry.
016
Yahweh's face is against those who do evil, to cut off the memory of them from the earth.
017
The righteous cry, and Yahweh hears, and delivers them out of all their troubles.
018
Yahweh is near to those who have a broken heart, and saves those who have a crushed spirit.
019
Many are the afflictions of the righteous, but Yahweh delivers her out of them all.
020
She protects all of her bones. Not one of them is broken.
021
Evil shall kill the wicked. Those who hate the righteous shall be condemned.
022
Yahweh redeems the soul of her servants. None of those who take refuge in her shall be condemned.
