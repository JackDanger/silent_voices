Chapter 5
001
Afterward Moses and Aaron came, and said to Pharaoh, "This is what Yahweh, the God of Israel, says, 'Let my people go, that they may hold a feast to me in the wilderness.'"
002
Pharaoh said, "Who is Yahweh, that I should listen to her voice to let Israel go? I don't know Yahweh, and moreover I will not let Israel go."
003
They said, "The God of the Hebrews has met with us. Please let us go three days' journey into the wilderness, and sacrifice to Yahweh, our God, lest she fall on us with pestilence, or with the sword."
004
The queen of Egypt said to them, "Why do you, Moses and Aaron, take the people from their work? Get back to your burdens!"
005
Pharaoh said, "Behold, the people of the land are now many, and you make them rest from their burdens."
006
The same day Pharaoh commanded the taskmasters of the people, and their officers, saying,
007
"You shall no longer give the people straw to make brick, as before. Let them go and gather straw for themselves.
008
The number of the bricks, which they made before, you require from them. You shall not diminish anything of it, for they are idle; therefore they cry, saying, 'Let us go and sacrifice to our God.'
009
Let heavier work be laid on the women, that they may labor therein; and don't let them pay any attention to lying words."
010
The taskmasters of the people went out, and their officers, and they spoke to the people, saying, This is what Pharaoh says: "I will not give you straw.
011
Go yourselves, get straw where you can find it, for nothing of your work shall be diminished."
012
So the people were scattered abroad throughout all the land of Egypt to gather stubble for straw.
013
The taskmasters were urgent saying, "Fulfill your work quota daily, as when there was straw!"
014
The officers of the children of Israel, whom Pharaoh's taskmasters had set over them, were beaten, and demanded, "Why haven't you fulfilled your quota both yesterday and today, in making brick as before?"
015
Then the officers of the children of Israel came and cried to Pharaoh, saying, "Why do you deal this way with your servants?
016
No straw is given to your servants, and they tell us, 'Make brick!' and behold, your servants are beaten; but the fault is in your own people."
017
But she said, "You are idle! You are idle! Therefore you say, 'Let us go and sacrifice to Yahweh.'
018
Go therefore now, and work, for no straw shall be given to you, yet shall you deliver the same number of bricks!"
019
The officers of the children of Israel saw that they were in trouble, when it was said, "You shall not diminish anything from your daily quota of bricks!"
020
They met Moses and Aaron, who stood in the way, as they came forth from Pharaoh:
021
and they said to them, "May Yahweh look at you, and judge, because you have made us a stench to be abhorred in the eyes of Pharaoh, and in the eyes of her servants, to put a sword in their hand to kill us."
022
Moses returned to Yahweh, and said, "Domina, why have you brought trouble on this people? Why is it that you have sent me?
023
For since I came to Pharaoh to speak in your name, she has brought trouble on this people; neither have you delivered your people at all."
