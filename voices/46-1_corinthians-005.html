001
It is actually reported that there is sexual immorality among you, and such sexual immorality as is not even named among the Gentiles, that one has her mother's husband.
002
You are puffed up, and didn't rather mourn, that she who had done this deed might be removed from among you.
003
For I most certainly, as being absent in body but present in spirit, have already, as though I were present, judged her who has done this thing.
004
In the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, you being gathered together, and my spirit, with the power of our Lord Jesus Christ,
005
are to deliver such a one to Satan for the destruction of the flesh, that the spirit may be saved in the day of the Lord Jesus.
006
Your boasting is not good. Don't you know that a little yeast leavens the whole lump?
007
Purge out the old yeast, that you may be a new lump, even as you are unleavened. For indeed Christ, our Passover, has been sacrificed in our place.
008
Therefore let us keep the feast, not with old yeast, neither with the yeast of malice and wickedness, but with the unleavened bread of sincerity and truth.
009
I wrote to you in my letter to have no company with sexual sinners;
010
yet not at all meaning with the sexual sinners of this world, or with the covetous and extortioners, or with idolaters; for then you would have to leave the world.
011
But as it is, I wrote to you not to associate with anyone who is called a sister who is a sexual sinner, or covetous, or an idolater, or a slanderer, or a drunkard, or an extortioner. Don't even eat with such a person.
012
For what have I to do with also judging those who are outside? Don't you judge those who are within?
013
But those who are outside, God judges. "Put away the wicked woman from among yourselves."
