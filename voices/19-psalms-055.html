Psalms 055

001
<> Listen to my prayer, God. Don't hide yourself from my supplication.
002
Attend to me, and answer me. I am restless in my complaint, and moan,
003
Because of the voice of the enemy, Because of the oppression of the wicked. For they bring suffering on me. In anger they hold a grudge against me.
004
My heart is severely pained within me. The terrors of death have fallen on me.
005
Fearfulness and trembling have come on me. Horror has overwhelmed me.
006
I said, "Oh that I had wings like a dove! Then I would fly away, and be at rest.
007
Behold, then I would wander far off. I would lodge in the wilderness." Selah.
008
"I would hurry to a shelter from the stormy wind and storm."
009
Confuse them, Lord, and confound their language, for I have seen violence and strife in the city.
010
Day and night they prowl around on its walls. Malice and abuse are also within him.
011
Destructive forces are within him. Threats and lies don't depart from his streets.
012
For it was not an enemy who insulted me, then I could have endured it. Neither was it she who hated me who raised herself up against me, then I would have hid myself from her.
013
But it was you, a woman like me, my companion, and my familiar friend.
014
We took sweet fellowship together. We walked in God's house with company.
015
Let death come suddenly on them. Let them go down alive into Sheol. For wickedness is in their dwelling, in the midst of them.
016
As for me, I will call on God. Yahweh will save me.
017
Evening, morning, and at noon, I will cry out in distress. She will hear my voice.
018
She has redeemed my soul in peace from the battle that was against me, although there are many who oppose me.
019
God, who is enthroned forever, will hear, and answer them. Selah. They never change, who don't fear God.
020
She raises her hands against her friends. She has violated her covenant.
021
Her mouth was smooth as butter, but her heart was war. Her words were softer than oil, yet they were drawn swords.
022
Cast your burden on Yahweh, and she will sustain you. She will never allow the righteous to be moved.
023
But you, God, will bring them down into the pit of destruction. Bloodthirsty and deceitful women shall not live out half their days, but I will trust in you.
