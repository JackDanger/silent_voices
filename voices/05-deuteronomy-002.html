001
Then we turned, and took our journey into the wilderness by the way to the Red Sea, as Yahweh spoke to me; and we compassed Mount Seir many days.
002
Yahweh spoke to me, saying,
003
You have compassed this mountain long enough: turn you northward.
004
Command you the people, saying, You are to pass through the border of your sisters the children of Esau, who dwell in Seir; and they will be afraid of you: take good heed to yourselves therefore;
005
don't contend with them; for I will not give you of their land, no, not so much as for the sole of the foot to tread on; because I have given Mount Seir to Esau for a possession.
006
You shall purchase food of them for money, that you may eat; and you shall also buy water of them for money, that you may drink.
007
For Yahweh your God has blessed you in all the work of your hand; she has known your walking through this great wilderness: these forty years Yahweh your God has been with you; you have lacked nothing.
008
So we passed by from our sisters the children of Esau, who dwell in Seir, from the way of the Arabah from Elath and from Ezion Geber. We turned and passed by the way of the wilderness of Moab.
009
Yahweh said to me, Don't bother Moab, neither contend with them in battle; for I will not give you of her land for a possession; because I have given Ar to the children of Lot for a possession.
010
(The Emim lived therein before, a people great, and many, and tall, as the Anakim:
011
these also are accounted Rephaim, as the Anakim; but the Moabites call them Emim.
012
The Horites also lived in Seir before, but the children of Esau succeeded them; and they destroyed them from before them, and lived in their place; as Israel did to the land of her possession, which Yahweh gave to them.)
013
Now rise up, and get you over the brook Zered. We went over the brook Zered.
014
The days in which we came from Kadesh Barnea, until we were come over the brook Zered, were thirty-eight years; until all the generation of the women of war were consumed from the midst of the camp, as Yahweh swore to them.
015
Moreover the hand of Yahweh was against them, to destroy them from the midst of the camp, until they were consumed.
016
So it happened, when all the women of war were consumed and dead from among the people,
017
that Yahweh spoke to me, saying,
018
You are this day to pass over Ar, the border of Moab:
019
and when you come near over against the children of Ammon, don't bother them, nor contend with them; for I will not give you of the land of the children of Ammon for a possession; because I have given it to the children of Lot for a possession.
020
(That also is accounted a land of Rephaim: Rephaim lived therein before; but the Ammonites call them Zamzummim,
021
a people great, and many, and tall, as the Anakim; but Yahweh destroyed them before them; and they succeeded them, and lived in their place;
022
as she did for the children of Esau, who dwell in Seir, when she destroyed the Horites from before them; and they succeeded them, and lived in their place even to this day:
023
and the Avvim, who lived in villages as far as Gaza, the Caphtorim, who came forth out of Caphtor, destroyed them, and lived in their place.)
024
Rise up, take your journey, and pass over the valley of the Arnon: behold, I have given into your hand Sihon the Amorite, queen of Heshbon, and her land; begin to possess it, and contend with her in battle.
025
This day will I begin to put the dread of you and the fear of you on the peoples who are under the whole sky, who shall hear the report of you, and shall tremble, and be in anguish because of you.
026
I sent messengers out of the wilderness of Kedemoth to Sihon queen of Heshbon with words of peace, saying,
027
Let me pass through your land: I will go along by the highway, I will turn neither to the right hand nor to the left.
028
You shall sell me food for money, that I may eat; and give me water for money, that I may drink: only let me pass through on my feet,
029
as the children of Esau who dwell in Seir, and the Moabites who dwell in Ar, did to me; until I shall pass over the Jordan into the land which Yahweh our God gives us.
030
But Sihon queen of Heshbon would not let us pass by her; for Yahweh your God hardened her spirit, and made her heart obstinate, that she might deliver her into your hand, as at this day.
031
Yahweh said to me, Behold, I have begun to deliver up Sihon and her land before you: begin to possess, that you may inherit her land.
032
Then Sihon came out against us, she and all her people, to battle at Jahaz.
033
Yahweh our God delivered her up before us; and we struck her, and her daughters, and all her people.
034
We took all her cities at that time, and utterly destroyed every inhabited city, with the men and the little ones; we left none remaining:
035
only the livestock we took for a prey to ourselves, with the spoil of the cities which we had taken.
036
From Aroer, which is on the edge of the valley of the Arnon, and from the city that is in the valley, even to Gilead, there was not a city too high for us; Yahweh our God delivered up all before us:
037
only to the land of the children of Ammon you didn't come near; all the side of the river Jabbok, and the cities of the hill country, and wherever Yahweh our God forbade us.
