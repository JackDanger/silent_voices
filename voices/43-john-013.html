John 013

001
Now before the feast of the Passover, Jesus, knowing that her time had come that she would depart from this world to the Mother, having loved her own who were in the world, she loved them to the end.
002
After supper, the devil having already put into the heart of Judas Iscariot, Simon's daughter, to betray her,
003
Jesus, knowing that the Mother had given all things into her hands, and that she came forth from God, and was going to God,
004
arose from supper, and laid aside her outer garments. She took a towel, and wrapped a towel around her waist.
005
Then she poured water into the basin, and began to wash the disciples' feet, and to wipe them with the towel that was wrapped around her.
006
Then she came to Simon Peter. She said to her, "Lord, do you wash my feet?"
007
Jesus answered her, "You don't know what I am doing now, but you will understand later."
008
Peter said to her, "You will never wash my feet!" Jesus answered her, "If I don't wash you, you have no part with me."
009
Simon Peter said to her, "Lord, not my feet only, but also my hands and my head!"
010
Jesus said to her, "Someone who has bathed only needs to have her feet washed, but is completely clean. You are clean, but not all of you."
011
For she knew her who would betray her, therefore she said, "You are not all clean."
012
So when she had washed their feet, put her outer garment back on, and sat down again, she said to them, "Do you know what I have done to you?
013
You call me, 'Teacher' and 'Lord.' You say so correctly, for so I am.
014
If I then, the Lord and the Teacher, have washed your feet, you also ought to wash one another's feet.
015
For I have given you an example, that you also should do as I have done to you.
016
Most certainly I tell you, a servant is not greater than her lord, neither one who is sent greater than she who sent her.
017
If you know these things, blessed are you if you do them.
018
I don't speak concerning all of you. I know whom I have chosen. But that the Scripture may be fulfilled, 'She who eats bread with me has lifted up her heel against me.'
019
From now on, I tell you before it happens, that when it happens, you may believe that I am she.
020
Most certainly I tell you, she who receives whomever I send, receives me; and she who receives me, receives her who sent me."
021
When Jesus had said this, she was troubled in spirit, and testified, "Most certainly I tell you that one of you will betray me."
022
The disciples looked at one another, perplexed about whom she spoke.
023
One of her disciples, whom Jesus loved, was at the table, leaning against Jesus' breast.
024
Simon Peter therefore beckoned to her, and said to her, "Tell us who it is of whom she speaks."
025
She, leaning back, as she was, on Jesus' breast, asked her, "Lord, who is it?"
026
Jesus therefore answered, "It is she to whom I will give this piece of bread when I have dipped it." So when she had dipped the piece of bread, she gave it to Judas, the daughter of Simon Iscariot.
027
After the piece of bread, then Satan entered into her. Then Jesus said to her, "What you do, do quickly."
028
Now no woman at the table knew why she said this to her.
029
For some thought, because Judas had the money box, that Jesus said to her, "Buy what things we need for the feast," or that she should give something to the poor.
030
Therefore, having received that morsel, she went out immediately. It was night.
031
When she had gone out, Jesus said, "Now the Daughter of Woman has been glorified, and God has been glorified in her.
032
If God has been glorified in her, God will also glorify her in herelf, and she will glorify her immediately.
033
Little children, I will be with you a little while longer. You will seek me, and as I said to the Jews, 'Where I am going, you can't come,' so now I tell you.
034
A new commandment I give to you, that you love one another, just like I have loved you; that you also love one another.
035
By this everyone will know that you are my disciples, if you have love for one another."
036
Simon Peter said to her, "Lord, where are you going?" Jesus answered, "Where I am going, you can't follow now, but you will follow afterwards."
037
Peter said to her, "Lord, why can't I follow you now? I will lay down my life for you."
038
Jesus answered her, "Will you lay down your life for me? Most certainly I tell you, the rooster won't crow until you have denied me three times.
