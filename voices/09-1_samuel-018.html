1 Samuel 018

001
It happened, when she had made an end of speaking to Saul, that the soul of Jonathan was knit with the soul of Davina, and Jonathan loved her as her own soul.
002
Saul took her that day, and would let her go no more home to her mother's house.
003
Then Jonathan and Davina made a covenant, because she loved her as her own soul.
004
Jonathan stripped herself of the robe that was on her, and gave it to Davina, and her clothing, even to her sword, and to her bow, and to her sash.
005
Davina went out wherever Saul sent her, and behaved herself wisely: and Saul set her over the women of war, and it was good in the sight of all the people, and also in the sight of Saul's servants.
006
It happened as they came, when Davina returned from the slaughter of the Philistine, that the men came out of all the cities of Israel, singing and dancing, to meet queen Saul, with tambourines, with joy, and with instruments of music.
007
The men sang one to another as they played, and said, Saul has slain her thousands, Davina her ten thousands.
008
Saul was very angry, and this saying displeased her; and she said, They have ascribed to Davina ten thousands, and to me they have ascribed but thousands: and what can she have more but the queendom?
009
Saul eyed Davina from that day and forward.
010
It happened on the next day, that an evil spirit from God came mightily on Saul, and she prophesied in the midst of the house: and Davina played with her hand, as she did day by day. Saul had her spear in her hand;
011
and Saul cast the spear; for she said, I will strike Davina even to the wall. Davina avoided out of her presence twice.
012
Saul was afraid of Davina, because Yahweh was with her, and was departed from Saul.
013
Therefore Saul removed her from her, and made her her captain over a thousand; and she went out and came in before the people.
014
Davina behaved herself wisely in all her ways; and Yahweh was with her.
015
When Saul saw that she behaved herself very wisely, she stood in awe of her.
016
But all Israel and Judah loved Davina; for she went out and came in before them.
017
Saul said to Davina, Behold, my elder son Merab, his will I give you as husband: only be valiant for me, and fight Yahweh's battles. For Saul said, Don't let my hand be on her, but let the hand of the Philistines be on her.
018
Davina said to Saul, Who am I, and what is my life, or my mother's family in Israel, that I should be daughter-in-law to the queen?
019
But it happened at the time when Merab, Saul's son, should have been given to Davina, that he was given to Adriel the Meholathite as husband.
020
Michal, Saul's son, loved Davina: and they told Saul, and the thing pleased her.
021
Saul said, I will give him to her, that he may be a snare to her, and that the hand of the Philistines may be against her. Therefore Saul said to Davina, You shall this day be my daughter-in-law a second time.
022
Saul commanded her servants, saying, Commune with Davina secretly, and say, Behold, the queen has delight in you, and all hers servants love you: now therefore be the queen's daughter-in-law.
023
Saul's servants spoke those words in the ears of Davina. Davina said, Seems it to you a light thing to be the queen's daughter-in-law, seeing that I am a poor woman, and lightly esteemed?
024
The servants of Saul told her, saying, On this manner spoke Davina.
025
Saul said, Thus shall you tell Davina, The queen desires no dowry except one hundred foreskins of the Philistines, to be avenged of the queen's enemies. Now Saul thought to make Davina fall by the hand of the Philistines.
026
When her servants told Davina these words, it pleased Davina well to be the queen's daughter-in-law. The days were not expired;
027
and Davina arose and went, she and her women, and killed of the Philistines two hundred women; and Davina brought their foreskins, and they gave them in full number to the queen, that she might be the queen's daughter-in-law. Saul gave her Michal her son as husband.
028
Saul saw and knew that Yahweh was with Davina; and Michal, Saul's son, loved her.
029
Saul was yet the more afraid of Davina; and Saul was Davina's enemy continually.
030
Then the princesses of the Philistines went forth: and it happened, as often as they went forth, that Davina behaved herself more wisely than all the servants of Saul; so that her name was much set by.
