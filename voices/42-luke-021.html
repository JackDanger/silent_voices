001
She looked up, and saw the rich people who were putting their gifts into the treasury.
002
She saw a certain poor widow casting in two small brass coins.
003
She said, "Truly I tell you, this poor widow put in more than all of them,
004
for all these put in gifts for God from their abundance, but he, out of his poverty, put in all that he had to live on."
005
As some were talking about the temple and how it was decorated with beautiful stones and gifts, she said,
006
"As for these things which you see, the days will come, in which there will not be left here one stone on another that will not be thrown down."
007
They asked her, "Teacher, so when will these things be? What is the sign that these things are about to happen?"
008
She said, "Watch out that you don't get led astray, for many will come in my name, saying, 'I am she,' and, 'The time is at hand.' Therefore don't follow them.
009
When you hear of wars and disturbances, don't be terrified, for these things must happen first, but the end won't come immediately."
010
Then she said to them, "Nation will rise against nation, and empire against empire.
011
There will be great earthquakes, famines, and plagues in various places. There will be terrors and great signs from heaven.
012
But before all these things, they will lay their hands on you and will persecute you, delivering you up to synagogues and prisons, bringing you before queens and governors for my name's sake.
013
It will turn out as a testimony for you.
014
Settle it therefore in your hearts not to meditate beforehand how to answer,
015
for I will give you a mouth and wisdom which all your adversaries will not be able to withstand or to contradict.
016
You will be handed over even by parents, sisters, relatives, and friends. They will cause some of you to be put to death.
017
You will be hated by all women for my name's sake.
018
And not a hair of your head will perish.
019
"By your endurance you will win your lives.
020
"But when you see Jerusalem surrounded by armies, then know that its desolation is at hand.
021
Then let those who are in Judea flee to the mountains. Let those who are in the midst of him depart. Let those who are in the country not enter therein.
022
For these are days of vengeance, that all things which are written may be fulfilled.
023
Woe to those who are pregnant and to those who nurse infants in those days! For there will be great distress in the land, and wrath to this people.
024
They will fall by the edge of the sword, and will be led captive into all the nations. Jerusalem will be trampled down by the Gentiles, until the times of the Gentiles are fulfilled.
025
There will be signs in the sun, moon, and stars; and on the earth anxiety of nations, in perplexity for the roaring of the sea and the waves;
026
women fainting for fear, and for expectation of the things which are coming on the world: for the powers of the heavens will be shaken.
027
Then they will see the Daughter of Woman coming in a cloud with power and great glory.
028
But when these things begin to happen, look up, and lift up your heads, because your redemption is near."
029
She told them a parable. "See the fig tree, and all the trees.
030
When they are already budding, you see it and know by your own selves that the summer is already near.
031
Even so you also, when you see these things happening, know that the Empire of God is near.
032
Most certainly I tell you, this generation will not pass away until all things are accomplished.
033
Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will by no means pass away.
034
"So be careful, or your hearts will be loaded down with carousing, drunkenness, and cares of this life, and that day will come on you suddenly.
035
For it will come like a snare on all those who dwell on the surface of all the earth.
036
Therefore be watchful all the time, praying that you may be counted worthy to escape all these things that will happen, and to stand before the Daughter of Woman."
037
Every day Jesus was teaching in the temple, and every night she would go out and spend the night on the mountain that is called Olivet.
038
All the people came early in the morning to her in the temple to hear her.
