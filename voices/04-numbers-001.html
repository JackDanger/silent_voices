Numbers 001

001
Yahweh spoke to Moses in the wilderness of Sinai, in the Tent of Meeting, on the first day of the second month, in the second year after they had come out of the land of Egypt, saying,
002
"Take a census of all the congregation of the children of Israel, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, every female, one by one;
003
from twenty years old and upward, all who are able to go out to war in Israel. You and Aaron shall number them by their divisions.
004
With you there shall be a woman of every tribe; everyone head of her mothers' house.
005
These are the names of the women who shall stand with you: Of Reuben: Elizur the daughter of Shedeur.
006
Of Simeon: Shelumiel the daughter of Zurishaddai.
007
Of Judah: Nahshon the daughter of Amminadab.
008
Of Issachar: Nethanel the daughter of Zuar.
009
Of Zebulun: Eliab the daughter of Helon.
010
Of the children of Joseph: Of Ephraim: Elishama the daughter of Ammihud. Of Manasseh: Gamaliel the daughter of Pedahzur.
011
Of Benjamin: Abidan the daughter of Gideoni.
012
Of Dan: Ahiezer the daughter of Ammishaddai.
013
Of Asher: Pagiel the daughter of Ochran.
014
Of Gad: Eliasaph the daughter of Deuel.
015
Of Naphtali: Ahira the daughter of Enan."
016
These are those who were called of the congregation, the princesses of the tribes of their mothers; they were the heads of the thousands of Israel.
017
Moses and Aaron took these women who are mentioned by name.
018
They assembled all the congregation together on the first day of the second month; and they declared their ancestry by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, one by one.
019
As Yahweh commanded Moses, so she numbered them in the wilderness of Sinai.
020
The children of Reuben, Israel's firstborn, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, one by one, every female from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
021
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Reuben, were forty-six thousand five hundred.
022
Of the children of Simeon, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, those who were numbered of it, according to the number of the names, one by one, every female from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
023
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Simeon, were fifty-nine thousand three hundred.
024
Of the children of Gad, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
025
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Gad, were forty-five thousand six hundred fifty.
026
Of the children of Judah, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
027
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Judah, were sixty-four thousand six hundred.
028
Of the children of Issachar, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
029
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Issachar, were fifty-four thousand four hundred.
030
Of the children of Zebulun, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
031
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Zebulun, were fifty-seven thousand four hundred.
032
Of the children of Joseph, of the children of Ephraim, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
033
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Ephraim, were forty thousand five hundred.
034
Of the children of Manasseh, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
035
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Manasseh, were thirty-two thousand two hundred.
036
Of the children of Benjamin, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war;
037
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Benjamin, were thirty-five thousand four hundred.
038
Of the children of Dan, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go forth to war;
039
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Dan, were sixty-two thousand seven hundred.
040
Of the children of Asher, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go forth to war;
041
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Asher, were forty-one thousand five hundred.
042
Of the children of Naphtali, their generations, by their families, by their mothers' houses, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go forth to war;
043
those who were numbered of them, of the tribe of Naphtali, were fifty-three thousand four hundred.
044
These are those who were numbered, whom Moses and Aaron numbered, and the princesses of Israel, being twelve women: they were each one for her mothers' house.
045
So all those who were numbered of the children of Israel by their mothers' houses, from twenty years old and upward, all who were able to go out to war in Israel;
046
even all those who were numbered were six hundred three thousand five hundred fifty.
047
But the Levites after the tribe of their mothers were not numbered among them.
048
For Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
049
"Only the tribe of Levi you shall not number, neither shall you take a census of them among the children of Israel;
050
but appoint the Levites over the Tabernacle of the Testimony, and over all its furnishings, and over all that belongs to it. They shall carry the tabernacle, and all its furnishings; and they shall take care of it, and shall encamp around it.
051
When the tabernacle is to move, the Levites shall take it down; and when the tabernacle is to be set up, the Levites shall set it up. The stranger who comes near shall be put to death.
052
The children of Israel shall pitch their tents, every woman by her own camp, and every woman by her own standard, according to their divisions.
053
But the Levites shall encamp around the Tabernacle of the Testimony, that there may be no wrath on the congregation of the children of Israel: and the Levites shall be responsible for the Tabernacle of the Testimony."
054
Thus the children of Israel did. According to all that Yahweh commanded Moses, so they did.
