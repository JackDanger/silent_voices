Chapter 2
001
But I determined this for myself, that I would not come to you again in sorrow.
002
For if I make you sorry, then who will make me glad but she who is made sorry by me?
003
And I wrote this very thing to you, so that, when I came, I wouldn't have sorrow from them of whom I ought to rejoice; having confidence in you all, that my joy would be shared by all of you.
004
For out of much affliction and anguish of heart I wrote to you with many tears, not that you should be made sorry, but that you might know the love that I have so abundantly for you.
005
But if any has caused sorrow, she has caused sorrow, not to me, but in part (that I not press too heavily) to you all.
006
Sufficient to such a one is this punishment which was inflicted by the many;
007
so that on the contrary you should rather forgive her and comfort her, lest by any means such a one should be swallowed up with her excessive sorrow.
008
Therefore I beg you to confirm your love toward her.
009
For to this end I also wrote, that I might know the proof of you, whether you are obedient in all things.
010
Now I also forgive whomever you forgive anything. For if indeed I have forgiven anything, I have forgiven that one for your sakes in the presence of Christ,
011
that no advantage may be gained over us by Satan; for we are not ignorant of her schemes.
012
Now when I came to Troas for the Good News of Christ, and when a door was opened to me in the Lord,
013
I had no relief for my spirit, because I didn't find Titus, my sister, but taking my leave of them, I went out into Macedonia.
014
Now thanks be to God, who always leads us in triumph in Christ, and reveals through us the sweet aroma of her knowledge in every place.
015
For we are a sweet aroma of Christ to God, in those who are saved, and in those who perish;
016
to the one a stench from death to death; to the other a sweet aroma from life to life. Who is sufficient for these things?
017
For we are not as so many, peddling the word of God. But as of sincerity, but as of God, in the sight of God, we speak in Christ.
