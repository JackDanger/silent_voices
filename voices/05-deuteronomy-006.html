Deuteronomy 006

001
Now this is the commandment, the statutes, and the ordinances, which Yahweh your God commanded to teach you, that you might do them in the land where you go over to possess it;
002
that you might fear Yahweh your God, to keep all her statutes and her commandments, which I command you, you, and your daughter, and your daughter's daughter, all the days of your life; and that your days may be prolonged.
003
Hear therefore, Israel, and observe to do it; that it may be well with you, and that you may increase mightily, as Yahweh, the God of your mothers, has promised to you, in a land flowing with milk and honey.
004
Hear, Israel: Yahweh is our God; Yahweh is one:
005
and you shall love Yahweh your God with all your heart, and with all your soul, and with all your might.
006
These words, which I command you this day, shall be on your heart;
007
and you shall teach them diligently to your children, and shall talk of them when you sit in your house, and when you walk by the way, and when you lie down, and when you rise up.
008
You shall bind them for a sign on your hand, and they shall be for symbols between your eyes.
009
You shall write them on the door posts of your house, and on your gates.
010
It shall be, when Yahweh your God shall bring you into the land which she swore to your mothers, to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob, to give you, great and goodly cities, which you didn't build,
011
and houses full of all good things, which you didn't fill, and cisterns dug out, which you didn't dig, vineyards and olive trees, which you didn't plant, and you shall eat and be full;
012
then beware lest you forget Yahweh, who brought you forth out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage.
013
You shall fear Yahweh your God; and her shall you serve, and shall swear by her name.
014
You shall not go after other gods, of the gods of the peoples who are round about you;
015
for Yahweh your God in the midst of you is a jealous God; lest the anger of Yahweh your God be kindled against you, and she destroy you from off the face of the earth.
016
You shall not tempt Yahweh your God, as you tempted her in Massah.
017
You shall diligently keep the commandments of Yahweh your God, and her testimonies, and her statutes, which she has commanded you.
018
You shall do that which is right and good in the sight of Yahweh; that it may be well with you, and that you may go in and possess the good land which Yahweh swore to your mothers,
019
to thrust out all your enemies from before you, as Yahweh has spoken.
020
When your daughter asks you in time to come, saying, What mean the testimonies, and the statutes, and the ordinances, which Yahweh our God has commanded you?
021
then you shall tell your daughter, We were Pharaoh's bondservants in Egypt: and Yahweh brought us out of Egypt with a mighty hand;
022
and Yahweh showed great and awesome signs and wonders on Egypt, on Pharaoh, and on all her house, before our eyes;
023
and she brought us out from there, that she might bring us in, to give us the land which she swore to our mothers.
024
Yahweh commanded us to do all these statutes, to fear Yahweh our God, for our good always, that she might preserve us alive, as at this day.
025
It shall be righteousness to us, if we observe to do all this commandment before Yahweh our God, as she has commanded us.
