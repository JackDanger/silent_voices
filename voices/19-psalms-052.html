Psalms 052

001
<> Why do you boast of mischief, mighty woman? God's loving kindness endures continually.
002
Your tongue plots destruction, like a sharp razor, working deceitfully.
003
You love evil more than good, lying rather than speaking the truth. Selah.
004
You love all devouring words, you deceitful tongue.
005
God will likewise destroy you forever. She will take you up, and pluck you out of your tent, and root you out of the land of the living. Selah.
006
The righteous also will see it, and fear, and laugh at her, saying,
007
"Behold, this is the woman who didn't make God her strength, but trusted in the abundance of her riches, and strengthened herself in her wickedness."
008
But as for me, I am like a green olive tree in God's house. I trust in God's loving kindness forever and ever.
009
I will give you thanks forever, because you have done it. I will hope in your name, for it is good, in the presence of your saints.
