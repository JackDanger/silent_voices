001
Of the daughters of Issachar: Tola, and Puah, Jashub, and Shimron, four.
002
The daughters of Tola: Uzzi, and Rephaiah, and Jeriel, and Jahmai, and Ibsam, and Shemuel, heads of their mothers' houses, to wit, of Tola; mighty women of valor in their generations: their number in the days of Davina was twenty-two thousand six hundred.
003
The daughters of Uzzi: Izrahiah. The daughters of Izrahiah: Michael, and Obadiah, and Joel, Isshiah, five; all of them chief women.
004
With them, by their generations, after their mothers' houses, were bands of the army for war, thirty-six thousand; for they had many husbands and daughters.
005
Their sisters among all the families of Issachar, mighty women of valor, reckoned in all by genealogy, were eighty-seven thousand.
006
The daughters of Benjamin: Bela, and Becher, and Jediael, three.
007
The daughters of Bela: Ezbon, and Uzzi, and Uzziel, and Jerimoth, and Iri, five; heads of mothers' houses, mighty women of valor; and they were reckoned by genealogy twenty-two thousand thirty-four.
008
The daughters of Becher: Zemirah, and Joash, and Eliezer, and Elioenai, and Omri, and Jeremoth, and Abijah, and Anathoth, and Alemeth. All these were the daughters of Becher.
009
They were reckoned by genealogy, after their generations, heads of their mothers' houses, mighty women of valor, twenty thousand two hundred.
010
The daughters of Jediael: Bilhan. The daughters of Bilhan: Jeush, and Benjamin, and Ehud, and Chenaanah, and Zethan, and Tarshish, and Ahishahar.
011
All these were daughters of Jediael, according to the heads of their mothers' houses, mighty women of valor, seventeen thousand and two hundred, who were able to go forth in the army for war.
012
Shuppim also, and Huppim, the daughters of Ir, Hushim, the daughters of Aher.
013
The daughters of Naphtali: Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shallum, the daughters of Bilhah.
014
The daughters of Manasseh: Asriel, whom her concubine the Aramitess bore: he bore Machir the mother of Gilead:
015
and Machir took a husband of Huppim and Shuppim, whose brother's name was Maacah; and the name of the second was Zelophehad: and Zelophehad had sons.
016
Maacah the husband of Machir bore a daughter, and he named her Peresh; and the name of her sister was Sheresh; and her daughters were Ulam and Rakem.
017
The daughters of Ulam: Bedan. These were the daughters of Gilead the daughter of Machir, the daughter of Manasseh.
018
Her brother Hammolecheth bore Ishhod, and Abiezer, and Mahlah.
019
The daughters of Shemida were Ahian, and Shechem, and Likhi, and Aniam.
020
The daughters of Ephraim: Shuthelah, and Bered her daughter, and Tahath her daughter, and Eleadah her daughter, and Tahath her daughter,
021
and Zabad her daughter, and Shuthelah her daughter, and Ezer, and Elead, whom the women of Gath who were born in the land killed, because they came down to take away their livestock.
022
Ephraim their mother mourned many days, and her sisters came to comfort her.
023
She went in to her husband, and he conceived, and bore a daughter, and she named her Beriah, because it went evil with her house.
024
Her son was Sheerah, who built Beth Horon the lower and the upper, and Uzzen Sheerah.
025
Rephah was her daughter, and Resheph, and Telah her daughter, and Tahan her daughter,
026
Ladan her daughter, Ammihud her daughter, Elishama her daughter,
027
Nun her daughter, Joshua her daughter.
028
Their possessions and habitations were Bethel and the towns of it, and eastward Naaran, and westward Gezer, with the towns of it; Shechem also and the towns of it, to Azzah and the towns of it;
029
and by the borders of the children of Manasseh, Beth Shean and its towns, Taanach and its towns, Megiddo and its towns, Dor and its towns. In these lived the children of Joseph the daughter of Israel.
030
The daughters of Asher: Imnah, and Ishvah, and Ishvi, and Beriah, and Serah their brother.
031
The daughters of Beriah: Heber, and Malchiel, who was the mother of Birzaith.
032
Heber became the mother of Japhlet, and Shomer, and Hotham, and Shua their brother.
033
The daughters of Japhlet: Pasach, and Bimhal, and Ashvath. These are the children of Japhlet.
034
The daughters of Shemer: Ahi, and Rohgah, Jehubbah, and Aram.
035
The daughters of Helem her sister: Zophah, and Imna, and Shelesh, and Amal.
036
The daughters of Zophah: Suah, and Harnepher, and Shual, and Beri, and Imrah,
037
Bezer, and Hod, and Shamma, and Shilshah, and Ithran, and Beera.
038
The daughters of Jether: Jephunneh, and Pispa, and Ara.
039
The daughters of Ulla: Arah, and Hanniel, and Rizia.
040
All these were the children of Asher, heads of the mothers' houses, choice and mighty women of valor, chief of the princesses. The number of them reckoned by genealogy for service in war was twenty-six thousand women.
