Isaiah 003

001
For, behold, the Lord, Yahweh of Armies, takes away from Jerusalem and from Judah supply and support, the whole supply of bread, and the whole supply of water;
002
the mighty woman, the woman of war, the judge, the prophet, the diviner, the elder,
003
the captain of fifty, the honorable woman, the counselor, the skilled craftswoman, and the clever enchanter.
004
I will give girls to be their princesses, and children shall rule over them.
005
The people will be oppressed, everyone by another, and everyone by her neighbor. The child will behave herself proudly against the old woman, and the base against the honorable.
006
Indeed a woman shall take hold of her sister in the house of her mother, saying, "You have clothing, you be our ruler, and let this ruin be under your hand."
007
In that day she will cry out, saying, I will not be a healer; for in my house is neither bread nor clothing. You shall not make me ruler of the people.
008
For Jerusalem is ruined, and Judah is fallen; because their tongue and their doings are against Yahweh, to provoke the eyes of her glory.
009
The look of their faces testify against them. They parade their sin like Sodom. They don't hide it. Woe to their soul! For they have brought disaster upon themselves.
010
Tell the righteous "Good!" For they shall eat the fruit of their deeds.
011
Woe to the wicked! Disaster is upon them; for the deeds of her hands will be paid back to her.
012
As for my people, children are their oppressors, and men rule over them. My people, those who lead you cause you to err, and destroy the way of your paths.
013
Yahweh stands up to contend, and stands to judge the peoples.
014
Yahweh will enter into judgment with the elders of her people, and their leaders: "It is you who have eaten up the vineyard. The spoil of the poor is in your houses.
015
What do you mean that you crush my people, and grind the face of the poor?" says the Lord, Yahweh of Armies.
016
Moreover Yahweh said, "Because the sons of Zion are haughty, and walk with outstretched necks and flirting eyes, walking to trip as they go, jingling ornaments on their feet;
017
therefore the Lord brings sores on the crown of the head of the men of Zion, and Yahweh will make their scalps bald."
018
In that day the Lord will take away the beauty of their anklets, the headbands, the crescent necklaces,
019
the earrings, the bracelets, the veils,
020
the headdresses, the ankle chains, the sashes, the perfume bottles, the charms,
021
the signet rings, the nose rings,
022
the fine robes, the capes, the cloaks, the purses,
023
the hand mirrors, the fine linen garments, the tiaras, and the shawls.
024
It shall happen that instead of sweet spices, there shall be rottenness; instead of a belt, a rope; instead of well set hair, baldness; instead of a robe, a girding of sackcloth; and branding instead of beauty.
025
Your women shall fall by the sword, and your mighty in the war.
026
his gates shall lament and mourn; and he shall be desolate and sit on the ground.
