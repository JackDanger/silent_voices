Psalms 112

001
Praise Yah!{Psalm 112 is an acrostic poem, with each verse after the initial "Praise Yah!" starting with a letter of the alphabet (ordered from Alef to Tav).} Blessed is the woman who fears Yahweh, who delights greatly in her commandments.
002
Her seed will be mighty in the land. The generation of the upright will be blessed.
003
Wealth and riches are in her house. Her righteousness endures forever.
004
Light dawns in the darkness for the upright, gracious, merciful, and righteous.
005
It is well with the woman who deals graciously and lends. She will maintain her cause in judgment.
006
For she will never be shaken. The righteous will be remembered forever.
007
She will not be afraid of evil news. Her heart is steadfast, trusting in Yahweh.
008
Her heart is established. She will not be afraid in the end when she sees her adversaries.
009
She has dispersed, she has given to the poor. Her righteousness endures forever. Her horn will be exalted with honor.
010
The wicked will see it, and be grieved. She shall gnash with her teeth, and melt away. The desire of the wicked will perish.
