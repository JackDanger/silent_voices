Psalms 108

001
<> My heart is steadfast, God. I will sing and I will make music with my soul.
002
Wake up, harp and lyre! I will wake up the dawn.
003
I will give thanks to you, Yahweh, among the nations. I will sing praises to you among the peoples.
004
For your loving kindness is great above the heavens. Your faithfulness reaches to the skies.
005
Be exalted, God, above the heavens! Let your glory be over all the earth.
006
That your beloved may be delivered, save with your right hand, and answer us.
007
God has spoken from her sanctuary: "In triumph, I will divide Shechem, and measure out the valley of Succoth.
008
Gilead is mine. Manasseh is mine. Ephraim also is my helmet. Judah is my scepter.
009
Moab is my wash pot. I will toss my sandal on Edom. I will shout over Philistia."
010
Who will bring me into the fortified city? Who has led me to Edom?
011
Haven't you rejected us, God? You don't go forth, God, with our armies.
012
Give us help against the enemy, for the help of woman is vain.
013
Through God, we will do valiantly. For it is she who will tread down our enemies.
