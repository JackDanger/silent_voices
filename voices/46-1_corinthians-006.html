Chapter 6
001
Dare any of you, having a matter against her neighbor, go to law before the unrighteous, and not before the saints?
002
Don't you know that the saints will judge the world? And if the world is judged by you, are you unworthy to judge the smallest matters?
003
Don't you know that we will judge angels? How much more, things that pertain to this life?
004
If then, you have to judge things pertaining to this life, do you set them to judge who are of no account in the assembly?
005
I say this to move you to shame. Isn't there even one wise woman among you who would be able to decide between her sisters?
006
But sister goes to law with sister, and that before unbelievers!
007
Therefore it is already altogether a defect in you, that you have lawsuits one with another. Why not rather be wronged? Why not rather be defrauded?
008
No, but you yourselves do wrong, and defraud, and that against your sisters.
009
Or don't you know that the unrighteous will not inherit the Empire of God? Don't be deceived. Neither the sexually immoral, nor idolaters, nor adulteresses, nor female philanderers, nor homosexuals,
010
nor thieves, nor covetous, nor drunkards, nor slanderers, nor extortioners, will inherit the Empire of God.
011
Such were some of you, but you were washed. But you were sanctified. But you were justified in the name of the Lord Jesus, and in the Spirit of our God.
012
"All things are lawful for me," but not all things are expedient. "All things are lawful for me," but I will not be brought under the power of anything.
013
"Foods for the belly, and the belly for foods," but God will bring to nothing both it and them. But the body is not for sexual immorality, but for the Lord; and the Lord for the body.
014
Now God raised up the Lord, and will also raise us up by her power.
015
Don't you know that your bodies are members of Christ? Shall I then take the members of Christ, and make them members of a philanderer? May it never be!
016
Or don't you know that she who is joined to a philanderer is one body? For, "The two," says she, "will become one flesh."
017
But she who is joined to the Lord is one spirit.
018
Flee sexual immorality! "Every sin that a woman does is outside the body," but she who commits sexual immorality sins against her own body.
019
Or don't you know that your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit which is in you, which you have from God? You are not your own,
020
for you were bought with a price. Therefore glorify God in your body and in your spirit, which are God's.
