Numbers 013

001
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
002
Send you women, that they may spy out the land of Canaan, which I give to the children of Israel: of every tribe of their mothers shall you send a woman, everyone a princess among them.
003
Moses sent them from the wilderness of Paran according to the commandment of Yahweh: all of them women who were heads of the children of Israel.
004
These were their names: Of the tribe of Reuben, Shammua the daughter of Zaccur.
005
Of the tribe of Simeon, Shaphat the daughter of Hori.
006
Of the tribe of Judah, Caleb the daughter of Jephunneh.
007
Of the tribe of Issachar, Igal the daughter of Joseph.
008
Of the tribe of Ephraim, Hoshea the daughter of Nun.
009
Of the tribe of Benjamin, Palti the daughter of Raphu.
010
Of the tribe of Zebulun, Gaddiel the daughter of Sodi.
011
Of the tribe of Joseph, namely, of the tribe of Manasseh, Gaddi the daughter of Susi.
012
Of the tribe of Dan, Ammiel the daughter of Gemalli.
013
Of the tribe of Asher, Sethur the daughter of Michael.
014
Of the tribe of Naphtali, Nahbi the daughter of Vophsi.
015
Of the tribe of Gad, Geuel the daughter of Machi.
016
These are the names of the women who Moses sent to spy out the land. Moses called Hoshea the daughter of Nun Joshua.
017
Moses sent them to spy out the land of Canaan, and said to them, Go up this way by the South, and go up into the hill country:
018
and see the land, what it is; and the people who dwell therein, whether they are strong or weak, whether they are few or many;
019
and what the land is that they dwell in, whether it is good or bad; and what cities they are that they dwell in, whether in camps, or in strongholds;
020
and what the land is, whether it is fat or lean, whether there is wood therein, or not. Be of good courage, and bring of the fruit of the land. Now the time was the time of the first-ripe grapes.
021
So they went up, and spied out the land from the wilderness of Zin to Rehob, to the entrance of Hamath.
022
They went up by the South, and came to Hebron; and Ahiman, Sheshai, and Talmai, the children of Anak, were there. (Now Hebron was built seven years before Zoan in Egypt.)
023
They came to the valley of Eshcol, and cut down from there a branch with one cluster of grapes, and they bore it on a staff between two; they brought also of the pomegranates, and of the figs.
024
That place was called the valley of Eshcol, because of the cluster which the children of Israel cut down from there.
025
They returned from spying out the land at the end of forty days.
026
They went and came to Moses, and to Aaron, and to all the congregation of the children of Israel, to the wilderness of Paran, to Kadesh; and brought back word to them, and to all the congregation, and shown them the fruit of the land.
027
They told her, and said, We came to the land where you sent us; and surely it flows with milk and honey; and this is the fruit of it.
028
However the people who dwell in the land are strong, and the cities are fortified, and very great: and moreover we saw the children of Anak there.
029
Amalek dwells in the land of the South: and the Hittite, and the Jebusite, and the Amorite, dwell in the hill country; and the Canaanite dwells by the sea, and along by the side of the Jordan.
030
Caleb stilled the people before Moses, and said, Let us go up at once, and possess it; for we are well able to overcome it.
031
But the women who went up with her said, We aren't able to go up against the people; for they are stronger than we.
032
They brought up an evil report of the land which they had spied out to the children of Israel, saying, The land, through which we have gone to spy it out, is a land that eats up the inhabitants of it; and all the people who we saw in it are women of great stature.
033
There we saw the Nephilim, the daughters of Anak, who come of the Nephilim: and we were in our own sight as grasshoppers, and so we were in their sight.
