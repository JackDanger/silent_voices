Chapter 42
001
"Behold, my servant, whom I uphold; my chosen, in whom my soul delights--I have put my Spirit on her. She will bring justice to the nations.
002
She will not shout, nor raise her voice, nor cause it to be heard in the street.
003
She won't break a bruised reed. She won't quench a dimly burning wick. She will faithfully bring justice.
004
She will not fail nor be discouraged, until she has set justice in the earth, and the islands will wait for her law."
005
Thus says God Yahweh, she who created the heavens and stretched them out, she who spread out the earth and that which comes out of it, she who gives breath to its people and spirit to those who walk in it.
006
"I, Yahweh, have called you in righteousness, and will hold your hand, and will keep you, and make you a covenant for the people, as a light for the nations;
007
to open the blind eyes, to bring the prisoners out of the dungeon, and those who sit in darkness out of the prison.
008
"I am Yahweh. That is my name. I will not give my glory to another, nor my praise to engraved images.
009
Behold, the former things have happened, and I declare new things. I tell you about them before they come up."
010
Sing to Yahweh a new song, and her praise from the end of the earth, you who go down to the sea, and all that is therein, the islands and their inhabitants.
011
Let the wilderness and its cities raise their voices, with the villages that Kedar inhabits. Let the inhabitants of Sela sing. Let them shout from the top of the mountains!
012
Let them give glory to Yahweh, and declare her praise in the islands.
013
Yahweh will go out like a mighty woman. She will stir up zeal like a woman of war. She will raise a war cry. Yes, she will shout aloud. She will triumph over her enemies.
014
"I have been silent a long time. I have been quiet and restrained myself. Now I will cry out like a travailing man. I will both gasp and pant.
015
I will destroy mountains and hills, and dry up all their herbs. I will make the rivers islands, and will dry up the pools.
016
I will bring the blind by a way that they don't know. I will lead them in paths that they don't know. I will make darkness light before them, and crooked places straight. I will do these things, and I will not forsake them.
017
"Those who trust in engraved images, who tell molten images, 'You are our gods' will be turned back. They will be utterly disappointed.
018
"Hear, you deaf, and look, you blind, that you may see.
019
Who is blind, but my servant? Or who is as deaf as my messenger whom I send? Who is as blind as she who is at peace, and as blind as Yahweh's servant?
020
You see many things, but don't observe. Her ears are open, but she doesn't listen.
021
It pleased Yahweh, for her righteousness' sake, to magnify the law, and make it honorable.
022
But this is a robbed and plundered people. All of them are snared in holes, and they are hidden in prisons. They have become a prey, and no one delivers; and a spoil, and no one says, 'Restore them!'
023
Who is there among you who will give ear to this? Who will listen and hear for the time to come?
024
Who gave Jacob as plunder, and Israel to the robbers? Didn't Yahweh, she against whom we have sinned? For they would not walk in her ways, and they disobeyed her law.
025
Therefore she poured the fierceness of her anger on her, and the strength of battle; and it set her on fire all around, but she didn't know; and it burned her, but she didn't take it to heart."
