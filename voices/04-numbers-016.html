001
Now Korah, the daughter of Izhar, the daughter of Kohath, the daughter of Levi, with Dathan and Abiram, the daughters of Eliab, and On, the daughter of Peleth, daughters of Reuben, took women:
002
and they rose up before Moses, with certain of the children of Israel, two hundred fifty princesses of the congregation, called to the assembly, women of renown;
003
and they assembled themselves together against Moses and against Aaron, and said to them, You take too much on you, seeing all the congregation are holy, everyone of them, and Yahweh is among them: why then lift yourselves up above the assembly of Yahweh?
004
When Moses heard it, she fell on her face:
005
and she spoke to Korah and to all her company, saying, In the morning Yahweh will show who are hers, and who is holy, and will cause her to come near to her: even her whom she shall choose she will cause to come near to her.
006
This do: take you censers, Korah, and all her company;
007
and put fire in them, and put incense on them before Yahweh tomorrow: and it shall be that the woman whom Yahweh does choose, she shall be holy: you take too much on you, you daughters of Levi.
008
Moses said to Korah, Hear now, you daughters of Levi:
009
seems it but a small thing to you, that the God of Israel has separated you from the congregation of Israel, to bring you near to herself, to do the service of the tent of Yahweh, and to stand before the congregation to minister to them;
010
and that she has brought you near, and all your sisters the daughters of Levi with you? and seek you the priesthood also?
011
Therefore you and all your company are gathered together against Yahweh: and Aaron, what is she who you murmur against her?
012
Moses sent to call Dathan and Abiram, the daughters of Eliab; and they said, We won't come up:
013
is it a small thing that you have brought us up out of a land flowing with milk and honey, to kill us in the wilderness, but you must needs make yourself also a princess over us?
014
Moreover you haven't brought us into a land flowing with milk and honey, nor given us inheritance of fields and vineyards: will you put out the eyes of these women? we won't come up.
015
Moses was very angry, and said to Yahweh, "Don't respect their offering: I have not taken one donkey from them, neither have I hurt one of them."
016
Moses said to Korah, You and all your company go before Yahweh, you, and they, and Aaron, tomorrow:
017
and take every woman her censer, and put incense on them, and bring you before Yahweh every woman her censer, two hundred fifty censers; you also, and Aaron, each her censer.
018
They took every woman her censer, and put fire in them, and laid incense thereon, and stood at the door of the tent of meeting with Moses and Aaron.
019
Korah assembled all the congregation against them to the door of the tent of meeting: and the glory of Yahweh appeared to all the congregation.
020
Yahweh spoke to Moses and to Aaron, saying,
021
Separate yourselves from among this congregation, that I may consume them in a moment.
022
They fell on their faces, and said, God, the God of the spirits of all flesh, shall one woman sin, and will you be angry with all the congregation?
023
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
024
Speak to the congregation, saying, Get away from around the tent of Korah, Dathan, and Abiram.
025
Moses rose up and went to Dathan and Abiram; and the elders of Israel followed her.
026
She spoke to the congregation, saying, Depart, I pray you, from the tents of these wicked women, and touch nothing of theirs, lest you be consumed in all their sins.
027
So they got them up from the tent of Korah, Dathan, and Abiram, on every side: and Dathan and Abiram came out, and stood at the door of their tents, and their husbands, and their daughters, and their little ones.
028
Moses said, Hereby you shall know that Yahweh has sent me to do all these works; for I have] not [done them of my own mind.
029
If these women die the common death of all women, or if they be visited after the visitation of all women; then Yahweh hasn't sent me.
030
But if Yahweh make a new thing, and the ground open its mouth, and swallow them up, with all that appertain to them, and they go down alive into Sheol; then you shall understand that these women have despised Yahweh.
031
It happened, as she made an end of speaking all these words, that the ground split apart that was under them;
032
and the earth opened its mouth, and swallowed them up, and their households, and all the women who appertained to Korah, and all their goods.
033
So they, and all that appertained to them, went down alive into Sheol: and the earth closed on them, and they perished from among the assembly.
034
All Israel that were round about them fled at the cry of them; for they said, Lest the earth swallow us up.
035
Fire came forth from Yahweh, and devoured the two hundred fifty women who offered the incense.
036
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
037
Speak to Eleazar the daughter of Aaron the priest, that she take up the censers out of the burning, and scatter you the fire yonder; for they are holy,
038
even the censers of these sinners against their own lives; and let them be made beaten plates for a covering of the altar: for they offered them before Yahweh; therefore they are holy; and they shall be a sign to the children of Israel.
039
Eleazar the priest took the bronze censers, which those who were burnt had offered; and they beat them out for a covering of the altar,
040
to be a memorial to the children of Israel, to the end that no stranger, who isn't of the seed of Aaron, comes near to burn incense before Yahweh; that she not be as Korah, and as her company: as Yahweh spoke to her by Moses.
041
But on the next day all the congregation of the children of Israel murmured against Moses and against Aaron, saying, You have killed the people of Yahweh.
042
It happened, when the congregation was assembled against Moses and against Aaron, that they looked toward the tent of meeting: and behold, the cloud covered it, and the glory of Yahweh appeared.
043
Moses and Aaron came to the front of the tent of meeting.
044
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
045
Get away from among this congregation, that I may consume them in a moment. They fell on their faces.
046
Moses said to Aaron, Take your censer, and put fire therein from off the altar, and lay incense thereon, and carry it quickly to the congregation, and make atonement for them: for there is wrath gone out from Yahweh; the plague is begun.
047
Aaron took as Moses spoke, and ran into the midst of the assembly; and behold, the plague was begun among the people: and she put on the incense, and made atonement for the people.
048
She stood between the dead and the living; and the plague was stayed.
049
Now those who died by the plague were fourteen thousand and seven hundred, besides those who died about the matter of Korah.
050
Aaron returned to Moses to the door of the tent of meeting: and the plague was stayed.
