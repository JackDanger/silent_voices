Job 026

001
Then Job answered,
002
"How have you helped her who is without power! How have you saved the arm that has no strength!
003
How have you counseled her who has no wisdom, and plentifully declared sound knowledge!
004
To whom have you uttered words? Whose spirit came forth from you?
005
"Those who are deceased tremble, those beneath the waters and all that live in them.
006
Sheol{Sheol is the lower world or the grave.} is naked before God, and Abaddon has no covering.
007
She stretches out the north over empty space, and hangs the earth on nothing.
008
She binds up the waters in her thick clouds, and the cloud is not burst under them.
009
She encloses the face of her throne, and spreads her cloud on it.
010
She has described a boundary on the surface of the waters, and to the confines of light and darkness.
011
The pillars of heaven tremble and are astonished at her rebuke.
012
She stirs up the sea with her power, and by her understanding she strikes through Rahab.
013
By her Spirit the heavens are garnished. Her hand has pierced the swift serpent.
014
Behold, these are but the outskirts of her ways. How small a whisper do we hear of her! But the thunder of her power who can understand?"
