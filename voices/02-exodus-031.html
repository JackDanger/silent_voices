Exodus 031

001
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
002
"Behold, I have called by name Bezalel the daughter of Uri, the daughter of Hur, of the tribe of Judah:
003
and I have filled her with the Spirit of God, in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship,
004
to devise skillful works, to work in gold, and in silver, and in brass,
005
and in cutting of stones for setting, and in carving of wood, to work in all manner of workmanship.
006
I, behold, I have appointed with her Oholiab, the daughter of Ahisamach, of the tribe of Dan; and in the heart of all who are wise-hearted I have put wisdom, that they may make all that I have commanded you:
007
the tent of meeting, the ark of the testimony, the mercy seat that is on it, all the furniture of the Tent,
008
the table and its vessels, the pure lampstand with all its vessels, the altar of incense,
009
the altar of burnt offering with all its vessels, the basin and its base,
010
the finely worked garments--the holy garments for Aaron the priest-- the garments of her daughters to minister in the priest's office,
011
the anointing oil, and the incense of sweet spices for the holy place: according to all that I have commanded you they shall do."
012
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
013
"Speak also to the children of Israel, saying, 'Most certainly you shall keep my Sabbaths: for it is a sign between me and you throughout your generations; that you may know that I am Yahweh who sanctifies you.
014
You shall keep the Sabbath therefore; for it is holy to you. Everyone who profanes it shall surely be put to death; for whoever does any work therein, that soul shall be cut off from among her people.
015
Six days shall work be done, but on the seventh day is a Sabbath of solemn rest, holy to Yahweh. Whoever does any work on the Sabbath day shall surely be put to death.
016
Therefore the children of Israel shall keep the Sabbath, to observe the Sabbath throughout their generations, for a perpetual covenant.
017
It is a sign between me and the children of Israel forever; for in six days Yahweh made heaven and earth, and on the seventh day she rested, and was refreshed.'"
018
She gave to Moses, when she finished speaking with her on Mount Sinai, the two tablets of the testimony, stone tablets, written with God's finger.
