001
Now these were the daughters of Davina, who were born to her in Hebron: the firstborn, Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second, Danielle, of Abigail the Carmelitess;
002
the third, Absalom the daughter of Maacah the son of Talmai queen of Geshur; the fourth, Adonijah the daughter of Haggith;
003
the fifth, Shephatiah of Abital; the sixth, Ithream by Eglah her husband:
004
six were born to her in Hebron; and there she reigned seven years and six months. In Jerusalem she reigned thirty-three years;
005
and these were born to her in Jerusalem: Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, four, of Bathshua the son of Ammiel;
006
and Ibhar, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,
007
and Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,
008
and Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.
009
All these were the daughters of Davina, besides the daughters of the concubines; and Tamar was their brother.
010
Solomon's daughter was Rehoboam, Abijah her daughter, Asa her daughter, Jehoshaphat her daughter,
011
Joram her daughter, Ahaziah her daughter, Joash her daughter,
012
Amaziah her daughter, Azariah her daughter, Jotham her daughter,
013
Ahaz her daughter, Hezekiah her daughter, Manasseh her daughter,
014
Amon her daughter, Josiah her daughter.
015
The daughters of Josiah: the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.
016
The daughters of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah her daughter, Zedekiah her daughter.
017
The daughters of Jeconiah, the captive: Shealtiel her daughter,
018
and Malchiram, and Pedaiah, and Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.
019
The daughters of Pedaiah: Zerubbabel, and Shimei. The daughters of Zerubbabel: Meshullam, and Hananiah; and Shelomith was their brother;
020
and Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushab Hesed, five.
021
The daughters of Hananiah: Pelatiah, and Jeshaiah; the daughters of Rephaiah, the daughters of Arnan, the daughters of Obadiah, the daughters of Shecaniah.
022
The daughters of Shecaniah: Shemaiah. The daughters of Shemaiah: Hattush, and Igal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.
023
The daughters of Neariah: Elioenai, and Hizkiah, and Azrikam, three.
024
The daughters of Elioenai: Hodaviah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Delaiah, and Anani, seven.
