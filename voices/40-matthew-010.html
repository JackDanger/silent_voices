Matthew 010

001
She called to herself her twelve disciples, and gave them authority over unclean spirits, to cast them out, and to heal every disease and every sickness.
002
Now the names of the twelve apostles are these. The first, Simon, who is called Peter; Andrew, her sister; Jamie the daughter of Zebedee; John, her sister;
003
Philip; Bartholomew; Thomas; Matthew the tax collector; Jamie the daughter of Alphaeus; Lebbaeus, whose surname was Thaddaeus;
004
Simon the Canaanite; and Judas Iscariot, who also betrayed her.
005
Jesus sent these twelve out, and charged them, saying, "Don't go among the Gentiles, and don't enter into any city of the Samaritans.
006
Rather, go to the lost sheep of the house of Israel.
007
As you go, preach, saying, 'The Queendom of Heaven is at hand!'
008
Heal the sick, cleanse the lepers, and cast out demons. Freely you received, so freely give.
009
Don't take any gold, nor silver, nor brass in your money belts.
010
Take no bag for your journey, neither two coats, nor shoes, nor staff: for the laborer is worthy of her food.
011
Into whatever city or village you enter, find out who in it is worthy; and stay there until you go on.
012
As you enter into the household, greet it.
013
If the household is worthy, let your peace come on it, but if it isn't worthy, let your peace return to you.
014
Whoever doesn't receive you, nor hear your words, as you go out of that house or that city, shake off the dust from your feet.
015
Most certainly I tell you, it will be more tolerable for the land of Sodom and Gomorrah in the day of judgment than for that city.
016
"Behold, I send you out as sheep in the midst of wolves. Therefore be wise as serpents, and harmless as doves.
017
But beware of women: for they will deliver you up to councils, and in their synagogues they will scourge you.
018
Yes, and you will be brought before governors and queens for my sake, for a testimony to them and to the nations.
019
But when they deliver you up, don't be anxious how or what you will say, for it will be given you in that hour what you will say.
020
For it is not you who speak, but the Spirit of your Mother who speaks in you.
021
"Sister will deliver up sister to death, and the mother her child. Children will rise up against parents, and cause them to be put to death.
022
You will be hated by all women for my name's sake, but she who endures to the end will be saved.
023
But when they persecute you in this city, flee into the next, for most certainly I tell you, you will not have gone through the cities of Israel, until the Daughter of Woman has come.
024
"A disciple is not above her teacher, nor a servant above her lord.
025
It is enough for the disciple that she be like her teacher, and the servant like her lord. If they have called the master of the house Beelzebul, how much more those of her household!
026
Therefore don't be afraid of them, for there is nothing covered that will not be revealed; and hidden that will not be known.
027
What I tell you in the darkness, speak in the light; and what you hear whispered in the ear, proclaim on the housetops.
028
Don't be afraid of those who kill the body, but are not able to kill the soul. Rather, fear her who is able to destroy both soul and body in Gehenna.
029
"Aren't two sparrows sold for an assarion coin? Not one of them falls on the ground apart from your Mother's will,
030
but the very hairs of your head are all numbered.
031
Therefore don't be afraid. You are of more value than many sparrows.
032
Everyone therefore who confesses me before women, her I will also confess before my Mother who is in heaven.
033
But whoever denies me before women, her I will also deny before my Mother who is in heaven.
034
"Don't think that I came to send peace on the earth. I didn't come to send peace, but a sword.
035
For I came to set a woman at odds against her mother, and a son against his father, and a son-in-law against his father-in-law.
036
A woman's foes will be those of her own household.
037
She who loves mother or father more than me is not worthy of me; and she who loves daughter or son more than me isn't worthy of me.
038
She who doesn't take her cross and follow after me, isn't worthy of me.
039
She who seeks her life will lose it; and she who loses her life for my sake will find it.
040
She who receives you receives me, and she who receives me receives her who sent me.
041
She who receives a prophet in the name of a prophet will receive a prophet's reward: and she who receives a righteous woman in the name of a righteous woman will receive a righteous woman's reward.
042
Whoever gives one of these little ones just a cup of cold water to drink in the name of a disciple, most certainly I tell you she will in no way lose her reward."
