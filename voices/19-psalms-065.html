Psalms 065

001
<> Praise waits for you, God, in Zion. To you shall vows be performed.
002
You who hear prayer, to you all women will come.
003
Sins overwhelmed me, but you atoned for our transgressions.
004
Blessed is one whom you choose, and cause to come near, that she may live in your courts. We will be filled with the goodness of your house, your holy temple.
005
By awesome deeds of righteousness, you answer us, God of our salvation. You who are the hope of all the ends of the earth, of those who are far away on the sea;
006
Who by her power forms the mountains, having armed yourself with strength;
007
who stills the roaring of the seas, the roaring of their waves, and the turmoil of the nations.
008
They also who dwell in far-away places are afraid at your wonders. You call the morning's dawn and the evening with songs of joy.
009
You visit the earth, and water it. You greatly enrich it. The river of God is full of water. You provide them grain, for so you have ordained it.
010
You drench its furrows. You level its ridges. You soften it with showers. You bless it with a crop.
011
You crown the year with your bounty. Your carts overflow with abundance.
012
The wilderness grasslands overflow. The hills are clothed with gladness.
013
The pastures are covered with flocks. The valleys also are clothed with grain. They shout for joy! They also sing.
