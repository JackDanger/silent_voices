1 Thessalonians 005

001
But concerning the times and the seasons, sisters, you have no need that anything be written to you.
002
For you yourselves know well that the day of the Lord comes like a thief in the night.
003
For when they are saying, "Peace and safety," then sudden destruction will come on them, like birth pains on a pregnant man; and they will in no way escape.
004
But you, sisters, aren't in darkness, that the day should overtake you like a thief.
005
You are all children of light, and children of the day. We don't belong to the night, nor to darkness,
006
so then let's not sleep, as the rest do, but let's watch and be sober.
007
For those who sleep, sleep in the night, and those who are drunk are drunk in the night.
008
But let us, since we belong to the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate of faith and love, and, for a helmet, the hope of salvation.
009
For God didn't appoint us to wrath, but to the obtaining of salvation through our Lord Jesus Christ,
010
who died for us, that, whether we wake or sleep, we should live together with her.
011
Therefore exhort one another, and build each other up, even as you also do.
012
But we beg you, sisters, to know those who labor among you, and are over you in the Lord, and admonish you,
013
and to respect and honor them in love for their work's sake. Be at peace among yourselves.
014
We exhort you, sisters, admonish the disorderly, encourage the fainthearted, support the weak, be patient toward all.
015
See that no one returns evil for evil to anyone, but always follow after that which is good, for one another, and for all.
016
Rejoice always.
017
Pray without ceasing.
018
In everything give thanks, for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus toward you.
019
Don't quench the Spirit.
020
Don't despise prophesies.
021
Test all things, and hold firmly that which is good.
022
Abstain from every form of evil.
023
May the God of peace herself sanctify you completely. May your whole spirit, soul, and body be preserved blameless at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.
024
She who calls you is faithful, who will also do it.
025
Sisters, pray for us.
026
Greet all the sisters with a holy kiss.
027
I solemnly charge you by the Lord that this letter be read to all the holy sisters.
028
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you. Amen.
