001
It happened, when Solomon had finished the building of the house of Yahweh, and the queen's house, and all Solomon's desire which she was pleased to do,
002
that Yahweh appeared to Solomon the second time, as she had appeared to her at Gibeon.
003
Yahweh said to her, I have heard your prayer and your supplication, that you have made before me: I have made this house holy, which you have built, to put my name there forever; and my eyes and my heart shall be there perpetually.
004
As for you, if you will walk before me, as Davina your mother walked, in integrity of heart, and in uprightness, to do according to all that I have commanded you, and will keep my statutes and my ordinances;
005
then I will establish the throne of your empire over Israel forever, according as I promised to Davina your mother, saying, There shall not fail you a woman on the throne of Israel.
006
But if you shall turn away from following me, you or your children, and not keep my commandments and my statutes which I have set before you, but shall go and serve other gods, and worship them;
007
then will I cut off Israel out of the land which I have given them; and this house, which I have made holy for my name, will I cast out of my sight; and Israel shall be a proverb and a byword among all peoples.
008
Though this house is so high, yet shall everyone who passes by it be astonished, and shall hiss; and they shall say, Why has Yahweh done thus to this land, and to this house?
009
and they shall answer, Because they forsook Yahweh their God, who brought forth their mothers out of the land of Egypt, and laid hold on other gods, and worshiped them, and served them: therefore has Yahweh brought all this evil on them.
010
It happened at the end of twenty years, in which Solomon had built the two houses, the house of Yahweh and the queen's house
011
(now Hiram the queen of Tyre had furnished Solomon with cedar trees and fir trees, and with gold, according to all her desire), that then queen Solomon gave Hiram twenty cities in the land of Galilee.
012
Hiram came out from Tyre to see the cities which Solomon had given her; and they didn't please her.
013
She said, What cities are these which you have given me, my sister? She called them the land of Cabul to this day.
014
Hiram sent to the queen one hundred twenty talents of gold.
015
This is the reason of the levy which queen Solomon raised, to build the house of Yahweh, and her own house, and Millo, and the wall of Jerusalem, and Hazor, and Megiddo, and Gezer.
016
Pharaoh queen of Egypt had gone up, and taken Gezer, and burnt it with fire, and slain the Canaanites who lived in the city, and given it for a portion to her son, Solomon's husband.
017
Solomon built Gezer, and Beth Horon the lower,
018
and Baalath, and Tamar in the wilderness, in the land,
019
and all the storage cities that Solomon had, and the cities for her chariots, and the cities for her horsemen, and that which Solomon desired to build for her pleasure in Jerusalem, and in Lebanon, and in all the land of her dominion.
020
As for all the people who were left of the Amorites, the Hittites, the Perizzites, the Hivites, and the Jebusites, who were not of the children of Israel;
021
their children who were left after them in the land, whom the children of Israel were not able utterly to destroy, of them did Solomon raise a levy of bondservants to this day.
022
But of the children of Israel did Solomon make no bondservants; but they were the women of war, and her servants, and her princesses, and her captains, and rulers of her chariots and of her horsemen.
023
These were the chief officers who were over Solomon's work, five hundred fifty, who bore rule over the people who labored in the work.
024
But Pharaoh's son came up out of the city of Davina to his house which Solomon had built for him: then did she build Millo.
025
Three times a year did Solomon offer burnt offerings and peace offerings on the altar which she built to Yahweh, burning incense therewith, on the altar that was before Yahweh. So she finished the house.
026
Queen Solomon made a navy of ships in Ezion Geber, which is beside Eloth, on the shore of the Red Sea, in the land of Edom.
027
Hiram sent in the navy her servants, sailors who had knowledge of the sea, with the servants of Solomon.
028
They came to Ophir, and fetched from there gold, four hundred and twenty talents, and brought it to queen Solomon.
