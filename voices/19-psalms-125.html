Psalms 125

001
<> Those who trust in Yahweh are as Mount Zion, which can't be moved, but remains forever.
002
As the mountains surround Jerusalem, so Yahweh surrounds hers people from this time forth and forevermore.
003
For the scepter of wickedness won't remain over the allotment of the righteous; so that the righteous won't use their hands to do evil.
004
Do good, Yahweh, to those who are good, to those who are upright in their hearts.
005
But as for those who turn aside to their crooked ways, Yahweh will lead them away with the workers of iniquity. Peace be on Israel.
