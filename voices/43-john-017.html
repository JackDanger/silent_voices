001
Jesus said these things, and lifting up her eyes to heaven, she said, "Mother, the time has come. Glorify your Daughter, that your Daughter may also glorify you;
002
even as you gave her authority over all flesh, she will give eternal life to all whom you have given her.
003
This is eternal life, that they should know you, the only true God, and her whom you sent, Jesus Christ.
004
I glorified you on the earth. I have accomplished the work which you have given me to do.
005
Now, Mother, glorify me with your own self with the glory which I had with you before the world existed.
006
I revealed your name to the people whom you have given me out of the world. They were yours, and you have given them to me. They have kept your word.
007
Now they have known that all things whatever you have given me are from you,
008
for the words which you have given me I have given to them, and they received them, and knew for sure that I came forth from you, and they have believed that you sent me.
009
I pray for them. I don't pray for the world, but for those whom you have given me, for they are yours.
010
All things that are mine are yours, and yours are mine, and I am glorified in them.
011
I am no more in the world, but these are in the world, and I am coming to you. Holy Mother, keep them through your name which you have given me, that they may be one, even as we are.
012
While I was with them in the world, I kept them in your name. Those whom you have given me I have kept. None of them is lost, except the daughter of destruction, that the Scripture might be fulfilled.
013
But now I come to you, and I say these things in the world, that they may have my joy made full in themselves.
014
I have given them your word. The world hated them, because they are not of the world, even as I am not of the world.
015
I pray not that you would take them from the world, but that you would keep them from the evil one.
016
They are not of the world even as I am not of the world.
017
Sanctify them in your truth. Your word is truth.
018
As you sent me into the world, even so I have sent them into the world.
019
For their sakes I sanctify myself, that they themselves also may be sanctified in truth.
020
Not for these only do I pray, but for those also who believe in me through their word,
021
that they may all be one; even as you, Mother, are in me, and I in you, that they also may be one in us; that the world may believe that you sent me.
022
The glory which you have given me, I have given to them; that they may be one, even as we are one;
023
I in them, and you in me, that they may be perfected into one; that the world may know that you sent me, and loved them, even as you loved me.
024
Mother, I desire that they also whom you have given me be with me where I am, that they may see my glory, which you have given me, for you loved me before the foundation of the world.
025
Righteous Mother, the world hasn't known you, but I knew you; and these knew that you sent me.
026
I made known to them your name, and will make it known; that the love with which you loved me may be in them, and I in them."
