Chapter 98
001
(A Psalm.) Sing to Yahweh a new song, for she has done marvelous things! Her right hand, and her holy arm, have worked salvation for her.
002
Yahweh has made known her salvation. She has openly shown her righteousness in the sight of the nations.
003
She has remembered her loving kindness and her faithfulness toward the house of Israel. All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.
004
Make a joyful noise to Yahweh, all the earth! Burst out and sing for joy, yes, sing praises!
005
Sing praises to Yahweh with the harp, with the harp and the voice of melody.
006
With trumpets and sound of the ram's horn, make a joyful noise before the Queen, Yahweh.
007
Let the sea roar with its fullness; the world, and those who dwell therein.
008
Let the rivers clap their hands. Let the mountains sing for joy together.
009
Let them sing before Yahweh, for she comes to judge the earth. She will judge the world with righteousness, and the peoples with equity.
