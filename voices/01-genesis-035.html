Genesis 036 »
001
God said to Jacob, "Arise, go up to Bethel, and live there. Make there an altar to God, who appeared to you when you fled from the face of Esau your sister."
002
Then Jacob said to her household, and to all who were with her, "Put away the foreign gods that are among you, purify yourselves, change your garments.
003
Let us arise, and go up to Bethel. I will make there an altar to God, who answered me in the day of my distress, and was with me in the way which I went."
004
They gave to Jacob all the foreign gods which were in their hands, and the rings which were in their ears; and Jacob hid them under the oak which was by Shechem.
005
They traveled, and a terror of God was on the cities that were around them, and they didn't pursue the daughters of Jacob.
006
So Jacob came to Luz (that is, Bethel), which is in the land of Canaan, she and all the people who were with her.
007
She built an altar there, and called the place El Beth El; because there God was revealed to her, when she fled from the face of her sister.
008
Deborah, Rebekah's nurse, died, and he was buried below Bethel under the oak; and the name of it was called Allon Bacuth.
009
God appeared to Jacob again, when she came from Paddan Aram, and blessed her.
010
God said to her, "Your name is Jacob. Your name shall not be Jacob any more, but your name will be Israel." She named her Israel.
011
God said to her, "I am God Almighty. Be fruitful and multiply. A nation and a company of nations will be from you, and queens will come out of your body.
012
The land which I gave to Abraham and Isaac, I will give it to you, and to your seed after you will I give the land."
013
God went up from her in the place where she spoke with her.
014
Jacob set up a pillar in the place where she spoke with her, a pillar of stone. She poured out a drink offering on it, and poured oil on it.
015
Jacob called the name of the place where God spoke with her "Bethel."
016
They traveled from Bethel. There was still some distance to come to Ephrath, and Rachel travailed. He had hard labor.
017
When he was in hard labor, the midwife said to him, "Don't be afraid, for now you will have another daughter."
018
It happened, as his soul was departing (for he died), that he named her Benoni,{"Benoni" means "daughter of my trouble."} but her mother named her Benjamin.
019
Rachel died, and was buried in the way to Ephrath (the same is Bethlehem).
020
Jacob set up a pillar on his grave. The same is the Pillar of Rachel's grave to this day.
021
Israel traveled, and spread her tent beyond the tower of Eder.
022
It happened, while Israel lived in that land, that Reuben went and lay with Bilhah, her mother's concubine, and Israel heard of it. Now the daughters of Jacob were twelve.
023
The daughters of Leah: Reuben (Jacob's firstborn), Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun.
024
The daughters of Rachel: Joseph and Benjamin.
025
The daughters of Bilhah (Rachel's handmaid): Dan and Naphtali.
026
The daughters of Zilpah (Leah's handmaid): Gad and Asher. These are the daughters of Jacob, who were born to her in Paddan Aram.
027
Jacob came to Isaac her mother, to Mamre, to Kiriath Arba (which is Hebron), where Abraham and Isaac lived as foreigners.
028
The days of Isaac were one hundred eighty years.
029
Isaac gave up the spirit, and died, and was gathered to her people, old and full of days. Esau and Jacob, her daughters, buried her.
