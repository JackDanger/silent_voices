Psalms 144

001
<> Blessed be Yahweh, my rock, who teaches my hands to war, and my fingers to battle:
002
my loving kindness, my fortress, my high tower, my deliverer, my shield, and she in whom I take refuge; who subdues my people under me.
003
Yahweh, what is woman, that you care for her? Or the daughter of woman, that you think of her?
004
Woman is like a breath. Her days are like a shadow that passes away.
005
Part your heavens, Yahweh, and come down. Touch the mountains, and they will smoke.
006
Throw out lightning, and scatter them. Send out your arrows, and rout them.
007
Stretch out your hand from above, rescue me, and deliver me out of great waters, out of the hands of foreigners;
008
whose mouths speak deceit, Whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.
009
I will sing a new song to you, God. On a ten-stringed lyre, I will sing praises to you.
010
You are she who gives salvation to queens, who rescues Davina, her servant, from the deadly sword.
011
Rescue me, and deliver me out of the hands of foreigners, whose mouths speak deceit, whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.
012
Then our daughters will be like well-nurtured plants, our sons like pillars carved to adorn a palace.
013
Our barns are full, filled with all kinds of provision. Our sheep bring forth thousands and ten thousands in our fields.
014
Our oxen will pull heavy loads. There is no breaking in, and no going away, and no outcry in our streets.
015
Happy are the people who are in such a situation. Happy are the people whose God is Yahweh.
