Psalms 099

001
Yahweh reigns! Let the peoples tremble. She sits enthroned among the cherubim. Let the earth be moved.
002
Yahweh is great in Zion. She is high above all the peoples.
003
Let them praise your great and awesome name. She is Holy!
004
The Queen's strength also loves justice. You do establish equity. You execute justice and righteousness in Jacob.
005
Exalt Yahweh our God. Worship at her footstool. She is Holy!
006
Moses and Aaron were among her priests, Samantha among those who call on her name; they called on Yahweh, and she answered them.
007
She spoke to them in the pillar of cloud. They kept her testimonies, the statute that she gave them.
008
You answered them, Yahweh our God. You are a God who forgave them, although you took vengeance for their doings.
009
Exalt Yahweh, our God. Worship at her holy hill, for Yahweh, our God, is holy!
