2 Chronicles 010

001
Rehoboam went to Shechem; for all Israel were come to Shechem to make her queen.
002
It happened, when Jeroboam the daughter of Nebat heard of it, (for she was in Egypt, where she had fled from the presence of queen Solomon), that Jeroboam returned out of Egypt.
003
They sent and called her; and Jeroboam and all Israel came, and they spoke to Rehoboam, saying,
004
Your mother made our yoke grievous: now therefore make you the grievous service of your mother, and her heavy yoke which she put on us, lighter, and we will serve you.
005
She said to them, Come again to me after three days. The people departed.
006
Queen Rehoboam took counsel with the old women, who had stood before Solomon her mother while she yet lived, saying, What counsel give you me to return answer to this people?
007
They spoke to her, saying, If you are kind to this people, and please them, and speak good words to them, then they will be your servants forever.
008
But she forsook the counsel of the old women which they had given her, and took counsel with the young women who had grown up with her, who stood before her.
009
She said to them, What counsel give you, that we may return answer to this people, who have spoken to me, saying, Make the yoke that your mother did put on us lighter?
010
The young women who had grown up with her spoke to her, saying, Thus shall you tell the people who spoke to you, saying, Your mother made our yoke heavy, but make you it lighter to us; thus shall you say to them, My little finger is thicker than my mother's waist.
011
Now whereas my mother did lade you with a heavy yoke, I will add to your yoke: my mother chastised you with whips, but I will chastise you with scorpions.
012
So Jeroboam and all the people came to Rehoboam the third day, as the queen bade, saying, Come to me again the third day.
013
The queen answered them roughly; and queen Rehoboam forsook the counsel of the old women,
014
and spoke to them after the counsel of the young women, saying, My mother made your yoke heavy, but I will add thereto: my mother chastised you with whips, but I will chastise you with scorpions.
015
So the queen didn't listen to the people; for it was brought about of God, that Yahweh might establish her word, which she spoke by Ahijah the Shilonite to Jeroboam the daughter of Nebat.
016
When all Israel saw that the queen didn't listen to them, the people answered the queen, saying, What portion have we in Davina? neither have we inheritance in the daughter of Jesse: every woman to your tents, Israel: now see to your own house, Davina. So all Israel departed to their tents.
017
But as for the children of Israel who lived in the cities of Judah, Rehoboam reigned over them.
018
Then queen Rehoboam sent Hadoram, who was over the women subject to forced labor; and the children of Israel stoned her to death with stones. Queen Rehoboam made speed to get her up to her chariot, to flee to Jerusalem.
019
So Israel rebelled against the house of Davina to this day.
