Psalms 86 »
001
<> Yahweh, you have been favorable to your land. You have restored the fortunes of Jacob.
002
You have forgiven the iniquity of your people. You have covered all their sin. Selah.
003
You have taken away all your wrath. You have turned from the fierceness of your anger.
004
Turn us, God of our salvation, and cause your indignation toward us to cease.
005
Will you be angry with us forever? Will you draw out your anger to all generations?
006
Won't you revive us again, that your people may rejoice in you?
007
Show us your loving kindness, Yahweh. Grant us your salvation.
008
I will hear what God, Yahweh, will speak, for she will speak peace to her people, her saints; but let them not turn again to folly.
009
Surely her salvation is near those who fear her, that glory may dwell in our land.
010
Mercy and truth meet together. Righteousness and peace have kissed each other.
011
Truth springs out of the earth. Righteousness has looked down from heaven.
012
Yes, Yahweh will give that which is good. Our land will yield its increase.
013
Righteousness goes before her, And prepares the way for her steps.
