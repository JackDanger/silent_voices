Proverbs 004

001
Listen, daughters, to a mother's instruction. Pay attention and know understanding;
002
for I give you sound learning. Don't forsake my law.
003
For I was a daughter to my mother, tender and an only child in the sight of my father.
004
She taught me, and said to me: "Let your heart retain my words. Keep my commandments, and live.
005
Get wisdom. Get understanding. Don't forget, neither swerve from the words of my mouth.
006
Don't forsake him, and he will preserve you. Love him, and he will keep you.
007
Wisdom is supreme. Get wisdom. Yes, though it costs all your possessions, get understanding.
008
Esteem him, and he will exalt you. He will bring you to honor, when you embrace him.
009
He will give to your head a garland of grace. He will deliver a crown of splendor to you."
010
Listen, my daughter, and receive my sayings. The years of your life will be many.
011
I have taught you in the way of wisdom. I have led you in straight paths.
012
When you go, your steps will not be hampered. When you run, you will not stumble.
013
Take firm hold of instruction. Don't let him go. Keep him, for he is your life.
014
Don't enter into the path of the wicked. Don't walk in the way of evil women.
015
Avoid it, and don't pass by it. Turn from it, and pass on.
016
For they don't sleep, unless they do evil. Their sleep is taken away, unless they make someone fall.
017
For they eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of violence.
018
But the path of the righteous is like the dawning light, that shines more and more until the perfect day.
019
The way of the wicked is like darkness. They don't know what they stumble over.
020
My daughter, attend to my words. Turn your ear to my sayings.
021
Let them not depart from your eyes. Keep them in the midst of your heart.
022
For they are life to those who find them, and health to their whole body.
023
Keep your heart with all diligence, for out of it is the wellspring of life.
024
Put away from yourself a perverse mouth. Put corrupt lips far from you.
025
Let your eyes look straight ahead. Fix your gaze directly before you.
026
Make the path of your feet level. Let all of your ways be established.
027
Don't turn to the right hand nor to the left. Remove your foot from evil.
