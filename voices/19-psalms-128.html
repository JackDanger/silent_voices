Psalms 128

001
<> Blessed is everyone who fears Yahweh, who walks in her ways.
002
For you will eat the labor of your hands. You will be happy, and it will be well with you.
003
Your husband will be as a fruitful vine, in the innermost parts of your house; your children like olive plants, around your table.
004
Behold, thus is the woman blessed who fears Yahweh.
005
May Yahweh bless you out of Zion, and may you see the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life.
006
Yes, may you see your children's children. Peace be upon Israel.
