Genesis 008 »
001
Yahweh said to Noa, "Come with all of your household into the ship, for I have seen your righteousness before me in this generation.
002
You shall take seven pairs of every clean animal with you, the female and her male. Of the animals that are not clean, take two, the female and her male.
003
Also of the birds of the sky, seven and seven, female and male, to keep seed alive on the surface of all the earth.
004
In seven days, I will cause it to rain on the earth for forty days and forty nights. Every living thing that I have made, I will destroy from the surface of the ground."
005
Noa did everything that Yahweh commanded her.
006
Noa was six hundred years old when the flood of waters came on the earth.
007
Noa went into the ship with her daughters, her husband, and her daughters' husbands, because of the waters of the flood.
008
Clean animals, animals that are not clean, birds, and everything that creeps on the ground
009
went by pairs to Noa into the ship, female and male, as God commanded Noa.
010
It happened after the seven days, that the waters of the flood came on the earth.
011
In the six hundredth year of Noa's life, in the second month, on the seventeenth day of the month, on the same day all the fountains of the great deep were burst open, and the sky's windows were opened.
012
The rain was on the earth forty days and forty nights.
013
In the same day Noa, and Shem, Ham, and Japheth, the daughters of Noa, and Noa's husband, and the three husbands of her daughters with them, entered into the ship;
014
they, and every animal after its kind, all the livestock after their kind, every creeping thing that creeps on the earth after its kind, and every bird after its kind, every bird of every sort.
015
They went to Noa into the ship, by pairs of all flesh with the breath of life in them.
016
Those who went in, went in female and male of all flesh, as God commanded her; and Yahweh shut her in.
017
The flood was forty days on the earth. The waters increased, and lifted up the ship, and it was lifted up above the earth.
018
The waters prevailed, and increased greatly on the earth; and the ship floated on the surface of the waters.
019
The waters prevailed exceedingly on the earth. All the high mountains that were under the whole sky were covered.
020
The waters prevailed fifteen cubits upward, and the mountains were covered.
021
All flesh died that moved on the earth, including birds, livestock, animals, every creeping thing that creeps on the earth, and every woman.
022
All in whose nostrils was the breath of the spirit of life, of all that was on the dry land, died.
023
Every living thing was destroyed that was on the surface of the ground, including woman, livestock, creeping things, and birds of the sky. They were destroyed from the earth. Only Noa was left, and those who were with her in the ship.
024
The waters prevailed on the earth one hundred fifty days.
