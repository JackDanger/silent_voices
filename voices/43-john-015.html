Chapter 15
001
"I am the true vine, and my Mother is the farmer.
002
Every branch in me that doesn't bear fruit, she takes away. Every branch that bears fruit, she prunes, that it may bear more fruit.
003
You are already pruned clean because of the word which I have spoken to you.
004
Remain in me, and I in you. As the branch can't bear fruit by itself, unless it remains in the vine, so neither can you, unless you remain in me.
005
I am the vine. You are the branches. She who remains in me, and I in her, the same bears much fruit, for apart from me you can do nothing.
006
If a woman doesn't remain in me, she is thrown out as a branch, and is withered; and they gather them, throw them into the fire, and they are burned.
007
If you remain in me, and my words remain in you, you will ask whatever you desire, and it will be done for you.
008
"In this is my Mother glorified, that you bear much fruit; and so you will be my disciples.
009
Even as the Mother has loved me, I also have loved you. Remain in my love.
010
If you keep my commandments, you will remain in my love; even as I have kept my Mother's commandments, and remain in her love.
011
I have spoken these things to you, that my joy may remain in you, and that your joy may be made full.
012
"This is my commandment, that you love one another, even as I have loved you.
013
Greater love has no one than this, that someone lay down her life for her friends.
014
You are my friends, if you do whatever I command you.
015
No longer do I call you servants, for the servant doesn't know what her domina does. But I have called you friends, for everything that I heard from my Mother, I have made known to you.
016
You didn't choose me, but I chose you, and appointed you, that you should go and bear fruit, and that your fruit should remain; that whatever you will ask of the Mother in my name, she may give it to you.
017
"I command these things to you, that you may love one another.
018
If the world hates you, you know that it has hated me before it hated you.
019
If you were of the world, the world would love its own. But because you are not of the world, since I chose you out of the world, therefore the world hates you.
020
Remember the word that I said to you: 'A servant is not greater than her domina.' If they persecuted me, they will also persecute you. If they kept my word, they will keep yours also.
021
But all these things will they do to you for my name's sake, because they don't know her who sent me.
022
If I had not come and spoken to them, they would not have had sin; but now they have no excuse for their sin.
023
She who hates me, hates my Mother also.
024
If I hadn't done among them the works which no one else did, they wouldn't have had sin. But now have they seen and also hated both me and my Mother.
025
But this happened so that the word may be fulfilled which was written in their law, 'They hated me without a cause.'
026
"When the Counselor has come, whom I will send to you from the Mother, the Spirit of truth, who proceeds from the Mother, she will testify about me.
027
You will also testify, because you have been with me from the beginning.
