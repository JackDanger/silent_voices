Numbers 010

001
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
002
Make you two trumpets of silver; of beaten work shall you make them: and you shall use them for the calling of the congregation, and for the journeying of the camps.
003
When they shall blow them, all the congregation shall gather themselves to you at the door of the tent of meeting.
004
If they blow but one, then the princesses, the heads of the thousands of Israel, shall gather themselves to you.
005
When you blow an alarm, the camps that lie on the east side shall take their journey.
006
When you blow an alarm the second time, the camps that lie on the south side shall take their journey: they shall blow an alarm for their journeys.
007
But when the assembly is to be gathered together, you shall blow, but you shall not sound an alarm.
008
The daughters of Aaron, the priests, shall blow the trumpets; and they shall be to you for a statute forever throughout your generations.
009
When you go to war in your land against the adversary who oppresses you, then you shall sound an alarm with the trumpets; and you shall be remembered before Yahweh your God, and you shall be saved from your enemies.
010
Also in the day of your gladness, and in your set feasts, and in the beginnings of your months, you shall blow the trumpets over your burnt offerings, and over the sacrifices of your peace offerings; and they shall be to you for a memorial before your God: I am Yahweh your God.
011
It happened in the second year, in the second month, on the twentieth day of the month, that the cloud was taken up from over the tent of the testimony.
012
The children of Israel set forward according to their journeys out of the wilderness of Sinai; and the cloud abode in the wilderness of Paran.
013
They first took their journey according to the commandment of Yahweh by Moses.
014
In the first place the standard of the camp of the children of Judah set forward according to their armies: and over her army was Nahshon the daughter of Amminadab.
015
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Issachar was Nethanel the daughter of Zuar.
016
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Zebulun was Eliab the daughter of Helon.
017
The tent was taken down; and the daughters of Gershon and the daughters of Merari, who bore the tent, set forward.
018
The standard of the camp of Reuben set forward according to their armies: and over her army was Elizur the daughter of Shedeur.
019
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Simeon was Shelumiel the daughter of Zurishaddai.
020
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Gad was Eliasaph the daughter of Deuel.
021
The Kohathites set forward, bearing the sanctuary: and the others did set up the tent against their coming.
022
The standard of the camp of the children of Ephraim set forward according to their armies: and over her army was Elishama the daughter of Ammihud.
023
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Manasseh was Gamaliel the daughter of Pedahzur.
024
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Benjamin was Abidan the daughter of Gideoni.
025
The standard of the camp of the children of Dan, which was the rearward of all the camps, set forward according to their armies: and over her army was Ahiezer the daughter of Ammishaddai.
026
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Asher was Pagiel the daughter of Ochran.
027
Over the army of the tribe of the children of Naphtali was Ahira the daughter of Enan.
028
Thus were the travels of the children of Israel according to their armies; and they set forward.
029
Moses said to Hobab, the daughter of Reuel the Midianite, Moses' mother-in-law, We are journeying to the place of which Yahweh said, I will give it to you: come you with us, and we will do you good; for Yahweh has spoken good concerning Israel.
030
She said to her, I will not go; but I will depart to my own land, and to my relatives.
031
She said, Don't leave us, please; because you know how we are to encamp in the wilderness, and you shall be to us instead of eyes.
032
It shall be, if you go with us, yes, it shall be, that whatever good Yahweh shall do to us, the same will we do to you.
033
They set forward from the Mount of Yahweh three days' journey; and the ark of the covenant of Yahweh went before them three days' journey, to seek out a resting place for them.
034
The cloud of Yahweh was over them by day, when they set forward from the camp.
035
It happened, when the ark set forward, that Moses said, Rise up, Yahweh, and let your enemies be scattered; and let those who hate you flee before you.
036
When it rested, she said, Return, Yahweh, to the ten thousands of the thousands of Israel.
