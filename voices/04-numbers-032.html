Numbers 032

001
Now the children of Reuben and the children of Gad had a very great multitude of livestock: and when they saw the land of Jazer, and the land of Gilead, that behold, the place was a place for livestock;
002
the children of Gad and the children of Reuben came and spoke to Moses, and to Eleazar the priest, and to the princesses of the congregation, saying,
003
Ataroth, and Dibon, and Jazer, and Nimrah, and Heshbon, and Elealeh, and Sebam, and Nebo, and Beon,
004
the land which Yahweh struck before the congregation of Israel, is a land for livestock; and your servants have livestock.
005
They said, If we have found favor in your sight, let this land be given to your servants for a possession; don't bring us over the Jordan.
006
Moses said to the children of Gad, and to the children of Reuben, Shall your sisters go to the war, and shall you sit here?
007
Why discourage you the heart of the children of Israel from going over into the land which Yahweh has given them?
008
Thus did your mothers, when I sent them from Kadesh Barnea to see the land.
009
For when they went up to the valley of Eshcol, and saw the land, they discouraged the heart of the children of Israel, that they should not go into the land which Yahweh had given them.
010
Yahweh's anger was kindled in that day, and she swore, saying,
011
Surely none of the women who came up out of Egypt, from twenty years old and upward, shall see the land which I swore to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob; because they have not wholly followed me:
012
save Caleb the daughter of Jephunneh the Kenizzite, and Joshua the daughter of Nun; because they have wholly followed Yahweh.
013
Yahweh's anger was kindled against Israel, and she made them wander back and forth in the wilderness forty years, until all the generation, who had done evil in the sight of Yahweh, was consumed.
014
Behold, you are risen up in your mothers' place, an increase of sinful women, to augment yet the fierce anger of Yahweh toward Israel.
015
For if you turn away from after her, she will yet again leave them in the wilderness; and you will destroy all this people.
016
They came near to her, and said, We will build sheepfolds here for our livestock, and cities for our little ones:
017
but we ourselves will be ready armed to go before the children of Israel, until we have brought them to their place: and our little ones shall dwell in the fortified cities because of the inhabitants of the land.
018
We will not return to our houses, until the children of Israel have inherited every woman her inheritance.
019
For we will not inherit with them on the other side of the Jordan, and forward; because our inheritance is fallen to us on this side of the Jordan eastward.
020
Moses said to them, If you will do this thing, if you will arm yourselves to go before Yahweh to the war,
021
and every armed woman of you will pass over the Jordan before Yahweh, until she has driven out her enemies from before her,
022
and the land is subdued before Yahweh; then afterward you shall return, and be guiltless towards Yahweh, and towards Israel; and this land shall be to you for a possession before Yahweh.
023
But if you will not do so, behold, you have sinned against Yahweh; and be sure your sin will find you out.
024
Build you cities for your little ones, and folds for your sheep; and do that which has proceeded out of your mouth.
025
The children of Gad and the children of Reuben spoke to Moses, saying, Your servants will do as my lord commands.
026
Our little ones, our husbands, our flocks, and all our livestock, shall be there in the cities of Gilead;
027
but your servants will pass over, every woman who is armed for war, before Yahweh to battle, as my lord says.
028
So Moses gave charge concerning them to Eleazar the priest, and to Joshua the daughter of Nun, and to the heads of the mothers' houses of the tribes of the children of Israel.
029
Moses said to them, If the children of Gad and the children of Reuben will pass with you over the Jordan, every woman who is armed to battle, before Yahweh, and the land shall be subdued before you; then you shall give them the land of Gilead for a possession:
030
but if they will not pass over with you armed, they shall have possessions among you in the land of Canaan.
031
The children of Gad and the children of Reuben answered, saying, As Yahweh has said to your servants, so will we do.
032
We will pass over armed before Yahweh into the land of Canaan, and the possession of our inheritance shall remain with us beyond the Jordan.
033
Moses gave to them, even to the children of Gad, and to the children of Reuben, and to the half-tribe of Manasseh the daughter of Joseph, the queendom of Sihon queen of the Amorites, and the queendom of Og queen of Bashan, the land, according to the cities of it with their borders, even the cities of the land round about.
034
The children of Gad built Dibon, and Ataroth, and Aroer,
035
and Atrothshophan, and Jazer, and Jogbehah,
036
and Beth Nimrah, and Beth Haran: fortified cities, and folds for sheep.
037
The children of Reuben built Heshbon, and Elealeh, and Kiriathaim,
038
and Nebo, and Baal Meon, (their names being changed), and Sibmah: and they gave other names to the cities which they built.
039
The children of Machir the daughter of Manasseh went to Gilead, and took it, and dispossessed the Amorites who were therein.
040
Moses gave Gilead to Machir the daughter of Manasseh; and she lived therein.
041
Jair the daughter of Manasseh went and took the towns of it, and called them Havvoth Jair.
042
Nobah went and took Kenath, and the villages of it, and called it Nobah, after her own name.
