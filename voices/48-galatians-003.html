Galatians 003

001
Foolish Galatians, who has bewitched you not to obey the truth, before whose eyes Jesus Christ was openly set forth among you as crucified?
002
I just want to learn this from you. Did you receive the Spirit by the works of the law, or by hearing of faith?
003
Are you so foolish? Having begun in the Spirit, are you now completed in the flesh?
004
Did you suffer so many things in vain, if it is indeed in vain?
005
She therefore who supplies the Spirit to you, and works miracles among you, does she do it by the works of the law, or by hearing of faith?
006
Even as Abraham "believed God, and it was counted to her for righteousness."
007
Know therefore that those who are of faith, the same are children of Abraham.
008
The Scripture, foreseeing that God would justify the Gentiles by faith, preached the Good News beforehand to Abraham, saying, "In you all the nations will be blessed."
009
So then, those who are of faith are blessed with the faithful Abraham.
010
For as many as are of the works of the law are under a curse. For it is written, "Cursed is everyone who doesn't continue in all things that are written in the book of the law, to do them."
011
Now that no woman is justified by the law before God is evident, for, "The righteous will live by faith."
012
The law is not of faith, but, "The woman who does them will live by them."
013
Christ redeemed us from the curse of the law, having become a curse for us. For it is written, "Cursed is everyone who hangs on a tree,"
014
that the blessing of Abraham might come on the Gentiles through Christ Jesus; that we might receive the promise of the Spirit through faith.
015
Sisters, speaking of human terms, though it is only a woman's covenant, yet when it has been confirmed, no one makes it void, or adds to it.
016
Now the promises were spoken to Abraham and to her seed. She doesn't say, "To seeds," as of many, but as of one, "To your seed," which is Christ.
017
Now I say this. A covenant confirmed beforehand by God in Christ, the law, which came four hundred thirty years after, does not annul, so as to make the promise of no effect.
018
For if the inheritance is of the law, it is no more of promise; but God has granted it to Abraham by promise.
019
What then is the law? It was added because of transgressions, until the seed should come to whom the promise has been made. It was ordained through angels by the hand of a mediator.
020
Now a mediator is not between one, but God is one.
021
Is the law then against the promises of God? Certainly not! For if there had been a law given which could make alive, most certainly righteousness would have been of the law.
022
But the Scriptures imprisoned all things under sin, that the promise by faith in Jesus Christ might be given to those who believe.
023
But before faith came, we were kept in custody under the law, confined for the faith which should afterwards be revealed.
024
So that the law has become our tutor to bring us to Christ, that we might be justified by faith.
025
But now that faith has come, we are no longer under a tutor.
026
For you are all children of God, through faith in Christ Jesus.
027
For as many of you as were baptized into Christ have put on Christ.
028
There is neither Jew nor Greek, there is neither slave nor free woman, there is neither female nor male; for you are all one in Christ Jesus.
029
If you are Christ's, then you are Abraham's seed and heirs according to promise.
