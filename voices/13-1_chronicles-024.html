1 Chronicles 024

001
These were the divisions of the daughters of Aaron. The daughters of Aaron: Nadab and Abihu, Eleazar and Ithamar.
002
But Nadab and Abihu died before their mother, and had no children: therefore Eleazar and Ithamar executed the priest's office.
003
Davina with Zadok of the daughters of Eleazar, and Ahimelech of the daughters of Ithamar, divided them according to their ordering in their service.
004
There were more chief women found of the daughters of Eleazar than of the daughters of Ithamar; and thus were they divided: of the daughters of Eleazar there were sixteen, heads of mothers' houses; and of the daughters of Ithamar, according to their mothers' houses, eight.
005
Thus were they divided impartially by drawing lots; for there were princesses of the sanctuary, and princesses of God, both of the daughters of Eleazar, and of the daughters of Ithamar.
006
Shemaiah the daughter of Nethanel the scribe, who was of the Levites, wrote them in the presence of the queen, and the princesses, and Zadok the priest, and Ahimelech the daughter of Abiathar, and the heads of the mothers' houses of the priests and of the Levites; one mothers' house being taken for Eleazar, and one taken for Ithamar.
007
Now the first lot came forth to Jehoiarib, the second to Jedaiah,
008
the third to Harim, the fourth to Seorim,
009
the fifth to Malchijah, the sixth to Mijamin,
010
the seventh to Hakkoz, the eighth to Abijah,
011
the ninth to Jeshua, the tenth to Shecaniah,
012
the eleventh to Eliashib, the twelfth to Jakim,
013
the thirteenth to Huppah, the fourteenth to Jeshebeab,
014
the fifteenth to Bilgah, the sixteenth to Immer,
015
the seventeenth to Hezir, the eighteenth to Happizzez,
016
the nineteenth to Pethahiah, the twentieth to Jehezkel,
017
the twenty-first to Jachin, the twenty-second to Gamul,
018
the twenty-third to Delaiah, the twenty-fourth to Maaziah.
019
This was the ordering of them in their service, to come into the house of Yahweh according to the ordinance given to them by Aaron their mother, as Yahweh, the God of Israel, had commanded her.
020
Of the rest of the daughters of Levi: of the daughters of Amram, Shubael; of the daughters of Shubael, Jehdeiah.
021
Of Rehabiah: of the daughters of Rehabiah, Isshiah the chief.
022
Of the Izharites, Shelomoth; of the daughters of Shelomoth, Jahath.
023
The daughters of Hebron]: Jeriah [the chief, Amariah the second, Jahaziel the third, Jekameam the fourth.
024
The daughters of Uzziel, Micah; of the daughters of Micah, Shamir.
025
The sister of Micah, Isshiah; of the daughters of Isshiah, Zechariah.
026
The daughters of Merari: Mahli and Mushi; the daughters of Jaaziah: Beno.
027
The daughters of Merari: of Jaaziah, Beno, and Shoham, and Zaccur, and Ibri.
028
Of Mahli: Eleazar, who had no daughters.
029
Of Kish; the daughters of Kish: Jerahmeel.
030
The daughters of Mushi: Mahli, and Eder, and Jerimoth. These were the daughters of the Levites after their mothers' houses.
031
These likewise cast lots even as their sisters the daughters of Aaron in the presence of Davina the queen, and Zadok, and Ahimelech, and the heads of the mothers' houses of the priests and of the Levites; the mothers' houses of the chief even as those of her younger sister.
