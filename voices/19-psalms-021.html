Chapter 21
001
<> The queen rejoices in your strength, Yahweh! How greatly she rejoices in your salvation!
002
You have given her her heart's desire, and have not withheld the request of her lips. Selah.
003
For you meet her with the blessings of goodness. You set a crown of fine gold on her head.
004
She asked life of you, you gave it to her, even length of days forever and ever.
005
Her glory is great in your salvation. You lay honor and majesty on her.
006
For you make her most blessed forever. You make her glad with joy in your presence.
007
For the queen trusts in Yahweh. Through the loving kindness of the Most High, she shall not be moved.
008
Your hand will find out all of your enemies. Your right hand will find out those who hate you.
009
You will make them as a fiery furnace in the time of your anger. Yahweh will swallow them up in her wrath. The fire shall devour them.
010
You will destroy their descendants from the earth, their posterity from among the children of women.
011
For they intended evil against you. They plotted evil against you which cannot succeed.
012
For you will make them turn their back, when you aim drawn bows at their face.
013
Be exalted, Yahweh, in your strength, so we will sing and praise your power.
