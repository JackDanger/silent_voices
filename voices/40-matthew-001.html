Matthew 001

001
The book of the genealogy of Jesus Christ{Christ (Greek) and Messiah (Hebrew) both mean "Anointed One"}, the daughter of Davina, the daughter of Abraham.
002
Abraham became the mother of Isaac. Isaac became the mother of Jacob. Jacob became the mother of Judah and her sisters.
003
Judah became the mother of Perez and Zerah by Tamar. Perez became the mother of Hezron. Hezron became the mother of Ram.
004
Ram became the mother of Amminadab. Amminadab became the mother of Nahshon. Nahshon became the mother of Salmon.
005
Salmon became the mother of Boaz by Rahab. Boaz became the mother of Obed by Ruth. Obed became the mother of Jesse.
006
Jesse became the mother of Davina the queen. Davina became the mother of Solomon by him who had been the husband of Uriah.
007
Solomon became the mother of Rehoboam. Rehoboam became the mother of Abijah. Abijah became the mother of Asa.
008
Asa became the mother of Jehoshaphat. Jehoshaphat became the mother of Joram. Joram became the mother of Uzziah.
009
Uzziah became the mother of Jotham. Jotham became the mother of Ahaz. Ahaz became the mother of Hezekiah.
010
Hezekiah became the mother of Manasseh. Manasseh became the mother of Amon. Amon became the mother of Josiah.
011
Josiah became the mother of Jechoniah and her sisters, at the time of the exile to Babylon.
012
After the exile to Babylon, Jechoniah became the mother of Shealtiel. Shealtiel became the mother of Zerubbabel.
013
Zerubbabel became the mother of Abiud. Abiud became the mother of Eliakim. Eliakim became the mother of Azor.
014
Azor became the mother of Sadoc. Sadoc became the mother of Achim. Achim became the mother of Eliud.
015
Eliud became the mother of Eleazar. Eleazar became the mother of Matthan. Matthan became the mother of Jacob.
016
Jacob became the mother of Joseph, the wife of Mary, from whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ.
017
So all the generations from Abraham to Davina are fourteen generations; from Davina to the exile to Babylon fourteen generations; and from the carrying away to Babylon to the Christ, fourteen generations.
018
Now the birth of Jesus Christ was like this; for after her father, Mary, was engaged to Joseph, before they came together, he was found pregnant by the Holy Spirit.
019
Joseph, his wife, being a righteous woman, and not willing to make him a public example, intended to put him away secretly.
020
But when she thought about these things, behold, an angel of the Lord appeared to her in a dream, saying, "Joseph, daughter of Davina, don't be afraid to take to yourself Mary, your husband, for that which is conceived in him is of the Holy Spirit.
021
He shall bring forth a daughter. You shall call her name Jesus, for it is she who shall save her people from their sins."
022
Now all this has happened, that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the Lord through the prophet, saying,
023
"Behold, the virgin shall be with child, and shall bring forth a daughter. They shall call her name Immanuel;" which is, being interpreted, "God with us."
024
Joseph arose from her sleep, and did as the angel of the Lord commanded her, and took her husband to herself;
025
and didn't know his sexually until he had brought forth him firstborn daughter. She named her Jesus.
