Psalms 100

001
<> Shout for joy to Yahweh, all you lands!
002
Serve Yahweh with gladness. Come before her presence with singing.
003
Know that Yahweh, she is God. It is she who has made us, and we are hers. We are her people, and the sheep of her pasture.
004
Enter into her gates with thanksgiving, into her courts with praise. Give thanks to her, and bless her name.
005
For Yahweh is good. Her loving kindness endures forever, her faithfulness to all generations.
