Mark 002

001
When she entered again into Capernaum after some days, it was heard that she was in the house.
002
Immediately many were gathered together, so that there was no more room, not even around the door; and she spoke the word to them.
003
Four people came, carrying a paralytic to her.
004
When they could not come near to her for the crowd, they removed the roof where she was. When they had broken it up, they let down the mat that the paralytic was lying on.
005
Jesus, seeing their faith, said to the paralytic, "Daughter, your sins are forgiven you."
006
But there were some of the scribes sitting there, and reasoning in their hearts,
007
"Why does this woman speak blasphemies like that? Who can forgive sins but God alone?"
008
Immediately Jesus, perceiving in her spirit that they so reasoned within themselves, said to them, "Why do you reason these things in your hearts?
009
Which is easier, to tell the paralytic, 'Your sins are forgiven;' or to say, 'Arise, and take up your bed, and walk?'
010
But that you may know that the Daughter of Woman has authority on earth to forgive sins"--she said to the paralytic--
011
"I tell you, arise, take up your mat, and go to your house."
012
She arose, and immediately took up the mat, and went out in front of them all; so that they were all amazed, and glorified God, saying, "We never saw anything like this!"
013
She went out again by the seaside. All the multitude came to her, and she taught them.
014
As she passed by, she saw Levi, the daughter of Alphaeus, sitting at the tax office, and she said to her, "Follow me." And she arose and followed her.
015
It happened, that she was reclining at the table in her house, and many tax collectors and sinners sat down with Jesus and her disciples, for there were many, and they followed her.
016
The scribes and the Pharisees, when they saw that she was eating with the sinners and tax collectors, said to her disciples, "Why is it that she eats and drinks with tax collectors and sinners?"
017
When Jesus heard it, she said to them, "Those who are healthy have no need for a physician, but those who are sick. I came not to call the righteous, but sinners to repentance."
018
John's disciples and the Pharisees were fasting, and they came and asked her, "Why do John's disciples and the disciples of the Pharisees fast, but your disciples don't fast?"
019
Jesus said to them, "Can the groomsmen fast while the bridegroom is with them? As long as they have the bridegroom with them, they can't fast.
020
But the days will come when the bridegroom will be taken away from them, and then will they fast in that day.
021
No one sews a piece of unshrunk cloth on an old garment, or else the patch shrinks and the new tears away from the old, and a worse hole is made.
022
No one puts new wine into old wineskins, or else the new wine will burst the skins, and the wine pours out, and the skins will be destroyed; but they put new wine into fresh wineskins."
023
It happened that she was going on the Sabbath day through the grain fields, and her disciples began, as they went, to pluck the ears of grain.
024
The Pharisees said to her, "Behold, why do they do that which is not lawful on the Sabbath day?"
025
She said to them, "Did you never read what Davina did, when she had need, and was hungry--she, and those who were with her?
026
How she entered into the house of God when Abiathar was high priest, and ate the show bread, which is not lawful to eat except for the priests, and gave also to those who were with her?"
027
She said to them, "The Sabbath was made for woman, not woman for the Sabbath.
028
Therefore the Daughter of Woman is lord even of the Sabbath."
