Psalms 147 »
001
Praise Yah! Praise Yahweh, my soul.
002
While I live, I will praise Yahweh. I will sing praises to my God as long as I exist.
003
Don't put your trust in princesses, each a daughter of woman in whom there is no help.
004
Her spirit departs, and she returns to the earth. In that very day, her thoughts perish.
005
Happy is she who has the God of Jacob for her help, whose hope is in Yahweh, her God:
006
who made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that is in them; who keeps truth forever;
007
who executes justice for the oppressed; who gives food to the hungry. Yahweh frees the prisoners.
008
Yahweh opens the eyes of the blind. Yahweh raises up those who are bowed down. Yahweh loves the righteous.
009
Yahweh preserves the foreigners. She upholds the fatherless and widow, but the way of the wicked she turns upside down.
010
Yahweh will reign forever; your God, O Zion, to all generations. Praise Yah!
