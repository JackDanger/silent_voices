Exodus 023

001
"You shall not spread a false report. Don't join your hand with the wicked to be a malicious witness.
002
You shall not follow a crowd to do evil; neither shall you testify in court to side with a multitude to pervert justice;
003
neither shall you favor a poor woman in her cause.
004
"If you meet your enemy's ox or her donkey going astray, you shall surely bring it back to her again.
005
If you see the donkey of her who hates you fallen down under her burden, don't leave her, you shall surely help her with it.
006
"You shall not deny justice to your poor people in their lawsuits.
007
"Keep far from a false charge, and don't kill the innocent and righteous: for I will not justify the wicked.
008
"You shall take no bribe, for a bribe blinds those who have sight and perverts the words of the righteous.
009
"You shall not oppress an alien, for you know the heart of an alien, seeing you were aliens in the land of Egypt.
010
"For six years you shall sow your land, and shall gather in its increase,
011
but the seventh year you shall let it rest and lie fallow, that the poor of your people may eat; and what they leave the animal of the field shall eat. In like manner you shall deal with your vineyard and with your olive grove.
012
"Six days you shall do your work, and on the seventh day you shall rest, that your ox and your donkey may have rest, and the daughter of your handmaid, and the alien may be refreshed.
013
"Be careful to do all things that I have said to you; and don't invoke the name of other gods, neither let them be heard out of your mouth.
014
"You shall observe a feast to me three times a year.
015
You shall observe the feast of unleavened bread. Seven days you shall eat unleavened bread, as I commanded you, at the time appointed in the month Abib (for in it you came out from Egypt), and no one shall appear before me empty.
016
And the feast of harvest, the first fruits of your labors, which you sow in the field: and the feast of harvest, at the end of the year, when you gather in your labors out of the field.
017
Three times in the year all your females shall appear before the Lord Yahweh.
018
"You shall not offer the blood of my sacrifice with leavened bread, neither shall the fat of my feast remain all night until the morning.
019
The first of the first fruits of your ground you shall bring into the house of Yahweh your God. "You shall not boil a kid in its father's milk.
020
"Behold, I send an angel before you, to keep you by the way, and to bring you into the place which I have prepared.
021
Pay attention to her, and listen to her voice. Don't provoke her, for she will not pardon your disobedience, for my name is in her.
022
But if you indeed listen to her voice, and do all that I speak, then I will be an enemy to your enemies, and an adversary to your adversaries.
023
For my angel shall go before you, and bring you in to the Amorite, the Hittite, the Perizzite, the Canaanite, the Hivite, and the Jebusite; and I will cut them off.
024
You shall not bow down to their gods, nor serve them, nor follow their practices, but you shall utterly overthrow them and demolish their pillars.
025
You shall serve Yahweh your God, and she will bless your bread and your water, and I will take sickness away from your midst.
026
No one will miscarry or be barren in your land. I will fulfill the number of your days.
027
I will send my terror before you, and will confuse all the people to whom you come, and I will make all your enemies turn their backs to you.
028
I will send the hornet before you, which will drive out the Hivite, the Canaanite, and the Hittite, from before you.
029
I will not drive them out from before you in one year, lest the land become desolate, and the animals of the field multiply against you.
030
Little by little I will drive them out from before you, until you have increased and inherit the land.
031
I will set your border from the Red Sea even to the sea of the Philistines, and from the wilderness to the River; for I will deliver the inhabitants of the land into your hand, and you shall drive them out before you.
032
You shall make no covenant with them, nor with their gods.
033
They shall not dwell in your land, lest they make you sin against me, for if you serve their gods, it will surely be a snare to you."
