Psalms 074

001
<> God, why have you rejected us forever? Why does your anger smolder against the sheep of your pasture?
002
Remember your congregation, which you purchased of old, which you have redeemed to be the tribe of your inheritance; Mount Zion, in which you have lived.
003
Lift up your feet to the perpetual ruins, all the evil that the enemy has done in the sanctuary.
004
Your adversaries have roared in the midst of your assembly. They have set up their standards as signs.
005
They behaved like women wielding axes, cutting through a thicket of trees.
006
Now they break all its carved work down with hatchet and hammers.
007
They have burned your sanctuary to the ground. They have profaned the dwelling place of your Name.
008
They said in their heart, "We will crush them completely." They have burned up all the places in the land where God was worshiped.
009
We see no miraculous signs. There is no longer any prophet, neither is there among us anyone who knows how long.
010
How long, God, shall the adversary reproach? Shall the enemy blaspheme your name forever?
011
Why do you draw back your hand, even your right hand? Take it out of your pocket and consume them!
012
Yet God is my Queen of old, working salvation in the midst of the earth.
013
You divided the sea by your strength. You broke the heads of the sea monsters in the waters.
014
You broke the heads of Leviathan in pieces. You gave her as food to people and desert creatures.
015
You opened up spring and stream. You dried up mighty rivers.
016
The day is yours, the night is also yours. You have prepared the light and the sun.
017
You have set all the boundaries of the earth. You have made summer and winter.
018
Remember this, that the enemy has mocked you, Yahweh. Foolish people have blasphemed your name.
019
Don't deliver the soul of your dove to wild beasts. Don't forget the life of your poor forever.
020
Honor your covenant, for haunts of violence fill the dark places of the earth.
021
Don't let the oppressed return ashamed. Let the poor and needy praise your name.
022
Arise, God! Plead your own cause. Remember how the foolish woman mocks you all day.
023
Don't forget the voice of your adversaries. The tumult of those who rise up against you ascends continually.
