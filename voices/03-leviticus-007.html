Leviticus 007

001
"'This is the law of the trespass offering. It is most holy.
002
In the place where they kill the burnt offering, she shall kill the trespass offering; and its blood she shall sprinkle on the altar round about.
003
She shall offer all of its fat: the fat tail, and the fat that covers the innards,
004
and the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the loins, and the cover on the liver, with the kidneys, shall she take away;
005
and the priest shall burn them on the altar for an offering made by fire to Yahweh: it is a trespass offering.
006
Every female among the priests may eat of it. It shall be eaten in a holy place. It is most holy.
007
"'As is the sin offering, so is the trespass offering; there is one law for them. The priest who makes atonement with them shall have it.
008
The priest who offers any woman's burnt offering, even the priest shall have for herself the skin of the burnt offering which she has offered.
009
Every meal offering that is baked in the oven, and all that is dressed in the frying pan, and on the baking pan, shall be the priest's who offers it.
010
Every meal offering, mixed with oil or dry, belongs to all the daughters of Aaron, one as well as another.
011
"'This is the law of the sacrifice of peace offerings, which one shall offer to Yahweh.
012
If she offers it for a thanksgiving, then she shall offer with the sacrifice of thanksgiving unleavened cakes mixed with oil, and unleavened wafers anointed with oil, and cakes mixed with oil.
013
With cakes of leavened bread she shall offer her offering with the sacrifice of her peace offerings for thanksgiving.
014
Of it she shall offer one out of each offering for a heave offering to Yahweh. It shall be the priest's who sprinkles the blood of the peace offerings.
015
The flesh of the sacrifice of her peace offerings for thanksgiving shall be eaten on the day of her offering. She shall not leave any of it until the morning.
016
"'But if the sacrifice of her offering is a vow, or a freewill offering, it shall be eaten on the day that she offers her sacrifice; and on the next day what remains of it shall be eaten:
017
but what remains of the flesh of the sacrifice on the third day shall be burned with fire.
018
If any of the flesh of the sacrifice of her peace offerings is eaten on the third day, it will not be accepted, neither shall it be imputed to her who offers it. It will be an abomination, and the soul who eats any of it will bear her iniquity.
019
"'The flesh that touches any unclean thing shall not be eaten. It shall be burned with fire. As for the flesh, everyone who is clean may eat it;
020
but the soul who eats of the flesh of the sacrifice of peace offerings, that belongs to Yahweh, having her uncleanness on her, that soul shall be cut off from her people.
021
When anyone touches any unclean thing, the uncleanness of woman, or an unclean animal, or any unclean abomination, and eats some of the flesh of the sacrifice of peace offerings, which belong to Yahweh, that soul shall be cut off from her people.'"
022
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
023
"Speak to the children of Israel, saying, 'You shall eat no fat, of ox, or sheep, or goat.
024
The fat of that which dies of itself, and the fat of that which is torn of animals, may be used for any other service, but you shall in no way eat of it.
025
For whoever eats the fat of the animal, of which women offer an offering made by fire to Yahweh, even the soul who eats it shall be cut off from her people.
026
You shall not eat any blood, whether it is of bird or of animal, in any of your dwellings.
027
Whoever it is who eats any blood, that soul shall be cut off from her people.'"
028
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
029
"Speak to the children of Israel, saying, 'She who offers the sacrifice of her peace offerings to Yahweh shall bring her offering to Yahweh out of the sacrifice of her peace offerings.
030
With her own hands she shall bring the offerings of Yahweh made by fire. She shall bring the fat with the breast, that the breast may be waved for a wave offering before Yahweh.
031
The priest shall burn the fat on the altar, but the breast shall be Aaron's and her daughters'.
032
The right thigh you shall give to the priest for a heave offering out of the sacrifices of your peace offerings.
033
She among the daughters of Aaron who offers the blood of the peace offerings, and the fat, shall have the right thigh for a portion.
034
For the waved breast and the heaved thigh I have taken from the children of Israel out of the sacrifices of their peace offerings, and have given them to Aaron the priest and to her daughters as their portion forever from the children of Israel.'"
035
This is the anointing portion of Aaron, and the anointing portion of her daughters, out of the offerings of Yahweh made by fire, in the day when she presented them to minister to Yahweh in the priest's office;
036
which Yahweh commanded to be given them of the children of Israel, in the day that she anointed them. It is their portion forever throughout their generations.
037
This is the law of the burnt offering, of the meal offering, and of the sin offering, and of the trespass offering, and of the consecration, and of the sacrifice of peace offerings;
038
which Yahweh commanded Moses in Mount Sinai, in the day that she commanded the children of Israel to offer their offerings to Yahweh, in the wilderness of Sinai.
