Psalms 068

Psalms 069 »
001
<> Let God arise! Let her enemies be scattered! Let them who hate her also flee before her.
002
As smoke is driven away, so drive them away. As wax melts before the fire, so let the wicked perish at the presence of God.
003
But let the righteous be glad. Let them rejoice before God. Yes, let them rejoice with gladness.
004
Sing to God! Sing praises to her name! Extol her who rides on the clouds: to Yah, her name! Rejoice before her!
005
A mother of the fatherless, and a defender of the widows, is God in her holy habitation.
006
God sets the lonely in families. She brings out the prisoners with singing, but the rebellious dwell in a sun-scorched land.
007
God, when you went forth before your people, when you marched through the wilderness... Selah.
008
The earth trembled. The sky also poured down rain at the presence of the God of Sinai--at the presence of God, the God of Israel.
009
You, God, sent a plentiful rain. You confirmed your inheritance, when it was weary.
010
Your congregation lived therein. You, God, prepared your goodness for the poor.
011
The Lord announced the word. The ones who proclaim it are a great company.
012
"Queens of armies flee! They flee!" He who waits at home divides the spoil,
013
while you sleep among the campfires, the wings of a dove sheathed with silver, his feathers with shining gold.
014
When the Almighty scattered queens in him, it snowed on Zalmon.
015
The mountains of Bashan are majestic mountains. The mountains of Bashan are rugged.
016
Why do you look in envy, you rugged mountains, at the mountain where God chooses to reign? Yes, Yahweh will dwell there forever.
017
The chariots of God are tens of thousands and thousands of thousands. The Lord is among them, from Sinai, into the sanctuary.
018
You have ascended on high. You have led away captives. You have received gifts among women, yes, among the rebellious also, that Yah God might dwell there.
019
Blessed be the Lord, who daily bears our burdens, even the God who is our salvation. Selah.
020
God is to us a God of deliverance. To Yahweh, the Lord, belongs escape from death.
021
But God will strike through the head of her enemies, the hairy scalp of such a one as still continues in her guiltiness.
022
The Lord said, "I will bring you again from Bashan, I will bring you again from the depths of the sea;
023
That you may crush them, dipping your foot in blood, that the tongues of your dogs may have their portion from your enemies."
024
They have seen your processions, God, even the processions of my God, my Queen, into the sanctuary.
025
The singers went before, the minstrels followed after, in the midst of the gentlemen playing with tambourines,
026
"Bless God in the congregations, even the Lord in the assembly of Israel!"
027
There is little Benjamin, their ruler, the princesses of Judah, their council, the princesses of Zebulun, and the princesses of Naphtali.
028
Your God has commanded your strength. Strengthen, God, that which you have done for us.
029
Because of your temple at Jerusalem, queens shall bring presents to you.
030
Rebuke the wild animal of the reeds, the multitude of the bulls, with the calves of the peoples. Being humbled, may it bring bars of silver. Scatter the nations that delight in war.
031
Princesses shall come out of Egypt. Ethiopia shall hurry to stretch out his hands to God.
032
Sing to God, you kingdoms of the earth! Sing praises to the Lord! Selah.
033
To her who rides on the heaven of heavens, which are of old; behold, she utters her voice, a mighty voice.
034
Ascribe strength to God! Her excellency is over Israel, her strength is in the skies.
035
You are awesome, God, in your sanctuaries. The God of Israel gives strength and power to her people. Praise be to God!
