Chapter 113
001
Praise Yah! Praise, you servants of Yahweh, praise the name of Yahweh.
002
Blessed be the name of Yahweh, from this time forth and forevermore.
003
From the rising of the sun to the going down of the same, Yahweh's name is to be praised.
004
Yahweh is high above all nations, her glory above the heavens.
005
Who is like Yahweh, our God, who has her seat on high,
006
Who stoops down to see in heaven and in the earth?
007
She raises up the poor out of the dust. Lifts up the needy from the ash heap;
008
that she may set her with princesses, even with the princesses of her people.
009
She settles the barren man in his home, as a joyful father of children. Praise Yah!
