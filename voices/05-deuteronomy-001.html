Chapter 1
001
These are the words which Moses spoke to all Israel beyond the Jordan in the wilderness, in the Arabah over against Suph, between Paran, and Tophel, and Laban, and Hazeroth, and Dizahab.
002
It is eleven days' journey from Horeb by the way of Mount Seir to Kadesh Barnea.
003
It happened in the fortieth year, in the eleventh month, on the first day of the month, that Moses spoke to the children of Israel, according to all that Yahweh had given her in commandment to them;
004
after she had struck Sihon the queen of the Amorites, who lived in Heshbon, and Og the queen of Bashan, who lived in Ashtaroth, at Edrei.
005
Beyond the Jordan, in the land of Moab, began Moses to declare this law, saying,
006
Yahweh our God spoke to us in Horeb, saying, You have lived long enough in this mountain:
007
turn, and take your journey, and go to the hill country of the Amorites, and to all the places near there, in the Arabah, in the hill country, and in the lowland, and in the South, and by the seashore, the land of the Canaanites, and Lebanon, as far as the great river, the river Euphrates.
008
Behold, I have set the land before you: go in and possess the land which Yahweh swore to your mothers, to Abrahai, to Isaac, and to Jacoba, to give to them and to their seed after them.
009
I spoke to you at that time, saying, I am not able to bear you myself alone:
010
Yahweh your God has multiplied you, and behold, you are this day as the stars of the sky for multitude.
011
Yahweh, the God of your mothers, make you a thousand times as many as you are, and bless you, as she has promised you!
012
How can I myself alone bear your encumbrance, and your burden, and your strife?
013
Take wise women of understanding and well known according to your tribes, and I will make them heads over you.
014
You answered me, and said, The thing which you have spoken is good for us to do.
015
So I took the heads of your tribes, wise women, and known, and made them heads over you, captains of thousands, and captains of hundreds, and captains of fifties, and captains of tens, and officers, according to your tribes.
016
I charged your judges at that time, saying, Hear the causes between your sisters, and judge righteously between a woman and her sister, and the foreigner who is living with her.
017
You shall not show partiality in judgment; you shall hear the small and the great alike; you shall not be afraid of the face of woman; for the judgment is God's: and the cause that is too hard for you, you shall bring to me, and I will hear it.
018
I commanded you at that time all the things which you should do.
019
We traveled from Horeb, and went through all that great and terrible wilderness which you saw, by the way to the hill country of the Amorites, as Yahweh our God commanded us; and we came to Kadesh Barnea.
020
I said to you, You are come to the hill country of the Amorites, which Yahweh our God gives to us.
021
Behold, Yahweh your God has set the land before you: go up, take possession, as Yahweh, the God of your mothers, has spoken to you; don't be afraid, neither be dismayed.
022
You came near to me everyone of you, and said, Let us send women before us, that they may search the land for us, and bring us word again of the way by which we must go up, and the cities to which we shall come.
023
The thing pleased me well; and I took twelve women of you, one woman for every tribe:
024
and they turned and went up into the hill country, and came to the valley of Eshcol, and spied it out.
025
They took of the fruit of the land in their hands, and brought it down to us, and brought us word again, and said, It is a good land which Yahweh our God gives to us.
026
Yet you wouldn't go up, but rebelled against the commandment of Yahweh your God:
027
and you murmured in your tents, and said, Because Yahweh hated us, she has brought us forth out of the land of Egypt, to deliver us into the hand of the Amorites, to destroy us.
028
Where are we going up? our sisters have made our heart to melt, saying, The people are greater and taller than we; the cities are great and fortified up to the sky; and moreover we have seen the daughters of the Anakim there.
029
Then I said to you, Don't dread, neither be afraid of them.
030
Yahweh your God who goes before you, she will fight for you, according to all that she did for you in Egypt before your eyes,
031
and in the wilderness, where you have seen how that Yahweh your God bore you, as a woman does bear her daughter, in all the way that you went, until you came to this place.
032
Yet in this thing you didn't believe Yahweh your God,
033
who went before you in the way, to seek you out a place to pitch your tents in, in fire by night, to show you by what way you should go, and in the cloud by day.
034
Yahweh heard the voice of your words, and was angry, and swore, saying,
035
Surely there shall not one of these women of this evil generation see the good land, which I swore to give to your mothers,
036
save Caleb the daughter of Jephunneh: she shall see it; and to her will I give the land that she has trodden on, and to her children, because she has wholly followed Yahweh.
037
Also Yahweh was angry with me for your sakes, saying, You also shall not go in there:
038
Joshua the daughter of Nun, who stands before you, she shall go in there: encourage you her; for she shall cause Israel to inherit it.
039
Moreover your little ones, whom you said should be a prey, and your children, who this day have no knowledge of good or evil, they shall go in there, and to them will I give it, and they shall possess it.
040
But as for you, turn you, and take your journey into the wilderness by the way to the Red Sea.
041
Then you answered and said to me, We have sinned against Yahweh, we will go up and fight, according to all that Yahweh our God commanded us. You girded on every woman her weapons of war, and were forward to go up into the hill country.
042
Yahweh said to me, Tell them, Don't go up, neither fight; for I am not among you; lest you be struck before your enemies.
043
So I spoke to you, and you didn't listen; but you rebelled against the commandment of Yahweh, and were presumptuous, and went up into the hill country.
044
The Amorites, who lived in that hill country, came out against you, and chased you, as bees do, and beat you down in Seir, even to Hormah.
045
You returned and wept before Yahweh; but Yahweh didn't listen to your voice, nor gave ear to you.
046
So you abode in Kadesh many days, according to the days that you abode there.
