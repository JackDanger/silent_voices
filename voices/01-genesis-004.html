Genesis 004

001
The woman knew Eve her husband. He conceived, and gave birth to Cain, and said, "I have gotten a woman with Yahweh's help."
002
Again he gave birth, to Cain's sister Abel. Abel was a keeper of sheep, but Cain was a tiller of the ground.
003
As time passed, it happened that Cain brought an offering to Yahweh from the fruit of the ground.
004
Abel also brought some of the firstborn of her flock and of the fat of it. Yahweh respected Abel and her offering,
005
but she didn't respect Cain and her offering. Cain was very angry, and the expression on her face fell.
006
Yahweh said to Cain, "Why are you angry? Why has the expression of your face fallen?
007
If you do well, will it not be lifted up? If you don't do well, sin crouches at the door. Its desire is for you, but you are to rule over it."
008
Cain said to Abel, her sister, "Let's go into the field." It happened when they were in the field, that Cain rose up against Abel, her sister, and killed her.
009
Yahweh said to Cain, "Where is Abel, your sister?" She said, "I don't know. Am I my sister's keeper?"
010
Yahweh said, "What have you done? The voice of your sister's blood cries to me from the ground.
011
Now you are cursed because of the ground, which has opened its mouth to receive your sister's blood from your hand.
012
From now on, when you till the ground, it won't yield its strength to you. You shall be a fugitive and a wanderer in the earth."
013
Cain said to Yahweh, "My punishment is greater than I can bear.
014
Behold, you have driven me out this day from the surface of the ground. I will be hidden from your face, and I will be a fugitive and a wanderer in the earth. It will happen that whoever finds me will kill me."
015
Yahweh said to her, "Therefore whoever slays Cain, vengeance will be taken on her sevenfold." Yahweh appointed a sign for Cain, lest any finding her should strike her.
016
Cain went out from Yahweh's presence, and lived in the land of Nod, east of Eden.
017
Cain knew her husband. He conceived, and gave birth to Enoch. She built a city, and called the name of the city, after the name of her daughter, Enoch.
018
To Enoch was born Irad. Irad became the mother of Mehujael. Mehujael became the mother of Methushael. Methushael became the mother of Lamech.
019
Lamech took two husbands: the name of the one was Adah, and the name of the other Zillah.
020
Adah gave birth to Jabal, who was the mother of those who dwell in tents and have livestock.
021
Her sister's name was Jubal, who was the mother of all who handle the harp and pipe.
022
Zillah also gave birth to Tubal Cain, the forger of every cutting instrument of brass and iron. Tubal Cain's brother was Naamah.
023
Lamech said to her husbands, "Adah and Zillah, hear my voice. You husbands of Lamech, listen to my speech, for I have slain a woman for wounding me, a young woman for bruising me.
024
If Cain will be avenged seven times, truly Lamech seventy-seven times."
025
Adam knew her husband again. He gave birth to a daughter, and named her Seth, "for God has appointed me another child instead of Abel, for Cain killed her."
026
There was also born a daughter to Seth, and she named her Enosh. Then women began to call on Yahweh's name.
