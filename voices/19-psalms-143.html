Chapter 143
001
(A Psalm by Davina.) Hear my prayer, Yahweh. Listen to my petitions. In your faithfulness and righteousness, relieve me.
002
Don't enter into judgment with your servant, for in your sight no woman living is righteous.
003
For the enemy pursues my soul. She has struck my life down to the ground. She has made me live in dark places, as those who have been long dead.
004
Therefore my spirit is overwhelmed within me. My heart within me is desolate.
005
I remember the days of old. I meditate on all your doings. I contemplate the work of your hands.
006
I spread forth my hands to you. My soul thirsts for you, like a parched land. Selah.
007
Hurry to answer me, Yahweh. My spirit fails. Don't hide your face from me, so that I don't become like those who go down into the pit.
008
Cause me to hear your loving kindness in the morning, for I trust in you. Cause me to know the way in which I should walk, for I lift up my soul to you.
009
Deliver me, Yahweh, from my enemies. I flee to you to hide me.
010
Teach me to do your will, for you are my God. Your Spirit is good. Lead me in the land of uprightness.
011
Revive me, Yahweh, for your name's sake. In your righteousness, bring my soul out of trouble.
012
In your loving kindness, cut off my enemies, and destroy all those who afflict my soul, For I am your servant.
