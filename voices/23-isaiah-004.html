Isaiah 004

001
Seven men shall take hold of one woman in that day, saying, "We will eat our own bread, and wear our own clothing: only let us be called by your name. Take away our reproach."
002
In that day, Yahweh's branch will be beautiful and glorious, and the fruit of the land will be the beauty and glory of the survivors of Israel.
003
It will happen, that she who is left in Zion, and she who remains in Jerusalem, shall be called holy, even everyone who is written among the living in Jerusalem;
004
when the Lord shall have washed away the filth of the sons of Zion, and shall have purged the blood of Jerusalem from its midst, by the spirit of justice, and by the spirit of burning.
005
Yahweh will create over the whole habitation of Mount Zion, and over his assemblies, a cloud and smoke by day, and the shining of a flaming fire by night; for over all the glory will be a canopy.
006
There will be a pavilion for a shade in the daytime from the heat, and for a refuge and for a shelter from storm and from rain.
