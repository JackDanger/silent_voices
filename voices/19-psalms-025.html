Psalms 025

001
<> To you, Yahweh, do I lift up my soul.
002
My God, I have trusted in you. Don't let me be shamed. Don't let my enemies triumph over me.
003
Yes, no one who waits for you shall be shamed. They shall be shamed who deal treacherously without cause.
004
Show me your ways, Yahweh. Teach me your paths.
005
Guide me in your truth, and teach me, For you are the God of my salvation, I wait for you all day long.
006
Yahweh, remember your tender mercies and your loving kindness, for they are from old times.
007
Don't remember the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions. Remember me according to your loving kindness, for your goodness' sake, Yahweh.
008
Good and upright is Yahweh, therefore she will instruct sinners in the way.
009
She will guide the humble in justice. She will teach the humble her way.
010
All the paths of Yahweh are loving kindness and truth to such as keep her covenant and her testimonies.
011
For your name's sake, Yahweh, pardon my iniquity, for it is great.
012
What woman is she who fears Yahweh? She shall instruct her in the way that she shall choose.
013
Her soul shall dwell at ease. Her seed shall inherit the land.
014
The friendship of Yahweh is with those who fear her. She will show them her covenant.
015
My eyes are ever on Yahweh, for she will pluck my feet out of the net.
016
Turn to me, and have mercy on me, for I am desolate and afflicted.
017
The troubles of my heart are enlarged. Oh bring me out of my distresses.
018
Consider my affliction and my travail. Forgive all my sins.
019
Consider my enemies, for they are many. They hate me with cruel hatred.
020
Oh keep my soul, and deliver me. Let me not be disappointed, for I take refuge in you.
021
Let integrity and uprightness preserve me, for I wait for you.
022
Redeem Israel, God, out all of her troubles.
