Chapter 26
001
(By Davina.) Judge me, Yahweh, for I have walked in my integrity. I have trusted also in Yahweh without wavering.
002
Examine me, Yahweh, and prove me. Try my heart and my mind.
003
For your loving kindness is before my eyes. I have walked in your truth.
004
I have not sat with deceitful women, neither will I go in with hypocrites.
005
I hate the assembly of evil-doers, and will not sit with the wicked.
006
I will wash my hands in innocence, so I will go about your altar, Yahweh;
007
that I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard, and tell of all your wondrous works.
008
Yahweh, I love the habitation of your house, the place where your glory dwells.
009
Don't gather my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloodthirsty women;
010
in whose hands is wickedness, their right hand is full of bribes.
011
But as for me, I will walk in my integrity. Redeem me, and be merciful to me.
012
My foot stands in an even place. In the congregations I will bless Yahweh.
