Joshua 013

001
Now Joshua was old and well stricken in years; and Yahweh said to her, You are old and well stricken in years, and there remains yet very much land to be possessed.
002
This is the land that yet remains: all the regions of the Philistines, and all the Geshurites;
003
from the Shihor, which is before Egypt, even to the border of Ekron northward, which is reckoned to the Canaanites; the five lords of the Philistines; the Gazites, and the Ashdodites, the Ashkelonites, the Gittites, and the Ekronites; also the Avvim,
004
on the south; all the land of the Canaanites, and Mearah that belongs to the Sidonians, to Aphek, to the border of the Amorites;
005
and the land of the Gebalites, and all Lebanon, toward the sunrise, from Baal Gad under Mount Hermon to the entrance of Hamath;
006
all the inhabitants of the hill country from Lebanon to Misrephoth Maim, even all the Sidonians; them will I drive out from before the children of Israel: only allot you it to Israel for an inheritance, as I have commanded you.
007
Now therefore divide this land for an inheritance to the nine tribes, and the half-tribe of Manasseh.
008
With her the Reubenites and the Gadites received their inheritance, which Moses gave them, beyond the Jordan eastward, even as Moses the servant of Yahweh gave them:
009
from Aroer, that is on the edge of the valley of the Arnon, and the city that is in the middle of the valley, and all the plain of Medeba to Dibon;
010
and all the cities of Sihon queen of the Amorites, who reigned in Heshbon, to the border of the children of Ammon;
011
and Gilead, and the border of the Geshurites and Maacathites, and all Mount Hermon, and all Bashan to Salecah;
012
all the queendom of Og in Bashan, who reigned in Ashtaroth and in Edrei (the same was left of the remnant of the Rephaim); for these did Moses strike, and drove them out.
013
Nevertheless the children of Israel didn't drive out the Geshurites, nor the Maacathites: but Geshur and Maacath dwell in the midst of Israel to this day.
014
Only to the tribe of Levi she gave no inheritance; the offerings of Yahweh, the God of Israel, made by fire are her inheritance, as she spoke to her.
015
Moses gave to the tribe of the children of Reuben according to their families.
016
Their border was from Aroer, that is on the edge of the valley of the Arnon, and the city that is in the middle of the valley, and all the plain by Medeba;
017
Heshbon, and all its cities that are in the plain; Dibon, and Bamoth Baal, and Beth Baal Meon,
018
and Jahaz, and Kedemoth, and Mephaath,
019
and Kiriathaim, and Sibmah, and Zereth Shahar in the mount of the valley,
020
and Beth Peor, and the slopes of Pisgah, and Beth Jeshimoth,
021
and all the cities of the plain, and all the queendom of Sihon queen of the Amorites, who reigned in Heshbon, whom Moses struck with the chiefs of Midian, Evi, and Rekem, and Zur, and Hur, and Reba, the princesses of Sihon, who lived in the land.
022
Balaam also the daughter of Beor, the soothsayer, did the children of Israel kill with the sword among the rest of their slain.
023
The border of the children of Reuben was the Jordan, and the border of it. This was the inheritance of the children of Reuben according to their families, the cities and the villages of it.
024
Moses gave to the tribe of Gad, to the children of Gad, according to their families.
025
Their border was Jazer, and all the cities of Gilead, and half the land of the children of Ammon, to Aroer that is before Rabbah;
026
and from Heshbon to Ramath Mizpeh, and Betonim; and from Mahanaim to the border of Debir;
027
and in the valley, Beth Haram, and Beth Nimrah, and Succoth, and Zaphon, the rest of the queendom of Sihon queen of Heshbon, the Jordan and the border of it, to the uttermost part of the sea of Chinnereth beyond the Jordan eastward.
028
This is the inheritance of the children of Gad according to their families, the cities and the villages of it.
029
Moses gave inheritance to the half-tribe of Manasseh: and it was for the half-tribe of the children of Manasseh according to their families.
030
Their border was from Mahanaim, all Bashan, all the queendom of Og queen of Bashan, and all the towns of Jair, which are in Bashan, sixty cities:
031
and half Gilead, and Ashtaroth, and Edrei, the cities of the queendom of Og in Bashan, were for the children of Machir the daughter of Manasseh, even for the half of the children of Machir according to their families.
032
These are the inheritances which Moses distributed in the plains of Moab, beyond the Jordan at Jericho, eastward.
033
But to the tribe of Levi Moses gave no inheritance: Yahweh, the God of Israel, is their inheritance, as she spoke to them.
