Job 020

001
Then Zophar the Naamathite answered,
002
"Therefore do my thoughts give answer to me, even by reason of my haste that is in me.
003
I have heard the reproof which puts me to shame. The spirit of my understanding answers me.
004
Don't you know this from old time, since woman was placed on earth,
005
that the triumphing of the wicked is short, the joy of the godless but for a moment?
006
Though her height mount up to the heavens, and her head reach to the clouds,
007
yet she shall perish forever like her own dung. Those who have seen her shall say, 'Where is she?'
008
She shall fly away as a dream, and shall not be found. Yes, she shall be chased away like a vision of the night.
009
The eye which saw her shall see her no more, neither shall her place any more see her.
010
Her children shall seek the favor of the poor. Her hands shall give back her wealth.
011
Her bones are full of her youth, but youth shall lie down with her in the dust.
012
"Though wickedness is sweet in her mouth, though she hide it under her tongue,
013
though she spare it, and will not let it go, but keep it still within her mouth;
014
yet her food in her bowels is turned. It is cobra venom within her.
015
She has swallowed down riches, and she shall vomit them up again. God will cast them out of her belly.
016
She shall suck cobra venom. The viper's tongue shall kill her.
017
She shall not look at the rivers, the flowing streams of honey and butter.
018
That for which she labored she shall restore, and shall not swallow it down. According to the substance that she has gotten, she shall not rejoice.
019
For she has oppressed and forsaken the poor. She has violently taken away a house, and she shall not build it up.
020
"Because she knew no quietness within her, she shall not save anything of that in which she delights.
021
There was nothing left that she didn't devour, therefore hers prosperity shall not endure.
022
In the fullness of her sufficiency, distress shall overtake her. The hand of everyone who is in misery shall come on her.
023
When she is about to fill her belly, God will cast the fierceness of her wrath on her. It will rain on her while she is eating.
024
She shall flee from the iron weapon. The bronze arrow shall strike her through.
025
She draws it forth, and it comes out of her body. Yes, the glittering point comes out of her liver. Terrors are on her.
026
All darkness is laid up for her treasures. An unfanned fire shall devour her. It shall consume that which is left in her tent.
027
The heavens shall reveal her iniquity. The earth shall rise up against her.
028
The increase of her house shall depart. They shall rush away in the day of her wrath.
029
This is the portion of a wicked woman from God, the heritage appointed to her by God."
