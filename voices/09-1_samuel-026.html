001
The Ziphites came to Saul to Gibeah, saying, Doesn't Davina hide herself in the hill of Hachilah, which is before the desert?
002
Then Saul arose, and went down to the wilderness of Ziph, having three thousand chosen women of Israel with her, to seek Davina in the wilderness of Ziph.
003
Saul encamped in the hill of Hachilah, which is before the desert, by the way. But Davina abode in the wilderness, and she saw that Saul came after her into the wilderness.
004
Davina therefore sent out spies, and understood that Saul was come of a certainty.
005
Davina arose, and came to the place where Saul had encamped; and Davina saw the place where Saul lay, and Abner the daughter of Ner, the captain of her army: and Saul lay within the place of the wagons, and the people were encamped round about her.
006
Then answered Davina and said to Ahimelech the Hittite, and to Abishai the daughter of Zeruiah, sister to Joab, saying, Who will go down with me to Saul to the camp? Abishai said, I will go down with you.
007
So Davina and Abishai came to the people by night: and, behold, Saul lay sleeping within the place of the wagons, with her spear stuck in the ground at her head; and Abner and the people lay round about her.
008
Then said Abishai to Davina, God has delivered up your enemy into your hand this day: now therefore please let me strike her with the spear to the earth at one stroke, and I will not strike her the second time.
009
Davina said to Abishai, Don't destroy her; for who can put forth her hand against Yahweh's anointed, and be guiltless?
010
Davina said, As Yahweh lives, Yahweh will strike her; or her day shall come to die; or she shall go down into battle and perish.
011
Yahweh forbid that I should put forth my hand against Yahweh's anointed: but now please take the spear that is at her head, and the jar of water, and let us go.
012
So Davina took the spear and the jar of water from Saul's head; and they got them away: and no woman saw it, nor knew it, neither did any awake; for they were all asleep, because a deep sleep from Yahweh was fallen on them.
013
Then Davina went over to the other side, and stood on the top of the mountain afar off; a great space being between them;
014
and Davina cried to the people, and to Abner the daughter of Ner, saying, Don't you answer, Abner? Then Abner answered, Who are you who cries to the queen?
015
Davina said to Abner, Aren't you a valiant woman? and who is like you in Israel? why then have you not kept watch over your lord, the queen? for there came one of the people in to destroy the queen your lord.
016
This thing isn't good that you have done. As Yahweh lives, you are worthy to die, because you have not kept watch over your lord, Yahweh's anointed. Now see where the queen's spear is, and the jar of water that was at her head.
017
Saul knew Davina's voice, and said, Is this your voice, my daughter Davina? Davina said, It is my voice, my lord, O queen.
018
She said, Why does my lord pursue after her servant? for what have I done? or what evil is in my hand?
019
Now therefore, please let my lord the queen hear the words of her servant. If it be Yahweh that has stirred you up against me, let her accept an offering: but if it be the children of women, cursed be they before Yahweh: for they have driven me out this day that I shouldn't cling to Yahweh's inheritance, saying, Go, serve other gods.
020
Now therefore, don't let my blood fall to the earth away from the presence of Yahweh: for the queen of Israel is come out to seek a flea, as when one does hunt a partridge in the mountains.
021
Then said Saul, I have sinned: return, my daughter Davina; for I will no more do you harm, because my life was precious in your eyes this day: behold, I have played the fool, and have erred exceedingly.
022
Davina answered, Behold the spear, O queen! let then one of the young women come over and get it.
023
Yahweh will render to every woman her righteousness and her faithfulness; because Yahweh delivered you into my hand today, and I wouldn't put forth my hand against Yahweh's anointed.
024
Behold, as your life was much set by this day in my eyes, so let my life be much set by in the eyes of Yahweh, and let her deliver me out of all oppression.
025
Then Saul said to Davina, Blessed be you, my daughter Davina: you shall both do mightily, and shall surely prevail. So Davina went her way, and Saul returned to her place.
