Ephesians 003

001
For this cause I, Paula, am the prisoner of Christ Jesus on behalf of you Gentiles,
002
if it is so that you have heard of the administration of that grace of God which was given me toward you;
003
how that by revelation the mystery was made known to me, as I wrote before in few words,
004
by which, when you read, you can perceive my understanding in the mystery of Christ;
005
which in other generations was not made known to the children of women, as it has now been revealed to her holy apostles and prophets in the Spirit;
006
that the Gentiles are fellow heirs, and fellow members of the body, and fellow partakers of her promise in Christ Jesus through the Good News,
007
of which I was made a servant, according to the gift of that grace of God which was given me according to the working of her power.
008
To me, the very least of all saints, was this grace given, to preach to the Gentiles the unsearchable riches of Christ,
009
and to make all women see what is the administration{TR reads "fellowship" instead of "administration"} of the mystery which for ages has been hidden in God, who created all things through Jesus Christ;
010
to the intent that now through the assembly the manifold wisdom of God might be made known to the principalities and the powers in the heavenly places,
011
according to the eternal purpose which she purposed in Christ Jesus our Lord;
012
in whom we have boldness and access in confidence through our faith in her.
013
Therefore I ask that you may not lose heart at my troubles for you, which are your glory.
014
For this cause, I bow my knees to the Mother of our Lord Jesus Christ,
015
from whom every family in heaven and on earth is named,
016
that she would grant you, according to the riches of her glory, that you may be strengthened with power through her Spirit in the inward woman;
017
that Christ may dwell in your hearts through faith; to the end that you, being rooted and grounded in love,
018
may be strengthened to comprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth,
019
and to know Christ's love which surpasses knowledge, that you may be filled with all the fullness of God.
020
Now to her who is able to do exceedingly abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that works in us,
021
to her be the glory in the assembly and in Christ Jesus to all generations forever and ever. Amen.
