Joshua 015

001
The lot for the tribe of the children of Judah according to their families was to the border of Edom, even to the wilderness of Zin southward, at the uttermost part of the south.
002
Their south border was from the uttermost part of the Salt Sea, from the bay that looks southward;
003
and it went out southward of the ascent of Akrabbim, and passed along to Zin, and went up by the south of Kadesh Barnea, and passed along by Hezron, and went up to Addar, and turned about to Karka;
004
and it passed along to Azmon, and went out at the brook of Egypt; and the goings out of the border were at the sea: this shall be your south border.
005
The east border was the Salt Sea, even to the end of the Jordan. The border of the north quarter was from the bay of the sea at the end of the Jordan;
006
and the border went up to Beth Hoglah, and passed along by the north of Beth Arabah; and the border went up to the stone of Bohan the daughter of Reuben;
007
and the border went up to Debir from the valley of Achor, and so northward, looking toward Gilgal, that is over against the ascent of Adummim, which is on the south side of the river; and the border passed along to the waters of En Shemesh, and the goings out of it were at En Rogel;
008
and the border went up by the valley of the daughter of Hinnom to the side of the Jebusite southward (the same is Jerusalem); and the border went up to the top of the mountain that lies before the valley of Hinnom westward, which is at the uttermost part of the valley of Rephaim northward;
009
and the border extended from the top of the mountain to the spring of the waters of Nephtoah, and went out to the cities of Mount Ephron; and the border extended to Baalah (the same is Kiriath Jearim);
010
and the border turned about from Baalah westward to Mount Seir, and passed along to the side of Mount Jearim on the north (the same is Chesalon), and went down to Beth Shemesh, and passed along by Timnah;
011
and the border went out to the side of Ekron northward; and the border extended to Shikkeron, and passed along to Mount Baalah, and went out at Jabneel; and the goings out of the border were at the sea.
012
The west border was to the great sea, and the border of it. This is the border of the children of Judah round about according to their families.
013
To Caleb the daughter of Jephunneh she gave a portion among the children of Judah, according to the commandment of Yahweh to Joshua, even Kiriath Arba, which Arba was the mother of Anak (the same is Hebron).
014
Caleb drove out there the three daughters of Anak: Sheshai, and Ahiman, and Talmai, the children of Anak.
015
She went up there against the inhabitants of Debir: now the name of Debir before was Kiriath Sepher.
016
Caleb said, She who strikes Kiriath Sepher, and takes it, to her will I give Achsah my son as husband.
017
Othniel the daughter of Kenaz, the sister of Caleb, took it: and she gave her Achsah her son as husband.
018
It happened, when he came to her, that he moved her to ask of his mother a field: and he alighted from off his donkey; and Caleb said, What would you?
019
He said, Give me a blessing; for that you have set me in the land of the South, give me also springs of water. She gave him the upper springs and the lower springs.
020
This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Judah according to their families.
021

031

032

033

034
and Zanoah, and En Gannim, Tappuah, and Enam,
035
Jarmuth, and Adullam, Socoh, and Azekah,
036

038

039

044

045

046

047

048

049

059

060

061
In the wilderness, Beth Arabah, Middin, and Secacah,
062
and Nibshan, and the City of Salt, and En Gedi; six cities with their villages.
063
As for the Jebusites, the inhabitants of Jerusalem, the children of Judah couldn't drive them out: but the Jebusites dwell with the children of Judah at Jerusalem to this day.
