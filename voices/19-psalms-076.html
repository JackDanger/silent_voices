Psalms 077 »
001
<> In Judah, God is known. Her name is great in Israel.
002
Her tent is also in Salem; Her dwelling place in Zion.
003
There she broke the flaming arrows of the bow, the shield, and the sword, and the weapons of war. Selah.
004
Glorious are you, and excellent, more than mountains of game.
005
Valiant women lie plundered, they have slept their last sleep. None of the women of war can lift their hands.
006
At your rebuke, God of Jacob, both chariot and horse are cast into a deep sleep.
007
You, even you, are to be feared. Who can stand in your sight when you are angry?
008
You pronounced judgment from heaven. The earth feared, and was silent,
009
when God arose to judgment, to save all the afflicted ones of the earth. Selah.
010
Surely the wrath of woman praises you. The survivors of your wrath are restrained.
011
Make vows to Yahweh your God, and fulfill them! Let all of her neighbors bring presents to her who is to be feared.
012
She will cut off the spirit of princesses. She is feared by the queens of the earth.
