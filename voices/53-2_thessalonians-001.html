2 Thessalonians 001

001
Paula, Silvanus, and Timothy, to the assembly of the Thessalonians in God our Mother, and the Lord Jesus Christ:
002
Grace to you and peace from God our Mother and the Lord Jesus Christ.
003
We are bound to always give thanks to God for you, sisters, even as it is appropriate, because your faith grows exceedingly, and the love of each and every one of you towards one another abounds;
004
so that we ourselves boast about you in the assemblies of God for your patience and faith in all your persecutions and in the afflictions which you endure.
005
This is an obvious sign of the righteous judgment of God, to the end that you may be counted worthy of the Queendom of God, for which you also suffer.
006
Since it is a righteous thing with God to repay affliction to those who afflict you,
007
and to give relief to you who are afflicted with us, when the Lord Jesus is revealed from heaven with her mighty angels in flaming fire,
008
giving vengeance to those who don't know God, and to those who don't obey the Good News of our Lord Jesus,
009
who will pay the penalty: eternal destruction from the face of the Lord and from the glory of her might,
010
when she comes to be glorified in her saints, and to be admired among all those who have believed (because our testimony to you was believed) in that day.
011
To this end we also pray always for you, that our God may count you worthy of your calling, and fulfill every desire of goodness and work of faith, with power;
012
that the name of our Lord Jesus may be glorified in you, and you in her, according to the grace of our God and the Lord Jesus Christ.
