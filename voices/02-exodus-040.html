Exodus 040

001
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
002
"On the first day of the first month you shall raise up the tent of the Tent of Meeting.
003
You shall put the ark of the testimony in it, and you shall screen the ark with the veil.
004
You shall bring in the table, and set in order the things that are on it. You shall bring in the lampstand, and light the lamps of it.
005
You shall set the golden altar for incense before the ark of the testimony, and put the screen of the door to the tent.
006
You shall set the altar of burnt offering before the door of the tent of the tent of meeting.
007
You shall set the basin between the tent of meeting and the altar, and shall put water therein.
008
You shall set up the court around it, and hang up the screen of the gate of the court.
009
You shall take the anointing oil, and anoint the tent, and all that is in it, and shall make it holy, and all its furniture: and it will be holy.
010
You shall anoint the altar of burnt offering, with all its vessels, and sanctify the altar: and the altar will be most holy.
011
You shall anoint the basin and its base, and sanctify it.
012
You shall bring Aaron and her daughters to the door of the tent of meeting, and shall wash them with water.
013
You shall put on Aaron the holy garments; and you shall anoint her, and sanctify her, that she may minister to me in the priest's office.
014
You shall bring her daughters, and put coats on them.
015
You shall anoint them, as you anointed their mother, that they may minister to me in the priest's office. Their anointing shall be to them for an everlasting priesthood throughout their generations."
016
Moses did so. According to all that Yahweh commanded her, so she did.
017
It happened in the first month in the second year, on the first day of the month, that the tent was raised up.
018
Moses raised up the tent, and laid its sockets, and set up the boards of it, and put in the bars of it, and raised up its pillars.
019
She spread the covering over the tent, and put the roof of the tent above on it, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
020
She took and put the testimony into the ark, and set the poles on the ark, and put the mercy seat above on the ark.
021
She brought the ark into the tent, and set up the veil of the screen, and screened the ark of the testimony, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
022
She put the table in the tent of meeting, on the side of the tent northward, outside of the veil.
023
She set the bread in order on it before Yahweh, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
024
She put the lampstand in the tent of meeting, opposite the table, on the side of the tent southward.
025
She lit the lamps before Yahweh, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
026
She put the golden altar in the tent of meeting before the veil;
027
and she burnt incense of sweet spices on it, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
028
She put up the screen of the door to the tent.
029
She set the altar of burnt offering at the door of the tent of the tent of meeting, and offered on it the burnt offering and the meal offering, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
030
She set the basin between the tent of meeting and the altar, and put water therein, with which to wash.
031
Moses, Aaron, and her daughters washed their hands and their feet there.
032
When they went into the tent of meeting, and when they came near to the altar, they washed, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
033
She raised up the court around the tent and the altar, and set up the screen of the gate of the court. So Moses finished the work.
034
Then the cloud covered the tent of meeting, and the glory of Yahweh filled the tent.
035
Moses wasn't able to enter into the tent of meeting, because the cloud stayed on it, and Yahweh's glory filled the tent.
036
When the cloud was taken up from over the tent, the children of Israel went onward, throughout all their journeys;
037
but if the cloud wasn't taken up, then they didn't travel until the day that it was taken up.
038
For the cloud of Yahweh was on the tent by day, and there was fire in the cloud by night, in the sight of all the house of Israel, throughout all their journeys.
