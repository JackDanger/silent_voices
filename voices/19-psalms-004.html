Psalms 004

001
<> Answer me when I call, God of my righteousness. Give me relief from my distress. Have mercy on me, and hear my prayer.
002
You daughters of women, how long shall my glory be turned into dishonor? Will you love vanity, and seek after falsehood? Selah.
003
But know that Yahweh has set apart for herself her who is godly: Yahweh will hear when I call to her.
004
Stand in awe, and don't sin. Search your own heart on your bed, and be still. Selah.
005
Offer the sacrifices of righteousness. Put your trust in Yahweh.
006
Many say, "Who will show us any good?" Yahweh, let the light of your face shine on us.
007
You have put gladness in my heart, more than when their grain and their new wine are increased.
008
In peace I will both lay myself down and sleep, for you, Yahweh alone, make me live in safety.
