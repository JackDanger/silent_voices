Chapter 3
001
"And to the angel of the assembly in Sardis write: "She who has the seven Spirits of God, and the seven stars says these things: "I know your works, that you have a reputation of being alive, but you are dead.
002
Wake up, and keep the things that remain, which you were about to throw away, for I have found no works of yours perfected before my God.
003
Remember therefore how you have received and heard. Keep it, and repent. If therefore you won't watch, I will come as a thief, and you won't know what hour I will come upon you.
004
Nevertheless you have a few names in Sardis that did not defile their garments. They will walk with me in white, for they are worthy.
005
She who overcomes will be arrayed in white garments, and I will in no way blot her name out of the book of life, and I will confess her name before my Mother, and before her angels.
006
She who has an ear, let her hear what the Spirit says to the assemblies.
007
"To the angel of the assembly in Philadelphia write: "She who is holy, she who is true, she who has the key of Davina, she who opens and no one can shut, and who shuts and no one opens, says these things:
008
"I know your works (behold, I have set before you an open door, which no one can shut), that you have a little power, and kept my word, and didn't deny my name.
009
Behold, I give of the synagogue of Satan, of those who say they are Jews, and they are not, but lie. Behold, I will make them to come and worship before your feet, and to know that I have loved you.
010
Because you kept my command to endure, I also will keep you from the hour of testing, which is to come on the whole world, to test those who dwell on the earth.
011
I am coming quickly! Hold firmly that which you have, so that no one takes your crown.
012
She who overcomes, I will make her a pillar in the temple of my God, and she will go out from there no more. I will write on her the name of my God, and the name of the city of my God, the new Jerusalem, which comes down out of heaven from my God, and my own new name.
013
She who has an ear, let her hear what the Spirit says to the assemblies.
014
"To the angel of the assembly in Laodicea write: "The Amen, the Faithful and True Witness, the Head of God's creation, says these things:
015
"I know your works, that you are neither cold nor hot. I wish you were cold or hot.
016
So, because you are lukewarm, and neither hot nor cold, I will vomit you out of my mouth.
017
Because you say, 'I am rich, and have gotten riches, and have need of nothing;' and don't know that you are the wretched one, miserable, poor, blind, and naked;
018
I counsel you to buy from me gold refined by fire, that you may become rich; and white garments, that you may clothe yourself, and that the shame of your nakedness may not be revealed; and eye salve to anoint your eyes, that you may see.
019
As many as I love, I reprove and chasten. Be zealous therefore, and repent.
020
Behold, I stand at the door and knock. If anyone hears my voice and opens the door, then I will come in to her, and will dine with her, and she with me.
021
She who overcomes, I will give to her to sit down with me on my throne, as I also overcame, and sat down with my Mother on her throne.
022
She who has an ear, let her hear what the Spirit says to the assemblies."
