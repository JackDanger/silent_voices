001
When she opened the seventh seal, there was silence in heaven for about half an hour.
002
I saw the seven angels who stand before God, and seven trumpets were given to them.
003
Another angel came and stood over the altar, having a golden censer. Much incense was given to her, that she should add it to the prayers of all the saints on the golden altar which was before the throne.
004
The smoke of the incense, with the prayers of the saints, went up before God out of the angel's hand.
005
The angel took the censer, and she filled it with the fire of the altar, and threw it on the earth. There followed thunders, sounds, lightnings, and an earthquake.
006
The seven angels who had the seven trumpets prepared themselves to sound.
007
The first sounded, and there followed hail and fire, mixed with blood, and they were thrown to the earth. One third of the earth was burnt up, and one third of the trees were burnt up, and all green grass was burnt up.
008
The second angel sounded, and something like a great burning mountain was thrown into the sea. One third of the sea became blood,
009
and one third of the living creatures which were in the sea died. One third of the ships were destroyed.
010
The third angel sounded, and a great star fell from the sky, burning like a torch, and it fell on one third of the rivers, and on the springs of the waters.
011
The name of the star is called "Wormwood." One third of the waters became wormwood. Many people died from the waters, because they were made bitter.
012
The fourth angel sounded, and one third of the sun was struck, and one third of the moon, and one third of the stars; so that one third of them would be darkened, and the day wouldn't shine for one third of it, and the night in the same way.
013
I saw, and I heard an eagle, flying in mid heaven, saying with a loud voice, "Woe! Woe! Woe for those who dwell on the earth, because of the other voices of the trumpets of the three angels, who are yet to sound!"
