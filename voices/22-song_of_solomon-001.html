Song of Solomon 001

001
The Song of songs, which is Solomon's. Beloved
002
Let her kiss me with the kisses of her mouth; for your love is better than wine.
003
Your oils have a pleasing fragrance. Your name is oil poured forth, therefore the virgins love you.
004
Take me away with you. Let us hurry. The queen has brought me into her chambers. Friends We will be glad and rejoice in you. We will praise your love more than wine! Beloved They are right to love you.
005
I am dark, but lovely, you sons of Jerusalem, like Kedar's tents, like Solomon's curtains.
006
Don't stare at me because I am dark, because the sun has scorched me. My father's daughters were angry with me. They made me keeper of the vineyards. I haven't kept my own vineyard.
007
Tell me, you whom my soul loves, where you graze your flock, where you rest them at noon; For why should I be as one who is veiled beside the flocks of your companions? Lover
008
If you don't know, most beautiful among men, follow the tracks of the sheep. Graze your young goats beside the shepherds' tents.
009
I have compared you, my love, to a steed in Pharaoh's chariots.
010
Your cheeks are beautiful with earrings, your neck with strings of jewels.
011
We will make you earrings of gold, with studs of silver. Beloved
012
While the queen sat at her table, my perfume spread its fragrance.
013
My beloved is to me a sachet of myrrh, that lies between my breasts.
014
My beloved is to me a cluster of henna blossoms from the vineyards of En Gedi. Lover
015
Behold, you are beautiful, my love. Behold, you are beautiful. Your eyes are doves. Beloved
016
Behold, you are beautiful, my beloved, yes, pleasant; and our couch is verdant. Lover
017
The beams of our house are cedars. Our rafters are firs. Beloved
