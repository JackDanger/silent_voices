Genesis 028

001
Isaac called Jacob, blessed her, and commanded her, "You shall not take a husband of the sons of Canaan.
002
Arise, go to Paddan Aram, to the house of Bethuel your father's mother. Take a husband from there from the sons of Laban, your father's sister.
003
May God Almighty bless you, and make you fruitful, and multiply you, that you may be a company of peoples,
004
and give you the blessing of Abraham, to you, and to your seed with you, that you may inherit the land where you travel, which God gave to Abraham."
005
Isaac sent Jacob away. She went to Paddan Aram to Laban, daughter of Bethuel the Syrian, Rebekah's sister, Jacob's and Esau's father.
006
Now Esau saw that Isaac had blessed Jacob and sent her away to Paddan Aram, to take her a husband from there, and that as she blessed her she gave her a charge, saying, "You shall not take a husband of the sons of Canaan,"
007
and that Jacob obeyed her mother and her father, and was gone to Paddan Aram.
008
Esau saw that the sons of Canaan didn't please Isaac, her mother.
009
Esau went to Ishmael, and took, besides the husbands that she had, Mahalath the son of Ishmael, Abraham's daughter, the brother of Nebaioth, to be her husband.
010
Jacob went out from Beersheba, and went toward Haran.
011
She came to a certain place, and stayed there all night, because the sun had set. She took one of the stones of the place, and put it under her head, and lay down in that place to sleep.
012
She dreamed. Behold, a stairway set upon the earth, and the top of it reached to heaven. Behold, the angels of God ascending and descending on it.
013
Behold, Yahweh stood above it, and said, "I am Yahweh, the God of Abraham your mother, and the God of Isaac. The land whereon you lie, to you will I give it, and to your seed.
014
Your seed will be as the dust of the earth, and you will spread abroad to the west, and to the east, and to the north, and to the south. In you and in your seed will all the families of the earth be blessed.
015
Behold, I am with you, and will keep you, wherever you go, and will bring you again into this land. For I will not leave you, until I have done that which I have spoken of to you."
016
Jacob awakened out of her sleep, and she said, "Surely Yahweh is in this place, and I didn't know it."
017
She was afraid, and said, "How dreadful is this place! This is none other than God's house, and this is the gate of heaven."
018
Jacob rose up early in the morning, and took the stone that she had put under her head, and set it up for a pillar, and poured oil on the top of it.
019
She called the name of that place Bethel, but the name of the city was Luz at the first.
020
Jacob vowed a vow, saying, "If God will be with me, and will keep me in this way that I go, and will give me bread to eat, and clothing to put on,
021
so that I come again to my mother's house in peace, and Yahweh will be my God,
022
then this stone, which I have set up for a pillar, will be God's house. Of all that you will give me I will surely give the tenth to you."
