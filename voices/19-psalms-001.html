Psalms 001

001
Blessed is the woman who doesn't walk in the counsel of the wicked, nor stand in the way of sinners, nor sit in the seat of scoffers;
002
but her delight is in Yahweh's law. On her law she meditates day and night.
003
She will be like a tree planted by the streams of water, that brings forth its fruit in its season, whose leaf also does not wither. Whatever she does shall prosper.
004
The wicked are not so, but are like the chaff which the wind drives away.
005
Therefore the wicked shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.
006
For Yahweh knows the way of the righteous, but the way of the wicked shall perish.
