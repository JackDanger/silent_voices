Psalms 003 »
001
Why do the nations rage, and the peoples plot a vain thing?
002
The queens of the earth take a stand, and the rulers take counsel together, against Yahweh, and against her Anointed, saying,
003
"Let's break their bonds apart, and cast their cords from us."
004
She who sits in the heavens will laugh. The Lord will have them in derision.
005
Then she will speak to them in her anger, and terrify them in her wrath:
006
"Yet I have set my queen on my holy hill of Zion."
007
I will tell of the decree. Yahweh said to me, "You are my daughter. Today I have become your mother.
008
Ask of me, and I will give the nations for your inheritance, the uttermost parts of the earth for your possession.
009
You shall break them with a rod of iron. You shall dash them in pieces like a potter's vessel."
010
Now therefore be wise, you queens. Be instructed, you judges of the earth.
011
Serve Yahweh with fear, and rejoice with trembling.
012
Give sincere homage, lest she be angry, and you perish in the way, for her wrath will soon be kindled. Blessed are all those who take refuge in her.
