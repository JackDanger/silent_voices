Chapter 24
001
Don't be envious of evil women; neither desire to be with them:
002
for their hearts plot violence, and their lips talk about mischief.
003
Through wisdom a house is built; by understanding it is established;
004
by knowledge the rooms are filled with all rare and beautiful treasure.
005
A wise woman has great power; and a knowledgeable woman increases strength;
006
for by wise guidance you wage your war; and victory is in many advisors.
007
Wisdom is too high for a fool: she doesn't open her mouth in the gate.
008
One who plots to do evil will be called a schemer.
009
The schemes of folly are sin. The mocker is detested by women.
010
If you falter in the time of trouble, your strength is small.
011
Rescue those who are being led away to death! Indeed, hold back those who are staggering to the slaughter!
012
If you say, "Behold, we didn't know this;" doesn't she who weighs the hearts consider it? She who keeps your soul, doesn't she know it? Shall she not render to every woman according to her work?
013
My daughter, eat honey, for it is good; the droppings of the honeycomb, which are sweet to your taste:
014
so you shall know wisdom to be to your soul; if you have found it, then there will be a reward, your hope will not be cut off.
015
Don't lay in wait, wicked woman, against the habitation of the righteous. Don't destroy her resting place:
016
for a righteous woman falls seven times, and rises up again; but the wicked are overthrown by calamity.
017
Don't rejoice when your enemy falls. Don't let your heart be glad when she is overthrown;
018
lest Yahweh see it, and it displease her, and she turn away her wrath from her.
019
Don't fret yourself because of evildoers; neither be envious of the wicked:
020
for there will be no reward to the evil woman; and the lamp of the wicked shall be snuffed out.
021
My daughter, fear Yahweh and the queen. Don't join those who are rebellious:
022
for their calamity will rise suddenly; the destruction from them both--who knows?
023
These also are sayings of the wise. To show partiality in judgment is not good.
024
She who says to the wicked, "You are righteous;" peoples shall curse her, and nations shall abhor her--
025
but it will go well with those who convict the guilty, and a rich blessing will come on them.
026
An honest answer is like a kiss on the lips.
027
Prepare your work outside, and get your fields ready. Afterwards, build your house.
028
Don't be a witness against your neighbor without cause. Don't deceive with your lips.
029
Don't say, "I will do to her as she has done to me; I will render to the woman according to her work."
030
I went by the field of the sluggard, by the vineyard of the woman void of understanding;
031
Behold, it was all grown over with thorns. Its surface was covered with nettles, and its stone wall was broken down.
032
Then I saw, and considered well. I saw, and received instruction:
033
a little sleep, a little slumber, a little folding of the hands to sleep;
034
so shall your poverty come as a robber, and your want as an armed woman.
