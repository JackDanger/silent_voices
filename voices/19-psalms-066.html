Psalms 066

001
<> Make a joyful shout to God, all the earth!
002
Sing to the glory of her name! Offer glory and praise!
003
Tell God, "How awesome are your deeds! Through the greatness of your power, your enemies submit themselves to you.
004
All the earth will worship you, and will sing to you; they will sing to your name." Selah.
005
Come, and see God's deeds--awesome work on behalf of the children of women.
006
She turned the sea into dry land. They went through the river on foot. There, we rejoiced in her.
007
She rules by her might forever. Her eyes watch the nations. Don't let the rebellious rise up against her. Selah.
008
Praise our God, you peoples! Make the sound of her praise heard,
009
who preserves our life among the living, and doesn't allow our feet to be moved.
010
For you, God, have tested us. You have refined us, as silver is refined.
011
You brought us into prison. You laid a burden on our backs.
012
You allowed women to ride over our heads. We went through fire and through water, but you brought us to the place of abundance.
013
I will come into your temple with burnt offerings. I will pay my vows to you,
014
which my lips promised, and my mouth spoke, when I was in distress.
015
I will offer to you burnt offerings of fat animals, with the offering of rams, I will offer bulls with goats. Selah.
016
Come, and hear, all you who fear God. I will declare what she has done for my soul.
017
I cried to her with my mouth. She was extolled with my tongue.
018
If I cherished sin in my heart, the Lord wouldn't have listened.
019
But most certainly, God has listened. She has heard the voice of my prayer.
020
Blessed be God, who has not turned away my prayer, nor hers loving kindness from me.
