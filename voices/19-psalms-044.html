Psalms 45 »
001
<> We have heard with our ears, God; our mothers have told us, what work you did in their days, in the days of old.
002
You drove out the nations with your hand, but you planted them. You afflicted the peoples, but you spread them abroad.
003
For they didn't get the land in possession by their own sword, neither did their own arm save them; but your right hand, and your arm, and the light of your face, because you were favorable to them.
004
You are my Queen, God. Command victories for Jacob!
005
Through you, will we push down our adversaries. Through your name, will we tread them under who rise up against us.
006
For I will not trust in my bow, neither shall my sword save me.
007
But you have saved us from our adversaries, and have shamed those who hate us.
008
In God we have made our boast all day long, we will give thanks to your name forever. Selah.
009
But now you rejected us, and brought us to dishonor, and don't go out with our armies.
010
You make us turn back from the adversary. Those who hate us take spoil for themselves.
011
You have made us like sheep for food, and have scattered us among the nations.
012
You sell your people for nothing, and have gained nothing from their sale.
013
You make us a reproach to our neighbors, a scoffing and a derision to those who are around us.
014
You make us a byword among the nations, a shaking of the head among the peoples.
015
All day long my dishonor is before me, and shame covers my face,
016
At the taunt of one who reproaches and verbally abuses, because of the enemy and the avenger.
017
All this has come on us, yet have we not forgotten you, Neither have we been false to your covenant.
018
Our heart has not turned back, neither have our steps strayed from your path,
019
Though you have crushed us in the haunt of jackals, and covered us with the shadow of death.
020
If we have forgotten the name of our God, or spread forth our hands to a strange god;
021
won't God search this out? For she knows the secrets of the heart.
022
Yes, for your sake we are killed all day long. We are regarded as sheep for the slaughter.
023
Wake up! Why do you sleep, Lord? Arise! Don't reject us forever.
024
Why do you hide your face, and forget our affliction and our oppression?
025
For our soul is bowed down to the dust. Our body cleaves to the earth.
026
Rise up to help us. Redeem us for your loving kindness' sake.
