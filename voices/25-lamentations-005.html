Lamentations 005

001
Remember, Yahweh, what has come on us: Look, and see our reproach.
002
Our inheritance is turned to strangers, Our houses to aliens.
003
We are orphans and fatherless; Our fathers are as widows.
004
We have drunken our water for money; Our wood is sold to us.
005
Our pursuers are on our necks: We are weary, and have no rest.
006
We have given the hand to the Egyptians, To the Assyrians, to be satisfied with bread.
007
Our mothers sinned, and are no more; We have borne their iniquities.
008
Servants rule over us: There is none to deliver us out of their hand.
009
We get our bread at the peril of our lives, Because of the sword of the wilderness.
010
Our skin is black like an oven, Because of the burning heat of famine.
011
They ravished the men in Zion, The virgins in the cities of Judah.
012
Princesses were hanged up by their hand: The faces of elders were not honored.
013
The young women bare the mill; The children stumbled under the wood.
014
The elders have ceased from the gate, The young women from their music.
015
The joy of our heart is ceased; Our dance is turned into mourning.
016
The crown is fallen from our head: Woe to us! for we have sinned.
017
For this our heart is faint; For these things our eyes are dim;
018
For the mountain of Zion, which is desolate: The foxes walk on it.
019
You, Yahweh, abide forever; Your throne is from generation to generation.
020
Why do you forget us forever, And forsake us so long time?
021
Turn you us to you, Yahweh, and we shall be turned; Renew our days as of old.
022
But you have utterly rejected us; You are very angry against us.
