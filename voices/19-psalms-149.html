Psalms 149

001
Praise Yahweh! Sing to Yahweh a new song, her praise in the assembly of the saints.
002
Let Israel rejoice in her who made them. Let the children of Zion be joyful in their Queen.
003
Let them praise her name in the dance! Let them sing praises to her with tambourine and harp!
004
For Yahweh takes pleasure in her people. She crowns the humble with salvation.
005
Let the saints rejoice in honor. Let them sing for joy on their beds.
006
May the high praises of God be in their mouths, and a two-edged sword in their hand;
007
To execute vengeance on the nations, and punishments on the peoples;
008
To bind their queens with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron;
009
to execute on them the written judgment. All her saints have this honor. Praise Yah!
