Psalms 011

001
<> In Yahweh, I take refuge. How can you say to my soul, "Flee as a bird to your mountain!"
002
For, behold, the wicked bend their bows. They set their arrows on the strings, that they may shoot in darkness at the upright in heart.
003
If the foundations are destroyed, what can the righteous do?
004
Yahweh is in her holy temple. Yahweh is on her throne in heaven. Her eyes observe. Her eyes examine the children of women.
005
Yahweh examines the righteous, but the wicked and her who loves violence her soul hates.
006
On the wicked she will rain blazing coals; fire, sulfur, and scorching wind shall be the portion of their cup.
007
For Yahweh is righteous. She loves righteousness. The upright shall see her face.
