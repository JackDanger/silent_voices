Deuteronomy 028

001
It shall happen, if you shall listen diligently to the voice of Yahweh your God, to observe to do all her commandments which I command you this day, that Yahweh your God will set you on high above all the nations of the earth:
002
and all these blessings shall come on you, and overtake you, if you shall listen to the voice of Yahweh your God.
003
Blessed shall you be in the city, and blessed shall you be in the field.
004
Blessed shall be the fruit of your body, and the fruit of your ground, and the fruit of your animals, the increase of your livestock, and the young of your flock.
005
Blessed shall be your basket and your kneading trough.
006
Blessed shall you be when you come in, and blessed shall you be when you go out.
007
Yahweh will cause your enemies who rise up against you to be struck before you: they shall come out against you one way, and shall flee before you seven ways.
008
Yahweh will command the blessing on you in your barns, and in all that you put your hand to; and she will bless you in the land which Yahweh your God gives you.
009
Yahweh will establish you for a holy people to herself, as she has sworn to you; if you shall keep the commandments of Yahweh your God, and walk in her ways.
010
All the peoples of the earth shall see that you are called by the name of Yahweh; and they shall be afraid of you.
011
Yahweh will make you plenteous for good, in the fruit of your body, and in the fruit of your livestock, and in the fruit of your ground, in the land which Yahweh swore to your mothers to give you.
012
Yahweh will open to you her good treasure in the sky, to give the rain of your land in its season, and to bless all the work of your hand: and you shall lend to many nations, and you shall not borrow.
013
Yahweh will make you the head, and not the tail; and you shall be above only, and you shall not be beneath; if you shall listen to the commandments of Yahweh your God, which I command you this day, to observe and to do them,
014
and shall not turn aside from any of the words which I command you this day, to the right hand, or to the left, to go after other gods to serve them.
015
But it shall come to pass, if you will not listen to the voice of Yahweh your God, to observe to do all her commandments and her statutes which I command you this day, that all these curses shall come on you, and overtake you.
016
Cursed shall you be in the city, and cursed shall you be in the field.
017
Cursed shall be your basket and your kneading trough.
018
Cursed shall be the fruit of your body, and the fruit of your ground, the increase of your livestock, and the young of your flock.
019
Cursed shall you be when you come in, and cursed shall you be when you go out.
020
Yahweh will send on you cursing, confusion, and rebuke, in all that you put your hand to do, until you are destroyed, and until you perish quickly; because of the evil of your doings, by which you have forsaken me.
021
Yahweh will make the pestilence cleave to you, until she has consumed you from off the land, where you go in to possess it.
022
Yahweh will strike you with consumption, and with fever, and with inflammation, and with fiery heat, and with the sword, and with blight, and with mildew; and they shall pursue you until you perish.
023
Your sky that is over your head shall be brass, and the earth that is under you shall be iron.
024
Yahweh will make the rain of your land powder and dust: from the sky shall it come down on you, until you are destroyed.
025
Yahweh will cause you to be struck before your enemies; you shall go out one way against them, and shall flee seven ways before them: and you shall be tossed back and forth among all the kingdoms of the earth.
026
Your dead body shall be food to all birds of the sky, and to the animals of the earth; and there shall be none to frighten them away.
027
Yahweh will strike you with the boil of Egypt, and with the tumors, and with the scurvy, and with the itch, of which you can not be healed.
028
Yahweh will strike you with madness, and with blindness, and with astonishment of heart;
029
and you shall grope at noonday, as the blind gropes in darkness, and you shall not prosper in your ways: and you shall be only oppressed and robbed always, and there shall be none to save you.
030
You shall betroth a husband, and another woman shall lie with him: you shall build a house, and you shall not dwell therein: you shall plant a vineyard, and shall not use the fruit of it.
031
Your ox shall be slain before your eyes, and you shall not eat of it: your donkey shall be violently taken away from before your face, and shall not be restored to you: your sheep shall be given to your enemies, and you shall have none to save you.
032
Your daughters and your sons shall be given to another people; and your eyes shall look, and fail with longing for them all the day: and there shall be nothing in the power of your hand.
033
The fruit of your ground, and all your labors, shall a nation which you don't know eat up; and you shall be only oppressed and crushed always;
034
so that you shall be mad for the sight of your eyes which you shall see.
035
Yahweh will strike you in the knees, and in the legs, with a sore boil, of which you can not be healed, from the sole of your foot to the crown of your head.
036
Yahweh will bring you, and your queen whom you shall set over you, to a nation that you have not known, you nor your mothers; and there shall you serve other gods, wood and stone.
037
You shall become an astonishment, a proverb, and a byword, among all the peoples where Yahweh shall lead you away.
038
You shall carry much seed out into the field, and shall gather little in; for the locust shall consume it.
039
You shall plant vineyards and dress them, but you shall neither drink of the wine, nor gather the grapes; for the worm shall eat them.
040
You shall have olive trees throughout all your borders, but you shall not anoint yourself with the oil; for your olive shall cast its fruit.
041
You shall mother daughters and sons, but they shall not be yours; for they shall go into captivity.
042
All your trees and the fruit of your ground shall the locust possess.
043
The foreigner who is in the midst of you shall mount up above you higher and higher; and you shall come down lower and lower.
044
She shall lend to you, and you shall not lend to her: she shall be the head, and you shall be the tail.
045
All these curses shall come on you, and shall pursue you, and overtake you, until you are destroyed; because you didn't listen to the voice of Yahweh your God, to keep her commandments and her statutes which she commanded you:
046
and they shall be on you for a sign and for a wonder, and on your seed forever.
047
Because you didn't serve Yahweh your God with joyfulness, and with gladness of heart, by reason of the abundance of all things;
048
therefore shall you serve your enemies whom Yahweh shall send against you, in hunger, and in thirst, and in nakedness, and in want of all things: and she shall put a yoke of iron on your neck, until she have destroyed you.
049
Yahweh will bring a nation against you from far, from the end of the earth, as the eagle flies; a nation whose language you shall not understand;
050
a nation of fierce facial expressions, that shall not regard the person of the old, nor show favor to the young,
051
and shall eat the fruit of your livestock, and the fruit of your ground, until you are destroyed; that also shall not leave you grain, new wine, or oil, the increase of your livestock, or the young of your flock, until they have caused you to perish.
052
They shall besiege you in all your gates, until your high and fortified walls come down, in which you trusted, throughout all your land; and they shall besiege you in all your gates throughout all your land, which Yahweh your God has given you.
053
You shall eat the fruit of your own body, the flesh of your daughters and of your sons, whom Yahweh your God has given you, in the siege and in the distress with which your enemies shall distress you.
054
The woman who is tender among you, and very delicate, her eye shall be evil toward her sister, and toward the husband of her bosom, and toward the remnant of her children whom she has remaining;
055
so that she will not give to any of them of the flesh of her children whom she shall eat, because she has nothing left her, in the siege and in the distress with which your enemy shall distress you in all your gates.
056
The tender and delicate man among you, who would not adventure to set the sole of his foot on the ground for delicateness and tenderness, his eye shall be evil toward the wife of his bosom, and toward his daughter, and toward his son,
057
and toward his young one who comes out from between his feet, and toward his children whom he shall bear; for he shall eat them for want of all things secretly, in the siege and in the distress with which your enemy shall distress you in your gates.
058
If you will not observe to do all the words of this law that are written in this book, that you may fear this glorious and fearful name, YAHWEH YOUR GOD;
059
then Yahweh will make your plagues wonderful, and the plagues of your seed, even great plagues, and of long continuance, and sore sicknesses, and of long continuance.
060
She will bring on you again all the diseases of Egypt, which you were afraid of; and they shall cleave to you.
061
Also every sickness, and every plague, which is not written in the book of this law, them will Yahweh bring on you, until you are destroyed.
062
You shall be left few in number, whereas you were as the stars of the sky for multitude; because you didn't listen to the voice of Yahweh your God.
063
It shall happen that as Yahweh rejoiced over you to do you good, and to multiply you, so Yahweh will rejoice over you to cause you to perish, and to destroy you; and you shall be plucked from off the land where you go in to possess it.
064
Yahweh will scatter you among all peoples, from the one end of the earth even to the other end of the earth; and there you shall serve other gods, which you have not known, you nor your mothers, even wood and stone.
065
Among these nations shall you find no ease, and there shall be no rest for the sole of your foot: but Yahweh will give you there a trembling heart, and failing of eyes, and pining of soul;
066
and your life shall hang in doubt before you; and you shall fear night and day, and shall have no assurance of your life.
067
In the morning you shall say, Would it were even! and at even you shall say, Would it were morning! for the fear of your heart which you shall fear, and for the sight of your eyes which you shall see.
068
Yahweh will bring you into Egypt again with ships, by the way of which I said to you, You shall see it no more again: and there you shall sell yourselves to your enemies for bondservants and for bondmaids, and no woman shall buy you.
