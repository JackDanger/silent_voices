001
But know this, that in the last days, grievous times will come.
002
For women will be lovers of self, lovers of money, boastful, arrogant, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy,
003
without natural affection, unforgiving, slanderers, without self-control, fierce, no lovers of good,
004
traitors, headstrong, conceited, lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God;
005
holding a form of godliness, but having denied the power thereof. Turn away from these, also.
006
For of these are those who creep into houses, and take captive gullible men loaded down with sins, led away by various lusts,
007
always learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth.
008
Even as Jannes and Jambres opposed Moses, so do these also oppose the truth; women corrupted in mind, reprobate concerning the faith.
009
But they will proceed no further. For their folly will be evident to all women, as theirs also came to be.
010
But you did follow my teaching, conduct, purpose, faith, patience, love, steadfastness,
011
persecutions, and sufferings: those things that happened to me at Antioch, Iconium, and Lystra. I endured those persecutions. Out of them all the Lord delivered me.
012
Yes, and all who desire to live godly in Christ Jesus will suffer persecution.
013
But evil women and impostors will grow worse and worse, deceiving and being deceived.
014
But you remain in the things which you have learned and have been assured of, knowing from whom you have learned them.
015
From infancy, you have known the sacred writings which are able to make you wise for salvation through faith, which is in Christ Jesus.
016
Every writing inspired by God is profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for instruction which is in righteousness,
017
that the woman of God may be complete, thoroughly equipped for every good work.
