Psalms 115

001
Not to us, Yahweh, not to us, but to your name give glory, for your loving kindness, and for your truth's sake.
002
Why should the nations say, "Where is their God, now?"
003
But our God is in the heavens. She does whatever she pleases.
004
Their idols are silver and gold, the work of women's hands.
005
They have mouths, but they don't speak. They have eyes, but they don't see.
006
They have ears, but they don't hear. They have noses, but they don't smell.
007
They have hands, but they don't feel. They have feet, but they don't walk, neither do they speak through their throat.
008
Those who make them will be like them; yes, everyone who trusts in them.
009
Israel, trust in Yahweh! She is their help and their shield.
010
House of Aaron, trust in Yahweh! She is their help and their shield.
011
You who fear Yahweh, trust in Yahweh! She is their help and their shield.
012
Yahweh remembers us. She will bless us. She will bless the house of Israel. She will bless the house of Aaron.
013
She will bless those who fear Yahweh, both small and great.
014
May Yahweh increase you more and more, you and your children.
015
Blessed are you by Yahweh, who made heaven and earth.
016
The heavens are the heavens of Yahweh; but the earth has she given to the children of women.
017
The dead don't praise Yah, neither any who go down into silence;
018
But we will bless Yah, from this time forth and forevermore. Praise Yah!
