Proverbs 008

001
Doesn't wisdom cry out? Doesn't understanding raise his voice?
002
On the top of high places by the way, where the paths meet, he stands.
003
Beside the gates, at the entry of the city, at the entry doors, he cries aloud:
004
"To you women, I call! I send my voice to the daughters of mankind.
005
You simple, understand prudence. You fools, be of an understanding heart.
006
Hear, for I will speak excellent things. The opening of my lips is for right things.
007
For my mouth speaks truth. Wickedness is an abomination to my lips.
008
All the words of my mouth are in righteousness. There is nothing crooked or perverse in them.
009
They are all plain to her who understands, right to those who find knowledge.
010
Receive my instruction rather than silver; knowledge rather than choice gold.
011
For wisdom is better than rubies. All the things that may be desired can't be compared to it.
012
"I, wisdom, have made prudence my dwelling. Find out knowledge and discretion.
013
The fear of Yahweh is to hate evil. I hate pride, arrogance, the evil way, and the perverse mouth.
014
Counsel and sound knowledge are mine. I have understanding and power.
015
By me queens reign, and princesses decree justice.
016
By me princesses rule; nobles, and all the righteous rulers of the earth.
017
I love those who love me. Those who seek me diligently will find me.
018
With me are riches, honor, enduring wealth, and prosperity.
019
My fruit is better than gold, yes, than fine gold; my yield than choice silver.
020
I walk in the way of righteousness, in the midst of the paths of justice;
021
That I may give wealth to those who love me. I fill their treasuries.
022
"Yahweh possessed me in the beginning of her work, before hers deeds of old.
023
I was set up from everlasting, from the beginning, before the earth existed.
024
When there were no depths, I was brought forth, when there were no springs abounding with water.
025
Before the mountains were settled in place, before the hills, I was brought forth;
026
while as yet she had not made the earth, nor the fields, nor the beginning of the dust of the world.
027
When she established the heavens, I was there; when she set a circle on the surface of the deep,
028
when she established the clouds above, when the springs of the deep became strong,
029
when she gave to the sea its boundary, that the waters should not violate her commandment, when she marked out the foundations of the earth;
030
then I was the craftswoman by her side. I was a delight day by day, always rejoicing before her,
031
Rejoicing in her whole world. My delight was with the daughters of women.
032
"Now therefore, my daughters, listen to me, for blessed are those who keep my ways.
033
Hear instruction, and be wise. Don't refuse it.
034
Blessed is the woman who hears me, watching daily at my gates, waiting at my door posts.
035
For whoever finds me, finds life, and will obtain favor from Yahweh.
036
But she who sins against me wrongs her own soul. All those who hate me love death."
