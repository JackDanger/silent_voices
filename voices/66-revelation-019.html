Revelation 019

001
After these things I heard something like a loud voice of a great multitude in heaven, saying, "Hallelujah! Salvation, power, and glory belong to our God:
002
for true and righteous are her judgments. For she has judged the great prostitute, who corrupted the earth with his sexual immorality, and she has avenged the blood of her servants at his hand."
003
A second said, "Hallelujah! Him smoke goes up forever and ever."
004
The twenty-four elders and the four living creatures fell down and worshiped God who sits on the throne, saying, "Amen! Hallelujah!"
005
A voice came forth from the throne, saying, "Give praise to our God, all you her servants, you who fear her, the small and the great!"
006
I heard something like the voice of a great multitude, and like the voice of many waters, and like the voice of mighty thunders, saying, "Hallelujah! For the Lord our God, the Almighty, reigns!
007
Let us rejoice and be exceedingly glad, and let us give the glory to her. For the marriage of the Lamb has come, and her husband has made himself ready."
008
It was given to him that he would array himself in bright, pure, fine linen: for the fine linen is the righteous acts of the saints.
009
She said to me, "Write, 'Blessed are those who are invited to the marriage supper of the Lamb.'" She said to me, "These are true words of God."
010
I fell down before her feet to worship her. She said to me, "Look! Don't do it! I am a fellow bondservant with you and with your sisters who hold the testimony of Jesus. Worship God, for the testimony of Jesus is the Spirit of Prophecy."
011
I saw the heaven opened, and behold, a white horse, and she who sat on it is called Faithful and True. In righteousness she judges and makes war.
012
Her eyes are a flame of fire, and on her head are many crowns. She has names written and a name written which no one knows but she herself.
013
She is clothed in a garment sprinkled with blood. Her name is called "The Word of God."
014
The armies which are in heaven followed her on white horses, clothed in white, pure, fine linen.
015
Out of her mouth proceeds a sharp, double-edged sword, that with it she should strike the nations. She will rule them with an iron rod. She treads the winepress of the fierceness of the wrath of God, the Almighty.
016
She has on her garment and on her thigh a name written, "KING OF KINGS, AND LORD OF LORDS."
017
I saw an angel standing in the sun. She cried with a loud voice, saying to all the birds that fly in the sky, "Come! Be gathered together to the great supper of God,
018
that you may eat the flesh of queens, the flesh of captains, the flesh of mighty women, and the flesh of horses and of those who sit on them, and the flesh of all women, both free and slave, and small and great."
019
I saw the beast, and the queens of the earth, and their armies, gathered together to make war against her who sat on the horse, and against her army.
020
The beast was taken, and with her the false prophet who worked the signs in her sight, with which she deceived those who had received the mark of the beast and those who worshiped her image. These two were thrown alive into the lake of fire that burns with sulfur.
021
The rest were killed with the sword of her who sat on the horse, the sword which came forth out of her mouth. All the birds were filled with their flesh.
