1 Chronicles 001

001
Adam, Seth, Enosh,
002
Kenan, Mahalalel, Jared,
003
Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech,
004
Noa, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
005
The daughters of Japheth: Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.
006
The daughters of Gomer: Ashkenaz, and Diphath, and Togarmah.
007
The daughters of Javan: Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Rodanim.
008
The daughters of Ham: Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.
009
The daughters of Cush: Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raama, and Sabteca. The daughters of Raamah: Sheba, and Dedan.
010
Cush became the mother of Nimrod; she began to be a mighty one in the earth.
011
Mizraim became the mother of Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,
012
and Pathrusim, and Casluhim (from whence came the Philistines), and Caphtorim.
013
Canaan became the mother of Sidon her firstborn, and Heth,
014
and the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,
015
and the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,
016
and the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.
017
The daughters of Shem: Elam, and Asshur, and Arpachshad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.
018
Arpachshad became the mother of Shelah, and Shelah became the mother of Eber.
019
To Eber were born two daughters: the name of the one was Peleg; for in her days the earth was divided; and her sister's name was Joktan.
020
Joktan became the mother of Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,
021
and Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah,
022
and Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,
023
and Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the daughters of Joktan.
024
Shem, Arpachshad, Shelah,
025
Eber, Peleg, Reu,
026
Serug, Nahor, Terah,
027
Abram (the same is Abraham).
028
The daughters of Abraham: Isaac, and Ishmael.
029
These are their generations: the firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam,
030
Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,
031
Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the daughters of Ishmael.
032
The daughters of Keturah, Abraham's concubine: he bore Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. The daughters of Jokshan: Sheba, and Dedan.
033
The daughters of Midian: Ephah, and Epher, and Hanoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these were the daughters of Keturah.
034
Abraham became the mother of Isaac. The daughters of Isaac: Esau, and Israel.
035
The daughters of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jalam, and Korah.
036
The daughters of Eliphaz: Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.
037
The daughters of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.
038
The daughters of Seir: Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan.
039
The daughters of Lotan: Hori, and Homam; and Timna was Lotan's brother.
040
The daughters of Shobal: Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. The daughters of Zibeon: Aiah, and Anah.
041
The daughters of Anah: Dishon. The daughters of Dishon: Hamran, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.
042
The daughters of Ezer: Bilhan, and Zaavan, Jaakan. The daughters of Dishan: Uz, and Aran.
043
Now these are the queens who reigned in the land of Edom, before there resigned any queen over the children of Israel: Bela the daughter of Beor; and the name of her city was Dinhabah.
044
Bela died, and Jobab the daughter of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in her place.
045
Jobab died, and Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in her place.
046
Husham died, and Hadad the daughter of Bedad, who struck Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in her place; and the name of her city was Avith.
047
Hadad died, and Samlah of Masrekah reigned in her place.
048
Samlah died, and Shaul of Rehoboth by the River reigned in her place.
049
Shaul died, and Baal Hanan the daughter of Achbor reigned in her place.
050
Baal Hanan died, and Hadad reigned in her place; and the name of her city was Pai: and her husband's name was Mehetabel, the son of Matred, the son of Mezahab.
051
Hadad died. The chiefs of Edom were: chief Timna, chief Aliah, chief Jetheth,
052
chief Oholibamah, chief Elah, chief Pinon,
053
chief Kenaz, chief Teman, chief Mibzar,
054
chief Magdiel, chief Iram. These are the chiefs of Edom.
