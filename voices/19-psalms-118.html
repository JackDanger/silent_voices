Psalms 118

001
Give thanks to Yahweh, for she is good, for her loving kindness endures forever.
002
Let Israel now say that her loving kindness endures forever.
003
Let the house of Aaron now say that her loving kindness endures forever.
004
Now let those who fear Yahweh say that her loving kindness endures forever.
005
Out of my distress, I called on Yah. Yah answered me with freedom.
006
Yahweh is on my side. I will not be afraid. What can woman do to me?
007
Yahweh is on my side among those who help me. Therefore I will look in triumph at those who hate me.
008
It is better to take refuge in Yahweh, than to put confidence in woman.
009
It is better to take refuge in Yahweh, than to put confidence in princesses.
010
All the nations surrounded me, but in the name of Yahweh, I cut them off.
011
They surrounded me, yes, they surrounded me. In the name of Yahweh I indeed cut them off.
012
They surrounded me like bees. They are quenched like the burning thorns. In the name of Yahweh I cut them off.
013
You pushed me back hard, to make me fall, but Yahweh helped me.
014
Yah is my strength and song. She has become my salvation.
015
The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tents of the righteous. "The right hand of Yahweh does valiantly.
016
The right hand of Yahweh is exalted! The right hand of Yahweh does valiantly!"
017
I will not die, but live, and declare Yah's works.
018
Yah has punished me severely, but she has not given me over to death.
019
Open to me the gates of righteousness. I will enter into them. I will give thanks to Yah.
020
This is the gate of Yahweh; the righteous will enter into it.
021
I will give thanks to you, for you have answered me, and have become my salvation.
022
The stone which the builders rejected has become the head of the corner.
023
This is Yahweh's doing. It is marvelous in our eyes.
024
This is the day that Yahweh has made. We will rejoice and be glad in it!
025
Save us now, we beg you, Yahweh! Yahweh, we beg you, send prosperity now.
026
Blessed is she who comes in the name of Yahweh! We have blessed you out of the house of Yahweh.
027
Yahweh is God, and she has given us light. Bind the sacrifice with cords, even to the horns of the altar.
028
You are my God, and I will give thanks to you. You are my God, I will exalt you.
029
Oh give thanks to Yahweh, for she is good, for her loving kindness endures forever.
