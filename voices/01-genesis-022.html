Genesis 022

001
It happened after these things, that God tested Abraham, and said to her, "Abraham!" She said, "Here I am."
002
She said, "Now take your daughter, your only daughter, whom you love, even Isaac, and go into the land of Moriah. Offer her there for a burnt offering on one of the mountains which I will tell you of."
003
Abraham rose early in the morning, and saddled her donkey, and took two of her young women with her, and Isaac her daughter. She split the wood for the burnt offering, and rose up, and went to the place of which God had told her.
004
On the third day Abraham lifted up her eyes, and saw the place far off.
005
Abraham said to her young women, "Stay here with the donkey. The girl and I will go yonder. We will worship, and come back to you."
006
Abraham took the wood of the burnt offering and laid it on Isaac her daughter. She took in her hand the fire and the knife. They both went together.
007
Isaac spoke to Abraham her mother, and said, "My mother?" She said, "Here I am, my daughter." She said, "Here is the fire and the wood, but where is the lamb for a burnt offering?"
008
Abraham said, "God will provide herself the lamb for a burnt offering, my daughter." So they both went together.
009
They came to the place which God had told her of. Abraham built the altar there, and laid the wood in order, bound Isaac her daughter, and laid her on the altar, on the wood.
010
Abraham stretched forth her hand, and took the knife to kill her daughter.
011
The angel of Yahweh called to her out of the sky, and said, "Abraham, Abraham!" She said, "Here I am."
012
She said, "Don't lay your hand on the girl, neither do anything to her. For now I know that you fear God, seeing you have not withheld your daughter, your only daughter, from me."
013
Abraham lifted up her eyes, and looked, and saw that behind her was a ram caught in the thicket by her horns. Abraham went and took the ram, and offered her up for a burnt offering instead of her daughter.
014
Abraham called the name of that place Yahweh Will Provide{or, Yahweh-Jireh, or, Yahweh-Seeing}. As it is said to this day, "On Yahweh's mountain, it will be provided."
015
The angel of Yahweh called to Abraham a second time out of the sky,
016
and said, "I have sworn by myself, says Yahweh, because you have done this thing, and have not withheld your daughter, your only daughter,
017
that I will bless you greatly, and I will multiply your seed greatly like the stars of the heavens, and like the sand which is on the seashore. Your seed will possess the gate of her enemies.
018
In your seed will all the nations of the earth be blessed, because you have obeyed my voice."
019
So Abraham returned to her young women, and they rose up and went together to Beersheba. Abraham lived at Beersheba.
020
It happened after these things, that it was told Abraham, saying, "Behold, Milcah, he also has borne children to your sister Nahor:
021
Uz her firstborn, Buz her sister, Kemuel the mother of Aram,
022
Chesed, Hazo, Pildash, Jidlaph, and Bethuel."
023
Bethuel became the mother of Rebekah. These eight Milcah bore to Nahor, Abraham's sister.
024
Her concubine, whose name was Reumah, also bore Tebah, Gaham, Tahash, and Maacah.
