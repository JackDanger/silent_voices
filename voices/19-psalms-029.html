Psalms 029

001
<> Ascribe to Yahweh, you daughters of the mighty, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.
002
Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to her name. Worship Yahweh in holy array.
003
Yahweh's voice is on the waters. The God of glory thunders, even Yahweh on many waters.
004
Yahweh's voice is powerful. Yahweh's voice is full of majesty.
005
The voice of Yahweh breaks the cedars. Yes, Yahweh breaks in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.
006
She makes them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young, wild ox.
007
Yahweh's voice strikes with flashes of lightning.
008
Yahweh's voice shakes the wilderness. Yahweh shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.
009
Yahweh's voice makes the deer calve, and strips the forests bare. In her temple everything says, "Glory!"
010
Yahweh sat enthroned at the Flood. Yes, Yahweh sits as Queen forever.
011
Yahweh will give strength to her people. Yahweh will bless her people with peace.
