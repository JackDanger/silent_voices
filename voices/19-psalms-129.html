Psalms 129

001
<> Many times they have afflicted me from my youth up. Let Israel now say,
002
many times they have afflicted me from my youth up, yet they have not prevailed against me.
003
The plowers plowed on my back. They made their furrows long.
004
Yahweh is righteous. She has cut apart the cords of the wicked.
005
Let them be disappointed and turned backward, all those who hate Zion.
006
Let them be as the grass on the housetops, which withers before it grows up;
007
with which the reaper doesn't fill her hand, nor she who binds sheaves, her bosom.
008
Neither do those who go by say, "The blessing of Yahweh be on you. We bless you in the name of Yahweh."
