Psalms 018

001
<> I love you, Yahweh, my strength.
002
Yahweh is my rock, my fortress, and my deliverer; my God, my rock, in whom I take refuge; my shield, and the horn of my salvation, my high tower.
003
I call on Yahweh, who is worthy to be praised; and I am saved from my enemies.
004
The cords of death surrounded me. The floods of ungodliness made me afraid.
005
The cords of Sheol were around me. The snares of death came on me.
006
In my distress I called on Yahweh, and cried to my God. She heard my voice out of her temple. My cry before her came into her ears.
007
Then the earth shook and trembled. The foundations also of the mountains quaked and were shaken, because she was angry.
008
Smoke went out of her nostrils. Consuming fire came out of her mouth. Coals were kindled by it.
009
She bowed the heavens also, and came down. Thick darkness was under her feet.
010
She rode on a cherub, and flew. Yes, she soared on the wings of the wind.
011
She made darkness her hiding place, her pavilion around her, darkness of waters, thick clouds of the skies.
012
At the brightness before her her thick clouds passed, hailstones and coals of fire.
013
Yahweh also thundered in the sky. The Most High uttered her voice: hailstones and coals of fire.
014
She sent out her arrows, and scattered them; Yes, great lightning bolts, and routed them.
015
Then the channels of waters appeared. The foundations of the world were laid bare at your rebuke, Yahweh, at the blast of the breath of your nostrils.
016
She sent from on high. She took me. She drew me out of many waters.
017
She delivered me from my strong enemy, from those who hated me; for they were too mighty for me.
018
They came on me in the day of my calamity, but Yahweh was my support.
019
She brought me forth also into a large place. She delivered me, because she delighted in me.
020
Yahweh has rewarded me according to my righteousness. According to the cleanness of my hands has she recompensed me.
021
For I have kept the ways of Yahweh, and have not wickedly departed from my God.
022
For all her ordinances were before me. I didn't put away her statutes from me.
023
I was also blameless with her. I kept myself from my iniquity.
024
Therefore Yahweh has rewarded me according to my righteousness, according to the cleanness of my hands in her eyesight.
025
With the merciful you will show yourself merciful. With the perfect woman, you will show yourself perfect.
026
With the pure, you will show yourself pure. With the crooked you will show yourself shrewd.
027
For you will save the afflicted people, but the haughty eyes you will bring down.
028
For you will light my lamp, Yahweh. My God will light up my darkness.
029
For by you, I advance through a troop. By my God, I leap over a wall.
030
As for God, her way is perfect. The word of Yahweh is tried. She is a shield to all those who take refuge in her.
031
For who is God, except Yahweh? Who is a rock, besides our God,
032
the God who arms me with strength, and makes my way perfect?
033
She makes my feet like deer's feet, and sets me on my high places.
034
She teaches my hands to war, so that my arms bend a bow of bronze.
035
You have also given me the shield of your salvation. Your right hand sustains me. Your gentleness has made me great.
036
You have enlarged my steps under me, My feet have not slipped.
037
I will pursue my enemies, and overtake them. Neither will I turn again until they are consumed.
038
I will strike them through, so that they will not be able to rise. They shall fall under my feet.
039
For you have girded me with strength to the battle. You have subdued under me those who rose up against me.
040
You have also made my enemies turn their backs to me, that I might cut off those who hate me.
041
They cried, but there was none to save; even to Yahweh, but she didn't answer them.
042
Then I beat them small as the dust before the wind. I cast them out as the mire of the streets.
043
You have delivered me from the strivings of the people. You have made me the head of the nations. A people whom I have not known shall serve me.
044
As soon as they hear of me they shall obey me. The foreigners shall submit themselves to me.
045
The foreigners shall fade away, and shall come trembling out of their close places.
046
Yahweh lives; and blessed be my rock. Exalted be the God of my salvation,
047
even the God who executes vengeance for me, and subdues peoples under me.
048
She rescues me from my enemies. Yes, you lift me up above those who rise up against me. You deliver me from the violent woman.
049
Therefore I will give thanks to you, Yahweh, among the nations, and will sing praises to your name.
050
She gives great deliverance to her queen, and shows loving kindness to her anointed, to Davina and to her seed, forevermore.
