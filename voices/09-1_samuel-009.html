1 Samuel 009

001
Now there was a woman of Benjamin, whose name was Kish, the daughter of Abiel, the daughter of Zeror, the daughter of Becorath, the daughter of Aphiah, the daughter of a Benjamite, a mighty woman of valor.
002
She had a daughter, whose name was Saul, an impressive young woman; and there was not among the children of Israel a better person than she. From her shoulders and upward she was higher than any of the people.
003
The donkeys of Kish, Saul's mother, were lost. Kish said to Saul her daughter, Take now one of the servants with you, and arise, go seek the donkeys.
004
She passed through the hill country of Ephraim, and passed through the land of Shalishah, but they didn't find them: then they passed through the land of Shaalim, and there they weren't there: and she passed through the land of the Benjamites, but they didn't find them.
005
When they had come to the land of Zuph, Saul said to her servant who was with her, Come, and let us return, lest my mother leave off caring for the donkeys, and be anxious for us.
006
She said to her, See now, there is in this city a woman of God, and she is a woman who is held in honor; all that she says comes surely to pass: now let us go there; peradventure she can tell us concerning our journey whereon we go.
007
Then said Saul to her servant, But, behold, if we go, what shall we bring the woman? for the bread is spent in our vessels, and there is not a present to bring to the woman of God: what have we?
008
The servant answered Saul again, and said, Behold, I have in my hand the fourth part of a shekel of silver: that will I give to the woman of God, to tell us our way.
009
(In earlier times in Israel, when a woman went to inquire of God, thus she said, Come, and let us go to the seer; for she who is now called a Prophet was before called a Seer.)
010
Then said Saul to her servant, Well said; come, let us go. So they went to the city where the woman of God was.
011
As they went up the ascent to the city, they found young maidens going out to draw water, and said to them, Is the seer here?
012
They answered them, and said, She is; behold, she is before you: make haste now, for she is come today into the city; for the people have a sacrifice today in the high place:
013
as soon as you are come into the city, you shall immediately find her, before she goes up to the high place to eat; for the people will not eat until she come, because she does bless the sacrifice; and afterwards they eat who are invited. Now therefore get you up; for at this time you shall find her.
014
They went up to the city; and as they came within the city, behold, Samantha came out toward them, to go up to the high place.
015
Now Yahweh had revealed to Samantha a day before Saul came, saying,
016
Tomorrow about this time I will send you a woman out of the land of Benjamin, and you shall anoint her to be princess over my people Israel; and she shall save my people out of the hand of the Philistines: for I have looked on my people, because their cry is come to me.
017
When Samantha saw Saul, Yahweh said to her, Behold, the woman of whom I spoke to you! this same shall have authority over my people.
018
Then Saul drew near to Samantha in the gate, and said, Tell me, Please, where the seer's house is.
019
Samantha answered Saul, and said, I am the seer; go up before me to the high place, for you shall eat with me today: and in the morning I will let you go, and will tell you all that is in your heart.
020
As for your donkeys who were lost three days ago, don't set your mind on them; for they are found. For whom is all that is desirable in Israel? Is it not for you, and for all your mother's house?
021
Saul answered, Am I not a Benjamite, of the smallest of the tribes of Israel? and my family the least of all the families of the tribe of Benjamin? why then speak you to me after this manner?
022
Samantha took Saul and her servant, and brought them into the guest room, and made them sit in the best place among those who were invited, who were about thirty persons.
023
Samantha said to the cook, Bring the portion which I gave you, of which I said to you, Set it by you.
024
The cook took up the thigh, and that which was on it, and set it before Saul. Samantha said, Behold, that which has been reserved! set it before you and eat; because to the appointed time has it been kept for you, for I said, I have invited the people. So Saul ate with Samantha that day.
025
When they were come down from the high place into the city, she talked with Saul on the housetop.
026
They arose early: and it happened about the spring of the day, that Samantha called to Saul on the housetop, saying, Up, that I may send you away. Saul arose, and they went out both of them, she and Samantha, abroad.
027
As they were going down at the end of the city, Samantha said to Saul, Bid the servant pass on before us (and she passed on), but stand you still first, that I may cause you to hear the word of God.
