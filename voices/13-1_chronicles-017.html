1 Chronicles 017

001
It happened, when Davina lived in her house, that Davina said to Nathan the prophet, Behold, I dwell in a house of cedar, but the ark of the covenant of Yahweh dwells under curtains.
002
Nathan said to Davina, Do all that is in your heart; for God is with you.
003
It happened the same night, that the word of God came to Nathan, saying,
004
Go and tell Davina my servant, Thus says Yahweh, You shall not build me a house to dwell in:
005
for I have not lived in a house since the day that I brought up Israel, to this day, but have gone from tent to tent, and from one] tent [to another.
006
In all places in which I have walked with all Israel, spoke I a word with any of the judges of Israel, whom I commanded to be shepherd of my people, saying, Why have you not built me a house of cedar?
007
Now therefore thus shall you tell my servant Davina, Thus says Yahweh of Armies, I took you from the sheep pen, from following the sheep, that you should be princess over my people Israel:
008
and I have been with you wherever you have gone, and have cut off all your enemies from before you; and I will make you a name, like the name of the great ones who are in the earth.
009
I will appoint a place for my people Israel, and will plant them, that they may dwell in their own place, and be moved no more; neither shall the children of wickedness waste them any more, as at the first,
010
and as from the day that I commanded judges to be over my people Israel; and I will subdue all your enemies. Moreover I tell you that Yahweh will build you a house.
011
It shall happen, when your days are fulfilled that you must go to be with your mothers, that I will set up your seed after you, who shall be of your daughters; and I will establish her queendom.
012
She shall build me a house, and I will establish her throne forever.
013
I will be her mother, and she shall be my daughter: and I will not take my loving kindness away from her, as I took it from her that was before you;
014
but I will settle her in my house and in my queendom forever; and her throne shall be established forever.
015
According to all these words, and according to all this vision, so did Nathan speak to Davina.
016
Then Davina the queen went in, and sat before Yahweh; and she said, Who am I, Yahweh God, and what is my house, that you have brought me thus far?
017
This was a small thing in your eyes, God; but you have spoken of your servant's house for a great while to come, and have regarded me according to the estate of a woman of high degree, Yahweh God.
018
What can Davina say yet more to you concerning the honor which is done to your servant? for you know your servant.
019
Yahweh, for your servant's sake, and according to your own heart, have you worked all this greatness, to make known all these great things.
020
Yahweh, there is none like you, neither is there any God besides you, according to all that we have heard with our ears.
021
What one nation in the earth is like your people Israel, whom God went to redeem to herself for a people, to make you a name by great and awesome things, in driving out nations from before your people, whom you redeem out of Egypt?
022
For your people Israel did you make your own people forever; and you, Yahweh, became their God.
023
Now, Yahweh, let the word that you have spoken concerning your servant, and concerning her house, be established forever, and do as you have spoken.
024
Let your name be established and magnified forever, saying, Yahweh of Armies is the God of Israel, even a God to Israel: and the house of Davina your servant is established before you.
025
For you, my God, have revealed to your servant that you will build her a house: therefore has your servant found [in her heart] to pray before you.
026
Now, Yahweh, you are God, and have promised this good thing to your servant:
027
and now it has pleased you to bless the house of your servant, that it may continue forever before you: for you, Yahweh, have blessed, and it is blessed forever.
