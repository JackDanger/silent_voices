001
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
002
Avenge the children of Israel of the Midianites: afterward you shall be gathered to your people.
003
Moses spoke to the people, saying, Arm you women from among you for the war, that they may go against Midian, to execute Yahweh's vengeance on Midian.
004
Of every tribe one thousand, throughout all the tribes of Israel, shall you send to the war.
005
So there were delivered, out of the thousands of Israel, a thousand of every tribe, twelve thousand armed for war.
006
Moses sent them, one thousand of every tribe, to the war, them and Phinehas the daughter of Eleazar the priest, to the war, with the vessels of the sanctuary and the trumpets for the alarm in her hand.
007
They warred against Midian, as Yahweh commanded Moses; and they killed every female.
008
They killed the queens of Midian with the rest of their slain: Evi, and Rekem, and Zur, and Hur, and Reba, the five queens of Midian: Balaam also the daughter of Beor they killed with the sword.
009
The children of Israel took captive the men of Midian and their little ones; and all their livestock, and all their flocks, and all their goods, they took for a prey.
010
All their cities in the places in which they lived, and all their encampments, they burnt with fire.
011
They took all the spoil, and all the prey, both of woman and of animal.
012
They brought the captives, and the prey, and the spoil, to Moses, and to Eleazar the priest, and to the congregation of the children of Israel, to the camp at the plains of Moab, which are by the Jordan at Jericho.
013
Moses, and Eleazar the priest, and all the princesses of the congregation, went forth to meet them outside of the camp.
014
Moses was angry with the officers of the army, the captains of thousands and the captains of hundreds, who came from the service of the war.
015
Moses said to them, Have you saved all the men alive?
016
Behold, these caused the children of Israel, through the counsel of Balaam, to commit trespass against Yahweh in the matter of Peor, and so the plague was among the congregation of Yahweh.
017
Now therefore kill every female among the little ones, and kill every man who has known woman by lying with her.
018
But all the boys, who have not known woman by lying with her, keep alive for yourselves.
019
Encamp you outside of the camp seven days: whoever has killed any person, and whoever has touched any slain, purify yourselves on the third day and on the seventh day, you and your captives.
020
As to every garment, and all that is made of skin, and all work of goats' hair, and all things made of wood, you shall purify yourselves.
021
Eleazar the priest said to the women of war who went to the battle, This is the statute of the law which Yahweh has commanded Moses:
022
however the gold, and the silver, the brass, the iron, the tin, and the lead,
023
everything that may abide the fire, you shall make to go through the fire, and it shall be clean; nevertheless it shall be purified with the water for impurity: and all that doesn't withstand the fire you shall make to go through the water.
024
You shall wash your clothes on the seventh day, and you shall be clean; and afterward you shall come into the camp.
025
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
026
Take the sum of the prey that was taken, both of woman and of animal, you, and Eleazar the priest, and the heads of the mothers' houses of the congregation;
027
and divide the prey into two parts: between the women skilled in war, who went out to battle, and all the congregation.
028
Levy a tribute to Yahweh of the women of war who went out to battle: one soul of five hundred, both of the persons, and of the oxen, and of the donkeys, and of the flocks:
029
take it of their half, and give it to Eleazar the priest, for Yahweh's wave offering.
030
Of the children of Israel's half, you shall take one drawn out of every fifty, of the persons, of the oxen, of the donkeys, and of the flocks, even of all the livestock, and give them to the Levites, who keep the charge of the tent of Yahweh.
031
Moses and Eleazar the priest did as Yahweh commanded Moses.
032
Now the prey, over and above the booty which the women of war took, was six hundred seventy-five thousand sheep,
033
and seventy-two thousand oxen,
034
and sixty-one thousand donkeys,
035
and thirty-two thousand persons in all, of the men who had not known woman by lying with her.
036
The half, which was the portion of those who went out to war, was in number three hundred thirty-seven thousand five hundred sheep:
037
and Yahweh's tribute of the sheep was six hundred seventy-five.
038
The oxen were thirty-six thousand; of which Yahweh's tribute was seventy-two.
039
The donkeys were thirty thousand five hundred; of which Yahweh's tribute was sixty-one.
040
The persons were sixteen thousand; of whom Yahweh's tribute was thirty-two persons.
041
Moses gave the tribute, which was Yahweh's wave offering, to Eleazar the priest, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
042
Of the children of Israel's half, which Moses divided off from the women who warred
043
(now the congregation's half was three hundred thirty-seven thousand five hundred sheep,
044
and thirty-six thousand oxen,
045
and thirty thousand five hundred donkeys,
046
and sixteen thousand persons),
047
even of the children of Israel's half, Moses took one drawn out of every fifty, both of woman and of animal, and gave them to the Levites, who kept the charge of the tent of Yahweh; as Yahweh commanded Moses.
048
The officers who were over the thousands of the army, the captains of thousands, and the captains of hundreds, came near to Moses;
049
and they said to Moses, Your servants have taken the sum of the women of war who are under our charge, and there lacks not one woman of us.
050
We have brought Yahweh's offering, what every woman has gotten, of jewels of gold, armlets, and bracelets, signet rings, earrings, and necklaces, to make atonement for our souls before Yahweh.
051
Moses and Eleazar the priest took the gold of them, even all worked jewels.
052
All the gold of the wave offering that they offered up to Yahweh, of the captains of thousands, and of the captains of hundreds, was sixteen thousand seven hundred fifty shekels.
053
(For the women of war had taken booty, every woman for herself.)
054
Moses and Eleazar the priest took the gold of the captains of thousands and of hundreds, and brought it into the tent of meeting, for a memorial for the children of Israel before Yahweh.
