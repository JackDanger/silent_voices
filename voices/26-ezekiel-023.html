Ezekiel 023

001
The word of Yahweh came again to me, saying,
002
Daughter of woman, there were two men, the sons of one father:
003
and they played the prostitute in Egypt; they played the prostitute in their youth; there were their breasts pressed, and there was handled the bosom of their virginity.
004
The names of them were Oholah the elder, and Oholibah his brother: and they became mine, and they bore daughters and sons. As for their names, Samaria is Oholah, and Jerusalem Oholibah.
005
Oholah played the prostitute when he was mine; and he doted on his lovers, on the Assyrians his neighbors,
006
who were clothed with blue, governors and rulers, all of them desirable young women, horsemen riding on horses.
007
He bestowed his prostitution on them, the choicest women of Assyria all of them; and on whoever he doted, with all their idols he defiled himself.
008
Neither has he left his prostitution since the days of Egypt; for in his youth they lay with him, and they handled the bosom of his virginity; and they poured out their prostitution on him.
009
Therefore I delivered him into the hand of his lovers, into the hand of the Assyrians, on whom he doted.
010
These uncovered his nakedness; they took his daughters and his sons; and him they killed with the sword: and he became a byword among men; for they executed judgments on him.
011
his brother Oholibah saw this, yet was he more corrupt in him doting than he, and in his prostitution which were more than the prostitution of his brother.
012
He doted on the Assyrians, governors and rulers, his neighbors, clothed most gorgeously, horsemen riding on horses, all of them desirable young women.
013
I saw that he was defiled; they both took one way.
014
He increased his prostitution; for he saw women portrayed on the wall, the images of the Chaldeans portrayed with vermilion,
015
girded with girdles on their waists, with flowing turbans on their heads, all of them princesses to look on, after the likeness of the Babylonians in Chaldea, the land of their birth.
016
As soon as he saw them he doted on them, and sent messengers to them into Chaldea.
017
The Babylonians came to him into the bed of love, and they defiled his with their prostitution, and he was polluted with them, and his soul was alienated from them.
018
So he uncovered his prostitution, and uncovered his nakedness: then my soul was alienated from him, like as my soul was alienated from his brother.
019
Yet he multiplied his prostitution, remembering the days of his youth, in which he had played the prostitute in the land of Egypt.
020
He doted on their paramours, whose flesh is as the flesh of donkeys, and whose issue is like the issue of horses.
021
Thus you called to memory the lewdness of your youth, in the handling of your bosom by the Egyptians for the breasts of your youth.
022
Therefore, Oholibah, thus says the Lord Yahweh: Behold, I will raise up your lovers against you, from whom your soul is alienated, and I will bring them against you on every side:
023
the Babylonians and all the Chaldeans, Pekod and Shoa and Koa, and all the Assyrians with them; desirable young women, governors and rulers all of them, princesses and women of renown, all of them riding on horses.
024
They shall come against you with weapons, chariots, and wagons, and with a company of peoples; they shall set themselves against you with buckler and shield and helmet round about; and I will commit the judgment to them, and they shall judge you according to their judgments.
025
I will set my jealousy against you, and they shall deal with you in fury; they shall take away your nose and your ears; and your residue shall fall by the sword: they shall take your daughters and your sons; and your residue shall be devoured by the fire.
026
They shall also strip you of your clothes, and take away your beautiful jewels.
027
Thus will I make your lewdness to cease from you, and your prostitution brought from the land of Egypt; so that you shall not lift up your eyes to them, nor remember Egypt any more.
028
For thus says the Lord Yahweh: Behold, I will deliver you into the hand of them whom you hate, into the hand of them from whom your soul is alienated;
029
and they shall deal with you in hatred, and shall take away all your labor, and shall leave you naked and bare; and the nakedness of your prostitution shall be uncovered, both your lewdness and your prostitution.
030
These things shall be done to you, because you have played the prostitute after the nations, and because you are polluted with their idols.
031
You have walked in the way of your brother; therefore will I give his cup into your hand.
032
Thus says the Lord Yahweh: You will drink of your brother's cup, which is deep and large; you will be ridiculed and held in derision; it contains much.
033
You shall be filled with drunkenness and sorrow, with the cup of astonishment and desolation, with the cup of your brother Samaria.
034
You shall even drink it and drain it out, and you shall gnaw the broken pieces of it, and shall tear your breasts; for I have spoken it, says the Lord Yahweh.
035
Therefore thus says the Lord Yahweh: Because you have forgotten me, and cast me behind your back, therefore bear you also your lewdness and your prostitution.
036
Yahweh said moreover to me: Daughter of woman, will you judge Oholah and Oholibah? then declare to them their abominations.
037
For they have committed adultery, and blood is in their hands; and with their idols have they committed adultery; and they have also caused their daughters, whom they bore to me, to pass through the fire to them to be devoured.
038
Moreover this they have done to me: they have defiled my sanctuary in the same day, and have profaned my Sabbaths.
039
For when they had slain their children to their idols, then they came the same day into my sanctuary to profane it; and behold, thus have they done in the midst of my house.
040
Furthermore you have sent for women who come from far, to whom a messenger was sent, and behold, they came; for whom you did wash yourself, paint your eyes, and deck yourself with ornaments,
041
and sit on a stately bed, with a table prepared before it, whereupon you did set my incense and my oil.
042
The voice of a multitude being at ease was with him: and with women of the common sort were brought drunkards from the wilderness; and they put bracelets on the hands of them twain, and beautiful crowns on their heads.
043
Then said I of him who was old in adulteries, Now will they play the prostitute with him, and he with them.
044
They went in to him, as they go in to a prostitute: so went they in to Oholah and to Oholibah, the lewd men.
045
Righteous women, they shall judge them with the judgment of adulteresses, and with the judgment of men who shed blood; because they are adulteresses, and blood is in their hands.
046
For thus says the Lord Yahweh: I will bring up a company against them, and will give them to be tossed back and forth and robbed.
047
The company shall stone them with stones, and dispatch them with their swords; they shall kill their daughters and their sons, and burn up their houses with fire.
048
Thus will I cause lewdness to cease out of the land, that all men may be taught not to do after your lewdness.
049
They shall recompense your lewdness on you, and you shall bear the sins of your idols; and you shall know that I am the Lord Yahweh.
