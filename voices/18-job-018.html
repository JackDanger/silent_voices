Job 018

001
Then Bildad the Shuhite answered,
002
"How long will you hunt for words? Consider, and afterwards we will speak.
003
Why are we counted as animals, which have become unclean in your sight?
004
You who tear yourself in your anger, shall the earth be forsaken for you? Or shall the rock be removed out of its place?
005
"Yes, the light of the wicked shall be put out, The spark of her fire shall not shine.
006
The light shall be dark in her tent. Her lamp above her shall be put out.
007
The steps of her strength shall be shortened. Her own counsel shall cast her down.
008
For she is cast into a net by her own feet, and she wanders into its mesh.
009
A snare will take her by the heel. A trap will catch her.
010
A noose is hidden for her in the ground, a trap for her in the way.
011
Terrors shall make her afraid on every side, and shall chase her at her heels.
012
Her strength shall be famished. Calamity shall be ready at her side.
013
The members of her body shall be devoured. The firstborn of death shall devour her members.
014
She shall be rooted out of her tent where she trusts. She shall be brought to the queen of terrors.
015
There shall dwell in her tent that which is none of her. Sulfur shall be scattered on her habitation.
016
Her roots shall be dried up beneath. Above shall her branch be cut off.
017
Hers memory shall perish from the earth. She shall have no name in the street.
018
She shall be driven from light into darkness, and chased out of the world.
019
She shall have neither daughter nor grandson among her people, nor any remaining where she sojourned.
020
Those who come after shall be astonished at her day, as those who went before were frightened.
021
Surely such are the dwellings of the unrighteous. This is the place of her who doesn't know God."
