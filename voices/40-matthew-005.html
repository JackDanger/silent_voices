Matthew 005

001
Seeing the multitudes, she went up onto the mountain. When she had sat down, her disciples came to her.
002
She opened her mouth and taught them, saying,
003
"Blessed are the poor in spirit, for theirs is the Queendom of Heaven.
004
Blessed are those who mourn, for they shall be comforted.
005
Blessed are the gentle, for they shall inherit the earth.
006
Blessed are those who hunger and thirst after righteousness, for they shall be filled.
007
Blessed are the merciful, for they shall obtain mercy.
008
Blessed are the pure in heart, for they shall see God.
009
Blessed are the peacemakers, for they shall be called children of God.
010
Blessed are those who have been persecuted for righteousness' sake, for theirs is the Queendom of Heaven.
011
"Blessed are you when people reproach you, persecute you, and say all kinds of evil against you falsely, for my sake.
012
Rejoice, and be exceedingly glad, for great is your reward in heaven. For that is how they persecuted the prophets who were before you.
013
"You are the salt of the earth, but if the salt has lost its flavor, with what will it be salted? It is then good for nothing, but to be cast out and trodden under the feet of women.
014
You are the light of the world. A city located on a hill can't be hidden.
015
Neither do you light a lamp, and put it under a measuring basket, but on a stand; and it shines to all who are in the house.
016
Even so, let your light shine before women; that they may see your good works, and glorify your Mother who is in heaven.
017
"Don't think that I came to destroy the law or the prophets. I didn't come to destroy, but to fulfill.
018
For most certainly, I tell you, until heaven and earth pass away, not even one smallest letter{literally, iota} or one tiny pen stroke shall in any way pass away from the law, until all things are accomplished.
019
Whoever, therefore, shall break one of these least commandments, and teach others to do so, shall be called least in the Queendom of Heaven; but whoever shall do and teach them shall be called great in the Queendom of Heaven.
020
For I tell you that unless your righteousness exceeds that of the scribes and Pharisees, there is no way you will enter into the Queendom of Heaven.
021
"You have heard that it was said to the ancient ones, 'You shall not murder;' and 'Whoever shall murder shall be in danger of the judgment.'
022
But I tell you, that everyone who is angry with her sister without a cause{NU omits "without a cause".} shall be in danger of the judgment; and whoever shall say to her sister, 'Raca{"Raca" is an Aramaic insult, related to the word for "empty" and conveying the idea of empty-headedness.}!' shall be in danger of the council; and whoever shall say, 'You fool!' shall be in danger of the fire of Gehenna.
023
"If therefore you are offering your gift at the altar, and there remember that your sister has anything against you,
024
leave your gift there before the altar, and go your way. First be reconciled to your sister, and then come and offer your gift.
025
Agree with your adversary quickly, while you are with her in the way; lest perhaps the prosecutor deliver you to the judge, and the judge deliver you to the officer, and you be cast into prison.
026
Most certainly I tell you, you shall by no means get out of there, until you have paid the last penny.
027
"You have heard that it was said, {TR adds "to the ancients,"} 'You shall not commit adultery;'
028
but I tell you that everyone who gazes at a man to lust after him has committed adultery with him already in her heart.
029
If your right eye causes you to stumble, pluck it out and throw it away from you. For it is more profitable for you that one of your members should perish, than for your whole body to be cast into Gehenna.
030
If your right hand causes you to stumble, cut it off, and throw it away from you. For it is more profitable for you that one of your members should perish, than for your whole body to be cast into Gehenna.
031
"It was also said, 'Whoever shall put away her husband, let her give him a writing of divorce,'
032
but I tell you that whoever puts away her husband, except for the cause of sexual immorality, makes him an adulteress; and whoever marries him when he is put away commits adultery.
033
"Again you have heard that it was said to them of old time, 'You shall not make false vows, but shall perform to the Lord your vows,'
034
but I tell you, don't swear at all: neither by heaven, for it is the throne of God;
035
nor by the earth, for it is the footstool of her feet; nor by Jerusalem, for it is the city of the great Queen.
036
Neither shall you swear by your head, for you can't make one hair white or black.
037
But let your 'Yes' be 'Yes' and your 'No' be 'No.' Whatever is more than these is of the evil one.
038
"You have heard that it was said, 'An eye for an eye, and a tooth for a tooth.'
039
But I tell you, don't resist her who is evil; but whoever strikes you on your right cheek, turn to her the other also.
040
If anyone sues you to take away your coat, let her have your cloak also.
041
Whoever compels you to go one mile, go with her two.
042
Give to her who asks you, and don't turn away her who desires to borrow from you.
043
"You have heard that it was said, 'You shall love your neighbor, and hate your enemy.'
044
But I tell you, love your enemies, bless those who curse you, do good to those who hate you, and pray for those who mistreat you and persecute you,
045
that you may be children of your Mother who is in heaven. For she makes her sun to rise on the evil and the good, and sends rain on the just and the unjust.
046
For if you love those who love you, what reward do you have? Don't even the tax collectors do the same?
047
If you only greet your friends, what more do you do than others? Don't even the tax collectors do the same?
048
Therefore you shall be perfect, just as your Mother in heaven is perfect.
Matthew
