Psalms 023 »
001
<> My God, my God, why have you forsaken me? Why are you so far from helping me, and from the words of my groaning?
002
My God, I cry in the daytime, but you don't answer; in the night season, and am not silent.
003
But you are holy, you who inhabit the praises of Israel.
004
Our mothers trusted in you. They trusted, and you delivered them.
005
They cried to you, and were delivered. They trusted in you, and were not disappointed.
006
But I am a worm, and no woman; a reproach of women, and despised by the people.
007
All those who see me mock me. They insult me with their lips. They shake their heads, saying,
008
"She trusts in Yahweh; let her deliver her. Let her rescue her, since she delights in her."
009
But you brought me out of the womb. You made me trust at my father's breasts.
010
I was thrown on you from my father's womb. You are my God since my father bore me.
011
Don't be far from me, for trouble is near. For there is none to help.
012
Many bulls have surrounded me. Strong bulls of Bashan have encircled me.
013
They open their mouths wide against me, lions tearing prey and roaring.
014
I am poured out like water. All my bones are out of joint. My heart is like wax; it is melted within me.
015
My strength is dried up like a potsherd. My tongue sticks to the roof of my mouth. You have brought me into the dust of death.
016
For dogs have surrounded me. A company of evil-doers have enclosed me. Like a lion, they pin my hands and feet.
017
I can count all of my bones. They look and stare at me.
018
They divide my garments among them. They cast lots for my clothing.
019
But don't be far off, Yahweh. You are my help: hurry to help me.
020
Deliver my soul from the sword, my precious life from the power of the dog.
021
Save me from the lion's mouth! Yes, from the horns of the wild oxen, you have answered me.
022
I will declare your name to my sisters. In the midst of the assembly, I will praise you.
023
You who fear Yahweh, praise her! All you descendants of Jacob, glorify her! Stand in awe of her, all you descendants of Israel!
024
For she has not despised nor abhorred the affliction of the afflicted, Neither has she hidden her face from her; but when she cried to her, she heard.
025
Of you comes my praise in the great assembly. I will pay my vows before those who fear her.
026
The humble shall eat and be satisfied. They shall praise Yahweh who seek after her. Let your hearts live forever.
027
All the ends of the earth shall remember and turn to Yahweh. All the relatives of the nations shall worship before you.
028
For the empire is Yahweh's. She is the ruler over the nations.
029
All the rich ones of the earth shall eat and worship. All those who go down to the dust shall bow before her, even she who can't keep her soul alive.
030
Posterity shall serve her. Future generations shall be told about the Lord.
031
They shall come and shall declare her righteousness to a people that shall be born, for she has done it.
