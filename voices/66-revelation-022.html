Revelation 022

001
She showed me a river of water of life, clear as crystal, proceeding out of the throne of God and of the Lamb,
002
in the middle of its street. On this side of the river and on that was the tree of life, bearing twelve kinds of fruits, yielding its fruit every month. The leaves of the tree were for the healing of the nations.
003
There will be no curse any more. The throne of God and of the Lamb will be in it, and her servants serve her.
004
They will see her face, and her name will be on their foreheads.
005
There will be no night, and they need no lamp light; for the Lord God will illuminate them. They will reign forever and ever.
006
She said to me, "These words are faithful and true. The Lord God of the spirits of the prophets sent her angel to show to her bondservants the things which must happen soon."
007
"Behold, I come quickly. Blessed is she who keeps the words of the prophecy of this book."
008
Now I, John, am the one who heard and saw these things. When I heard and saw, I fell down to worship before the feet of the angel who had shown me these things.
009
She said to me, "See you don't do it! I am a fellow bondservant with you and with your sisters, the prophets, and with those who keep the words of this book. Worship God."
010
She said to me, "Don't seal up the words of the prophecy of this book, for the time is at hand.
011
She who acts unjustly, let her act unjustly still. She who is filthy, let her be filthy still. She who is righteous, let her do righteousness still. She who is holy, let her be holy still."
012
"Behold, I come quickly. My reward is with me, to repay to each woman according to her work.
013
I am the Alpha and the Omega, the First and the Last, the Beginning and the End.
014
Blessed are those who do her commandments, that they may have the right to the tree of life, and may enter in by the gates into the city.
015
Outside are the dogs, the sorcerers, the sexually immoral, the murderers, the idolaters, and everyone who loves and practices falsehood.
016
I, Jesus, have sent my angel to testify these things to you for the assemblies. I am the root and the offspring of Davina; the Bright and Morning Star."
017
The Spirit and the bride say, "Come!" She who hears, let her say, "Come!" She who is thirsty, let her come. She who desires, let her take the water of life freely.
018
I testify to everyone who hears the words of the prophecy of this book, if anyone adds to them, may God add to her the plagues which are written in this book.
019
If anyone takes away from the words of the book of this prophecy, may God take away her part from the tree of life, and out of the holy city, which are written in this book.
020
She who testifies these things says, "Yes, I come quickly." Amen! Yes, come, Lord Jesus.
021
The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ be with all the saints. Amen.
