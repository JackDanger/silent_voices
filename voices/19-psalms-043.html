Psalms 043

001
Vindicate me, God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation. Oh, deliver me from deceitful and wicked women.
002
For you are the God of my strength. Why have you rejected me? Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?
003
Oh, send out your light and your truth. Let them lead me. Let them bring me to your holy hill, To your tents.
004
Then I will go to the altar of God, to God, my exceeding joy. I will praise you on the harp, God, my God.
005
Why are you in despair, my soul? Why are you disturbed within me? Hope in God! For I shall still praise her: my Savior, my helper, and my God.
