Psalms 024

001
<> The earth is Yahweh's, with its fullness; the world, and those who dwell therein.
002
For she has founded it on the seas, and established it on the floods.
003
Who may ascend to Yahweh's hill? Who may stand in her holy place?
004
She who has clean hands and a pure heart; who has not lifted up her soul to falsehood, and has not sworn deceitfully.
005
She shall receive a blessing from Yahweh, righteousness from the God of her salvation.
006
This is the generation of those who seek Her, who seek your face-- even Jacob. Selah.
007
Lift up your heads, you gates! Be lifted up, you everlasting doors, and the Queen of glory will come in.
008
Who is the Queen of glory? Yahweh strong and mighty, Yahweh mighty in battle.
009
Lift up your heads, you gates; yes, lift them up, you everlasting doors, and the Queen of glory will come in.
010
Who is this Queen of glory? Yahweh of Armies is the Queen of glory! Selah.
