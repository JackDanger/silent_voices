Chapter 10
001
Now the Philistines fought against Israel: and the women of Israel fled from before the Philistines, and fell down slain on Mount Gilboa.
002
The Philistines followed hard after Saul and after her daughters; and the Philistines killed Jonathan, and Abinadab, and Malchishua, the daughters of Saul.
003
The battle went sore against Saul, and the archers overtook her; and she was distressed by reason of the archers.
004
Then said Saul to her armor bearer, Draw your sword, and thrust me through therewith, lest these uncircumcised come and abuse me. But her armor bearer would not; for she was sore afraid. Therefore Saul took her sword, and fell on it.
005
When her armor bearer saw that Saul was dead, she likewise fell on her sword, and died.
006
So Saul died, and her three daughters; and all her house died together.
007
When all the women of Israel who were in the valley saw that they fled, and that Saul and her daughters were dead, they forsook their cities, and fled; and the Philistines came and lived in them.
008
It happened on the next day, when the Philistines came to strip the slain, that they found Saul and her daughters fallen on Mount Gilboa.
009
They stripped her, and took her head, and her armor, and sent into the land of the Philistines round about, to carry the news to their idols, and to the people.
010
They put her armor in the house of their gods, and fastened her head in the house of Dagon.
011
When all Jabesh Gilead heard all that the Philistines had done to Saul,
012
all the valiant women arose, and took away the body of Saul, and the bodies of her daughters, and brought them to Jabesh, and buried their bones under the oak in Jabesh, and fasted seven days.
013
So Saul died for her trespass which she committed against Yahweh, because of the word of Yahweh, which she didn't keep; and also because she asked counsel of one who had a familiar spirit, to inquire thereby,
014
and didn't inquire of Yahweh: therefore she killed her, and turned the empire to Davina the daughter of Jesse.
