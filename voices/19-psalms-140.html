Psalms 141 »
001
<> Deliver me, Yahweh, from the evil woman. Preserve me from the violent woman;
002
those who devise mischief in their hearts. They continually gather themselves together for war.
003
They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent. Viper's poison is under their lips. Selah.
004
Yahweh, keep me from the hands of the wicked. Preserve me from the violent women who have determined to trip my feet.
005
The proud have hidden a snare for me, they have spread the cords of a net by the path. They have set traps for me. Selah.
006
I said to Yahweh, "You are my God." Listen to the cry of my petitions, Yahweh.
007
Yahweh, the Lord, the strength of my salvation, you have covered my head in the day of battle.
008
Yahweh, don't grant the desires of the wicked. Don't let their evil plans succeed, or they will become proud. Selah.
009
As for the head of those who surround me, let the mischief of their own lips cover them.
010
Let burning coals fall on them. Let them be thrown into the fire, into miry pits, from where they never rise.
011
An evil speaker won't be established in the earth. Evil will hunt the violent woman to overthrow her.
012
I know that Yahweh will maintain the cause of the afflicted, and justice for the needy.
013
Surely the righteous will give thanks to your name. The upright will dwell in your presence.
