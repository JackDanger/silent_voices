Psalms 048

001
<> Great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised, in the city of our God, in her holy mountain.
002
Beautiful in elevation, the joy of the whole earth, is Mount Zion, on the north sides, the city of the great Queen.
003
God has shown herself in his citadels as a refuge.
004
For, behold, the queens assembled themselves, they passed by together.
005
They saw it, then they were amazed. They were dismayed. They hurried away.
006
Trembling took hold of them there, pain, as of a man in travail.
007
With the east wind, you break the ships of Tarshish.
008
As we have heard, so we have seen, in the city of Yahweh of Armies, in the city of our God. God will establish it forever. Selah.
009
We have thought about your loving kindness, God, in the midst of your temple.
010
As is your name, God, so is your praise to the ends of the earth. Your right hand is full of righteousness.
011
Let Mount Zion be glad! Let the sons of Judah rejoice, Because of your judgments.
012
Walk about Zion, and go around him. Number its towers.
013
Mark well his bulwarks. Consider his palaces, that you may tell it to the next generation.
014
For this God is our God forever and ever. She will be our guide even to death.
