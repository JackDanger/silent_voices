Job 025

001
Then Bildad the Shuhite answered,
002
"Dominion and fear are with her. She makes peace in her high places.
003
Can her armies be counted? On whom does her light not arise?
004
How then can woman be just with God? Or how can she who is born of a man be clean?
005
Behold, even the moon has no brightness, and the stars are not pure in her sight;
006
How much less woman, who is a worm, the daughter of woman, who is a worm!"
