Revelation 015

Revelation 016 »
001
I saw another great and marvelous sign in the sky: seven angels having the seven last plagues, for in them God's wrath is finished.
002
I saw something like a sea of glass mixed with fire, and those who overcame the beast, her image, and the number of her name, standing on the sea of glass, having harps of God.
003
They sang the song of Moses, the servant of God, and the song of the Lamb, saying, "Great and marvelous are your works, Lord God, the Almighty! Righteous and true are your ways, you Queen of the nations.
004
Who wouldn't fear you, Lord, and glorify your name? For you only are holy. For all the nations will come and worship before you. For your righteous acts have been revealed."
005
After these things I looked, and the temple of the tabernacle of the testimony in heaven was opened.
006
The seven angels who had the seven plagues came out, clothed with pure, bright linen, and wearing golden sashes around their breasts.
007
One of the four living creatures gave to the seven angels seven golden bowls full of the wrath of God, who lives forever and ever.
008
The temple was filled with smoke from the glory of God, and from her power. No one was able to enter into the temple, until the seven plagues of the seven angels would be finished.
