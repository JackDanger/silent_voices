Amos 005

001
Listen to this word which I take up for a lamentation over you, O house of Israel.
002
"The virgin of Israel has fallen; He shall rise no more. He is cast down on his land; there is no one to raise him up."
003
For thus says the Lord Yahweh: "The city that went forth a thousand shall have a hundred left, and that which went forth one hundred shall have ten left to the house of Israel."
004
For thus says Yahweh to the house of Israel: "Seek me, and you will live;
005
but don't seek Bethel, nor enter into Gilgal, and don't pass to Beersheba: for Gilgal shall surely go into captivity, and Bethel shall come to nothing.
006
Seek Yahweh, and you will live; lest she break out like fire in the house of Joseph, and it devour, and there be no one to quench it in Bethel.
007
You who turn justice to wormwood, and cast down righteousness to the earth:
008
seek her who made the Pleiades and Orion, and turns the shadow of death into the morning, and makes the day dark with night; who calls for the waters of the sea, and pours them out on the surface of the earth, Yahweh is her name,
009
who brings sudden destruction on the strong, so that destruction comes on the fortress.
010
They hate her who reproves in the gate, and they abhor her who speaks blamelessly.
011
Forasmuch therefore as you trample on the poor, and take taxes from her of wheat: You have built houses of hewn stone, but you will not dwell in them. You have planted pleasant vineyards, but you shall not drink their wine.
012
For I know how many your offenses, and how great are your sins-- you who afflict the just, who take a bribe, and who turn aside the needy in the courts.
013
Therefore a prudent person keeps silent in such a time, for it is an evil time.
014
Seek good, and not evil, that you may live; and so Yahweh, the God of Armies, will be with you, as you say.
015
Hate evil, love good, and establish justice in the courts. It may be that Yahweh, the God of Armies, will be gracious to the remnant of Joseph."
016
Therefore thus says Yahweh, the God of Armies, the Lord: "Wailing will be in all the broad ways; and they will say in all the streets, 'Alas! Alas!' and they will call the farmer to mourning, and those who are skillful in lamentation to wailing.
017
In all vineyards there will be wailing; for I will pass through the midst of you," says Yahweh.
018
"Woe to you who desire the day of Yahweh! Why do you long for the day of Yahweh? It is darkness, and not light.
019
As if a woman fled from a lion, and a bear met her; Or she went into the house and leaned her hand on the wall, and a snake bit her.
020
Won't the day of Yahweh be darkness, and not light? Even very dark, and no brightness in it?
021
I hate, I despise your feasts, and I can't stand your solemn assemblies.
022
Yes, though you offer me your burnt offerings and meal offerings, I will not accept them; neither will I regard the peace offerings of your fat animals.
023
Take away from me the noise of your songs! I will not listen to the music of your harps.
024
But let justice roll on like rivers, and righteousness like a mighty stream.
025
"Did you bring to me sacrifices and offerings in the wilderness forty years, house of Israel?
026
You also carried the tent of your queen and the shrine of your images, the star of your god, which you made for yourselves.
027
Therefore will I cause you to go into captivity beyond Damascus," says Yahweh, whose name is the God of Armies.
