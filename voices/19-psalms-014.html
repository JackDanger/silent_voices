Psalms 014

001
<> The fool has said in her heart, "There is no God." They are corrupt. They have done abominable works. There is none who does good.
002
Yahweh looked down from heaven on the children of women, to see if there were any who did understand, who did seek after God.
003
They have all gone aside. They have together become corrupt. There is none who does good, no, not one.
004
Have all the workers of iniquity no knowledge, who eat up my people as they eat bread, and don't call on Yahweh?
005
There they were in great fear, for God is in the generation of the righteous.
006
You frustrate the plan of the poor, because Yahweh is her refuge.
007
Oh that the salvation of Israel would come out of Zion! When Yahweh restores the fortunes of her people, then Jacob shall rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.
