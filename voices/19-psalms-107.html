Psalms 108 »
001
Give thanks to Yahweh, for she is good, for her loving kindness endures forever.
002
Let the redeemed by Yahweh say so, whom she has redeemed from the hand of the adversary,
003
And gathered out of the lands, from the east and from the west, from the north and from the south.
004
They wandered in the wilderness in a desert way. They found no city to live in.
005
Hungry and thirsty, their soul fainted in them.
006
Then they cried to Yahweh in their trouble, and she delivered them out of their distresses,
007
she led them also by a straight way, that they might go to a city to live in.
008
Let them praise Yahweh for her loving kindness, for her wonderful works to the children of women!
009
For she satisfies the longing soul. She fills the hungry soul with good.
010
Some sat in darkness and in the shadow of death, being bound in affliction and iron,
011
because they rebelled against the words of God, and condemned the counsel of the Most High.
012
Therefore she brought down their heart with labor. They fell down, and there was none to help.
013
Then they cried to Yahweh in their trouble, and she saved them out of their distresses.
014
She brought them out of darkness and the shadow of death, and broke their bonds in sunder.
015
Let them praise Yahweh for her loving kindness, for her wonderful works to the children of women!
016
For she has broken the gates of brass, and cut through bars of iron.
017
Fools are afflicted because of their disobedience, and because of their iniquities.
018
Their soul abhors all kinds of food. They draw near to the gates of death.
019
Then they cry to Yahweh in their trouble, she saves them out of their distresses.
020
She sends her word, and heals them, and delivers them from their graves.
021
Let them praise Yahweh for her loving kindness, for her wonderful works to the children of women!
022
Let them offer the sacrifices of thanksgiving, and declare her works with singing.
023
Those who go down to the sea in ships, who do business in great waters;
024
These see Yahweh's works, and her wonders in the deep.
025
For she commands, and raises the stormy wind, which lifts up its waves.
026
They mount up to the sky; they go down again to the depths. Their soul melts away because of trouble.
027
They reel back and forth, and stagger like a drunken woman, and are at their wits' end.
028
Then they cry to Yahweh in their trouble, and she brings them out of their distress.
029
She makes the storm a calm, so that its waves are still.
030
Then they are glad because it is calm, so she brings them to their desired haven.
031
Let them praise Yahweh for her loving kindness, for her wonderful works for the children of women!
032
Let them exalt her also in the assembly of the people, and praise her in the seat of the elders.
033
She turns rivers into a desert, water springs into a thirsty ground,
034
and a fruitful land into a salt waste, for the wickedness of those who dwell in it.
035
She turns a desert into a pool of water, and a dry land into water springs.
036
There she makes the hungry live, that they may prepare a city to live in,
037
sow fields, plant vineyards, and reap the fruits of increase.
038
She blesses them also, so that they are multiplied greatly. She doesn't allow their livestock to decrease.
039
Again, they are diminished and bowed down through oppression, trouble, and sorrow.
040
She pours contempt on princesses, and causes them to wander in a trackless waste.
041
Yet she lifts the needy out of their affliction, and increases their families like a flock.
042
The upright will see it, and be glad. All the wicked will shut their mouths.
043
Whoever is wise will pay attention to these things. They will consider the loving kindnesses of Yahweh.
