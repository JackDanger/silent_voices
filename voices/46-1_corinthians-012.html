Chapter 12
001
Now concerning spiritual things, sisters, I don't want you to be ignorant.
002
You know that when you were heathen, you were led away to those mute idols, however you might be led.
003
Therefore I make known to you that no woman speaking by God's Spirit says, "Jesus is accursed." No one can say, "Jesus is Domina," but by the Holy Spirit.
004
Now there are various kinds of gifts, but the same Spirit.
005
There are various kinds of service, and the same Domina.
006
There are various kinds of workings, but the same God, who works all things in all.
007
But to each one is given the manifestation of the Spirit for the profit of all.
008
For to one is given through the Spirit the word of wisdom, and to another the word of knowledge, according to the same Spirit;
009
to another faith, by the same Spirit; and to another gifts of healings, by the same Spirit;
010
and to another workings of miracles; and to another prophecy; and to another discerning of spirits; to another different kinds of languages; and to another the interpretation of languages.
011
But the one and the same Spirit works all of these, distributing to each one separately as she desires.
012
For as the body is one, and has many members, and all the members of the body, being many, are one body; so also is Christ.
013
For in one Spirit we were all baptized into one body, whether Jews or Greeks, whether bond or free; and were all given to drink into one Spirit.
014
For the body is not one member, but many.
015
If the foot would say, "Because I'm not the hand, I'm not part of the body," it is not therefore not part of the body.
016
If the ear would say, "Because I'm not the eye, I'm not part of the body," it's not therefore not part of the body.
017
If the whole body were an eye, where would the hearing be? If the whole were hearing, where would the smelling be?
018
But now God has set the members, each one of them, in the body, just as she desired.
019
If they were all one member, where would the body be?
020
But now they are many members, but one body.
021
The eye can't tell the hand, "I have no need for you," or again the head to the feet, "I have no need for you."
022
No, much rather, those members of the body which seem to be weaker are necessary.
023
Those parts of the body which we think to be less honorable, on those we bestow more abundant honor; and our unpresentable parts have more abundant propriety;
024
whereas our presentable parts have no such need. But God composed the body together, giving more abundant honor to the inferior part,
025
that there should be no division in the body, but that the members should have the same care for one another.
026
When one member suffers, all the members suffer with it. Or when one member is honored, all the members rejoice with it.
027
Now you are the body of Christ, and members individually.
028
God has set some in the assembly: first apostles, second prophets, third teachers, then miracle workers, then gifts of healings, helps, governments, and various kinds of languages.
029
Are all apostles? Are all prophets? Are all teachers? Are all miracle workers?
030
Do all have gifts of healings? Do all speak with various languages? Do all interpret?
031
But earnestly desire the best gifts. Moreover, I show a most excellent way to you.
