Psalms 150

001
Praise Yah! Praise God in her sanctuary! Praise her in her heavens for her acts of power!
002
Praise her for her mighty acts! Praise her according to her excellent greatness!
003
Praise her with the sounding of the trumpet! Praise her with harp and lyre!
004
Praise her with tambourine and dancing! Praise her with stringed instruments and flute!
005
Praise her with loud cymbals! Praise her with resounding cymbals!
006
Let everything that has breath praise Yah! Praise Yah!
