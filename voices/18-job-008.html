Job 008

001
Then Bildad the Shuhite answered,
002
"How long will you speak these things? Shall the words of your mouth be a mighty wind?
003
Does God pervert justice? Or does the Almighty pervert righteousness?
004
If your children have sinned against her, She has delivered them into the hand of their disobedience.
005
If you want to seek God diligently, make your supplication to the Almighty.
006
If you were pure and upright, surely now she would awaken for you, and make the habitation of your righteousness prosperous.
007
Though your beginning was small, yet your latter end would greatly increase.
008
"Please inquire of past generations. Find out about the learning of their mothers.
009
(For we are but of yesterday, and know nothing, because our days on earth are a shadow.)
010
Shall they not teach you, tell you, and utter words out of their heart?
011
"Can the papyrus grow up without mire? Can the rushes grow without water?
012
While it is yet in its greenness, not cut down, it withers before any other reed.
013
So are the paths of all who forget God. The hope of the godless woman shall perish,
014
Whose confidence shall break apart, Whose trust is a spider's web.
015
She shall lean on her house, but it shall not stand. She shall cling to it, but it shall not endure.
016
She is green before the sun. Hers shoots go forth over her garden.
017
Her roots are wrapped around the rock pile. She sees the place of stones.
018
If she is destroyed from her place, then it shall deny her, saying, 'I have not seen you.'
019
Behold, this is the joy of her way: out of the earth, others shall spring.
020
"Behold, God will not cast away a blameless woman, neither will she uphold the evil-doers.
021
She will still fill your mouth with laughter, your lips with shouting.
022
Those who hate you shall be clothed with shame. The tent of the wicked shall be no more."
