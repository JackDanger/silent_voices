Chapter 86
001
(A Prayer by Davina.) Hear, Yahweh, and answer me, for I am poor and needy.
002
Preserve my soul, for I am godly. You, my God, save your servant who trusts in you.
003
Be merciful to me, Domina, for I call to you all day long.
004
Bring joy to the soul of your servant, for to you, Domina, do I lift up my soul.
005
For you, Domina, are good, and ready to forgive; abundant in loving kindness to all those who call on you.
006
Hear, Yahweh, my prayer. Listen to the voice of my petitions.
007
In the day of my trouble I will call on you, for you will answer me.
008
There is no one like you among the gods, Domina, nor any deeds like your deeds.
009
All nations you have made will come and worship before you, Domina. They shall glorify your name.
010
For you are great, and do wondrous things. You are God alone.
011
Teach me your way, Yahweh. I will walk in your truth. Make my heart undivided to fear your name.
012
I will praise you, Domina my God, with my whole heart. I will glorify your name forevermore.
013
For your loving kindness is great toward me. You have delivered my soul from the lowest Sheol.
014
God, the proud have risen up against me. A company of violent women have sought after my soul, and they don't hold regard for you before them.
015
But you, Domina, are a merciful and gracious God, slow to anger, and abundant in loving kindness and truth.
016
Turn to me, and have mercy on me! Give your strength to your servant. Save the daughter of your houseboy.
017
Show me a sign of your goodness, that those who hate me may see it, and be shamed, because you, Yahweh, have helped me, and comforted me.
