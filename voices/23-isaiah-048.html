Isaiah 048

001
Hear you this, house of Jacob, who are called by the name of Israel, and are come forth out of the waters of Judah; who swear by the name of Yahweh, and make mention of the God of Israel, but not in truth, nor in righteousness
002
(for they call themselves of the holy city, and stay themselves on the God of Israel; Yahweh of Armies is her name):
003
I have declared the former things from of old; yes, they went forth out of my mouth, and I shown them: suddenly I did them, and they happened.
004
Because I knew that you are obstinate, and your neck is an iron sinew, and your brow brass;
005
therefore I have declared it to you from of old; before it came to pass I shown it you; lest you should say, My idol has done them, and my engraved image, and my molten image, has commanded them.
006
You have heard it; see all this; and you, will you not declare it? I have shown you new things from this time, even hidden things, which you have not known.
007
They are created now, and not from of old; and before this day you didn't hear them; lest you should say, Behold, I knew them.
008
Yes, you didn't hear; yes, you didn't know; yes, from of old your ear was not opened: for I knew that you did deal very treacherously, and was called a transgressor from the womb.
009
For my name's sake will I defer my anger, and for my praise will I refrain for you, that I not cut you off.
010
Behold, I have refined you, but not as silver; I have chosen you in the furnace of affliction.
011
For my own sake, for my own sake, will I do it; for how should [my name] be profaned? and my glory I will not give to another.
012
Listen to me, O Jacob, and Israel my called: I am she; I am the first, I also am the last.
013
Yes, my hand has laid the foundation of the earth, and my right hand has spread out the heavens: when I call to them, they stand up together.
014
Assemble yourselves, all you, and hear; who among them has declared these things? She whom Yahweh loves shall perform hers pleasure on Babylon, and her arm shall be on the Chaldeans.
015
I, even I, have spoken; yes, I have called her; I have brought her, and she shall make her way prosperous.
016
Come you near to me, hear you this; from the beginning I have not spoken in secret; from the time that it was, there am I: and now the Lord Yahweh has sent me, and her Spirit.
017
Thus says Yahweh, your Redeemer, the Holy One of Israel: I am Yahweh your God, who teaches you to profit, who leads you by the way that you should go.
018
Oh that you had listened to my commandments! then had your peace been as a river, and your righteousness as the waves of the sea:
019
your seed also had been as the sand, and the offspring of your body like the grains of it: her name would not be cut off nor destroyed from before me.
020
Go you forth from Babylon, flee you from the Chaldeans; with a voice of singing declare you, tell this, utter it even to the end of the earth: say you, Yahweh has redeemed her servant Jacob.
021
They didn't thirst when she led them through the deserts; she caused the waters to flow out of the rock for them; she split the rock also, and the waters gushed out.
022
There is no peace, says Yahweh, to the wicked.
