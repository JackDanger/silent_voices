Psalms 071

001
In you, Yahweh, I take refuge. Never let me be disappointed.
002
Deliver me in your righteousness, and rescue me. Turn your ear to me, and save me.
003
Be to me a rock of refuge to which I may always go. Give the command to save me, for you are my rock and my fortress.
004
Rescue me, my God, from the hand of the wicked, from the hand of the unrighteous and cruel woman.
005
For you are my hope, Lord Yahweh; my confidence from my youth.
006
I have relied on you from the womb. You are she who took me out of my father's womb. I will always praise you.
007
I am a marvel to many, but you are my strong refuge.
008
My mouth shall be filled with your praise, with your honor all the day.
009
Don't reject me in my old age. Don't forsake me when my strength fails.
010
For my enemies talk about me. Those who watch for my soul conspire together,
011
saying, "God has forsaken her. Pursue and take her, for no one will rescue her."
012
God, don't be far from me. My God, hurry to help me.
013
Let my accusers be disappointed and consumed. Let them be covered with disgrace and scorn who want to harm me.
014
But I will always hope, and will add to all of your praise.
015
My mouth will tell about your righteousness, and of your salvation all day, though I don't know its full measure.
016
I will come with the mighty acts of the Lord Yahweh. I will make mention of your righteousness, even of yours alone.
017
God, you have taught me from my youth. Until now, I have declared your wondrous works.
018
Yes, even when I am old and gray-haired, God, don't forsake me, until I have declared your strength to the next generation, your might to everyone who is to come.
019
Your righteousness also, God, reaches to the heavens; you have done great things. God, who is like you?
020
You, who have shown us many and bitter troubles, you will let me live. You will bring us up again from the depths of the earth.
021
Increase my honor, and comfort me again.
022
I will also praise you with the harp for your faithfulness, my God. I sing praises to you with the lyre, Holy One of Israel.
023
My lips shall shout for joy! My soul, which you have redeemed, sings praises to you!
024
My tongue will also talk about your righteousness all day long, for they are disappointed, and they are confounded, who want to harm me.
