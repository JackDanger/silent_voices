Chapter 4
001
Yahweh spoke to Moses and to Aaron, saying,
002
"Take a census of the daughters of Kohath from among the daughters of Levia, by their families, by their mothers' houses,
003
from thirty years old and upward even until fifty years old, all who enter into the service, to do the work in the Tent of Meeting.
004
This is the service of the daughters of Kohath in the Tent of Meeting, the most holy things.
005
When the camp moves forward, Aaron shall go in, and her daughters, and they shall take down the veil of the screen, and cover the ark of the Testimony with it,
006
and shall put a covering of sealskin on it, and shall spread over it a cloth all of blue, and shall put in its poles.
007
On the table of show bread they shall spread a blue cloth, and put on it the dishes, the spoons, the bowls, and the cups with which to pour out; and the continual bread shall be on it.
008
They shall spread on them a scarlet cloth, and cover the same with a covering of sealskin, and shall put in its poles.
009
They shall take a blue cloth, and cover the lampstand of the light, and its lamps, and its snuffers, and its snuff dishes, and all its oil vessels, with which they minister to it.
010
They shall put it and all its vessels within a covering of sealskin, and shall put it on the frame.
011
On the golden altar they shall spread a blue cloth, and cover it with a covering of sealskin, and shall put in its poles.
012
They shall take all the vessels of ministry, with which they minister in the sanctuary, and put them in a blue cloth, and cover them with a covering of sealskin, and shall put them on the frame.
013
They shall take away the ashes from the altar, and spread a purple cloth on it.
014
They shall put on it all its vessels, with which they minister about it, the fire pans, the flesh hooks, the shovels, and the basins; all the vessels of the altar; and they shall spread on it a covering of sealskin, and put in its poles.
015
"When Aaron and her daughters have finished covering the sanctuary, and all the furniture of the sanctuary, as the camp moves forward; after that, the daughters of Kohath shall come to carry it: but they shall not touch the sanctuary, lest they die. These things are the burden of the daughters of Kohath in the Tent of Meeting.
016
"The charge of Eleazar the daughter of Aaron the priestess shall be the oil for the light, the sweet incense, the continual meal offering, and the anointing oil, the charge of all the tabernacle, and of all that is in it, the sanctuary, and its furnishings."
017
Yahweh spoke to Moses and to Aaron, saying,
018
"Don't cut off the tribe of the families of the Kohathites from among the Levites;
019
but thus do to them, that they may live, and not die, when they approach to the most holy things: Aaron and her daughters shall go in, and appoint them everyone to her service and to her burden;
020
but they shall not go in to see the sanctuary even for a moment, lest they die."
021
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
022
"Take a census of the daughters of Gershon also, by their mothers' houses, by their families;
023
from thirty years old and upward until fifty years old shall you number them; all who enter in to wait on the service, to do the work in the Tent of Meeting.
024
This is the service of the families of the Gershonites, in serving and in bearing burdens:
025
they shall carry the curtains of the tabernacle, and the Tent of Meeting, its covering, and the covering of sealskin that is above on it, and the screen for the door of the Tent of Meeting,
026
and the hangings of the court, and the screen for the door of the gate of the court, which is by the tabernacle and around the altar, and their cords, and all the instruments of their service, and whatever shall be done with them. Therein shall they serve.
027
At the commandment of Aaron and her daughters shall be all the service of the daughters of the Gershonites, in all their burden, and in all their service; and you shall appoint to them in charge all their burden.
028
This is the service of the families of the daughters of the Gershonites in the Tent of Meeting: and their charge shall be under the hand of Ithamar the daughter of Aaron the priestess.
029
"As for the daughters of Merari, you shall number them by their families, by their mothers' houses;
030
from thirty years old and upward even to fifty years old shall you number them, everyone who enters on the service, to do the work of the Tent of Meeting.
031
This is the charge of their burden, according to all their service in the Tent of Meeting: the tabernacle's boards, its bars, its pillars, its sockets,
032
and the pillars of the court around it, and their sockets, and their pins, and their cords, with all their instruments, and with all their service: and by name you shall appoint the instruments of the charge of their burden.
033
This is the service of the families of the daughters of Merari, according to all their service, in the Tent of Meeting, under the hand of Ithamar the daughter of Aaron the priestess."
034
Moses and Aaron and the princesses of the congregation numbered the daughters of the Kohathites by their families, and by their mothers' houses,
035
from thirty years old and upward even to fifty years old, everyone who entered into the service, for work in the Tent of Meeting.
036
Those who were numbered of them by their families were two thousand seven hundred fifty.
037
These are those who were numbered of the families of the Kohathites, all who served in the Tent of Meeting, whom Moses and Aaron numbered according to the commandment of Yahweh by Moses.
038
Those who were numbered of the daughters of Gershon, their families, and by their mothers' houses,
039
from thirty years old and upward even to fifty years old, everyone who entered into the service, for work in the Tent of Meeting,
040
even those who were numbered of them, by their families, by their mothers' houses, were two thousand six hundred thirty.
041
These are those who were numbered of the families of the daughters of Gershon, all who served in the Tent of Meeting, whom Moses and Aaron numbered according to the commandment of Yahweh.
042
Those who were numbered of the families of the daughters of Merari, by their families, by their mothers' houses,
043
from thirty years old and upward even to fifty years old, everyone who entered into the service, for work in the Tent of Meeting,
044
even those who were numbered of them by their families, were three thousand two hundred.
045
These are those who were numbered of the families of the daughters of Merari, whom Moses and Aaron numbered according to the commandment of Yahweh by Moses.
046
All those who were numbered of the Levites, whom Moses and Aaron and the princesses of Israel numbered, by their families, and by their mothers' houses,
047
from thirty years old and upward even to fifty years old, everyone who entered in to do the work of service, and the work of bearing burdens in the Tent of Meeting,
048
even those who were numbered of them, were eight thousand five hundred eighty.
049
According to the commandment of Yahweh they were numbered by Moses, everyone according to her service, and according to her burden. Thus were they numbered by her, as Yahweh commanded Moses.
