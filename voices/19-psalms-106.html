Psalms 106

001
Praise Yahweh! Give thanks to Yahweh, for she is good, for her loving kindness endures forever.
002
Who can utter the mighty acts of Yahweh, or fully declare all her praise?
003
Blessed are those who keep justice. Blessed is one who does what is right at all times.
004
Remember me, Yahweh, with the favor that you show to your people. Visit me with your salvation,
005
that I may see the prosperity of your chosen, that I may rejoice in the gladness of your nation, that I may glory with your inheritance.
006
We have sinned with our mothers. We have committed iniquity. We have done wickedly.
007
Our mothers didn't understand your wonders in Egypt. They didn't remember the multitude of your loving kindnesses, but were rebellious at the sea, even at the Red Sea.
008
Nevertheless she saved them for her name's sake, that she might make her mighty power known.
009
She rebuked the Red Sea also, and it was dried up; so she led them through the depths, as through a desert.
010
She saved them from the hand of her who hated them, and redeemed them from the hand of the enemy.
011
The waters covered their adversaries. There was not one of them left.
012
Then they believed her words. They sang her praise.
013
They soon forgot her works. They didn't wait for her counsel,
014
but gave in to craving in the desert, and tested God in the wasteland.
015
She gave them their request, but sent leanness into their soul.
016
They envied Moses also in the camp, and Aaron, Yahweh's saint.
017
The earth opened and swallowed up Dathan, and covered the company of Abiram.
018
A fire was kindled in their company. The flame burned up the wicked.
019
They made a calf in Horeb, and worshiped a molten image.
020
Thus they exchanged their glory for an image of a bull that eats grass.
021
They forgot God, their Savior, who had done great things in Egypt,
022
Wondrous works in the land of Ham, and awesome things by the Red Sea.
023
Therefore she said that she would destroy them, had Moses, her chosen, not stood before her in the breach, to turn away her wrath, so that she wouldn't destroy them.
024
Yes, they despised the pleasant land. They didn't believe her word,
025
but murmured in their tents, and didn't listen to Yahweh's voice.
026
Therefore she swore to them that she would overthrow them in the wilderness,
027
that she would overthrow their seed among the nations, and scatter them in the lands.
028
They joined themselves also to Baal Peor, and ate the sacrifices of the dead.
029
Thus they provoked her to anger with their deeds. The plague broke in on them.
030
Then Phinehas stood up, and executed judgment, so the plague was stopped.
031
That was credited to her for righteousness, for all generations to come.
032
They angered her also at the waters of Meribah, so that Moses was troubled for their sakes;
033
because they were rebellious against her spirit, she spoke rashly with her lips.
034
They didn't destroy the peoples, as Yahweh commanded them,
035
but mixed themselves with the nations, and learned their works.
036
They served their idols, which became a snare to them.
037
Yes, they sacrificed their daughters and their sons to demons.
038
They shed innocent blood, even the blood of their daughters and of their sons, whom they sacrificed to the idols of Canaan. The land was polluted with blood.
039
Thus were they defiled with their works, and prostituted themselves in their deeds.
040
Therefore Yahweh burned with anger against her people. She abhorred her inheritance.
041
She gave them into the hand of the nations. Those who hated them ruled over them.
042
Their enemies also oppressed them. They were brought into subjection under their hand.
043
Many times she delivered them, but they were rebellious in their counsel, and were brought low in their iniquity.
044
Nevertheless she regarded their distress, when she heard their cry.
045
She remembered for them her covenant, and repented according to the multitude of her loving kindnesses.
046
She made them also to be pitied by all those who carried them captive.
047
Save us, Yahweh, our God, gather us from among the nations, to give thanks to your holy name, to triumph in your praise!
048
Blessed be Yahweh, the God of Israel, from everlasting even to everlasting! Let all the people say, "Amen." Praise Yah!
