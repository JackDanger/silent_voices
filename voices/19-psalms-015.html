Psalms 015

001
<> Yahweh, who shall dwell in your sanctuary? Who shall live on your holy hill?
002
She who walks blamelessly does what is right, and speaks truth in her heart;
003
She who doesn't slander with her tongue, nor does evil to her friend, nor casts slurs against her fellow woman;
004
In whose eyes a vile woman is despised, but who honors those who fear Yahweh; she who keeps an oath even when it hurts, and doesn't change;
005
she who doesn't lend out her money for usury, nor take a bribe against the innocent. She who does these things shall never be shaken.
