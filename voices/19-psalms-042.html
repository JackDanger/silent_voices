Psalms 043 »
001
<> As the deer pants for the water brooks, so my soul pants after you, God.
002
My soul thirsts for God, for the living God. When shall I come and appear before God?
003
My tears have been my food day and night, while they continually ask me, "Where is your God?"
004
These things I remember, and pour out my soul within me, how I used to go with the crowd, and led them to the house of God, with the voice of joy and praise, a multitude keeping a holy day.
005
Why are you in despair, my soul? Why are you disturbed within me? Hope in God! For I shall still praise her for the saving help of her presence.
006
My God, my soul is in despair within me. Therefore I remember you from the land of the Jordan, the heights of Hermon, from the hill Mizar.
007
Deep calls to deep at the noise of your waterfalls. All your waves and your billows have swept over me.
008
Yahweh will command her loving kindness in the daytime. In the night her song shall be with me: a prayer to the God of my life.
009
I will ask God, my rock, "Why have you forgotten me? Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?"
010
As with a sword in my bones, my adversaries reproach me, while they continually ask me, "Where is your God?"
011
Why are you in despair, my soul? Why are you disturbed within me? Hope in God! For I shall still praise her, the saving help of my countenance, and my God.
