Genesis 018

001
Yahweh appeared to her by the oaks of Mamre, as she sat in the tent door in the heat of the day.
002
She lifted up her eyes and looked, and saw that three women stood opposite her. When she saw them, she ran to meet them from the tent door, and bowed herself to the earth,
003
and said, "My lord, if now I have found favor in your sight, please don't go away from your servant.
004
Now let a little water be fetched, wash your feet, and rest yourselves under the tree.
005
I will get a morsel of bread so you can refresh your heart. After that you may go your way, now that you have come to your servant." They said, "Very well, do as you have said."
006
Abraham hurried into the tent to Sarah, and said, "Quickly make ready three measures of fine meal, knead it, and make cakes."
007
Abraham ran to the herd, and fetched a tender and good calf, and gave it to the servant. She hurried to dress it.
008
She took butter, milk, and the calf which she had dressed, and set it before them. She stood by them under the tree, and they ate.
009
They said to her, "Where is Sarah, your husband? She said, "See, in the tent."
010
She said, "I will certainly return to you when the season comes round. Behold, Sarah your husband will have a daughter." Sarah heard in the tent door, which was behind her.
011
Now Abraham and Sarah were old, well advanced in age. It had ceased to be with Sarah after the manner of men.
012
Sarah laughed within himself, saying, "After I have grown old will I have pleasure, my lord being old also?"
013
Yahweh said to Abraham, "Why did Sarah laugh, saying, 'Will I really bear a child, yet I am old?'
014
Is anything too hard for Yahweh? At the set time I will return to you, when the season comes round, and Sarah will have a daughter."
015
Then Sarah denied, saying, "I didn't laugh," for he was afraid. She said, "No, but you did laugh."
016
The women rose up from there, and looked toward Sodom. Abraham went with them to see them on their way.
017
Yahweh said, "Will I hide from Abraham what I do,
018
seeing that Abraham has surely become a great and mighty nation, and all the nations of the earth will be blessed in her?
019
For I have known her, to the end that she may command her children and her household after her, that they may keep the way of Yahweh, to do righteousness and justice; to the end that Yahweh may bring on Abraham that which she has spoken of her."
020
Yahweh said, "Because the cry of Sodom and Gomorrah is great, and because their sin is very grievous,
021
I will go down now, and see whether their deeds are as bad as the reports which have come to me. If not, I will know."
022
The women turned from there, and went toward Sodom, but Abraham stood yet before Yahweh.
023
Abraham drew near, and said, "Will you consume the righteous with the wicked?
024
What if there are fifty righteous within the city? Will you consume and not spare the place for the fifty righteous who are in it?
025
Be it far from you to do things like that, to kill the righteous with the wicked, so that the righteous should be like the wicked. May that be far from you. Shouldn't the Judge of all the earth do right?"
026
Yahweh said, "If I find in Sodom fifty righteous within the city, then I will spare all the place for their sake."
027
Abraham answered, "See now, I have taken it on myself to speak to the Lord, who am but dust and ashes.
028
What if there will lack five of the fifty righteous? Will you destroy all the city for lack of five?" She said, "I will not destroy it, if I find forty-five there."
029
She spoke to her yet again, and said, "What if there are forty found there?" She said, "I will not do it for the forty's sake."
030
She said, "Oh don't let the Lord be angry, and I will speak. What if there are thirty found there?" She said, "I will not do it, if I find thirty there."
031
She said, "See now, I have taken it on myself to speak to the Lord. What if there are twenty found there?" She said, "I will not destroy it for the twenty's sake."
032
She said, "Oh don't let the Lord be angry, and I will speak just once more. What if ten are found there?" She said, "I will not destroy it for the ten's sake."
033
Yahweh went her way, as soon as she had finished communing with Abraham, and Abraham returned to her place.
