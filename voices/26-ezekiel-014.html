Ezekiel 014

001
Then came certain of the elders of Israel to me, and sat before me.
002
The word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
003
Daughter of woman, these women have taken their idols into their heart, and put the stumbling block of their iniquity before their face: should I be inquired of at all by them?
004
Therefore speak to them, and tell them, Thus says the Lord Yahweh: Every woman of the house of Israel who takes her idols into her heart, and puts the stumbling block of her iniquity before her face, and comes to the prophet; I Yahweh will answer her therein according to the multitude of her idols;
005
that I may take the house of Israel in their own heart, because they are all estranged from me through their idols.
006
Therefore tell the house of Israel, Thus says the Lord Yahweh: Return you, and turn yourselves from your idols; and turn away your faces from all your abominations.
007
For everyone of the house of Israel, or of the strangers who sojourn in Israel, who separates herself from me, and takes her idols into her heart, and puts the stumbling block of her iniquity before her face, and comes to the prophet to inquire for herself of me; I Yahweh will answer her by myself:
008
and I will set my face against that woman, and will make her an astonishment, for a sign and a proverb, and I will cut her off from the midst of my people; and you shall know that I am Yahweh.
009
If the prophet be deceived and speak a word, I, Yahweh, have deceived that prophet, and I will stretch out my hand on her, and will destroy her from the midst of my people Israel.
010
They shall bear their iniquity: the iniquity of the prophet shall be even as the iniquity of her who seeks to her;
011
that the house of Israel may go no more astray from me, neither defile themselves any more with all their transgressions; but that they may be my people, and I may be their God, says the Lord Yahweh.
012
The word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
013
Daughter of woman, when a land sins against me by committing a trespass, and I stretch out my hand on it, and break the staff of the bread of it, and send famine on it, and cut off from it woman and animal;
014
though these three women, Noa, Danielle, and Job, were in it, they should deliver but their own souls by their righteousness, says the Lord Yahweh.
015
If I cause evil animals to pass through the land, and they ravage it, and it be made desolate, so that no woman may pass through because of the animals;
016
though these three women were in it, as I live, says the Lord Yahweh, they should deliver neither daughters nor sons; they only should be delivered, but the land should be desolate.
017
Or if I bring a sword on that land, and say, Sword, go through the land; so that I cut off from it woman and animal;
018
though these three women were in it, as I live, says the Lord Yahweh, they should deliver neither daughters nor sons, but they only should be delivered themselves.
019
Or if I send a pestilence into that land, and pour out my wrath on it in blood, to cut off from it woman and animal;
020
though Noa, Danielle, and Job, were in it, as I live, says the Lord Yahweh, they should deliver neither daughter nor son; they should but deliver their own souls by their righteousness.
021
For thus says the Lord Yahweh: How much more when I send my four sore judgments on Jerusalem, the sword, and the famine, and the evil animals, and the pestilence, to cut off from it woman and animal!
022
Yet, behold, therein shall be left a remnant that shall be carried forth, both daughters and sons: behold, they shall come forth to you, and you shall see their way and their doings; and you shall be comforted concerning the evil that I have brought on Jerusalem, even concerning all that I have brought on it.
023
They shall comfort you, when you see their way and their doings; and you shall know that I have not done without cause all that I have done in it, says the Lord Yahweh.
