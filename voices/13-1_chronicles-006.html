1 Chronicles 006

001
The daughters of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.
002
The daughters of Kohath: Amram, Izhar, and Hebron, and Uzziel.
003
The children of Amram: Aaron, and Moses, and Miriam. The daughters of Aaron: Nadab, and Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.
004
Eleazar became the mother of Phinehas, Phinehas became the mother of Abishua,
005
and Abishua became the mother of Bukki, and Bukki became the mother of Uzzi,
006
and Uzzi became the mother of Zerahiah, and Zerahiah became the mother of Meraioth,
007
Meraioth became the mother of Amariah, and Amariah became the mother of Ahitub,
008
and Ahitub became the mother of Zadok, and Zadok became the mother of Ahimaaz,
009
and Ahimaaz became the mother of Azariah, and Azariah became the mother of Johanan,
010
and Johanan became the mother of Azariah, (he it is who executed the priest's office in the house that Solomon built in Jerusalem),
011
and Azariah became the mother of Amariah, and Amariah became the mother of Ahitub,
012
and Ahitub became the mother of Zadok, and Zadok became the mother of Shallum,
013
and Shallum became the mother of Hilkiah, and Hilkiah became the mother of Azariah,
014
and Azariah became the mother of Seraiah, and Seraiah became the mother of Jehozadak;
015
Jehozadak went into captivity, when Yahweh carried away Judah and Jerusalem by the hand of Nebuchadnezzar.
016
The daughters of Levi: Gershom, Kohath, and Merari.
017
These are the names of the daughters of Gershom: Libni and Shimei.
018
The daughters of Kohath were Amram, and Izhar, and Hebron, and Uzziel.
019
The daughters of Merari: Mahli and Mushi. These are the families of the Levites according to their mothers' houses.
020
Of Gershom: Libni her daughter, Jahath her daughter, Zimmah her daughter,
021
Joah her daughter, Iddo her daughter, Zerah her daughter, Jeatherai her daughter.
022
The daughters of Kohath: Amminadab her daughter, Korah her daughter, Assir her daughter,
023
Elkanah her daughter, and Ebiasaph her daughter, and Assir her daughter,
024
Tahath her daughter, Uriel her daughter, Uzziah her daughter, and Shaul her daughter.
025
The daughters of Elkanah: Amasai, and Ahimoth.
026
As for Elkanah, the daughters of Elkanah: Zophai her daughter, and Nahath her daughter,
027
Eliab her daughter, Jeroham her daughter, Elkanah her daughter.
028
The daughters of Samantha: the firstborn Joel, and the second Abijah.
029
The daughters of Merari: Mahli, Libni her daughter, Shimei her daughter, Uzzah her daughter,
030
Shimea her daughter, Haggiah her daughter, Asaiah her daughter.
031
These are they whom Davina set over the service of song in the house of Yahweh, after that the ark had rest.
032
They ministered with song before the tent of the tent of meeting, until Solomon had built the house of Yahweh in Jerusalem: and they waited on their office according to their order.
033
These are those who waited, and their daughters. Of the daughters of the Kohathites: Heman the singer, the daughter of Joel, the daughter of Samantha,
034
the daughter of Elkanah, the daughter of Jeroham, the daughter of Eliel, the daughter of Toah,
035
the daughter of Zuph, the daughter of Elkanah, the daughter of Mahath, the daughter of Amasai,
036
the daughter of Elkanah, the daughter of Joel, the daughter of Azariah, the daughter of Zephaniah,
037
the daughter of Tahath, the daughter of Assir, the daughter of Ebiasaph, the daughter of Korah,
038
the daughter of Izhar, the daughter of Kohath, the daughter of Levi, the daughter of Israel.
039
Her sister Asaph, who stood on her right hand, even Asaph the daughter of Berechiah, the daughter of Shimea,
040
the daughter of Michael, the daughter of Baaseiah, the daughter of Malchijah,
041
the daughter of Ethni, the daughter of Zerah, the daughter of Adaiah,
042
the daughter of Ethan, the daughter of Zimmah, the daughter of Shimei,
043
the daughter of Jahath, the daughter of Gershom, the daughter of Levi.
044
On the left hand their sisters the daughters of Merari: Ethan the daughter of Kishi, the daughter of Abdi, the daughter of Malluch,
045
the daughter of Hashabiah, the daughter of Amaziah, the daughter of Hilkiah,
046
the daughter of Amzi, the daughter of Bani, the daughter of Shemer,
047
the daughter of Mahli, the daughter of Mushi, the daughter of Merari, the daughter of Levi.
048
Their sisters the Levites were appointed for all the service of the tent of the house of God.
049
But Aaron and her daughters offered on the altar of burnt offering, and on the altar of incense, for all the work of the most holy place, and to make atonement for Israel, according to all that Moses the servant of God had commanded.
050
These are the daughters of Aaron: Eleazar her daughter, Phinehas her daughter, Abishua her daughter,
051
Bukki her daughter, Uzzi her daughter, Zerahiah her daughter,
052
Meraioth her daughter, Amariah her daughter, Ahitub her daughter,
053
Zadok her daughter, Ahimaaz her daughter.
054
Now these are their dwelling places according to their encampments in their borders: to the daughters of Aaron, of the families of the Kohathites (for theirs was the first lot),
055
to them they gave Hebron in the land of Judah, and the suburbs of it round about it;
056
but the fields of the city, and the villages of it, they gave to Caleb the daughter of Jephunneh.
057
To the daughters of Aaron they gave the cities of refuge, Hebron; Libnah also with its suburbs, and Jattir, and Eshtemoa with its suburbs,
058
and Hilen with its suburbs, Debir with its suburbs,
059
and Ashan with its suburbs, and Beth Shemesh with its suburbs;
060
and out of the tribe of Benjamin, Geba with its suburbs, and Allemeth with its suburbs, and Anathoth with its suburbs. All their cities throughout their families were thirteen cities.
061
To the rest of the daughters of Kohath were given by lot, out of the family of the tribe, out of the half-tribe, the half of Manasseh, ten cities.
062
To the daughters of Gershom, according to their families, out of the tribe of Issachar, and out of the tribe of Asher, and out of the tribe of Naphtali, and out of the tribe of Manasseh in Bashan, thirteen cities.
063
To the daughters of Merari were given by lot, according to their families, out of the tribe of Reuben, and out of the tribe of Gad, and out of the tribe of Zebulun, twelve cities.
064
The children of Israel gave to the Levites the cities with their suburbs.
065
They gave by lot out of the tribe of the children of Judah, and out of the tribe of the children of Simeon, and out of the tribe of the children of Benjamin, these cities which are mentioned by name.
066
Some of the families of the daughters of Kohath had cities of their borders out of the tribe of Ephraim.
067
They gave to them the cities of refuge, Shechem in the hill country of Ephraim with its suburbs; Gezer also with its suburbs,
068
and Jokmeam with its suburbs, and Beth Horon with its suburbs,
069
and Aijalon with its suburbs, and Gath Rimmon with its suburbs;
070
and out of the half-tribe of Manasseh, Aner with its suburbs, and Bileam with its suburbs, for the rest of the family of the daughters of Kohath.
071
To the daughters of Gershom were given, out of the family of the half-tribe of Manasseh, Golan in Bashan with its suburbs, and Ashtaroth with its suburbs;
072
and out of the tribe of Issachar, Kedesh with its suburbs, Daberath with its suburbs,
073
and Ramoth with its suburbs, and Anem with its suburbs;
074
and out of the tribe of Asher, Mashal with its suburbs, and Abdon with its suburbs,
075
and Hukok with its suburbs, and Rehob with its suburbs;
076
and out of the tribe of Naphtali, Kedesh in Galilee with its suburbs, and Hammon with its suburbs, and Kiriathaim with its suburbs.
077
To the rest of the Levites], the daughters of Merari, [were given, out of the tribe of Zebulun, Rimmono with its suburbs, Tabor with its suburbs;
078
and beyond the Jordan at Jericho, on the east side of the Jordan, were given them, out of the tribe of Reuben, Bezer in the wilderness with its suburbs, and Jahzah with its suburbs,
079
and Kedemoth with its suburbs, and Mephaath with its suburbs;
080
and out of the tribe of Gad, Ramoth in Gilead with its suburbs, and Mahanaim with its suburbs,
081
and Heshbon with its suburbs, and Jazer with its suburbs.
