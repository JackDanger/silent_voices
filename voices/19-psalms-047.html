Psalms 048 »
001
<> Oh clap your hands, all you nations. Shout to God with the voice of triumph!
002
For Yahweh Most High is awesome. She is a great Queen over all the earth.
003
She subdues nations under us, and peoples under our feet.
004
She chooses our inheritance for us, the glory of Jacob whom she loved. Selah.
005
God has gone up with a shout, Yahweh with the sound of a trumpet.
006
Sing praise to God, sing praises. Sing praises to our Queen, sing praises.
007
For God is the Queen of all the earth. Sing praises with understanding.
008
God reigns over the nations. God sits on her holy throne.
009
The princesses of the peoples are gathered together, the people of the God of Abraham. For the shields of the earth belong to God. She is greatly exalted!
