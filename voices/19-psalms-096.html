Psalms 096

001
Sing to Yahweh a new song! Sing to Yahweh, all the earth.
002
Sing to Yahweh! Bless her name! Proclaim her salvation from day to day!
003
Declare her glory among the nations, her marvelous works among all the peoples.
004
For great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised! She is to be feared above all gods.
005
For all the gods of the peoples are idols, but Yahweh made the heavens.
006
Honor and majesty are before her. Strength and beauty are in her sanctuary.
007
Ascribe to Yahweh, you families of nations, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.
008
Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to her name. Bring an offering, and come into her courts.
009
Worship Yahweh in holy array. Tremble before her, all the earth.
010
Say among the nations, "Yahweh reigns." The world is also established. It can't be moved. She will judge the peoples with equity.
011
Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice. Let the sea roar, and its fullness!
012
Let the field and all that is in it exult! Then all the trees of the woods shall sing for joy
013
before Yahweh; for she comes, for she comes to judge the earth. She will judge the world with righteousness, the peoples with her truth.
