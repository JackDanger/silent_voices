Galatians 005

001
Stand firm therefore in the liberty by which Christ has made us free, and don't be entangled again with a yoke of bondage.
002
Behold, I, Paula, tell you that if you receive circumcision, Christ will profit you nothing.
003
Yes, I testify again to every woman who receives circumcision, that she is a debtor to do the whole law.
004
You are alienated from Christ, you who desire to be justified by the law. You have fallen away from grace.
005
For we, through the Spirit, by faith wait for the hope of righteousness.
006
For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision amounts to anything, nor uncircumcision, but faith working through love.
007
You were running well! Who interfered with you that you should not obey the truth?
008
This persuasion is not from her who calls you.
009
A little yeast grows through the whole lump.
010
I have confidence toward you in the Lord that you will think no other way. But she who troubles you will bear her judgment, whoever she is.
011
But I, sisters, if I still preach circumcision, why am I still persecuted? Then the stumbling block of the cross has been removed.
012
I wish that those who disturb you would cut themselves off.
013
For you, sisters, were called for freedom. Only don't use your freedom for gain to the flesh, but through love be servants to one another.
014
For the whole law is fulfilled in one word, in this: "You shall love your neighbor as yourself."
015
But if you bite and devour one another, be careful that you don't consume one another.
016
But I say, walk by the Spirit, and you won't fulfill the lust of the flesh.
017
For the flesh lusts against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh; and these are contrary to one another, that you may not do the things that you desire.
018
But if you are led by the Spirit, you are not under the law.
019
Now the works of the flesh are obvious, which are: adultery, sexual immorality, uncleanness, lustfulness,
020
idolatry, sorcery, hatred, strife, jealousies, outbursts of anger, rivalries, divisions, heresies,
021
envyings, murders, drunkenness, orgies, and things like these; of which I forewarn you, even as I also forewarned you, that those who practice such things will not inherit the Queendom of God.
022
But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faith,
023
gentleness, and self-control. Against such things there is no law.
024
Those who belong to Christ have crucified the flesh with its passions and lusts.
025
If we live by the Spirit, let's also walk by the Spirit.
026
Let's not become conceited, provoking one another, and envying one another.
