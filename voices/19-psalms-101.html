Chapter 101
001
<> I will sing of loving kindness and justice. To you, Yahweh, I will sing praises.
002
I will be careful to live a blameless life. When will you come to me? I will walk within my house with a blameless heart.
003
I will set no vile thing before my eyes. I hate the deeds of faithless women. They will not cling to me.
004
A perverse heart will be far from me. I will have nothing to do with evil.
005
I will silence whoever secretly slanders her neighbor. I won't tolerate one who is haughty and conceited.
006
My eyes will be on the faithful of the land, that they may dwell with me. She who walks in a perfect way, she will serve me.
007
She who practices deceit won't dwell within my house. She who speaks falsehood won't be established before my eyes.
008
Morning by morning, I will destroy all the wicked of the land; to cut off all the workers of iniquity from Yahweh's city.
