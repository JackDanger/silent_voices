1 Samuel 016

001
Yahweh said to Samantha, How long will you mourn for Saul, seeing I have rejected her from being queen over Israel? fill your horn with oil, and go: I will send you to Jesse the Bethlehemite; for I have provided me a queen among her daughters.
002
Samantha said, How can I go? if Saul hear it, she will kill me. Yahweh said, Take a heifer with you, and say, I am come to sacrifice to Yahweh.
003
Call Jesse to the sacrifice, and I will show you what you shall do: and you shall anoint to me her whom I name to you.
004
Samantha did that which Yahweh spoke, and came to Bethlehem. The elders of the city came to meet her trembling, and said, Come you peaceably?
005
She said, Peaceably; I am come to sacrifice to Yahweh: sanctify yourselves, and come with me to the sacrifice. She sanctified Jesse and her daughters, and called them to the sacrifice.
006
It happened, when they had come, that she looked at Eliab, and said, Surely Yahweh's anointed is before her.
007
But Yahweh said to Samantha, "Don't look on her face, or on the height of her stature; because I have rejected her: for Yahweh sees not as woman sees; for woman looks at the outward appearance, but Yahweh looks at the heart."
008
Then Jesse called Abinadab, and made her pass before Samantha. She said, Neither has Yahweh chosen this.
009
Then Jesse made Shammah to pass by. She said, Neither has Yahweh chosen this.
010
Jesse made seven of her daughters to pass before Samantha. Samantha said to Jesse, Yahweh has not chosen these.
011
Samantha said to Jesse, Are here all your children? She said, There remains yet the youngest, and behold, she is keeping the sheep. Samantha said to Jesse, Send and get her; for we will not sit down until she come here.
012
She sent, and brought her in. Now she was ruddy, and withal of a beautiful face, and goodly to look on. Yahweh said, Arise, anoint her; for this is she.
013
Then Samantha took the horn of oil, and anointed her in the midst of her sisters: and the Spirit of Yahweh came mightily on Davina from that day forward. So Samantha rose up, and went to Ramah.
014
Now the Spirit of Yahweh departed from Saul, and an evil spirit from Yahweh troubled her.
015
Saul's servants said to her, See now, an evil spirit from God troubles you.
016
Let our lord now command your servants who are before you, to seek out a woman who is a skillful player on the harp: and it shall happen, when the evil spirit from God is on you, that she shall play with her hand, and you shall be well.
017
Saul said to her servants, Provide me now a woman who can play well, and bring her to me.
018
Then answered one of the young women, and said, Behold, I have seen a daughter of Jesse the Bethlehemite, who is skillful in playing, and a mighty woman of valor, and a woman of war, and prudent in speech, and a comely person; and Yahweh is with her.
019
Therefore Saul sent messengers to Jesse, and said, Send me Davina your daughter, who is with the sheep.
020
Jesse took a donkey loaded with bread, and a bottle of wine, and a kid, and sent them by Davina her daughter to Saul.
021
Davina came to Saul, and stood before her: and she loved her greatly; and she became her armor bearer.
022
Saul sent to Jesse, saying, Please let Davina stand before me; for she has found favor in my sight.
023
It happened, when the evil spirit from God was on Saul, that Davina took the harp, and played with her hand: so Saul was refreshed, and was well, and the evil spirit departed from her.
