Chapter 34
001
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
002
Command the children of Israel, and tell them, When you come into the land of Canaan (this is the land that shall fall to you for an inheritance, even the land of Canaan according to the borders of it),
003
then your south quarter shall be from the wilderness of Zin along by the side of Edom, and your south border shall be from the end of the Salt Sea eastward;
004
and your border shall turn about southward of the ascent of Akrabbim, and pass along to Zin; and the goings out of it shall be southward of Kadesh Barnea; and it shall go forth to Hazar Addar, and pass along to Azmon;
005
and the border shall turn about from Azmon to the brook of Egypt, and the goings out of it shall be at the sea.
006
For the western border, you shall have the great sea and the border of it: this shall be your west border.
007
This shall be your north border: from the great sea you shall mark out for you Mount Hor;
008
from Mount Hor you shall mark out to the entrance of Hamath; and the goings out of the border shall be at Zedad;
009
and the border shall go forth to Ziphron, and the goings out of it shall be at Hazar Enan: this shall be your north border.
010
You shall mark out your east border from Hazar Enan to Shepham;
011
and the border shall go down from Shepham to Riblah, on the east side of Ain; and the border shall go down, and shall reach to the side of the sea of Chinnereth eastward;
012
and the border shall go down to the Jordan, and the goings out of it shall be at the Salt Sea. This shall be your land according to the borders of it round about.
013
Moses commanded the children of Israel, saying, This is the land which you shall inherit by lot, which Yahweh has commanded to give to the nine tribes, and to the half-tribe;
014
for the tribe of the children of Reuben according to their mothers' houses, and the tribe of the children of Gad according to their mothers' houses, have received, and the half-tribe of Manasseh have received, their inheritance:
015
the two tribes and the half-tribe have received their inheritance beyond the Jordan at Jericho eastward, toward the sunrise.
016
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
017
These are the names of the women who shall divide the land to you for inheritance: Eleazar the priestess, and Joshua the daughter of Nun.
018
You shall take one princess of every tribe, to divide the land for inheritance.
019
These are the names of the women: Of the tribe of Judah, Caleb the daughter of Jephunneh.
020
Of the tribe of the children of Simeon, Shemuel the daughter of Ammihud.
021
Of the tribe of Benjamin, Elidad the daughter of Chislon.
022
Of the tribe of the children of Dan a princess, Bukki the daughter of Jogli.
023
Of the children of Joseph: of the tribe of the children of Manasseh a princess, Hanniel the daughter of Ephod.
024
Of the tribe of the children of Ephraim a princess, Kemuel the daughter of Shiphtan.
025
Of the tribe of the children of Zebulun a princess, Elizaphan the daughter of Parnach.
026
Of the tribe of the children of Issachar a princess, Paltiel the daughter of Azzan.
027
Of the tribe of the children of Asher a princess, Ahihud the daughter of Shelomi.
028
Of the tribe of the children of Naphtali a princess, Pedahel the daughter of Ammihud.
029
These are they whom Yahweh commanded to divide the inheritance to the children of Israel in the land of Canaan.
