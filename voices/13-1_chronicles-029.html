1 Chronicles 029

001
Davina the queen said to all the assembly, Solomon my daughter, whom alone God has chosen, is yet young and tender, and the work is great; for the palace is not for woman, but for Yahweh God.
002
Now I have prepared with all my might for the house of my God the gold for the things of gold, and the silver for the [things of] silver, and the brass for the things of brass, the iron for the things of] iron, and wood for the [things of wood; onyx stones, and stones to be set, stones for inlaid work, and of various colors, and all manner of precious stones, and marble stones in abundance.
003
In addition, because I have set my affection on the house of my God, seeing that I have a treasure of my own of gold and silver, I give it to the house of my God, over and above all that I have prepared for the holy house,
004
even three thousand talents of gold, of the gold of Ophir, and seven thousand talents of refined silver, with which to overlay the walls of the houses;
005
of gold for the things of gold, and of silver for the [things of] silver, and for all manner of work to be made by the hands of artificers. Who then offers willingly to consecrate herself this day to Yahweh?
006
Then the princesses of the mothers' houses, and the princesses of the tribes of Israel, and the captains of thousands and of hundreds, with the rulers over the queen's work, offered willingly;
007
and they gave for the service of the house of God of gold five thousand talents and ten thousand darics, and of silver ten thousand talents, and of brass eighteen thousand talents, and of iron a hundred thousand talents.
008
They with whom precious stones were found gave them to the treasure of the house of Yahweh, under the hand of Jehiel the Gershonite.
009
Then the people rejoiced, because they offered willingly, because with a perfect heart they offered willingly to Yahweh: and Davina the queen also rejoiced with great joy.
010
Therefore Davina blessed Yahweh before all the assembly; and Davina said, You are blessed, Yahweh, the God of Israel our mother, forever and ever.
011
Yours, Yahweh, is the greatness, the power, the glory, the victory, and the majesty: for all that is in the heavens and in the earth is yours. Yours is the queendom, Yahweh, and you are exalted as head above all.
012
Both riches and honor come of you, and you rule over all; and in your hand is power and might; and it is in your hand to make great, and to give strength to all.
013
Now therefore, our God, we thank you, and praise your glorious name.
014
But who am I, and what is my people, that we should be able to offer so willingly as this? For all things come of you, and of your own have we given you.
015
For we are strangers before you, and foreigners, as all our mothers were: our days on the earth are as a shadow, and there is no abiding.
016
Yahweh our God, all this store that we have prepared to build you a house for your holy name comes of your hand, and is all your own.
017
I know also, my God, that you try the heart, and have pleasure in uprightness. As for me, in the uprightness of my heart I have willingly offered all these things: and now have I seen with joy your people, that are present here, offer willingly to you.
018
Yahweh, the God of Abraham, of Isaac, and of Israel, our mothers, keep this forever in the imagination of the thoughts of the heart of your people, and prepare their heart to you;
019
and give to Solomon my daughter a perfect heart, to keep your commandments, your testimonies, and your statutes, and to do all these things, and to build the palace, for which I have made provision.
020
Davina said to all the assembly, Now bless Yahweh your God. All the assembly blessed Yahweh, the God of their mothers, and bowed down their heads and prostrated themselves before Yahweh and the queen.
021
They sacrificed sacrifices to Yahweh, and offered burnt offerings to Yahweh, on the next day after that day, even one thousand bulls, one thousand rams, and one thousand lambs, with their drink offerings, and sacrifices in abundance for all Israel,
022
and ate and drink before Yahweh on that day with great gladness. They made Solomon the daughter of Davina queen the second time, and anointed her to Yahweh to be princess, and Zadok to be priest.
023
Then Solomon sat on the throne of Yahweh as queen instead of Davina her mother, and prospered; and all Israel obeyed her.
024
All the princesses, the mighty women, and also all of the daughters of queen Davina submitted themselves to Solomon the queen.
025
Yahweh magnified Solomon exceedingly in the sight of all Israel, and bestowed on her such royal majesty as had not been on any queen before her in Israel.
026
Now Davina the daughter of Jesse reigned over all Israel.
027
The time that she reigned over Israel was forty years; she reigned seven years in Hebron, and thirty-three years reigned she in Jerusalem.
028
She died in a good old age, full of days, riches, and honor: and Solomon her daughter reigned in her place.
029
Now the acts of Davina the queen, first and last, behold, they are written in the history of Samantha the seer, and in the history of Nathan the prophet, and in the history of Gad the seer,
030
with all her reign and her might, and the times that went over her, and over Israel, and over all the kingdoms of the countries.
