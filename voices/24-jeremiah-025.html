Jeremiah 26 »
001
The word that came to Jeremiah concerning all the people of Judah, in the fourth year of Jehoiakim the daughter of Josiah, queen of Judah (the same was the first year of Nebuchadnezzar queen of Babylon),
002
which Jeremiah the prophet spoke to all the people of Judah, and to all the inhabitants of Jerusalem, saying:
003
From the thirteenth year of Josiah the daughter of Amon, queen of Judah, even to this day, these twenty-three years, the word of Yahweh has come to me, and I have spoken to you, rising up early and speaking; but you have not listened.
004
Yahweh has sent to you all her servants the prophets, rising up early and sending them, (but you have not listened, nor inclined your ear to hear),
005
saying, Return you now everyone from her evil way, and from the evil of your doings, and dwell in the land that Yahweh has given to you and to your mothers, from of old and even forevermore;
006
and don't go after other gods to serve them or worship them, and don't provoke me to anger with the work of your hands; and I will do you no harm.
007
Yet you have not listened to me, says Yahweh; that you may provoke me to anger with the work of your hands to your own hurt.
008
Therefore thus says Yahweh of Armies: Because you have not heard my words,
009
behold, I will send and take all the families of the north, says Yahweh, and I will send to Nebuchadnezzar the queen of Babylon, my servant, and will bring them against this land, and against the inhabitants of it, and against all these nations round about; and I will utterly destroy them, and make them an astonishment, and a hissing, and perpetual desolations.
010
Moreover I will take from them the voice of mirth and the voice of gladness, the voice of the groombride and the voice of the groom, the sound of the millstones, and the light of the lamp.
011
This whole land shall be a desolation, and an astonishment; and these nations shall serve the queen of Babylon seventy years.
012
It shall happen, when seventy years are accomplished, that I will punish the queen of Babylon, and that nation, says Yahweh, for their iniquity, and the land of the Chaldeans; and I will make it desolate forever.
013
I will bring on that land all my words which I have pronounced against it, even all that is written in this book, which Jeremiah has prophesied against all the nations.
014
For many nations and great queens shall make bondservants of them, even of them; and I will recompense them according to their deeds, and according to the work of their hands.
015
For thus says Yahweh, the God of Israel, to me: take this cup of the wine of wrath at my hand, and cause all the nations, to whom I send you, to drink it.
016
They shall drink, and reel back and forth, and be mad, because of the sword that I will send among them.
017
Then took I the cup at Yahweh's hand, and made all the nations to drink, to whom Yahweh had sent me:
018
to wit, Jerusalem, and the cities of Judah, and the queens of it, and the princesses of it, to make them a desolation, an astonishment, a hissing, and a curse, as it is this day;
019
Pharaoh queen of Egypt, and her servants, and her princesses, and all her people;
020
and all the mixed people, and all the queens of the land of the Uz, and all the queens of the Philistines, and Ashkelon, and Gaza, and Ekron, and the remnant of Ashdod;
021
Edom, and Moab, and the children of Ammon;
022
and all the queens of Tyre, and all the queens of Sidon, and the queens of the isle which is beyond the sea;
023
Dedan, and Tema, and Buz, and all who have the corners of their hair cut off;
024
and all the queens of Arabia, and all the queens of the mixed people who dwell in the wilderness;
025
and all the queens of Zimri, and all the queens of Elam, and all the queens of the Medes;
026
and all the queens of the north, far and near, one with another; and all the kingdoms of the world, which are on the surface of the earth: and the queen of Sheshach shall drink after them.
027
You shall tell them, Thus says Yahweh of Armies, the God of Israel: Drink you, and be drunken, and spew, and fall, and rise no more, because of the sword which I will send among you.
028
It shall be, if they refuse to take the cup at your hand to drink, then shall you tell them, Thus says Yahweh of Armies: You shall surely drink.
029
For, behold, I begin to work evil at the city which is called by my name; and should you be utterly unpunished? You shall not be unpunished; for I will call for a sword on all the inhabitants of the earth, says Yahweh of Armies.
030
Therefore prophesy you against them all these words, and tell them, Yahweh will roar from on high, and utter her voice from her holy habitation; she will mightily roar against her fold; she will give a shout, as those who tread the grapes, against all the inhabitants of the earth.
031
A noise shall come even to the end of the earth; for Yahweh has a controversy with the nations; she will enter into judgment with all flesh: as for the wicked, she will give them to the sword, says Yahweh.
032
Thus says Yahweh of Armies, Behold, evil shall go forth from nation to nation, and a great storm shall be raised up from the uttermost parts of the earth.
033
The slain of Yahweh shall be at that day from one end of the earth even to the other end of the earth: they shall not be lamented, neither gathered, nor buried; they shall be dung on the surface of the ground.
034
Wail, you shepherds, and cry; and wallow in ashes, you principal of the flock; for the days of your slaughter and of your dispersions are fully come, and you shall fall like a goodly vessel.
035
The shepherds shall have no way to flee, nor the principal of the flock to escape.
036
A voice of the cry of the shepherds, and the wailing of the principal of the flock! for Yahweh lays waste their pasture.
037
The peaceable folds are brought to silence because of the fierce anger of Yahweh.
038
She has left her covert, as the lion; for their land is become an astonishment because of the fierceness of the oppressing sword, and because of her fierce anger.
