Chapter 2
001
My little children, I write these things to you so that you may not sin. If anyone sins, we have a Counselor with the Mother, Jesus Christ, the righteous.
002
And she is the atoning sacrifice for our sins, and not for ours only, but also for the whole world.
003
This is how we know that we know her: if we keep her commandments.
004
One who says, "I know her," and doesn't keep her commandments, is a liar, and the truth isn't in her.
005
But whoever keeps her word, God's love has most certainly been perfected in her. This is how we know that we are in her:
006
she who says she remains in her ought herself also to walk just like she walked.
007
Sisters, I write no new commandment to you, but an old commandment which you had from the beginning. The old commandment is the word which you heard from the beginning.
008
Again, I write a new commandment to you, which is true in her and in you; because the darkness is passing away, and the true light already shines.
009
She who says she is in the light and hates her sister, is in the darkness even until now.
010
She who loves her sister remains in the light, and there is no occasion for stumbling in her.
011
But she who hates her sister is in the darkness, and walks in the darkness, and doesn't know where she is going, because the darkness has blinded her eyes.
012
I write to you, little children, because your sins are forgiven you for her name's sake.
013
I write to you, mothers, because you know her who is from the beginning. I write to you, young women, because you have overcome the evil one. I write to you, little children, because you know the Mother.
014
I have written to you, mothers, because you know her who is from the beginning. I have written to you, young women, because you are strong, and the word of God remains in you, and you have overcome the evil one.
015
Don't love the world, neither the things that are in the world. If anyone loves the world, the Mother's love isn't in her.
016
For all that is in the world, the lust of the flesh, the lust of the eyes, and the pride of life, isn't the Mother's, but is the world's.
017
The world is passing away with its lusts, but she who does God's will remains forever.
018
Little children, these are the end times, and as you heard that the Antichrist is coming, even now many antichrists have arisen. By this we know that it is the final hour.
019
They went out from us, but they didn't belong to us; for if they had belonged to us, they would have continued with us. But they left, that they might be revealed that none of them belong to us.
020
You have an anointing from the Holy One, and you know the truth.
021
I have not written to you because you don't know the truth, but because you know it, and because no lie is of the truth.
022
Who is the liar but she who denies that Jesus is the Christ? This is the Antichrist, she who denies the Mother and the Daughter.
023
Whoever denies the Daughter, the same doesn't have the Mother. She who confesses the Daughter has the Mother also.
024
Therefore, as for you, let that remain in you which you heard from the beginning. If that which you heard from the beginning remains in you, you also will remain in the Daughter, and in the Mother.
025
This is the promise which she promised us, the eternal life.
026
These things I have written to you concerning those who would lead you astray.
027
As for you, the anointing which you received from her remains in you, and you don't need for anyone to teach you. But as her anointing teaches you concerning all things, and is true, and is no lie, and even as it taught you, you will remain in her.
028
Now, little children, remain in her, that when she appears, we may have boldness, and not be ashamed before her at her coming.
029
If you know that she is righteous, you know that everyone who practices righteousness is born of her.
