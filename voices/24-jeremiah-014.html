Jeremiah 014

001
The word of Yahweh that came to Jeremiah concerning the drought.
002
Judah mourns, and the gates of it languish, they sit in black on the ground; and the cry of Jerusalem is gone up.
003
Their nobles send their little ones to the waters: they come to the cisterns, and find no water; they return with their vessels empty; they are disappointed and confounded, and cover their heads.
004
Because of the ground which is cracked, because no rain has been in the land, the plowmen are disappointed, they cover their heads.
005
Yes, the hind also in the field calves, and forsakes his young, because there is no grass.
006
The wild donkeys stand on the bare heights, they pant for air like jackals; their eyes fail, because there is no herbage.
007
Though our iniquities testify against us, work you for your name's sake, Yahweh; for our backslidings are many; we have sinned against you.
008
You hope of Israel, the Savior of it in the time of trouble, why should you be as a foreigner in the land, and as a wayfaring woman who turns aside to stay for a night?
009
Why should you be like a scared woman, as a mighty woman who can't save? yet you, Yahweh, are in the midst of us, and we are called by your name; don't leave us.
010
Thus says Yahweh to this people, Even so have they loved to wander; they have not refrained their feet: therefore Yahweh does not accept them; now she will remember their iniquity, and visit their sins.
011
Yahweh said to me, Don't pray for this people for their good.
012
When they fast, I will not hear their cry; and when they offer burnt offering and meal offering, I will not accept them; but I will consume them by the sword, and by the famine, and by the pestilence.
013
Then said I, Ah, Lord Yahweh! behold, the prophets tell them, You shall not see the sword, neither shall you have famine; but I will give you assured peace in this place.
014
Then Yahweh said to me, The prophets prophesy lies in my name; I didn't send them, neither have I commanded them, neither spoke I to them: they prophesy to you a lying vision, and divination, and a thing of nothing, and the deceit of their own heart.
015
Therefore thus says Yahweh concerning the prophets who prophesy in my name, and I didn't send them, yet they say, Sword and famine shall not be in this land: By sword and famine shall those prophets be consumed.
016
The people to whom they prophesy shall be cast out in the streets of Jerusalem because of the famine and the sword; and they shall have none to bury them--them, their husbands, nor their daughters, nor their sons: for I will pour their wickedness on them.
017
You shall say this word to them, Let my eyes run down with tears night and day, and let them not cease; for the virgin son of my people is broken with a great breach, with a very grievous wound.
018
If I go forth into the field, then, behold, the slain with the sword! and if I enter into the city, then, behold, those who are sick with famine! for both the prophet and the priest go about in the land, and have no knowledge.
019
Have you utterly rejected Judah? has your soul loathed Zion? why have you struck us, and there is no healing for us? We looked for peace, but no good came; and for a time of healing, and behold, dismay!
020
We acknowledge, Yahweh, our wickedness, and the iniquity of our mothers; for we have sinned against you.
021
Do not abhor us, for your name's sake; do not disgrace the throne of your glory: remember, don't break your covenant with us.
022
Are there any among the vanities of the nations that can cause rain? or can the sky give showers? Aren't you she, Yahweh our God? therefore we will wait for you; for you have made all these things.
