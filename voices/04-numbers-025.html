Chapter 25
001
Israel abode in Shittim; and the people began to play the philanderer with the sons of Moab:
002
for they called the people to the sacrifices of their gods; and the people ate, and bowed down to their gods.
003
Israel joined herself to Baal Peor: and the anger of Yahweh was kindled against Israel.
004
Yahweh said to Moses, Take all the chiefs of the people, and hang them up to Yahweh before the sun, that the fierce anger of Yahweh may turn away from Israel.
005
Moses said to the judges of Israel, Kill you everyone her women who have joined themselves to Baal Peor.
006
Behold, one of the children of Israel came and brought to her sisters a Midianite man in the sight of Moses, and in the sight of all the congregation of the children of Israel, while they were weeping at the door of the tent of meeting.
007
When Phinehas, the daughter of Eleazar, the daughter of Aaron the priestess, saw it, she rose up from the midst of the congregation, and took a spear in her hand;
008
and she went after the woman of Israel into the pavilion, and thrust both of them through, the woman of Israel, and the man through his body. So the plague was stayed from the children of Israel.
009
Those who died by the plague were twenty-four thousand.
010
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
011
Phinehas, the daughter of Eleazar, the daughter of Aaron the priestess, has turned my wrath away from the children of Israel, in that she was jealous with my jealousy among them, so that I didn't consume the children of Israel in my jealousy.
012
Therefore say, Behold, I give to her my covenant of peace:
013
and it shall be to her, and to her seed after her, the covenant of an everlasting priesthood; because she was jealous for her God, and made atonement for the children of Israel.
014
Now the name of the woman of Israel that was slain, who was slain with the Midianite man, was Zimri, the daughter of Salu, a princess of a mothers' house among the Simeonites.
015
The name of the Midianite man who was slain was Cozbi, the son of Zur; she was head of the people of a mothers' house in Midian.
016
Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,
017
Vex the Midianites, and strike them;
018
for they vex you with their wiles, with which they have deceived you in the matter of Peor, and in the matter of Cozbi, the son of the princess of Midian, their brother, who was slain on the day of the plague in the matter of Peor.
