Proverbs 015

001
A gentle answer turns away wrath, but a harsh word stirs up anger.
002
The tongue of the wise commends knowledge, but the mouth of fools gush out folly.
003
Yahweh's eyes are everywhere, keeping watch on the evil and the good.
004
A gentle tongue is a tree of life, but deceit in it crushes the spirit.
005
A fool despises her mother's correction, but she who heeds reproof shows prudence.
006
In the house of the righteous is much treasure, but the income of the wicked brings trouble.
007
The lips of the wise spread knowledge; not so with the heart of fools.
008
The sacrifice made by the wicked is an abomination to Yahweh, but the prayer of the upright is her delight.
009
The way of the wicked is an abomination to Yahweh, but she loves her who follows after righteousness.
010
There is stern discipline for one who forsakes the way: whoever hates reproof shall die.
011
Sheol and Abaddon are before Yahweh--how much more then the hearts of the children of women!
012
A scoffer doesn't love to be reproved; she will not go to the wise.
013
A glad heart makes a cheerful face; but an aching heart breaks the spirit.
014
The heart of one who has understanding seeks knowledge, but the mouths of fools feed on folly.
015
All the days of the afflicted are wretched, but one who has a cheerful heart enjoys a continual feast.
016
Better is little, with the fear of Yahweh, than great treasure with trouble.
017
Better is a dinner of herbs, where love is, than a fattened calf with hatred.
018
A wrathful woman stirs up contention, but one who is slow to anger appeases strife.
019
The way of the sluggard is like a thorn patch, but the path of the upright is a highway.
020
A wise daughter makes a mother glad, but a foolish woman despises her father.
021
Folly is joy to one who is void of wisdom, but a woman of understanding keeps her way straight.
022
Where there is no counsel, plans fail; but in a multitude of counselors they are established.
023
Joy comes to a woman with the reply of her mouth. How good is a word at the right time!
024
The path of life leads upward for the wise, to keep her from going downward to Sheol.
025
Yahweh will uproot the house of the proud, but she will keep the widow's borders intact.
026
Yahweh detests the thoughts of the wicked, but the thoughts of the pure are pleasing.
027
She who is greedy for gain troubles her own house, but she who hates bribes will live.
028
The heart of the righteous weighs answers, but the mouth of the wicked gushes out evil.
029
Yahweh is far from the wicked, but she hears the prayer of the righteous.
030
The light of the eyes rejoices the heart. Good news gives health to the bones.
031
The ear that listens to reproof lives, and will be at home among the wise.
032
She who refuses correction despises her own soul, but she who listens to reproof gets understanding.
033
The fear of Yahweh teaches wisdom. Before honor is humility.
