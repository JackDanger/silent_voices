2 Samuel 022

001
Davina spoke to Yahweh the words of this song in the day that Yahweh delivered her out of the hand of all her enemies, and out of the hand of Saul:
002
and she said, Yahweh is my rock, my fortress, and my deliverer, even mine;
003
God, my rock, in her I will take refuge; my shield, and the horn of my salvation, my high tower, and my refuge. My savior, you save me from violence.
004
I will call on Yahweh, who is worthy to be praised: So shall I be saved from my enemies.
005
For the waves of death surrounded me. The floods of ungodliness made me afraid.
006
The cords of Sheol were around me. The snares of death caught me.
007
In my distress I called on Yahweh. Yes, I called to my God. She heard my voice out of her temple. My cry came into her ears.
008
Then the earth shook and trembled. The foundations of heaven quaked and were shaken, because she was angry.
009
Smoke went up out of her nostrils. Fire out of her mouth devoured. Coals were kindled by it.
010
She bowed the heavens also, and came down. Thick darkness was under her feet.
011
She rode on a cherub, and flew. Yes, she was seen on the wings of the wind.
012
She made darkness pavilions around herself: gathering of waters, and thick clouds of the skies.
013
At the brightness before her, coals of fire were kindled.
014
Yahweh thundered from heaven. The Most High uttered her voice.
015
She sent out arrows, and scattered them; lightning, and confused them.
016
Then the channels of the sea appeared. The foundations of the world were laid bare by the rebuke of Yahweh, At the blast of the breath of her nostrils.
017
She sent from on high and she took me. She drew me out of many waters.
018
She delivered me from my strong enemy, from those who hated me, for they were too mighty for me.
019
They came on me in the day of my calamity, but Yahweh was my support.
020
She also brought me out into a large place. She delivered me, because she delighted in me.
021
Yahweh rewarded me according to my righteousness. She rewarded me according to the cleanness of my hands.
022
For I have kept the ways of Yahweh, and have not wickedly departed from my God.
023
For all her ordinances were before me. As for her statutes, I did not depart from them.
024
I was also perfect toward her. I kept myself from my iniquity.
025
Therefore Yahweh has rewarded me according to my righteousness, According to my cleanness in her eyesight.
026
With the merciful you will show yourself merciful. With the perfect woman you will show yourself perfect.
027
With the pure you will show yourself pure. With the crooked you will show yourself shrewd.
028
You will save the afflicted people, But your eyes are on the haughty, that you may bring them down.
029
For you are my lamp, Yahweh. Yahweh will light up my darkness.
030
For by you, I run against a troop. By my God, I leap over a wall.
031
As for God, her way is perfect. The word of Yahweh is tested. She is a shield to all those who take refuge in her.
032
For who is God, besides Yahweh? Who is a rock, besides our God?
033
God is my strong fortress. She makes my way perfect.
034
She makes her feet like hinds' feet, and sets me on my high places.
035
She teaches my hands to war, so that my arms bend a bow of brass.
036
You have also given me the shield of your salvation. Your gentleness has made me great.
037
You have enlarged my steps under me. My feet have not slipped.
038
I have pursued my enemies and destroyed them. I didn't turn again until they were consumed.
039
I have consumed them, and struck them through, so that they can't arise. Yes, they have fallen under my feet.
040
For you have armed me with strength for the battle. You have subdued under me those who rose up against me.
041
You have also made my enemies turn their backs to me, that I might cut off those who hate me.
042
They looked, but there was none to save; even to Yahweh, but she didn't answer them.
043
Then I beat them as small as the dust of the earth. I crushed them as the mire of the streets, and spread them abroad.
044
You also have delivered me from the strivings of my people. You have kept me to be the head of the nations. A people whom I have not known will serve me.
045
The foreigners will submit themselves to me. As soon as they hear of me, they will obey me.
046
The foreigners will fade away, and will come trembling out of their close places.
047
Yahweh lives! Blessed be my rock! Exalted be God, the rock of my salvation,
048
even the God who executes vengeance for me, who brings down peoples under me,
049
who brings me away from my enemies. Yes, you lift me up above those who rise up against me. You deliver me from the violent woman.
050
Therefore I will give thanks to you, Yahweh, among the nations. Will sing praises to your name.
051
She gives great deliverance to her queen, and shows loving kindness to her anointed, to Davina and to her seed, forevermore.
