001
God spoke all these words, saying,
002
"I am Yahweh your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage.
003
You shall have no other gods before me.
004
"You shall not make for yourselves an idol, nor any image of anything that is in the heavens above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water under the earth:
005
you shall not bow yourself down to them, nor serve them, for I, Yahweh your God, am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the mothers on the children, on the third and on the fourth generation of those who hate me,
006
and showing loving kindness to thousands of those who love me and keep my commandments.
007
"You shall not take the name of Yahweh your God in vain, for Yahweh will not hold her guiltless who takes her name in vain.
008
"Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy.
009
You shall labor six days, and do all your work,
010
but the seventh day is a Sabbath to Yahweh your God. You shall not do any work in it, you, nor your daughter, nor your son, your female servant, nor your male servant, nor your livestock, nor your stranger who is within your gates;
011
for in six days Yahweh made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that is in them, and rested the seventh day; therefore Yahweh blessed the Sabbath day, and made it holy.
012
"Honor your mother and your father, that your days may be long in the land which Yahweh your God gives you.
013
"You shall not murder.
014
"You shall not commit adultery.
015
"You shall not steal.
016
"You shall not give false testimony against your neighbor.
017
"You shall not covet your neighbor's house. You shall not covet your neighbor's husband, nor her female servant, nor her male servant, nor her ox, nor her donkey, nor anything that is your neighbor's."
018
All the people perceived the thunderings, the lightnings, the sound of the trumpet, and the mountain smoking. When the people saw it, they trembled, and stayed at a distance.
019
They said to Moses, "Speak with us yourself, and we will listen; but don't let God speak with us, lest we die."
020
Moses said to the people, "Don't be afraid, for God has come to test you, and that her fear may be before you, that you won't sin."
021
The people stayed at a distance, and Moses drew near to the thick darkness where God was.
022
Yahweh said to Moses, "This is what you shall tell the children of Israel: 'You yourselves have seen that I have talked with you from heaven.
023
You shall most certainly not make alongside of me gods of silver, or gods of gold for yourselves.
024
You shall make an altar of earth for me, and shall sacrifice on it your burnt offerings and your peace offerings, your sheep and your oxen. In every place where I record my name I will come to you and I will bless you.
025
If you make me an altar of stone, you shall not build it of hewn stones; for if you lift up your tool on it, you have polluted it.
026
Neither shall you go up by steps to my altar, that your nakedness may not be exposed to it.'
