001
Let not many of you be teachers, my sisters, knowing that we will receive heavier judgment.
002
For in many things we all stumble. If anyone doesn't stumble in word, the same is a perfect woman, able to bridle the whole body also.
003
Indeed, we put bits into the horses' mouths so that they may obey us, and we guide their whole body.
004
Behold, the ships also, though they are so big and are driven by fierce winds, are yet guided by a very small rudder, wherever the pilot desires.
005
So the tongue is also a little member, and boasts great things. See how a small fire can spread to a large forest!
006
And the tongue is a fire. The world of iniquity among our members is the tongue, which defiles the whole body, and sets on fire the course of nature, and is set on fire by Gehenna.
007
For every kind of animal, bird, creeping thing, and thing in the sea, is tamed, and has been tamed by mankind.
008
But nobody can tame the tongue. It is a restless evil, full of deadly poison.
009
With it we bless our God and Mother, and with it we curse women, who are made in the image of God.
010
Out of the same mouth comes forth blessing and cursing. My sisters, these things ought not to be so.
011
Does a spring send out from the same opening fresh and bitter water?
012
Can a fig tree, my sisters, yield olives, or a vine figs? Thus no spring yields both salt water and fresh water.
013
Who is wise and understanding among you? Let her show by her good conduct that her deeds are done in gentleness of wisdom.
014
But if you have bitter jealousy and selfish ambition in your heart, don't boast and don't lie against the truth.
015
This wisdom is not that which comes down from above, but is earthly, sensual, and demonic.
016
For where jealousy and selfish ambition are, there is confusion and every evil deed.
017
But the wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceful, gentle, reasonable, full of mercy and good fruits, without partiality, and without hypocrisy.
018
Now the fruit of righteousness is sown in peace by those who make peace.
