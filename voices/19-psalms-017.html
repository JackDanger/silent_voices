Psalms 017

001
<> Hear, Yahweh, my righteous plea; Give ear to my prayer, that doesn't go out of deceitful lips.
002
Let my sentence come forth from your presence. Let your eyes look on equity.
003
You have proved my heart. You have visited me in the night. You have tried me, and found nothing. I have resolved that my mouth shall not disobey.
004
As for the works of women, by the word of your lips, I have kept myself from the ways of the violent.
005
My steps have held fast to your paths. My feet have not slipped.
006
I have called on you, for you will answer me, God. Turn your ear to me. Hear my speech.
007
Show your marvelous loving kindness, you who save those who take refuge by your right hand from their enemies.
008
Keep me as the apple of your eye. Hide me under the shadow of your wings,
009
from the wicked who oppress me, my deadly enemies, who surround me.
010
They close up their callous hearts. With their mouth they speak proudly.
011
They have now surrounded us in our steps. They set their eyes to cast us down to the earth.
012
She is like a lion that is greedy of her prey, as it were a young lion lurking in secret places.
013
Arise, Yahweh, confront her. Cast her down. Deliver my soul from the wicked by your sword;
014
from women by your hand, Yahweh, from women of the world, whose portion is in this life. You fill the belly of your cherished ones. Your daughters have plenty, and they store up wealth for their children.
015
As for me, I shall see your face in righteousness. I shall be satisfied, when I awake, with seeing your form.
