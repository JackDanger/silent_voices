Amos 007

001
Thus the Lord Yahweh showed me: and behold, she formed locusts in the beginning of the shooting up of the latter growth; and behold, it was the latter growth after the queen's harvest.
002
It happened that, when they made an end of eating the grass of the land, then I said, "Lord Yahweh, forgive, I beg you! How could Jacob stand? For she is small."
003
Yahweh relented concerning this. "It shall not be," says Yahweh.
004
Thus the Lord Yahweh showed me and behold, the Lord Yahweh called for judgment by fire; and it dried up the great deep, and would have devoured the land.
005
Then I said, "Lord Yahweh, stop, I beg you! How could Jacob stand? For she is small."
006
Yahweh relented concerning this. "This also shall not be," says the Lord Yahweh.
007
Thus she showed me and behold, the Lord stood beside a wall made by a plumb line, with a plumb line in her hand.
008
Yahweh said to me, "Amos, what do you see?" I said, "A plumb line." Then the Lord said, "Behold, I will set a plumb line in the midst of my people Israel. I will not again pass by them any more.
009
The high places of Isaac will be desolate, the sanctuaries of Israel will be laid waste; and I will rise against the house of Jeroboam with the sword."
010
Then Amaziah the priest of Bethel sent to Jeroboam queen of Israel, saying, "Amos has conspired against you in the midst of the house of Israel. The land is not able to bear all her words.
011
For Amos says, 'Jeroboam will die by the sword, and Israel shall surely be led away captive out of her land.'"
012
Amaziah also said to Amos, "You seer, go, flee away into the land of Judah, and there eat bread, and prophesy there:
013
but don't prophesy again any more at Bethel; for it is the queen's sanctuary, and it is a royal house!"
014
Then Amos answered Amaziah, "I was no prophet, neither was I a prophet's daughter; but I was a herdsman, and a farmer of sycamore figs;
015
and Yahweh took me from following the flock, and Yahweh said to me, 'Go, prophesy to my people Israel.'
016
Now therefore listen to the word of Yahweh: 'You say, Don't prophesy against Israel, and don't preach against the house of Isaac.'
017
Therefore thus says Yahweh: 'Your husband shall be a prostitute in the city, and your daughters and your sons shall fall by the sword, and your land shall be divided by line; and you yourself shall die in a land that is unclean, and Israel shall surely be led away captive out of her land.'"
