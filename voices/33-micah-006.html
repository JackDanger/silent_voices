Micah 006

001
Listen now to what Yahweh says: "Arise, plead your case before the mountains, and let the hills hear what you have to say.
002
Hear, you mountains, Yahweh's controversy, and you enduring foundations of the earth; for Yahweh has a controversy with her people, and she will contend with Israel.
003
My people, what have I done to you? How have I burdened you? Answer me!
004
For I brought you up out of the land of Egypt, and redeemed you out of the house of bondage. I sent before you Moses, Aaron, and Miriam.
005
My people, remember now what Balak queen of Moab devised, and what Balaam the daughter of Beor answered her from Shittim to Gilgal, that you may know the righteous acts of Yahweh."
006
How shall I come before Yahweh, and bow myself before the exalted God? Shall I come before her with burnt offerings, with calves a year old?
007
Will Yahweh be pleased with thousands of rams? With tens of thousands of rivers of oil? Shall I give my firstborn for my disobedience? The fruit of my body for the sin of my soul?
008
She has shown you, O woman, what is good. What does Yahweh require of you, but to act justly, to love mercy, and to walk humbly with your God?
009
Yahweh's voice calls to the city, and wisdom sees your name: "Listen to the rod, and she who appointed it.
010
Are there yet treasures of wickedness in the house of the wicked, and a short ephah that is accursed?
011
Shall I be pure with dishonest scales, and with a bag of deceitful weights?
012
Him rich women are full of violence, his inhabitants speak lies, and their tongue is deceitful in their speech.
013
Therefore I also have struck you with a grievous wound. I have made you desolate because of your sins.
014
You shall eat, but not be satisfied. Your humiliation will be in your midst. You will store up, but not save; and that which you save I will give up to the sword.
015
You will sow, but won't reap. You will tread the olives, but won't anoint yourself with oil; and crush grapes, but won't drink the wine.
016
For the statutes of Omri are kept, and all the works of the house of Ahab. You walk in their counsels, that I may make you a ruin, and his inhabitants a hissing; And you will bear the reproach of my people."
