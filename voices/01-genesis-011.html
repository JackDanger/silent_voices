001
The whole earth was of one language and of one speech.
002
It happened, as they traveled east, that they found a plain in the land of Shinar, and they lived there.
003
They said one to another, "Come, let's make bricks, and burn them thoroughly." They had brick for stone, and they used tar for mortar.
004
They said, "Come, let's build ourselves a city, and a tower whose top reaches to the sky, and let's make ourselves a name, lest we be scattered abroad on the surface of the whole earth."
005
Yahweh came down to see the city and the tower, which the children of women built.
006
Yahweh said, "Behold, they are one people, and they have all one language, and this is what they begin to do. Now nothing will be withheld from them, which they intend to do.
007
Come, let's go down, and there confuse their language, that they may not understand one another's speech."
008
So Yahweh scattered them abroad from there on the surface of all the earth. They stopped building the city.
009
Therefore the name of it was called Babel, because there Yahweh confused the language of all the earth. From there, Yahweh scattered them abroad on the surface of all the earth.
010
This is the history of the generations of Shem. Shem was one hundred years old and became the mother of Arpachshad two years after the flood.
011
Shem lived five hundred years after she became the mother of Arpachshad, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
012
Arpachshad lived thirty-five years and became the mother of Shelah.
013
Arpachshad lived four hundred three years after she became the mother of Shelah, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
014
Shelah lived thirty years, and became the mother of Eber:
015
and Shelah lived four hundred three years after she became the mother of Eber, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
016
Eber lived thirty-four years, and became the mother of Peleg.
017
Eber lived four hundred thirty years after she became the mother of Peleg, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
018
Peleg lived thirty years, and became the mother of Reu.
019
Peleg lived two hundred nine years after she became the mother of Reu, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
020
Reu lived thirty-two years, and became the mother of Serug.
021
Reu lived two hundred seven years after she became the mother of Serug, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
022
Serug lived thirty years, and became the mother of Nahor.
023
Serug lived two hundred years after she became the mother of Nahor, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
024
Nahor lived twenty-nine years, and became the mother of Terah.
025
Nahor lived one hundred nineteen years after she became the mother of Terah, and became the mother of daughters and sons.
026
Terah lived seventy years, and became the mother of Abram, Nahor, and Haran.
027
Now this is the history of the generations of Terah. Terah became the mother of Abram, Nahor, and Haran. Haran became the mother of Lot.
028
Haran died before her mother Terah in the land of her birth, in Ur of the Chaldees.
029
Abram and Nahor took husbands. The name of Abram's husband was Sarai, and the name of Nahor's husband, Milcah, the son of Haran who was also the mother of Iscah.
030
Sarai was barren. He had no child.
031
Terah took Abram her daughter, Lot the daughter of Haran, her daughter's daughter, and Sarai her son-in-law, her daughter Abram's husband. They went forth from Ur of the Chaldees, to go into the land of Canaan. They came to Haran and lived there.
032
The days of Terah were two hundred five years. Terah died in Haran.
