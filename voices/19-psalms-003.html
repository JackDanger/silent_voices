Psalms 003

001
<> Yahweh, how my adversaries have increased! Many are those who rise up against me.
002
Many there are who say of my soul, "There is no help for her in God." Selah.
003
But you, Yahweh, are a shield around me, my glory, and the one who lifts up my head.
004
I cry to Yahweh with my voice, and she answers me out of her holy hill. Selah.
005
I laid myself down and slept. I awakened; for Yahweh sustains me.
006
I will not be afraid of tens of thousands of people who have set themselves against me on every side.
007
Arise, Yahweh! Save me, my God! For you have struck all of my enemies on the cheek bone. You have broken the teeth of the wicked.
008
Salvation belongs to Yahweh. Your blessing be on your people. Selah.
