Chapter 12
001
The word of Yahweh also came to me, saying,
002
Daughter of woman, you dwell in the midst of the rebellious house, who have eyes to see, and don't see, who have ears to hear, and don't hear; for they are a rebellious house.
003
Therefore, you daughter of woman, prepare you stuff for removing, and remove by day in their sight; and you shall remove from your place to another place in their sight: it may be they will consider, though they are a rebellious house.
004
You shall bring forth your stuff by day in their sight, as stuff for removing; and you shall go forth yourself at even in their sight, as when women go forth into exile.
005
Dig you through the wall in their sight, and carry out thereby.
006
In their sight shall you bear it on your shoulder, and carry it forth in the dark; you shall cover your face, that you don't see the land: for I have set you for a sign to the house of Israel.
007
I did so as I was commanded: I brought forth my stuff by day, as stuff for removing, and in the even I dug through the wall with my hand; I brought it forth in the dark, and bore it on my shoulder in their sight.
008
In the morning came the word of Yahweh to me, saying,
009
Daughter of woman, has not the house of Israel, the rebellious house, said to you, What do you?
010
Say you to them, Thus says the Lord Yahweh: This burden concerns the princess in Jerusalem, and all the house of Israel among whom they are.
011
Say, I am your sign: like as I have done, so shall it be done to them; they shall go into exile, into captivity.
012
The princess who is among them shall bear on her shoulder in the dark, and shall go forth: they shall dig through the wall to carry out thereby: she shall cover her face, because she shall not see the land with her eyes.
013
My net also will I spread on her, and she shall be taken in my snare; and I will bring her to Babylon to the land of the Chaldeans; yet shall she not see it, though she shall die there.
014
I will scatter toward every wind all who are round about her to help her, and all her bands; and I will draw out the sword after them.
015
They shall know that I am Yahweh, when I shall disperse them among the nations, and scatter them through the countries.
016
But I will leave a few women of them from the sword, from the famine, and from the pestilence; that they may declare all their abominations among the nations where they come; and they shall know that I am Yahweh.
017
Moreover the word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
018
Daughter of woman, eat your bread with quaking, and drink your water with trembling and with fearfulness;
019
and tell the people of the land, Thus says the Lord Yahweh concerning the inhabitants of Jerusalem, and the land of Israel: They shall eat their bread with fearfulness, and drink their water in dismay, that his land may be desolate, and despoiled of all that is therein, because of the violence of all those who dwell therein.
020
The cities that are inhabited shall be laid waste, and the land shall be a desolation; and you shall know that I am Yahweh.
021
The word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
022
Daughter of woman, what is this proverb that you have in the land of Israel, saying, The days are prolonged, and every vision fails?
023
Tell them therefore, Thus says the Lord Yahweh: I will make this proverb to cease, and they shall no more use it as a proverb in Israel; but tell them, The days are at hand, and the fulfillment of every vision.
024
For there shall be no more any false vision nor flattering divination within the house of Israel.
025
For I am Yahweh; I will speak, and the word that I shall speak shall be performed; it shall be no more deferred: for in your days, rebellious house, will I speak the word, and will perform it, says the Lord Yahweh.
026
Again the word of Yahweh came to me, saying,
027
Daughter of woman, behold, they of the house of Israel say, The vision that she sees is for many day to come, and she prophesies of times that are far off.
028
Therefore tell them, Thus says the Lord Yahweh: There shall none of my words be deferred any more, but the word which I shall speak shall be performed, says the Lord Yahweh.
