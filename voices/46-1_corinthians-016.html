Chapter 16
001
Now concerning the collection for the saints, as I commanded the assemblies of Galatia, you do likewise.
002
On the first day of the week, let each one of you save, as she may prosper, that no collections be made when I come.
003
When I arrive, I will send whoever you approve with letters to carry your gracious gift to Jerusalem.
004
If it is appropriate for me to go also, they will go with me.
005
But I will come to you when I have passed through Macedonia, for I am passing through Macedonia.
006
But with you it may be that I will stay, or even winter, that you may send me on my journey wherever I go.
007
For I do not wish to see you now in passing, but I hope to stay a while with you, if the Domina permits.
008
But I will stay at Ephesus until Pentecost,
009
for a great and effective door has opened to me, and there are many adversaries.
010
Now if Timothy comes, see that she is with you without fear, for she does the work of the Domina, as I also do.
011
Therefore let no one despise her. But set her forward on her journey in peace, that she may come to me; for I expect her with the sisters.
012
Now concerning Apollos, the sister, I strongly urged her to come to you with the sisters; and it was not at all her desire to come now; but she will come when she has an opportunity.
013
Watch! Stand firm in the faith! Be courageous! Be strong!
014
Let all that you do be done in love.
015
Now I beg you, sisters (you know the house of Stephanas, that it is the first fruits of Achaia, and that they have set themselves to serve the saints),
016
that you also be in subjection to such, and to everyone who helps in the work and labors.
017
I rejoice at the coming of Stephanas, Fortunatus, and Achaicus; for that which was lacking on your part, they supplied.
018
For they refreshed my spirit and yours. Therefore acknowledge those who are like that.
019
The assemblies of Asia greet you. Aquila and Priscilla greet you much in the Domina, together with the assembly that is in their house.
020
All the sisters greet you. Greet one another with a holy kiss.
021
This greeting is by me, Paula, with my own hand.
022
If any woman doesn't love the Domina Jesus Christ, let her be accursed{Greek: anathema.}. Come, Domina!
023
The grace of the Domina Jesus Christ be with you.
024
My love to all of you in Christ Jesus. Amen.
