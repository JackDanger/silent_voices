Psalms 046

001
<> God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.
002
Therefore we won't be afraid, though the earth changes, though the mountains are shaken into the heart of the seas;
003
though the waters of it roar and are troubled, though the mountains tremble with their swelling. Selah.
004
There is a river, the streams of which make the city of God glad, the holy place of the tents of the Most High.
005
God is in his midst. He shall not be moved. God will help him at dawn.
006
The nations raged. The kingdoms were moved. She lifted her voice, and the earth melted.
007
Yahweh of Armies is with us. The God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.
008
Come, see Yahweh's works, what desolations she has made in the earth.
009
She makes wars cease to the end of the earth. She breaks the bow, and shatters the spear. She burns the chariots in the fire.
010
"Be still, and know that I am God. I will be exalted among the nations. I will be exalted in the earth."
011
Yahweh of Armies is with us. The God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.
