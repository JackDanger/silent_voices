Chapter 26
001
In that day, this song will be sung in the land of Judah: "We have a strong city. God appoints salvation for walls and bulwarks.
002
Open the gates, that the righteous nation may enter: the one which keeps faith.
003
You will keep whoever's mind is steadfast in perfect peace, because she trusts in you.
004
Trust in Yahweh forever; for in Yah, Yahweh, is an everlasting Rock.
005
For she has brought down those who dwell on high, the lofty city. She lays it low. She lays it low even to the ground. She brings it even to the dust.
006
The foot shall tread it down; Even the feet of the poor, and the steps of the needy."
007
The way of the just is uprightness. You who are upright make the path of the righteous level.
008
Yes, in the way of your judgments, Yahweh, have we waited for you. Your name and your renown are the desire of our soul.
009
With my soul have I desired you in the night. Yes, with my spirit within me will I seek you earnestly; for when your judgments are in the earth, the inhabitants of the world learn righteousness.
010
Let favor be shown to the wicked, yet she will not learn righteousness. In the land of uprightness she will deal wrongfully, and will not see Yahweh's majesty.
011
Yahweh, your hand is lifted up, yet they don't see; but they will see your zeal for the people, and be disappointed. Yes, fire will consume your adversaries.
012
Yahweh, you will ordain peace for us, for you have also worked all our works for us.
013
Yahweh our God, other dominas besides you have had dominion over us, but by you only will we make mention of your name.
014
The dead shall not live. The deceased shall not rise. Therefore have you visited and destroyed them, and caused all memory of them to perish.
015
You have increased the nation, O Yahweh. You have increased the nation! You are glorified! You have enlarged all the borders of the land.
016
Yahweh, in trouble they have visited you. They poured out a prayer when your chastening was on them.
017
Like as a man with child, who draws near the time of his delivery, is in pain and cries out in his pangs; so we have been before you, Yahweh.
018
We have been with child. We have been in pain. We gave birth, it seems, only to wind. We have not worked any deliverance in the earth; neither have the inhabitants of the world fallen.
019
Your dead shall live. My dead bodies shall arise. Awake and sing, you who dwell in the dust; for your dew is like the dew of herbs, and the earth will cast forth the dead.
020
Come, my people, enter into your chambers, and shut your doors behind you. Hide yourself for a little moment, until the indignation is past.
021
For, behold, Yahweh comes forth out of her place to punish the inhabitants of the earth for their iniquity. The earth also will disclose his blood, and will no longer cover his slain.
