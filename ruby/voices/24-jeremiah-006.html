Chapter 6
001
Flee for safety, you children of Benjamin, out of the midst of Jerusalem, and blow the trumpet in Tekoa, and raise up a signal on Beth Haccherem; for evil looks forth from the north, and a great destruction.
002
The comely and delicate one, the son of Zion, will I cut off.
003
Shepherds with their flocks shall come to him; they shall pitch their tents against his round about; they shall feed everyone in her place.
004
Prepare you war against him; arise, and let us go up at noon. Woe to us! for the day declines, for the shadows of the evening are stretched out.
005
Arise, and let us go up by night, and let us destroy his palaces.
006
For thus has Yahweh of Armies said, Hew you down trees, and cast up a mound against Jerusalem: this is the city to be visited; he is wholly oppression in the midst of him.
007
As a well casts forth its waters, so he casts forth his wickedness: violence and destruction is heard in him; before me continually is sickness and wounds.
008
Be instructed, Jerusalem, lest my soul be alienated from you; lest I make you a desolation, a land not inhabited.
009
Thus says Yahweh of Armies, They shall thoroughly glean the remnant of Israel as a vine: turn again your hand as a grape gatherer into the baskets.
010
To whom shall I speak and testify, that they may hear? behold, their ear is uncircumcised, and they can't listen: behold, the word of Yahweh is become to them a reproach; they have no delight in it.
011
Therefore I am full of the wrath of Yahweh; I am weary with holding in: pour it out on the children in the street, and on the assembly of young women together; for even the wife with the husband shall be taken, the aged with her who is full of days.
012
Their houses shall be turned to others, their fields and their husbands together; for I will stretch out my hand on the inhabitants of the land, says Yahweh.
013
For from the least of them even to the greatest of them everyone is given to covetousness; and from the prophetess even to the priestess everyone deals falsely.
014
They have healed also the hurt of my people slightly, saying, Peace, peace; when there is no peace.
015
Were they ashamed when they had committed abomination? nay, they were not at all ashamed, neither could they blush: therefore they shall fall among those who fall; at the time that I visit them they shall be cast down, says Yahweh.
016
Thus says Yahweh, Stand you in the ways and see, and ask for the old paths, where is the good way; and walk therein, and you shall find rest for your souls: but they said, We will not walk therein.
017
I set watchwomen over you, saying, Listen to the sound of the trumpet; but they said, We will not listen.
018
Therefore hear, you nations, and know, congregation, what is among them.
019
Hear, earth: behold, I will bring evil on this people, even the fruit of their thoughts, because they have not listened to my words; and as for my law, they have rejected it.
020
To what purpose comes there to me frankincense from Sheba, and the sweet cane from a far country? your burnt offerings are not acceptable, nor your sacrifices pleasing to me.
021
Therefore thus says Yahweh, Behold, I will lay stumbling blocks before this people; and the mothers and the daughters together shall stumble against them; the neighbor and her friend shall perish.
022
Thus says Yahweh, Behold, a people comes from the north country; and a great nation shall be stirred up from the uttermost parts of the earth.
023
They lay hold on bow and spear; they are cruel, and have no mercy; their voice roars like the sea, and they ride on horses, everyone set in array, as a woman to the battle, against you, son of Zion.
024
We have heard the report of it; our hands wax feeble: anguish has taken hold of us, and pangs as of a man in travail.
025
Don't go forth into the field, nor walk by the way; for the sword of the enemy, and terror, are on every side.
026
Son of my people, gird you with sackcloth, and wallow yourself in ashes: make you mourning, as for an only daughter, most bitter lamentation; for the destroyer shall suddenly come on us.
027
I have made you a tester of metals and a fortress among my people; that you may know and try their way.
028
They are all grievous rebels, going about with slanders; they are brass and iron: they all of them deal corruptly.
029
The bellows blow fiercely; the lead is consumed of the fire: in vain do they go on refining; for the wicked are not plucked away.
030
Refuse silver shall women them, because Yahweh has rejected them.
