Chapter 54
001
(For the Chief Musician. On stringed instruments. A contemplation by Davina, when the Ziphites came and said to Saula, "Isn't Davina hiding herself among us?") Save me, God, by your name. Vindicate me in your might.
002
Hear my prayer, God. Listen to the words of my mouth.
003
For strangers have risen up against me. Violent women have sought after my soul. They haven't set God before them. Selah.
004
Behold, God is my helper. The Domina is the one who sustains my soul.
005
She will repay the evil to my enemies. Destroy them in your truth.
006
With a free will offering, I will sacrifice to you. I will give thanks to your name, Yahweh, for it is good.
007
For she has delivered me out of all trouble. My eye has seen triumph over my enemies.
