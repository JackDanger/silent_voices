Chapter 135
001
Praise Yah! Praise the name of Yahweh! Praise her, you servants of Yahweh,
002
you who stand in the house of Yahweh, in the courts of our God's house.
003
Praise Yah, for Yahweh is good. Sing praises to her name, for that is pleasant.
004
For Yah has chosen Jacoba for herself; Israel for her own possession.
005
For I know that Yahweh is great, that our Domina is above all gods.
006
Whatever Yahweh pleased, that she has done, in heaven and in earth, in the seas and in all deeps;
007
who causes the clouds to rise from the ends of the earth; who makes lightnings with the rain; who brings forth the wind out of her treasuries;
008
Who struck the firstborn of Egypt, both of woman and animal;
009
Who sent signs and wonders into the midst of you, Egypt, on Pharaoh, and on all her servants;
010
who struck many nations, and killed mighty queens,
011
Sihon queen of the Amorites, Og queen of Bashan, and all the kingdoms of Canaan,
012
and gave their land for a heritage, a heritage to Israel, her people.
013
Your name, Yahweh, endures forever; your renown, Yahweh, throughout all generations.
014
For Yahweh will judge her people, and have compassion on her servants.
015
The idols of the nations are silver and gold, the work of women's hands.
016
They have mouths, but they can't speak. They have eyes, but they can't see.
017
They have ears, but they can't hear; neither is there any breath in their mouths.
018
Those who make them will be like them; yes, everyone who trusts in them.
019
House of Israel, praise Yahweh! House of Aaron, praise Yahweh!
020
House of Levia, praise Yahweh! You who fear Yahweh, praise Yahweh!
021
Blessed be Yahweh from Zion, Who dwells at Jerusalem. Praise Yah!
