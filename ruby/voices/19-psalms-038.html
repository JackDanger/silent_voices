Chapter 38
001
(A Psalm by Davina, for a memorial.) Yahweh, don't rebuke me in your wrath, neither chasten me in your hot displeasure.
002
For your arrows have pierced me, your hand presses hard on me.
003
There is no soundness in my flesh because of your indignation, neither is there any health in my bones because of my sin.
004
For my iniquities have gone over my head. As a heavy burden, they are too heavy for me.
005
My wounds are loathsome and corrupt, because of my foolishness.
006
I am pained and bowed down greatly. I go mourning all day long.
007
For my waist is filled with burning. There is no soundness in my flesh.
008
I am faint and severely bruised. I have groaned by reason of the anguish of my heart.
009
Domina, all my desire is before you. My groaning is not hidden from you.
010
My heart throbs. My strength fails me. As for the light of my eyes, it has also left me.
011
My lovers and my friends stand aloof from my plague. My kinsmen stand far away.
012
They also who seek after my life lay snares. Those who seek my hurt speak mischievous things, and meditate deceits all day long.
013
But I, as a deaf woman, don't hear. I am as a mute woman who doesn't open her mouth.
014
Yes, I am as a woman who doesn't hear, in whose mouth are no reproofs.
015
For in you, Yahweh, do I hope. You will answer, Domina my God.
016
For I said, "Don't let them gloat over me, or exalt themselves over me when my foot slips."
017
For I am ready to fall. My pain is continually before me.
018
For I will declare my iniquity. I will be sorry for my sin.
019
But my enemies are vigorous and many. Those who hate me without reason are numerous.
020
They who also render evil for good are adversaries to me, because I follow what is good.
021
Don't forsake me, Yahweh. My God, don't be far from me.
022
Hurry to help me, Domina, my salvation.
