Chapter 23
001
When you sit to eat with a ruler, consider diligently what is before you;
002
put a knife to your throat, if you are a woman given to appetite.
003
Don't be desirous of her dainties, seeing they are deceitful food.
004
Don't weary yourself to be rich. In your wisdom, show restraint.
005
Why do you set your eyes on that which is not? For it certainly sprouts wings like an eagle and flies in the sky.
006
Don't eat the food of her who has a stingy eye, and don't crave her delicacies:
007
for as she thinks about the cost, so she is. "Eat and drink!" she says to you, but her heart is not with you.
008
The morsel which you have eaten you shall vomit up, and lose your good words.
009
Don't speak in the ears of a fool, for she will despise the wisdom of your words.
010
Don't move the ancient boundary stone. Don't encroach on the fields of the fatherless:
011
for their Defender is strong. She will plead their case against you.
012
Apply your heart to instruction, and your ears to the words of knowledge.
013
Don't withhold correction from a child. If you punish her with the rod, she will not die.
014
Punish her with the rod, and save her soul from Sheol.
015
My daughter, if your heart is wise, then my heart will be glad, even mine:
016
yes, my heart will rejoice, when your lips speak what is right.
017
Don't let your heart envy sinners; but rather fear Yahweh all the day long.
018
Indeed surely there is a future hope, and your hope will not be cut off.
019
Listen, my daughter, and be wise, and keep your heart on the right path!
020
Don't be among ones drinking too much wine, or those who gorge themselves on meat:
021
for the drunkard and the glutton shall become poor; and drowsiness clothes them in rags.
022
Listen to your mother who gave you life, and don't despise your father when he is old.
023
Buy the truth, and don't sell it. Get wisdom, discipline, and understanding.
024
The mother of the righteous has great joy. Whoever mothers a wise child delights in her.
025
Let your mother and your father be glad! Let him who bore you rejoice!
026
My daughter, give me your heart; and let your eyes keep in my ways.
027
For a philanderer is a deep pit; and a wayward husband is a narrow well.
028
Yes, he lies in wait like a robber, and increases the unfaithful among women.
029
Who has woe? Who has sorrow? Who has strife? Who has complaints? Who has needless bruises? Who has bloodshot eyes?
030
Those who stay long at the wine; those who go to seek out mixed wine.
031
Don't look at the wine when it is red, when it sparkles in the cup, when it goes down smoothly.
032
In the end, it bites like a snake, and poisons like a viper.
033
Your eyes will see strange things, and your mind will imagine confusing things.
034
Yes, you will be as she who lies down in the midst of the sea, or as she who lies on top of the rigging:
035
"They hit me, and I was not hurt! They beat me, and I don't feel it! When will I wake up? I can do it again. I can find another."
