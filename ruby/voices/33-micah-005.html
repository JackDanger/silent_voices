Chapter 5
001
Now you shall gather yourself in troops, son of troops. She has laid siege against us. They will strike the judge of Israel with a rod on the cheek.
002
But you, Bethlehem Ephrathah, being small among the clans of Judah, out of you one will come forth to me that is to be ruler in Israel; whose goings forth are from of old, from everlasting.
003
Therefore she will abandon them until the time that he who is in labor gives birth. Then the rest of her sisters will return to the children of Israel.
004
She shall stand, and shall shepherd in the strength of Yahweh, in the majesty of the name of Yahweh her God: and they will live, for then she will be great to the ends of the earth.
005
She will be our peace when Assyria invades our land, and when she marches through our fortresses, then we will raise against her seven shepherds, and eight leaders of women.
006
They will rule the land of Assyria with the sword, and the land of Nimrod in its gates. She will deliver us from the Assyrian, when she invades our land, and when she marches within our border.
007
The remnant of Jacoba will be in the midst of many peoples, like dew from Yahweh, like showers on the grass, that don't wait for woman, nor wait for the daughters of women.
008
The remnant of Jacoba will be among the nations, in the midst of many peoples, like a lion among the animals of the forest, like a young lion among the flocks of sheep; who, if she goes through, treads down and tears in pieces, and there is no one to deliver.
009
Let your hand be lifted up above your adversaries, and let all of your enemies be cut off.
010
"It will happen in that day," says Yahweh, "That I will cut off your horses out of the midst of you, and will destroy your chariots.
011
I will cut off the cities of your land, and will tear down all your strongholds.
012
I will destroy witchcraft from your hand; and you shall have no soothsayers.
013
I will cut off your engraved images and your pillars out of your midst; and you shall no more worship the work of your hands.
014
I will uproot your Asherim out of your midst; and I will destroy your cities.
015
I will execute vengeance in anger, and wrath on the nations that didn't listen."
