Chapter 9
001
God blessed Noa and her daughters, and said to them, "Be fruitful, and multiply, and replenish the earth.
002
The fear of you and the dread of you will be on every animal of the earth, and on every bird of the sky. Everything that the ground teems with, and all the fish of the sea are delivered into your hand.
003
Every moving thing that lives will be food for you. As the green herb, I have given everything to you.
004
But flesh with the life of it, the blood of it, you shall not eat.
005
I will surely require your blood of your lives. At the hand of every animal I will require it. At the hand of woman, even at the hand of every woman's sister, I will require the life of woman.
006
Whoever sheds woman's blood, her blood will be shed by woman, for God made woman in her own image.
007
Be fruitful and multiply. Bring forth abundantly in the earth, and multiply in it."
008
God spoke to Noa and to her daughters with her, saying,
009
"As for me, behold, I establish my covenant with you, and with your offspring after you,
010
and with every living creature that is with you: the birds, the livestock, and every animal of the earth with you, of all that go out of the ship, even every animal of the earth.
011
I will establish my covenant with you: all flesh will not be cut off any more by the waters of the flood, neither will there ever again be a flood to destroy the earth."
012
God said, "This is the token of the covenant which I make between me and you and every living creature that is with you, for perpetual generations:
013
I set my rainbow in the cloud, and it will be for a sign of a covenant between me and the earth.
014
It will happen, when I bring a cloud over the earth, that the rainbow will be seen in the cloud,
015
and I will remember my covenant, which is between me and you and every living creature of all flesh, and the waters will no more become a flood to destroy all flesh.
016
The rainbow will be in the cloud. I will look at it, that I may remember the everlasting covenant between God and every living creature of all flesh that is on the earth."
017
God said to Noa, "This is the token of the covenant which I have established between me and all flesh that is on the earth."
018
The daughters of Noa who went forth from the ship were Shem, Ham, and Japheth. Ham is the mother of Canaan.
019
These three were the daughters of Noa, and from these, the whole earth was populated.
020
Noa began to be a farmer, and planted a vineyard.
021
She drank of the wine and got drunk. She was uncovered within her tent.
022
Ham, the mother of Canaan, saw the nakedness of her mother, and told her two sisters outside.
023
Shem and Japheth took a garment, and laid it on both their shoulders, went in backwards, and covered the nakedness of their mother. Their faces were backwards, and they didn't see their mother's nakedness.
024
Noa awoke from her wine, and knew what her youngest daughter had done to her.
025
She said, "Canaan is cursed. She will be servant of servants to her sisters."
026
She said, "Blessed be Yahweh, the God of Shem. Let Canaan be her servant.
027
May God enlarge Japheth. Let her dwell in the tents of Shem. Let Canaan be her servant."
028
Noa lived three hundred fifty years after the flood.
029
All the days of Noa were nine hundred fifty years, then she died.
