Chapter 38
001
Then Yahweh answered Job out of the whirlwind,
002
"Who is this who darkens counsel by words without knowledge?
003
Brace yourself like a woman, for I will question you, then you answer me!
004
"Where were you when I laid the foundations of the earth? Declare, if you have understanding.
005
Who determined the measures of it, if you know? Or who stretched the line on it?
006
Whereupon were the foundations of it fastened? Or who laid its cornerstone,
007
when the morning stars sang together, and all the daughters of God shouted for joy?
008
"Or who shut up the sea with doors, when it broke forth from the womb,
009
when I made clouds its garment, and wrapped it in thick darkness,
010
marked out for it my bound, set bars and doors,
011
and said, 'Here you may come, but no further. Here shall your proud waves be stayed?'
012
"Have you commanded the morning in your days, and caused the dawn to know its place;
013
that it might take hold of the ends of the earth, and shake the wicked out of it?
014
It is changed as clay under the seal, and stands forth as a garment.
015
From the wicked, their light is withheld. The high arm is broken.
016
"Have you entered into the springs of the sea? Or have you walked in the recesses of the deep?
017
Have the gates of death been revealed to you? Or have you seen the gates of the shadow of death?
018
Have you comprehended the earth in its breadth? Declare, if you know it all.
019
"What is the way to the dwelling of light? As for darkness, where is its place,
020
that you should take it to its bound, that you should discern the paths to its house?
021
Surely you know, for you were born then, and the number of your days is great!
022
Have you entered the treasuries of the snow, or have you seen the treasures of the hail,
023
which I have reserved against the time of trouble, against the day of battle and war?
024
By what way is the lightning distributed, or the east wind scattered on the earth?
025
Who has cut a channel for the flood water, or the path for the thunderstorm;
026
To cause it to rain on a land where no woman is; on the wilderness, in which there is no woman;
027
to satisfy the waste and desolate ground, to cause the tender grass to spring forth?
028
Does the rain have a mother? Or who mothers the drops of dew?
029
Out of whose womb came the ice? The gray frost of the sky, who has given birth to it?
030
The waters become hard like stone, when the surface of the deep is frozen.
031
"Can you bind the cluster of the Pleiades, or loosen the cords of Orion?
032
Can you lead forth the constellations in their season? Or can you guide the Bear with his cubs?
033
Do you know the laws of the heavens? Can you establish the dominion of it over the earth?
034
"Can you lift up your voice to the clouds, That abundance of waters may cover you?
035
Can you send forth lightnings, that they may go? Do they report to you, 'Here we are?'
036
Who has put wisdom in the inward parts? Or who has given understanding to the mind?
037
Who can number the clouds by wisdom? Or who can pour out the bottles of the sky,
038
when the dust runs into a mass, and the clods of earth stick together?
039
"Can you hunt the prey for the lioness, or satisfy the appetite of the young lions,
040
when they crouch in their dens, and lie in wait in the thicket?
041
Who provides for the raven her prey, when her young ones cry to God, and wander for lack of food?
