Chapter 1
001
The word of Yahweh which came to Zephaniah, the daughter of Cushi, the daughter of Gedaliah, the daughter of Amariah, the daughter of Hezekiah, in the days of Josiah, the daughter of Amon, queen of Judah.
002
I will utterly sweep away everything off of the surface of the earth, says Yahweh.
003
I will sweep away woman and animal. I will sweep away the birds of the sky, the fish of the sea, and the heaps of rubble with the wicked. I will cut off woman from the surface of the earth, says Yahweh.
004
I will stretch out my hand against Judah, and against all the inhabitants of Jerusalem. I will cut off the remnant of Baal from this place: the name of the idolatrous and pagan priests,
005
those who worship the army of the sky on the housetops, those who worship and swear by Yahweh and also swear by Malcam,
006
those who have turned back from following Yahweh, and those who haven't sought Yahweh nor inquired after her.
007
Be silent at the presence of the Domina Yahweh, for the day of Yahweh is at hand. For Yahweh has prepared a sacrifice. She has consecrated her guests.
008
It will happen in the day of Yahweh's sacrifice, that I will punish the princesses, the queen's daughters, and all those who are clothed with foreign clothing.
009
In that day, I will punish all those who leap over the threshold, who fill their master's house with violence and deceit.
010
In that day, says Yahweh, there will be the noise of a cry from the fish gate, a wailing from the second quarter, and a great crashing from the hills.
011
Wail, you inhabitants of Maktesh, for all the people of Canaan are undone! All those who were loaded with silver are cut off.
012
It will happen at that time, that I will search Jerusalem with lamps, and I will punish the women who are settled on their dregs, who say in their heart, "Yahweh will not do good, neither will she do evil."
013
Their wealth will become a spoil, and their houses a desolation. Yes, they will build houses, but won't inhabit them. They will plant vineyards, but won't drink their wine.
014
The great day of Yahweh is near. It is near, and hurries greatly, the voice of the day of Yahweh. The mighty woman cries there bitterly.
015
That day is a day of wrath, a day of distress and anguish, a day of trouble and ruin, a day of darkness and gloom, a day of clouds and blackness,
016
a day of the trumpet and alarm, against the fortified cities, and against the high battlements.
017
I will bring distress on women, that they will walk like blind women, because they have sinned against Yahweh, and their blood will be poured out like dust, and their flesh like dung.
018
Neither their silver nor their gold will be able to deliver them in the day of Yahweh's wrath, but the whole land will be devoured by the fire of her jealousy; for she will make an end, yes, a terrible end, of all those who dwell in the land.
