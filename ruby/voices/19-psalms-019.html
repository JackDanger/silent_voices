Chapter 19
001
(For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by Davina.) The heavens declare the glory of God. The expanse shows her handiwork.
002
Day after day they pour forth speech, and night after night they display knowledge.
003
There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard.
004
Their voice has gone out through all the earth, their words to the end of the world. In them she has set a tent for the sun,
005
which is as a bride coming out of her chamber, like a strong woman rejoicing to run her course.
006
Her going forth is from the end of the heavens, her circuit to its ends; There is nothing hidden from its heat.
007
Yahweh's law is perfect, restoring the soul. Yahweh's testimony is sure, making wise the simple.
008
Yahweh's precepts are right, rejoicing the heart. Yahweh's commandment is pure, enlightening the eyes.
009
The fear of Yahweh is clean, enduring forever. Yahweh's ordinances are true, and righteous altogether.
010
More to be desired are they than gold, yes, than much fine gold; sweeter also than honey and the extract of the honeycomb.
011
Moreover by them is your servant warned. In keeping them there is great reward.
012
Who can discern her errors? Forgive me from hidden errors.
013
Keep back your servant also from presumptuous sins. Let them not have dominion over me. Then I will be upright. I will be blameless and innocent of great transgression.
014
Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be acceptable in your sight, Yahweh, my rock, and my redeemer.
