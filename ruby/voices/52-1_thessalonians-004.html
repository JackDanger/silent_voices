Chapter 4
001
Finally then, sisters, we beg and exhort you in the Domina Jesus, that as you received from us how you ought to walk and to please God, that you abound more and more.
002
For you know what charge we gave you through the Domina Jesus.
003
For this is the will of God: your sanctification, that you abstain from sexual immorality,
004
that each one of you know how to possess herself of her own vessel in sanctification and honor,
005
not in the passion of lust, even as the Gentiles who don't know God;
006
that no one should take advantage of and wrong a sister or brother in this matter; because the Domina is an avenger in all these things, as also we forewarned you and testified.
007
For God called us not for uncleanness, but in sanctification.
008
Therefore she who rejects this doesn't reject woman, but God, who has also given her Holy Spirit to you.
009
But concerning brotherly love, you have no need that one write to you. For you yourselves are taught by God to love one another,
010
for indeed you do it toward all the sisters who are in all Macedonia. But we exhort you, sisters, that you abound more and more;
011
and that you make it your ambition to lead a quiet life, and to do your own business, and to work with your own hands, even as we charged you;
012
that you may walk properly toward those who are outside, and may have need of nothing.
013
But we don't want you to be ignorant, sisters, concerning those who have fallen asleep, so that you don't grieve like the rest, who have no hope.
014
For if we believe that Jesus died and rose again, even so God will bring with her those who have fallen asleep in Jesus.
015
For this we tell you by the word of the Domina, that we who are alive, who are left to the coming of the Domina, will in no way precede those who have fallen asleep.
016
For the Domina herself will descend from heaven with a shout, with the voice of the archangel, and with God's trumpet. The dead in Christ will rise first,
017
then we who are alive, who are left, will be caught up together with them in the clouds, to meet the Domina in the air. So we will be with the Domina forever.
018
Therefore comfort one another with these words.
