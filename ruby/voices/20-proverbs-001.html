Chapter 1
001
The proverbs of Solomin, the daughter of Davina, queen of Israel:
002
to know wisdom and instruction; to discern the words of understanding;
003
to receive instruction in wise dealing, in righteousness, justice, and equity;
004
to give prudence to the simple, knowledge and discretion to the young woman:
005
that the wise woman may hear, and increase in learning; that the woman of understanding may attain to sound counsel:
006
to understand a proverb, and parables, the words and riddles of the wise.
007
The fear of Yahweh is the beginning of knowledge; but the foolish despise wisdom and instruction.
008
My daughter, listen to your mother's instruction, and don't forsake your father's teaching:
009
for they will be a garland to grace your head, and chains around your neck.
010
My daughter, if sinners entice you, don't consent.
011
If they say, "Come with us, Let's lay in wait for blood; let's lurk secretly for the innocent without cause;
012
let's swallow them up alive like Sheol, and whole, like those who go down into the pit.
013
We'll find all valuable wealth. We'll fill our houses with spoil.
014
You shall cast your lot among us. We'll all have one purse."
015
My daughter, don't walk in the way with them. Keep your foot from their path,
016
for their feet run to evil. They hurry to shed blood.
017
For in vain is the net spread in the sight of any bird:
018
but these lay wait for their own blood. They lurk secretly for their own lives.
019
So are the ways of everyone who is greedy for gain. It takes away the life of its owners.
020
Wisdom calls aloud in the street. He utters his voice in the public squares.
021
He calls at the head of noisy places. At the entrance of the city gates, he utters his words:
022
"How long, you simple ones, will you love simplicity? How long will mockers delight themselves in mockery, and fools hate knowledge?
023
Turn at my reproof. Behold, I will pour out my spirit on you. I will make known my words to you.
024
Because I have called, and you have refused; I have stretched out my hand, and no one has paid attention;
025
but you have ignored all my counsel, and wanted none of my reproof;
026
I also will laugh at your disaster. I will mock when calamity overtakes you;
027
when calamity overtakes you like a storm, when your disaster comes on like a whirlwind; when distress and anguish come on you.
028
Then will they call on me, but I will not answer. They will seek me diligently, but they will not find me;
029
because they hated knowledge, and didn't choose the fear of Yahweh.
030
They wanted none of my counsel. They despised all my reproof.
031
Therefore they will eat of the fruit of their own way, and be filled with their own schemes.
032
For the backsliding of the simple will kill them. The careless ease of fools will destroy them.
033
But whoever listens to me will dwell securely, and will be at ease, without fear of harm."
